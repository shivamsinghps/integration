IndiaMART Interview Questions

About the Company:

IndiaMART is a B2B online platform that facilitates connections between buyers and suppliers in India. It is currently the second largest B2B marketplace worldwide, behind only Alibaba.

IndiaMART was established in the year 1999 with the goal of simplifying business. The company aims to facilitate seamless business transactions for its users.

IndiaMART was founded by Dinesh Agarwal and Brijesh Agarwal and is headquartered in Noida, India.

The company established a second office in Mumbai in 1998. Their first order was received from Nirulas.

Selection process:

IndiaMART has a 4-step placement process comprising of several rounds. These rounds include:

  • Aptitude Round
  • Group Discussion
  • Technical Interview
  • HR Interview

Aptitude round

The aptitude round is designed to assess a candidate's problem-solving skills. It typically consists of multiple-choice questions that cover quantitative, verbal, and reasoning topics. The aim of the test is to evaluate how well candidates can tackle different challenges.
