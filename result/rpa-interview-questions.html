RPA Interview Questions

Here are some commonly asked questions and answers that you may encounter during an interview for a position related to Robotic Process Automation (RPA).

1) What is Robotic Process Automation?

Robotic Process Automation, or RPA, refers to a type of automation that utilizes software robots to perform tasks that would typically be done by humans. These tasks are usually repetitive and rule-based. RPA enables companies to automate their digital processes without having to make changes to their existing systems or IT infrastructure. This can improve efficiency and productivity by reducing manual labor and streamlining workflows.

Robotic Robots refer to a group of codes that imitate the actions of humans.
Process The process refers to a series of actions that result in a purposeful and significant task.
Automation The term used to describe the actions carried out by robots independently, without requiring any involvement from humans.

3) Write down some benefits of RPA.

Outlined below are some significant advantages of employing RPA solutions:

Faster

Software robots have the capability to undergo operational processes and complete tasks quickly, which makes them advantageous compared to humans. They are efficient and can operate 24/7, thereby reducing work timeframes.

Cost-Effective

RPA robots have the advantage of never needing breaks or time off, which makes them a cost-effective solution compared to human workers.

Accuracy & Quality

RPA bots are known to significantly reduce the risk of human error as they operate according to a precise set of instructions. They maintain consistency in their function which enhances the overall accuracy and quality of business processes.

Increased Customer Satisfaction

RPA solutions are beneficial to companies as they enable faster, precise, and reliable output, thus resulting in improved customer satisfaction.

18) How is Robotic Automation different from screen scrapping or macro?

There is a common misconception that RPA technology is synonymous with screen scraping or macros. However, this overlooks the fact that RPA is a more comprehensive and powerful automation tool. RPA combines the functionalities of both screen scraping and macros, while also including additional features and optimizations. RPA provides a comprehensive solution for automating end-to-end processes.

24) What do you know about the process recorder in RPA?

Robotic Process Automation offers a range of tools for automating various business operations. One of the most useful features is the process recorder, which records the actions performed by humans while executing a digital task or business process. The recorded actions are used to automate the process accurately and quickly, eliminating the need for human intervention.

25) What do you understand by the term 'Process Studio' in Robotic Process Automation?

A process studio is a vital component of an RPA tool that enables users to construct an automated business flow. It incorporates elements like variables, control loops, and other logical components to create a visible and testable sequence of events. Process Management is often used in the implementation of logical rules for software robots. The process studio produces a sequence that shares similarities with the conventional flowchart.

26) What do you understand by the term 'Workflow Design' in Robotic Process Automation?

Workflow design involves creating a visual representation of the code required to automate processes, incorporating tasks that are based on conditions and decisions.
