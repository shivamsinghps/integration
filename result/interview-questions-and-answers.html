Interview Questions and Answers

The content provided contains a comprehensive list of interview questions and answers for over 100 different topics, including HR, programming languages such as C, C++, Java, .Net, PHP, Android, and Hadoop, as well as databases like SQL, Oracle, DBMS, and others. Each topic has a minimum of 20 interview questions and their respective answers.

Here is a selection of commonly asked questions during HR interview sessions:

  • Can you provide some information about yourself?
  • Could you please provide me with the original content so that I can rephrase it for you?
  • Can you tell me the reason for your departure from your previous job?
  • Could you tell me about your areas of expertise or skills that you excel in?
  • Could you please provide me with the original content that needs to be rephrased?
  • Can you please provide your anticipated salary range?
  • Can you evaluate your performance on a scale of 1 to 10?
  • Can you describe some of your personal accomplishments so far?
  • Can you share some of your interests and pastimes?
  • Can you elaborate on how your abilities and skills could benefit this particular organization?
  • Can you describe your personal definition of success?
  • Can you tell me how you became aware of our company?
  • Can you provide a single word that best represents your personality?
  • Can you share some examples of things that tend to make you feel aggravated or irritated?
  • Could you please share a significant error you have made in your life?
  • Can you describe what you hope to achieve from the organisation?
  • Can you provide me with any queries?
