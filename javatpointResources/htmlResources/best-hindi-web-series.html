Best Hindi Web Series

It is a series of non-scripted and scripted online videos usually featured in episodic form on the Internet. It first became known during the late 1990s and started gaining momentum in the early 2000s.

The emergence and rapidly growing OTT platforms have given young actors a chance to showcase their talent and bring exciting, thrilling, and suspenseful stories to the audience.

OTT and Indian Web Series are dominating and capturing India's entertainment industry. Best Hindi Web Series are dominating the list of web series. Here are some of the lists of best web series which are streaming on the OTT platforms:

1. Aspirants

Aspirant is a web series in which the story revolves around three people who are UPSC aspirant. It shows how their friendship affected and presents various aspects of the examination. The web series also gives a glimpse of the life of a serious UPSC aspirant. It is one of the highest rating web series.

  • Starring: Namita Dubey, Shivankit Parihar, Abhilash Thapliyal, Naveen Kasturia, Sunny Hinduja
  • Releasing Date: 7 April 2021
  • Number of Episodes: 5
  • Written By: Deepesh Sumitra Jagdish
  • Music by: Nilotpal Bora, Rohit Sharma, Tusshar Mallek
  • Production Company: The Viral Fever
  • IMDB Rating: 9.7/10

2. Kota Factory: Season 1 and Season 2

It is yet another premium content of the TVF. Kota Factory is shot in Black and White. The series depicts the student's life who are living and studying in a Coaching institute in Kota to clear the competitive examination like JEE (Joint Entrance Examination) and NEET (National Eligibility Entrance Test). They have relatable dialogues in their series that help them connect with the viewer.

  • IMDB Rating: 9.1/10
  • Genre: Comedy, Romance, Drama
  • Composer: Simran Hora, Karthik Rao
  • Writer: Sandeep Jain, Abhishek Yadav, Saurabh Khanna
  • Director: Raghav Subbu
  • Production: The Viral Fever
  • Release Date: 24 September 2021 ( Season 2)
  • Original Network: TVF and Netflix
