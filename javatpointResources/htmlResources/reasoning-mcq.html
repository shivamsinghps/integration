Reasoning MCQ's

1) If NOIDA is written as OPJEB, then what will be the code for DELHI?

  1. EFMAK
  2. EFAMK
  3. EFMIJ
  4. EFMIK

Answer: (c) EFMIJ

Explanation: Each letter in the word NOIDA is moving one step forward and forms the code OPJEB. Similarly, if the letters of the word DELHI moves one step forward, then D will be written as E, E as F, L as M, H as I, and I as J. So, the code for DELHI will be EFMIJ.

2) If AIRLINE is written as ENILRIA7, then RAILWAY will be written as -

  1. YAWLIAR7
  2. YAWLIAR8
  3. YAWILAR7
  4. YAWILAR8

Answer: (a) YAWLIAR7

Explanation: The letters of the word AIRLINE is written in reverse order, and the number on the right side of the coded word is the total number of characters in the original word. So, the AIRLINE will be coded as ENILRIA7. Similarly, the word RAILWAY will be coded as YAWLIAR7.

3) If CAT is coded as PATC, JOY is coded as POYJ; similarly the word WING will be coded as -

  1. PIGNW
  2. PINGW
  3. PGNIW
  4. PIWGN

Answer: (b) PINGW

Explanation: The first letter in the word CAT is moved at the last position, and the alphabet P is placed at the initial position in order to form the code. So, the code for CAT is PATC, and the word JOY is coded as POYJ. Similarly, the word WING will be coded as PINGW.

4) Which number should come next in the series 1, 2, 3, 10, ___

  1. 79
  2. 99
  3. 89
  4. 98

Answer: (b) 99

Explanation: The logic for the given series is the square of the appearing number and addition of 1 at first position and subtraction of 1 at second position. This simply means that -

12 + 1 = 2

22 - 1 = 3

32 + 1 = 10

Similarly, the number after 10 is -

102 - 1 = 99

5) Which number is wrong in the series 2, 6, 15, 31, 56, 93?

  1. 6
  2. 31
  3. 56
  4. 93

Answer: (d) 93

Explanation: In the given series, the difference between the numbers is the square of consecutive integers. That means the difference between 2 and 6 is 4 (22), 6 and 15 is 9 (32), 15 and 31 is 16 (42), 31 and 56 is 25 (52), so after 25 the difference should be 36 (62), but in the given series it is 37. So, after 56, the next number should be 92 to follow the sequence. But the number is 93 instead of 92. So, 93 is the wrong number.

6) The number comes next in the series 12, 36, 109, 329, ... -

  1. 900
  2. 990
  3. 890
  4. None of the above

Answer: (b) 990

Explanation: In the given series there is a increment of *3 + 0, then *3 + 1, *3 + 2, and so on. That means -

12 x 3 + 0 = 36

36 x 3 + 1 = 109

109 x 3 + 2 = 329

329 x 3 + 3 = 990

So, the next number in the series will be 990.

7) Suppose a series is 6, 11, 21, 36, 56, ? the number comes at the place of question mark in the given series is -

  1. 91
  2. 21
  3. 52
  4. 81

Answer: (d) 81

Explanation: In the given series there is an increment of the consecutive multiples of 5, i.e., +5, then +10, then +15, and so on. So,

6 + 5 = 11

11 + 10 = 21

21 + 15 = 36

36 + 20 = 56, and

56 + 25 = 81

So, the next number in the series will be 81.

8) Suppose a series is 1, 9, 25, 49, ?, 121, the number comes at the place of question mark in the given series is -

  1. 91
  2. 21
  3. 52
  4. 81

Answer: (d) 81

Explanation: In the given series there is a square of the consecutive odd integers i.e., 32, 52, 72, and so on.

12 = 1

32 = 9

52 = 25

72 = 49

92 = 81

112 = 121

So, the number that comes in place of the question mark is 81.

9) The number comes after the series 8, 28, 116, 584, ...... -

  1. 3504
  2. 3507
  3. 3508
  4. 3509

Explanation: In the given series the logic is -

(8 x 3) + 4 = 28

(28 x 4) + 4 = 116

(116 x 5) + 4 = 584

(584 x 6) + 4 = 3508

So, the next number in the series is 3508.

12) Which number comes next in the series 1536, 384, 96, ___?

  1. 23
  2. 24
  3. 28
  4. 18

Answer: (b) 24

Explanation: The given series is a simple division series. In this series, each number is divided by 4 to arrive at the next number. Therefore, 24 (96/4) would be the next number.

13) If PINK is coded as 1691411, then RED will be coded as -

  1. 1963
  2. 1854
  3. 1853
  4. 1954

Answer: (b) 1854

Explanation: The letters of the word are replaced by their positions in the English Alphabets. As in PINK, the position of P is 16th, I appears at 9th place, N at 14th, and K at 11. So, it is coded as 1691411.

Similarly, RED will be coded as 1854 as R is at 18th position, E is at 5th position, and D is at 4th position in English Alphabets.

15) Which of the following is the odd number from the given alternatives?

  1. 1090
  2. 962
  3. 626
  4. 841

Answer: (d) 841

Explanation: The number 841 is different from the list. This is because other numbers will be a perfect square if we subtract 1 from them. This means -

1090 - 1 = 1089 (332)

962 - 1 = 961 (312)

626 - 1 = 625 (252)

But,

841 -1 = 840, which is not a perfect square, so it is different from the other alternatives.

32) If the word 'LION' is coded as LMGJ. How is 'MILK' written in that code?

  1. JKFL
  2. KLIM
  3. KILM
  4. IJGK

Answer: (d) IJGK

Explanation: The order of the words is reversed, and then each letter is moved two steps backward. After reversing, LION becomes NOIL, and on moving the letters two steps back, N becomes L, O becomes M, I becomes G, and L becomes J. So LION will be coded as LMGJ.

Similarly, the reverse of MILK is KLIM. On moving letters two steps backward, K becomes I; L becomes J; I becomes G, and M becomes K. So, MILK will be coded as IJGK.

49) What will be the missing letters in the series BKK, DMM, FOO, __, JSS?

  1. HTT
  2. HQQ
  3. HPP
  4. None of the above

Answer: (b) HQQ

Explanation: The first letters of the series are in an alphabetical order in which a letter is skipped between each two letters; B, D, F, H, J. The second and third letters are repeated in each segment, and they are also in alphabetical order with a skipped letter; K, M, O, Q, S. So, the missing letters are HQQ.

50) What will be the missing letters in the series EQ, FS, HW, KA, __, TL?

  1. AL
  2. OF
  3. HP
  4. None of the above

Answer: (b) OF

Explanation: It is a mixed series.

The pattern for the first letter of each segment is;

E + 1, F + 2, H + 3, K + 4, O + 5, T + 6

The pattern for the second letter of each segment is;

Q + 2, S + 3, W + 4, A + 5, F + 6, L + 7

Therefore, the missing pair is OF.
