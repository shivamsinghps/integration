How to Reach Surat

Tourists visiting Surat can take aircraft, trains, or buses because these tourist destinations are provided with a wide range of benefits.

  • By Flight: Surat Airport is a domestic airport located around 12 kilometres from the city centre. This airport serves flights from major Indian cities. Direct flights to Surat are available from Mumbai, Delhi, Ahmedabad, etc.
  • By Train: Surat station has excellent train connections to several nation regions. Surat is located on the Delhi-Mumbai, Jaipur-Mumbai, and Mumbai-Ahmedabad routes, making it easily accessible from India's western and northern areas. Surat is also served by a double-decker train between Mumbai and Ahmedabad.   • By Road: Surat is extremely accessible by road. A 16-kilometre connection roadway connects the city to National Highway 8. A cab would cost you between Rs 10 and Rs 20 every kilometre.
