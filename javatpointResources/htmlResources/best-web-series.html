Best Web Series

If you're searching for a good option for your new watch, you've found the right place. There are several Web series available on the OTT platform, and the streaming platform continues to supply us with good and quality content web series. Today in this article, we will discuss Best Web Series to watch.

1. Chernobyl

With an IMDb rating of 9.5, this tragedy and historical drama stands out among the top online series globally and can be found on Disney + Hotstar. The events of the 1986 Chernobyl nuclear disaster and the Soviet Union's response are chronicled in this HBO limited series. The critically acclaimed series chronicled the events surrounding the Chernobyl nuclear disaster in 1986 and how the Soviet Union was able to recover.

Although the five-episode series, created by Craig Mazin, was praised for its research, many supporters of the Soviet Union alleged that the show was constructed with American propaganda in mind. Despite this, the show's plot is quite compelling, and it is still ranked among the best shows on IMDb.

2. Money Heist

Netflix's most beloved crime drama series is Money Heist, also known as La Casa de Papel. It is widely regarded as the best robbery web series in history. It is based on the story of eight thieves who kidnap or lock themselves in the Royal Mint of Spain to carry out the perfect robbery in its history. This heist has a mastermind who goes by the name of Professor. It's a Spanish criminal and heist drama series with 41 episodes divided into three seasons and five parts with an 8.3 IMDb rating.

3. Peaky Blinders

It's a well-known online series. It is a British television series that focuses on historical fiction and crime drama. The novel is set in Birmingham, England, after the First World War ended in November 1918, and follows a family of criminals of Irish immigrant or Romani descent. It's about the Peaky Blinders gang, their aims, and their boss Tommy Shelby. The series is divided into five seasons, each with 30 episodes.

4. Sacred Games

Sacred Games is one of the greatest online shows that effectively conveys action, crime, and drama. The narrative revolves primarily around Sartaj Singh, a Mumbai cop, and Gaitonde, a gangster. You'll be taken on a trip filled with mystery, suspense, deeper legal corruption, and lots of violence and action, all of which make it enjoyable to watch. The entire tale is intended as a part of a game or a play, and it keeps you engaged until the conclusion of each episode with much excitement.

Sartaj Singh has kept his integrity and discipline in the middle of all the turmoil of corruption, killings, and deception, but he somehow finds himself connected to the culprit. After getting possible mafia threats, this police officer makes every effort to protect Mumbai from disaster. This is the best online series for people of all generations, with a cast that features Saif Ali Khan, Radhika Apte, Nawazuddin Siddiqui, and others.

5. Special Ops

Another of Hotstar's best-known and well-filmed series is Special Ops. The best part is that you see Kay Kay Menon portray a role he has never performed before. The story is told through the eyes of Himmat Singh, who must contend with two decades of patriotic incidents and some covert intelligence operations in the background.

6. Vikings

This crime drama and action-adventure series, with six seasons and 89 episodes, falls within the historical epic and action-adventure genres. It's a drama series set in the past. The plot revolves around the legendary Viking Chieftain Ragnar Lothbrok and his squad. He is a ferocious warrior and a descendant of Odin, the god of battle and warriors. It also narrates the story of his family and sons that how rises to the position of king of the tribes. IMDb gives it an 8.5 out of 10 rating.

7. Narcos

Chris Brancato, Carlo Bernard, and Doug Miro Created this fantastic criminal thriller American drama series. The narrative of this series revolves around Pablo Escobar, a well-known drug dealer who had become a billionaire overnight via the manufacture and selling of cocaine. It is available on Netflix and contains 30 episodes separated into three seasons. This American historical crime drama television series has an IMDb rating of 8.8.

8. Rocket Boys

This drama Series follows India through three key decades in its history as it strives to become a great, courageous, and independent nation. Homi J. Bhabha and Dr. Vikram Sarabhai are two exceptional guys whose tale is portrayed in Rocket Boys. Jim Sarbh, Ishwak Singh, and Regina Cassandra feature in the film.

9. Scam 1992

Money is what keeps the world running. This web series is based on the story of Harshad Shantilal Mehta. The only Indian series to climb to the top of IMDb's list of best TV series is Scam 1992. This Web Series is set in Bombay during the 1980s and 1990s and follows Harshad Mehta, a trader who led the stock market to new heights before witnessing a catastrophic decline.

The Scam: Who Won, Who Lost, Who Got Away, written by reporter Sucheta Dalal and Debashish Basu in 1992, is based on the Indian stock market scam committed by stockbroker Harshad Mehta in 1992. Hansal Mehta's Sony LIV series, starring Pratik Gandhi, aired in 2020 and set a new bar for digital programming in India. Pratik Gandhi's superb performance is deserving of appreciation. This show became a huge hit in very few days, and Pratik Gandhi became popular overnight. SonLIV currently hosts this online series.

10. Game of Thrones

Many TV dramas and online series have come and gone, but the spine-chilling effect that Game of Thrones provides is still unrivalled. For almost a year, this online series has been regarded as one of the best. The series is set near Westeros, a mythical continent. The plot of this series begins when King Robert comes to the northern castle Winterfall to inform Ned Hand of his need for a right-hand helper after his last hand died under mysterious circumstances.

David Benioff and D. B. Weiss developed this American fantasy and serial drama television series based on the novels A Song of Ice and Fire. With an IMDb rating of 9.2, it is among the top online series globally and can be seen on Disney+ Hotstar or HBO Max. There are eight seasons and 73 episodes in all.

11. Breaking Bad

It's a fantastic series that you should see. The plot of this series revolves around a chemistry professor who becomes a vicious participant in the local methamphetamine drug market after being diagnosed with lung cancer and needing to provide for his family.

The series dramatically altered the television series landscape. Breaking Bad has it all: action, thrills, and drama, with plenty of mind-blowing moments. The neo-western, criminal, and serial drama genres are all represented in this American television series. Vince Gilligan developed the show, which spans five seasons and 62 episodes. It is available to watch on Netflix. It has a 9.4 IMDb rating, making it one of the finest online series in the world.

12. Stranger Things

This three-season, 25-episode American television series combines sci-fi, horror, and drama. The series revolves around a little child named Will Byers who goes lost near a top-secret government laboratory. Following that, his friends, family, and the local police are looking for him. They become entangled in a complex mystery involving secret government experiments, mystical powers, and a mysterious young girl. It's a fantastic online series.

13. Prison Break

It is a five-season, 90-episode American action and crime drama television series. The series revolves around two brothers named Lincoln Burrows and Michael Scofield. Burrows has been given the death penalty for a crime he never committed due to a political conspiracy; now, his only faith is his brother Scofield, who sees it as a goal and sends himself to the same prison, only for both of them to be released. There are five seasons of this series.

14. Friends

It's a popular show that follows the work and home life of six friends who reside in Manhattan. In this series, the pals have dealt with family, love, drama, friendship, and humour. It also depicts several characters' problems with their passion.

This American television sitcom, created by David Crane and Marta Kauffman, continues to rule people's hearts. It consists of 236 episodes and is separated into ten seasons. It has an IMDb rating of 8.8 out of 10 and is considered by viewers to be one of the top online series in the world. Netflix or HBO Max have the show available to stream.

15. Bard of Blood

Sharukh Khan's production company, Red Chillies Entertainment, produced a seven-episode online series Bard of Blood. Set in Pakistan's Balochistan province, the drama deals with cross-border terrorism and pits renegade Indian operatives against Pakistani intelligence agents. Emran Hashmi stars as a humiliated intelligence operative and goes to the field to rescue agents kidnapped by the Taliban.

16. Gullak

This Hindi family drama web series, Gullak, is refreshingly humorous and reminds us of our fond childhood memories. Gullak is a TVF web series that uses humor and drama to depict the life of an Indian middle-class family.

Gullak is an adorable, hilarious, tragic, and binge-worthy web sitcom series starring Geetanjali Kulkarni, Jameel Khan, Vaibhav Raj Gupta, and Harsh Mayar. A series tell Gullak of incidents in the life of Mishra (middle-class family in focus). You can watch this series on Sony Liv.

17. Aspirants

Another online series, Aspirants, is about friendships and is one of the best series to watch. This series got a lot of attention and praise for its realistic narrative. Naveen Kasturia, Shivankit Singh Parihar, and Abhilash Thapliyal play three buddies preparing for the U.P.S.C. Civil Services Examination. Through the journey of three friends, the online series depicts the challenges of U.P.S.C. aspirants. The focus is on their relationship and its determination in the face of difficulty. Relive the golden days of solid friendships by watching this episode on T.V.F.'s YouTube channel or T.V.F.'s specialized site.

18. The Family Man

The finest Hindi online series, "The Family Man," starring Manoj Bajpayee, is the best online web series to watch. It revolves around a middle-class man who works covertly for the T.A.S.C., a fictionalized department of the National Investigation Agency. The show is a drama and suspense-filled Indian online series.

This web series, developed by Raj and D.K., is a spying an investigative thriller. The audience observes an ordinary man struggling to reconcile his family and national duty. True occurrences served as inspiration for the web series' plot. The online series also includes a critique of domestic terrorism and a radical view of Muslim terrorism's legitimacy; of course, Manoj Bajpayee's consistently great performance will keep you riveted to the screen for the series duration.

Sherlock is one of the best web series to watch, and on 25 July 2010, the first episode of this series came. When the show gained popularity, some fans wore a "Sher-locked" T-Shirt. Sex Education, a popular British comedy-drama series, stars Asa Butterfield as an anxious adolescent and Gillian Anderson as his mother, a trained ex-therapist. This online series, which is narrated like a modern sex-ed course, is widely recognized as one of the greatest in the world. Sex Education succeeds in combining the best of both worlds: it is highly fun and engaging, making it ideal for binge-watching with friends.

This series will take you on a new adventure if you enjoy romantic tales. A pretty high school girl has little experience with love, so one of his classmates shows her how to make passionate love. You'll discover some new methods to make love in this series as Otis enlists the help of a classmate to help her with sex education. The tale revolved entirely around the classroom. Every season, however, brings a fresh answer to Otis' many issues. From erotica to the internet, there's a lot to talk about. John Huges tells her all she needs to know to reach the fantasy's edge. This romantic series is available online in three seasons. In each of the 16 episodes, a tale teaches fellows about sex education.

21. 13 Reasons Why

This drama is about a vibrant young girl who unfortunately murdered herself. The narrative continues when one of his peers discovers a mystery package containing her stuff. He obtains some Hannah recordings and falls in love with her voice, despite having a crush on her. These recordings expose the enigma behind her suicide for 13 reasons. In 39 episodes, 13 reasons for her death are revealed. You must have it immediately if you want to know why a beautiful young girl committed suicide. Three seasons are crammed into this lovely and fascinating drama.

22. The office

NBC aired nine seasons of The Office from March 24, 2005, through May 16, 2013, a sitcom set in Scranton, Pennsylvania, which followed the fictional Dunder Mifflin Paper Company, workers. This American sitcom is centered on the daily life of office workers and has a lot of mockery and humour. It is accessible on Peacock and Amazon Prime and consists of 201 episodes separated into 9 seasons. With an IMDb rating of 8.9 out of 10, it is regarded as one of the top online series in the world.

23. The Witcher

You must have a strong sense of action sequences if you've finished all of the 'Game of Thrones' chapters. The Witcher, regarded as one of the top online series in the world, features several intimate scenes and an epic medieval storyline.

This is a fantasy story based on short tales and books. The witchers' 'Titular' is at the center of the story. The story revolves around a struggle between a monster and a brave hero. It's terrifying, romantic, and action-packed. This eight-episode series is accessible on Netflix even though the second season of Witcher is also best to watch.

One of the greatest TV shows of all time, this miniseries, created by Tom Hanks and Steven Spielberg, aired in 2001 with 10 episodes, is still regarded as one of the best overall. The series is set during World War II and follows a group of American soldiers. With a large ensemble cast, each episode focuses on a different soldier's journey Damien Lewis, and Ron Livingston starred in the show.
