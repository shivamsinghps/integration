Manipal Academy of Higher Education

India's leading private university is the Manipal Academy of Higher Education (MAHE), also known as the Manipal Institute of Technology (MIT). NIRF 2021 gives MAHE a university ranking of 7th place and an Overall ranking of 15th place. Major changes have been made to MAHE's undergraduate, master's, and doctoral degree programs and post-doctoral programs.

MAHE was formed in 1993 as a deemed university. The Manipal Academy of Higher Education offers over 300 UG, PG, and Ph.D. degrees. It is possible to enter seven UG degrees at MAHE Manipal via lateral entry admissions. Fellowship and Ph.D. programs are available at the university's research level. As part of the admissions process at MAHE, students are required to take a national-level entrance exam and demonstrate their academic qualifications to be considered for the program's admissions requirements. The institution is listed between 751 and 800 in the QS World University Rankings 2022. The university's QS Asia rankings rose from 239 to 230 in 2021. Additionally, the institution has been ranked highly by NIRF, The Week, and Outlook.

Additionally, the institution has been ranked highly by NIRF, The Week, and Outlook. MAHE has campuses in Mangalore, Bangalore, Dubai (UAE), and Melaka, Malaysia (Malaysia). The world-class facilities at MAHE make studying a joyous experience. More than 250 universities throughout the globe, including the Universities of Birmingham, Boston, and Dublin, collaborate with MAHE. Both Asia's biggest anatomical museum and the world's largest life sciences library are located at one location.

Admissions to Manipal University

Undergraduate and graduate programs are also available for enrolment at this institution. Admission to 80 courses is available via M.A.H.E. Applicants must meet the prerequisites for admission. For certain courses at the Manipal Academy of Higher Studies, students must take the university's M.E.T. test, while for others, entrance is based solely on their high school grades. Additionally, the university will conduct a one-on-one interview with those who have been shortlisted for an interview. Several courses at M.A.H.E. are open to students who have taken the national-level entrance test. Candidates seeking admission to Manipal University must fill out the application form accessible on the university's website.

M.A.H.E. Undergraduate Admissions

The Manipal Academy of Higher Education offers a bachelor's degree in architecture, a bachelor's degree in economics, a bachelor's degree in pharmacy, and a bachelor's degree in various other fields. Students will be admitted to B.Arch programs depending on their N.A.T.A. scores. Following the Personal Interview, a round of counseling will be held to determine eligibility for certain M.A.H.E. undergraduate programs.

Master's Admissions

There are a variety of postgraduate degrees offered by universities, including the M.ARCH, M.Com, M.D, M.Lib, M.PHARMA, M.PHIL, M.Sc., M.Tech., M.A., M.A.S.L.P., M.OT, M.Optom. (Practitioner), M.B.A., M.C.A., M.C.H., M.D.S., ME, M.H.A., M.P.H., M.P.T., MS, M.S.W., etc. Admission to the M.Arch program is based on students' performance on standardized tests. A combination of an entrance exam and an interview is required for consideration for admission to various programs at the Manipal Academy of Higher Education. Candidates interested in applying to Manipal can visit the university's official website: manipal.edu.

Manipal University UG & PG Eligibility

  • Admission to the B.Pharm program is based on 10+2 and MET scores.
  • There are two levels of selection for BDS: a 10+2 level and a merit-based one.
  • The prerequisites for applying to the B.Arch program are a passing grade on the qualifying test and an intermediate level of education.
  • Admission to BTech requires a 10+2 level and MET exam marks.
  • MBBS 50 percent of marks are required at the intermediate level, and selection is based on the NEET test.
  • To be admitted to the M.Tech program, applicants must have a bachelor's degree in engineering and a high score on the MET test.
  • A bachelor's degree and scores on the CMAT, CAT, MAT, or MET exams are required for admission to an MBA program.
  • Admission to the M.Pharm program requires a bachelor's degree and an interview.
  • Admission to an M.Com. The program is based on performance on the qualifying test.

Placements

Placement cells at colleges and universities all around the country are dedicated to making it easier for students to find internships and jobs once they graduate. Every year, the placement cell of MAHE organizes a recruitment drive. With the help of the placement cell, students are guaranteed the greatest number of job prospects possible. Manipal Academy of Higher Studies hosts a placement fair where employers from a wide range of industries are allowed to exhibit. Employees from those firms are provided with tables or booths to collect resumes and business cards to be exchanged. Before the placement fair, firms are asked to participate in a pre-placement presentation, followed by an interactive session. Students at Manipal University may also apply for summer internships via the university's placement unit.

MAHE's placement report (NIRF report) for 2019-20 shows that 679 students of UG 3-year programs are qualified for placements. Six hundred and seventy-nine students chose to go to college, while another one hundred and seventy-one were put in other organizations. MAHE 2019-20 placements gave a median compensation of Rs. 3,00,000 to students enrolled in three-year UG programs.

Facilities

MAHE's students and employees have access to a wide range of resources. Facilities at the Manipal Academy of Higher Education include labs, dormitories, cafeterias, sports facilities, etc. Medical emergencies can be addressed at the university's health clinic. Cricket, badminton, and basketball are just a few of the many sports that may be played at Manipal Academy of Higher Education's complex. Students can also use the university's indoor swimming pool and table tennis courts. Girls and boys are housed in different blocks, and each block has a predetermined capacity. For students' convenience, there are a variety of lodging alternatives available. An auditorium, seminar rooms, lecture halls, classrooms with cutting-edge technology, and a video conference room are just some of the amenities available at Manipal Academy of Higher Education.
