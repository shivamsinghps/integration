Aliah University

Formerly known as Mohammedan College of Calcutta, Aliah University in Kolkata is often referred to as Madrasah-i-Aliah or Calcutta Madrasah. The University offers U.G., P.G., and Ph.D. programs, including B.A., MA, B.Sc, M.Sc, B.Tech, M.Tech, etc., that provide education in arts, science, and technology. All courses require entrance exams, which the institution itself administers. Applicants can also submit WBJEE or GATE exam results for B.Tech. or M.Tech. Admission. Admission applications must be submitted online. University graduates are hired by organizations like Amazon, Tech Mahindra, Capgemini, I.T.C., Berger, H.C.L., and others.

Admission to Aliah University

Admissions at Aliah University are primarily entrance-based. For course admissions for most programs, the University administers its Aliah University Admission Test (AUAT) test. Most major courses, including B.Tech, M.Tech, B.Sc, and M.Sc, are entrance-based programs. The following entrance exam results are accepted for various courses:

  • AUAT, WBJEE: B.Tech and B.Tech (Lateral)
  • B.Ed: Admission Test + Merits
  • MA, MCA, M.Sc, MJMC, LLM, M.Sc: AUAT BBA + MBA: AUAT M.Tech: GATE

How to Apply

  • Check out the official webpage.
  • Select Admission.
  • Publish the application form.
  • Fill out all the fields accurately, indicating the course you want to enroll in, upload your scanned photo and signature, and then select the location where you want to take the AUAT exam.
  • Students who fill out an online application can use a debit or credit card to pay the application cost.
  • Select "Submit."

What is AUAT?

Aliah University administers the online entrance exam known as the AUAT (Aliah University Admission Test). It determines who gets into different undergraduate, graduate, and five-year integrated programs. Before registering for the exam, candidates are urged to review the AUAT Eligibility Criteria. Candidates can only access the AUAT Application Form online.

University Placement

The cell regularly works to place students by coordinating with various businesses and organizations. A certain proportion of pupils were already successfully placed in the industry. It actively solicits business feedback and relays it to the appropriate departments, which trains students to follow the feedback, improving their employability. The institution also continues to hold various seminars and workshops to prepare first-year students in the corporate sector for their first jobs. Among the notable employers that frequently attend the University's recruiting sessions are Amazon, Tech Mahindra, Capgemini, I.T.C., Berger, H.C.L., Wipro, Sketch, Byju's, and many others.

Facilities at Aliah University

  • Rich Library: The Aliah University library contains a collection of rare works on Islamic Jurisprudence and Law, Islamic Theology, and Arabic, as well as many more textbooks, reference books, magazines, and journals on various themes linked to the courses.
  • Holistic Student Development: The University places a lot of emphasis on areas where students can advance and acquire knowledge and skills that will be useful in their future employment. Also offered here are several personal development courses.
  • High-End Hostel Accommodation: A large number of students can be housed in an eight-story hostel facility. The rooms are nicely furnished, tidy, and clean, providing the students with the bare necessities. The only criteria used to admit the pupils is their merit.
  • Placement Services for More Excellent Career Opportunities: The University occasionally welcomes some potential employers to provide students with good employment opportunities.
  • Programs for Doctorates are Available: Students who want to continue their studies can take advantage of the University's doctoral programs in various fields.
  • Facilities for Research and Development: The University also offers facilities for Research and Development to deserving students.
  • Student Motivation with Awards and Accolades: The occasional awards, prizes, and accolades inspire the students to put forth even more effort and attention to their studies.
  • Scholarships for Deserving Students: Deserving students receive scholarships following the University's policies. Thanks to this, they have been released from the grip of financial responsibility for the duration of their schooling.

MA, M.Sc., L.L.M., and MJMC PG Admissions at Aliah University

For M.A.s, M.A./M.Sc. in Geography and M.A./M.Sc. in MJMC, L.L.M.s, M.A.s. in Urdu, M.M./M.A.s. in Theology and M.Sc. in Statistics & Informatics, or 2-year M.Sc. in Mathematics:

  • Candidates who have completed a three-year degree program and received the minimum needed marks in the relevant honors or major topic from an accredited university may apply.
  • Candidates who have completed Part I and Part II exams, or an equivalent grade when combined, in their respective Honors or Major subjects from any recognized University are also eligible to take the AUAT; however, their admission will be revoked if they do not receive an overall score of 40% on Part III/Final Examination For L.L.M.

For L.L.M.

To be eligible to apply for an L.L.M., a candidate must have completed a 3-year L.L.B. program or an integrated 5-year L.L.B. program with at least a 45% overall grade point average.
