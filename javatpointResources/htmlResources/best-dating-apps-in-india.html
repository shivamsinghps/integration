Best Dating Apps in India

Regular development and updates in technology bought a radical change in dating someone in India. Those traditional dates are gone, and people are using new ways called dating apps on their smartphones to make their friends or find love within a few clicks.

The dating apps are designed to help single people to chat with new people and find happy relationships. With these apps, users can reach millions of others across geographies with the same interest and liking. You may also find people near your local area. Some apps also provide several free features like unique icebreakers to start long conversations while meeting single people.

Install a good dating app for yourself by creating an engaging profile about your hobbies, habits, tastes, interests, etc.

Once you create your profile on your preferred dating app, you may look for a casual partner or search for your soulmate. If you wish, you can give info about whether you are a smoker, have kids, want to date someone who has kids, and lots more. This info helps you to find your exact match with people. Millions of people found their right match with similar interests and tests on using these apps.

Some of the best dating apps in India are listed below:

  • Tinder
  • Bumble
  • OkCupid
  • Happn
  • Hinge
  • TrulyMadly
  • Woo
  • Badoo
  • POF (Plenty of Fish)

Tinder

The Tinder dating app is one of the most popular dating apps, widely used in India. The primary reason for its popularity is that it provides complete privacy protection, easy to get register, and simple to use.

To start using a Tinder dating app, first of all, you need to log in via your Facebook Id, or if you don't want to use your Facebook, then register it by filling all the details that the app asks about. Once you complete the registration from your side, you will receive a verification code from Tinder in your phone number. Enter this verification code to finish the registration process and get started with Tinder.

The Tinder app is uniquely designed using an algorithm that makes the matching process easy and connects with your partner and a person within a short time, even less than 2 minutes. It also allows you to search people based on filters.

The report says that it has more than 100 million active users, and each user spends an average of 90 minutes in a day. The Tinder dating app is more like a social app and includes mutual friends, like-option, etc. If someone likes your profile or sends you a match request, you will get a notification.

Tinder is available in both free and premium plans. However, the free plan offers limited features and match options. On the other hand, the premium user will enjoy more dating options.

Download Tinder for Android.

Bumble

Bumble app is one of India's widely used dating apps that lets you make online new friends and purposeful relationships. You can use this app to make new friends, expand your professional networks, or make partners. One of the exciting features of this app is that when a match is found, a female initiates the conversation. In this app, female members play a vital role. It means the male user can't be allowed to send a message first. If a female user doesn't initiate the conversation before 24 hours to their match request, it disappears.

One of the most critical features of the Bumble app is that no one can allow creating a fake profile. The app verifies the profile photo while creating an account on it. Bumble dating app focuses on finding matches and facilitates building healthy social relations with other users.

Bumble allows its user to find their match by applying top filters, including date, BFF, and Bizz. Here, you can make a video chat as well as regular text chat to your match. Apart from all the above features, the app maintains the security and privacy feature, where users can block unmatched profiles and even report if you find them as suspicious.

Download Bumble App for Android

OkCupid

OkCupid online dating app helps you to make a match based on who you are, what you love, and in which you show your interest. OkCupid is also widely used in India among youngsters. It is a unique dating app on which matches are not based on pictures. It finds the user's true match based on a series of questions. The questions ensure the similarity between the matches.

In this app, users can quickly get connected with others by sharing their common interests. Not only this, you can search and find your match across your preferred geo-location. Its local dating features help you to find and meet your match nearby your location. Here, you can make a text chat and flirt, make a video call, and set a meetup date.

Download the OkCupid app for Android

Happn

Happn is a dating app that facilitates you to find everyone you have crossed a lane with. When you cross a lane with a Happn user, their profile appears on your Happn application. If you find their profile interesting, like it in secret. Other users who cross you won't know anything unless they show interest in your profile. If they also show interest in you and like your profile back, congratulations, that is your match. Now you can make a video call or text chat with them.

The Happn dating app is free to use with its essential features. If you even want to access its more advanced features, you have to subscribe to its premium plan. The premium plan lets you access a list of other people who liked your profile and a lot more features.

The Happen app works on the principle of reciprocal: you will not get any messages from anyone unless you show interest. You will also not receive any irritating and unwanted messages from scammers and bots. The app algorithm firmly refuses the login of scammers that makes it one of the safest dating apps. The app will always enable your current location to show other users that you crossed paths with.

Download Happn for Android

Hinge

A Hinge dating app is mainly for persons who prefer to like and show their interest over physical appearance. The app is uniquely designed to learn your interests, pictures, and your likes. Based on the user interest type, you can better understand the other person and contact them later. It is better to connect and meet a person having a similar interest. Hinge helps you to find your perfect match over it. Even if you are new to it, the app works on your preferences and shows consistent results.

The app is available for free as well as in paid subscription. The paid plan starts from around $12.99 per month, and it offers some advanced features which are not available in its free service.

Download the Hinge for Android.

TrulyMadly

TrulyMadly dating app offers unique features along with a perfect algorithm that suggests matches on your interest. It also integrates a robust privacy and security policy that ensures it is free from scammers. Using the TrulyMady dating app, you can not only find your true soulmate, but also you can build your strong social network by finding friends, connecting with compatible people, etc. While doing the profile registration, make sure to give correct details, the app makes proper verification. Once the profile verification completes, you can search dates and like other profiles in which you are interested. When both use each other's profiles, then a match has been made. The app contains a feature known as a trust score. Profiles having a higher trust score have chances to get the right match.

Download TrulyMadly for Android.

Woo

Woo is another online dating app that is widely used among educated professionals. It also includes some exciting features such as direct messaging, question-answer, tag search, and voice intro. The app is always concerned about privacy policy. Woo dating app helps its users to meet similar and like-minded people. Using its in-built voice call system, you can talk to your match without disclosing your contact number. It is one of the safest dating sites, especially for women, and it doesn't share any personal information like phone number or location with their match.

Download Woo for Android.

Badoo

Badoo dating app is a social network that gives you a platform to match and find a partner or chat with other people and find a close friend. This app is available in more than 47 different languages across more than 190 countries in the world. You may find features similar to the Tinder dating app and lots of other features in this app.

To use the Badoo dating app, you first need to register on it by providing information such as your interest type, preferences, location, etc. The app offers several dates based on your interest and preferences. You can even get a list of all available dates based on your nearby location. You can meet people here based on nearby location, verified profiles (three-steps verified), and make a video chat to your matches.

The Badoo usage guidelines mentioned that they do not tolerate any inappropriate behavior.

If you honestly want a severe long-term relationship, then activate the Badoo premium plan. In the premium feature, you will get lots of exciting features missing in its free plan.

Download Badoo for Android

POF (Plenty of Fish)

POF is also termed Plenty of Fish, one of the best and most free online dating apps for Android. The app is designed to help single people to find happy relationships. With this app, users can reach millions of others who have the same interest and liking. You may also find people near your local area. The app provides several free features to meet singles people and contains unique icebreakers to start long conversations.

Plenty of Fish's free dating app is different from other dating apps such as Tinder or Badoo. POF app provides a complete experience and also focuses on what you are precisely looking for. To use this app, you have to register on it by providing information about your hobbies, habits, tastes, etc.

Once you create your profile on the POF (Plenty of Fish) dating app, you may also give the other info that helps you to find your exact match with people. It also allows you to search people based on filter options such as age, intent, ethnicity, and body type.

The POF dating app allows you to send messages to anyone free of cost. But it doesn't mean that the app doesn't ask you about money. If anyone likes your profile, the app notifies you to upgrade your account to see who it is. Upgrading your account to premium allows you to access a list of the newest users, and also you can unlock the extended profile of all other users. The premium plan starts at $19.99 for a single month.

Download Plenty of Fish for Android.
