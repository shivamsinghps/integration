Top 10 Hotel Management Colleges in India

The hospitality industry offers a wide range of career paths and opportunities for those who enjoy working with people and providing them with the assistance they need. One of these options is a career in hotel management, which comprises operating and overseeing a hotel establishment.

Aspirants in the hotel/hospitality management field must have a strong personality and outstanding communication abilities. India is currently seeing phenomenal growth in the hospitality management industry, mostly due to its reputation as one of the greatest worldwide travel destinations.

The tourism and hospitality industries have become more internationalized, which has benefited the Indian economy by creating a number of attractive job opportunities in these industries.

So here is the list of top 10 Hotel Management colleges in India to gain skills and expertise in this field:

  1. Institute of Hotel Management Catering and Nutrition, Pusa, New Delhi
  2. Welcomgroup Graduate School of Hotel Administration (WGSHA), Karnataka
  3. Institute of Hotel Management, Hyderabad
  4. Institute of Hotel Management, Banglore
  5. Institute of Hotel Management, Chennai
  6. Institute of Hotel Management, Lucknow
  7. Banarsidas Chandiwala Institute of Hotel Management and Catering Technology, Kalkaji, Delhi
  8. Institute of Hotel Management, Kolkata
  9. Army Institute of Hotel Management and Catering Technology
  10. Institute of Hotel Management, Mumbai

1) IHM, Pusa

The Institute of Hotel Management, Catering, and Nutrition is one of the leading institutions in the hospitality and catering industries, and it is situated in Pusa, New Delhi. It was founded in 1962 to offer top-notch undergraduate and graduate education. IHM Pusa has been ranked No. 1 by GHRDC in its annual ranking of hotel management schools in India for the academic year 2021-22. It received the Best Institute for All-Round Performance award.

Both online and offline application processes are available for admission to all courses. Admissions are based on merit and entrance exams, with NCHMCT JEE rank being weighed. Prestigious catering businesses, the hotel industry, and organizations like Fortune, Samsung, and PVR Cinemas hire graduates.

Placements By IHM Pusa

IHM Delhi continuously oversees recruitment efforts. Placement drives are organized for undergraduates pursuing the degree, PG Diploma, Certificate, and HSR programs at IHM Delhi.

IHM Delhi studies show that placement rates have been 100% during the past year.

  • There were around 40 companies who came for placement and the top Companies that visited IHM, Pusa for hiring include :
  • Hyatt
  • ITS
  • Oberoi Hotel & Resorts
  • PVR Cinemas
  • Fortune

Facilities at IHM Pusa

IHM Pusa offers its students cutting-edge infrastructure and amenities. The facilities at the Institute are as follows:

  • Hostel - There are 12 rooms at a young ladies' inn and 24 rooms at young men's lodging, totaling 36 rooms. Based on AIR in the NCHMCT JEE, the hostel is assigned. Students at the hostel can take advantage of 24-hour tea/espresso service, frozen yogurt, and non-mixed beverages.
  • Sports Complex - Facilities include a badminton, basketball, tennis, volleyball, and table tennis court.
  • Library: The library offers access to both internet and a large collection of books and cutting-edge technology.

Faculty of IHM Pusa

IHM Pusa, one of the best hotel management colleges in the nation and is renowned for having a highly competent faculty chair with years of expertise in teaching and supporting students.

2) WGSHA

This is a private higher education university. It was founded in 1986 and is called the Welcomgroup Graduate School of Hotel Administration (WGSHA). Moreover, it is located in Manipal, Karnataka.

According to rankings of all foreign institutions, WGSHA is the second-best privately owned institution in India for hospitality education and ranks 26th overall. It offers a four-year full-time BHM programme in addition to postgraduate degrees in M.Sc, MHM, and M.A.

Placements By WGSHA

The college invites various businesses for student placements in UG and PG degrees. Students at WGSHA can take advantage of a wide range of job opportunities thanks to the alumni network at the school.

Hotels like the Taj Group, Oberoi Hotels, Marriott Hotels, Qatar Airways, Domino's, ITC Hotels, and many more made deals with students in 2021. Students often received 5LPA as part of their annual packages. It began with the 4LPA package.

Facilities at WGSHA

The college offers its students the following facilities and opportunities:

  • Hostel: Both boys and girls can stay in hostels provided by WGSHA. Hostels are required for first-year students and run about INR 1,20,000 per year for standard rooms and INR 1,45,000 for AC. There are available rooms with attached bathrooms with air conditioning and those without. Breakfast, lunch, high tea, and dinner are served daily.
  • Senate: A student-run body that actively participates in all activities and makes suggestions for improvements to the curriculum.
  • Counseling: Each faculty member has 15-20 students under their instruction for counseling and practical instruction.
  • Industry Connections: WGSHA is the top option for aspirant students due to connections with the top names in the hospitality sector, such as Welcomgroup, the hotel business of ITC Ltd.
  • Collaborations On a Global Scale: The International Hotel Association, Paris, has also acknowledged the WGSHA's training and development program.

Faculty of WGSHA

College professors have the extensive industry knowledge and have worked in the field at a high level.

Since the college is a part of ITC Limited, they also have instructors visiting from the hotel chain ITC Group. Aside from that, they have technologically advanced study spaces and smart classrooms. Over 50 instructors at the college hold Bachelors's and Master's degrees in addition to their Doctoral Degrees. They have extensive training.
