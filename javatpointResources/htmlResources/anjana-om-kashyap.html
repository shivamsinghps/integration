Anjana Om Kashyap

She is an Indian journalist and anchor working in the India Today Group. She is a senior executive editor in Aaj Tak. She began her profession by joining Doordarshan, public broadcasting, and later moved to the Zee News before coming to Aaj Tak; she also worked for News 24. Kashyap grew up in a middle-class family with Omprakash Tiwari. Om Prakash Tiwari's father worked as a doctor in the Indian Army. He was in the Army for a short service commission. He served during the 1971 War.

Kashyap had been to Loreto Convent, a local Catholic School, and shifted to Delhi Public School, Ranchi. She holds an honors degree in Botany from the University of Delhi. She gave an All-India Pre-Medical Test and did not pass. Kashyap is a fierce debater and showed strong leadership skills during her childhood. She was the head girl in both schools and had taken the role of President of her college hostel. For post-graduation, she enrolled in the Delhi School of Social Work, and Kashyap says that this instilled the values of activism into her. Her initial job was as a counselor at Daewoo motors, but she left the company after working for one year. Later she enrolled in the NGO taken up the role of a legal counselor.

Career in Journalism

Kashyap had opted for Journalism in 2000 from Jamia Milia Islamia University. In the Doordarshan channel, she was asked to the news desk of Aankhon Dekhi. It was an investigative show, but she was given infrequent reporting duties. Later, she shifted to Zee News and wanted to take a full-time role, but the channel found that she had insufficient speaking finesse and gave her producing roles. She qualified for auditions easily and came as an anchor in a special feature show. She moved to News 24 in 2007 and asked to moderate the evening debate show as an anchor.

Nature of Journalism

Nikita Saxena had written a long piece on the nature of Kashyap journalism, and she was highly critical of her reporting. She noted Kashyap's aggressiveness and propagation of Hindutva and partisan reporting in favor of the Bhartiya Janta Party in several situations. A news portal called scroll. in has found that friendly news anchor for the ruling party.

Kashyap was one of the few reporters who got permission to interview Prime Minister Narendra Modi just before the 2019 Parliamentary election. Critics point out that she did not throw any challenging questions at the Prime Minister. She quizzed the Prime Minister to his comfort.

When the Supreme Court of India in 2019 was about to pronounce to verdict in a dispute related to the Ayodhya title. Her debates were highly partial, and they seemed in support of pro-Hindutva. She was seen seeking answers from the Muslim community to suffice themselves with 5 acres of land. Her news headlines were provocative, and she ended up welcoming the decision of the Supreme Court.

When encephalitis broke out in Bihar in 2019, she was seen in haranguing with the on-duty Pediatrician of Sri Krishna Medical College and Hospital. She was heavily criticized for this kind of attitude. The Indian Medical Association filed a complaint to gain bad publicity.

Personal Life

She is married to Delhi, Andaman, and Nicobar Islands Cadre IPS (Indian Police Service officer) Mangesh Kashyap. She met Mangesh during her days at Delhi University. Her husband had been Additional Deputy Commissioner of the Delhi Police and served as Chief Vigilance Officer in the South Delhi Municipal Corporation. Kashyap is a mother of a son and daughter.

Controversies

  • Anjana Om Kashyap was in controversy over the descendants of the Rani Padmavati. He made the statement during a heated argument between Rohit Sardana. Her remarks were the present-day female descendants of Rani Padmavati themselves speak in English and have modern lifestyles, so they hardly morally possess anything to give suggestions on the right portrayal of Rani Padmavati. She was heavily trolled on the social media site for the comment.
  • India Today Group came under heavy fire when its Journalist asked the question to Smriti Irani during the university of Delhi visit. She was addressing a gathering of students, and the journalist cum anchor asked her about her appointment as Human Resource Development Minister in the Union Cabinet by Prime Minister Narendra Modi despite having controversies over her degrees and not being so young. The nature of the question enraged the HRD Minister, and it was not well received by the audience. Kashyap was severely criticized for not speaking against his fellow journalist cum anchor. People accused her of being a mute spectator.
  • She landed into another controversy after interviewing Samajwadi Party chief Akhilesh Yadav. This interview happened during the 2022 Uttar Pradesh Assembly Election. In that interview, Kashyap had lost her cool, and Samajwadi Party had banned its spokesperson from appearing on its shows.
  • She also became a talking point when her off-record comment got aired. She made an unusual comparison of Aditya Thackeray with Rahul Gandhi. Later Anjana made a formal apology on its part; however, Aditya did not respond and showed maturity and dignity.
  • When Mangesh Kashyap became the CVO of the South Delhi Municipal Corporations, it was said that he received it due to the influence of Anjana Om Kashyap.
