HCL Company

HCL Full Form

HCL is an acronym for "Hindustan Computer Limited." It is a service-oriented company operating all over the world.

What is HCL?

HCL became an independent firm in 1991 when it joined the software services industry. HCL has a global Investment in research and development, "Innovation Labs," and "Delivery Centres" network. HCL has 44 offices worldwide, including the UK, the US, France, and Germany.

The Forbes Global 2000 list includes HCL Technologies. With a market valuation of $21.5 billion as of May 2019, it is one of India's top 20 publicly listed companies. HCL has a total workforce of 147,123 people.

The company's combined revenue, including its subsidiaries, was $9.3 billion as of September 2019.

History of HCL

It was one of India's early information technology enterprises created in 1976. It is a survey of modern computing, including the launch of 8-bit microprocessor computers in 1978.

Shiv Nadar launched a personal computer company in 1976 with a group of six engineers who were all former Delhi Cloth & General Mills workers. The members of Nadar's team included Arjun Malhotra, Ajai Chowdhry, DS Puri, Yogesh Vaidya, and Subhash Arora. Nadar and his colleagues began selling digital calculators to acquire funds for Micro comp Limited's main product.

Following this, on August 11, 1976, the company's name was changed to HCL or Hindustan Computers Limited. Then, on November 12, 1991, HCL Overseas Limited was established as a technology development services provider. It started operation after establishing a business on February 10, 1992.

Two years later, in July 1994, the firm changed its name to HCL Consulting Limited and subsequently to HCL Technologies Limited in October 1999. HCL Technologies is one of HCL Corporation's four subsidiaries, HCL Infosystems.

After that, HCL introduced HCL Healthcare in February 2014. HCL TalentCare is the company's fourth and most recent program.

HCL Technologies originated as the research and development arm of HCL Enterprise, a corporation that helped India's IT and computer sectors expand and develop.

In 1978, HCL Enterprise created an in-house microcomputer, followed by a networking operating system and client-server architecture in 1983. On November 12, 1991, HCL Technologies was formed as a distinct company to provide software services.

HCL Technologies was founded in the United Kingdom as HCL Overseas Limited. The organization's name was changed to HCL Consulting Limited on July 14, 1994. The firm was renamed HCL Technologies Limited on October 6, 1999, to represent its activities properly.

Who is the founder of HCL?

HCL was founded by Shiv Nadar. Shiv Nadar is a millionaire industrialist and social activist from India. His companies include HCL and the Shiv Nadar Foundation, of which he is the founder and chairman. Nadar was born in Mullapoji, a village in Tamil Nadu's Thoothukudi district, around 10 kilometers from Tiruchendur.

HCL was founded in the mid-1970s by Nadar, who transformed it from an IT hardware company to an IT enterprise over the next three decades. In 2008, Nadar received the Padma Bhushan award for his services to the IT industry.

Nadar's friends called him Magus, which means magician in Old Persian.

Who is the CEO of HCL?

C Vijayakumar is the CEO of HCL. Since 2016, C Vijayakumar has been the CEO of HCL Company. CVK and Vijay are other names of him. C. Vijayakumar began his career with HCL Technologies as a senior technical engineer on Comnet's original startup team in 1994.

Vijay has taken on a diverse range of roles in sales, technology, delivery, and operations. In July 2016, he was named the company's chief operating officer (COO). He is now in charge of directing the company's worldwide business operations, executing HCL's business plan, and guaranteeing operational excellence.

Vijay was the company's COO and chairman of HCL's infrastructure services segment until becoming CEO in March 2015.

C Vijayakumar is considered one of the first to introduce the remote infrastructure management (RIM) concept, which has since grown into a multibillion-dollar commercial area in the IT industry.
