Donald Trump

If we talk about the most powerful country globally, America is the country whose name is taken first. America is not only a powerful country today, but it is also a very developed country. Today America has the world's most powerful army and the world's largest economy. Most of the business in the world is done in US dollars only.

And whoever becomes the President of this powerful country is thought one of the most powerful President in the world. And becoming the President of a country like America is a big achievement in itself. In this article, we will talk about the 45th president of America, Donald Trump.

About

Donald Trump was the President of the world's most powerful country America. America has had 46 Presidents so far, out of which Donald Trump took over as the 45th President of America in 2017 and continued as the President of America till 2021. There are two parties in America, one is the Democratic Party, and the other is the Republican Party. So, in 2017, he was the candidate of the Republican Party, and he also won this presidential election.

Although everyone knows him by the name of Donald Trump, his full name is not Donald Trump, but it is Donald John Trump. Donald Trump is not only a politician but also a successful businessman, media personality, and television presenter. Donald Trump is a very successful entrepreneur. You will be surprised to know that Donald Trump does not take any intoxicants. Donald Trump says that he never drank alcohol, never took drugs or smoked cigarettes. Donald Trump has been one of America's richest presidents.

Early Life

On June 14, 1946, at Jamaica Hospital, Donald Trump was born. And this hospital is in Queens, and Queens is part of New York City. The largest city in the United States of America is New York., and it is one of the most important cities in America. Donald Trump was born into a very rich family, due to which he did not have to face any money-related problems in his early life.

Donald Trump was the fourth child of his parents. Fred Trump was his father, and Mary Anne McLeod Trump was his mother. Donald Trump's father was a successful businessman and real estate developer, and he was also the head of the Fred Trump Organization. Fred Trump and Mary Anne McLeod Trump have five children, out of which 2 daughters (Maryanne Trump Barry, Elizabeth) and 3 sons (Robert Stewart Trump, Fred Trump Jr, Donald John Trump) are included.

Educational Career

As we know that Donald Trump was born in a very rich and royal family, so due to this, Donald Trump did not have much interest in studying. Donald Trump's first school was Kew-Forest School in Queens, New York City. After this, when he turned 13, he was sent to New York Military Academy in Cornwall. Then he continued his further studies at the New York Military Academy. He was also a good player; of baseball; due to this, he was also appointed as the captain of the school's baseball team.

After studying at the Military School, Donald Trump went to Fordham University in 1964. This university is located in New York City; after this, Donald Trump was transferred to the Wharton School of the University of Pennsylvania in Philadelphia, Pennsylvania, two years later, in 1966. And Donald Trump completed his graduation in economics from here in 1968.

Business career

If we talk about his business career, Donald Trump was very successful in his business. Donald Trump made a lot of wealth and money through his business. After completing graduation in economics, Donald Trump decided to join his father's business.

Trump first worked in his father's real estate company. He became the company's president of The Trump Organization in 1971. In 1978, Donald Trump gained popularity by launching his family's first Manhattan property, the derelict Commodore Hotel. He worked in many real estate businesses. In his starting career, Donald Trump bought many Casinos and Golf Courses.

It was also said that Donald Trump took a lot of loans from the bank, and by 1990, he was completely immersed in debt. But Donald Trump got himself out with all these troubles and problems with his intelligence and understanding. Apart from this, Donald Trump has appeared in many films and television programs.

Political Career

Donald Trump has been a part of many political parties such as the Independence Party, Reform Party, Democratic Party, and Republican Party. In 1999, Donald Trump decided to enter into politics. He started his political career through Reform Party. Donald Trump wanted the Reform Party to make him a presidential candidate in the 2000 presidential election. But he took out his name from the presidential election of 2000 in February 2000 due to some reason.

After this, Donald Trump decided to focus on his real estate business. But in the year 2012, he decided to enter into politics again. Donald Trump again wanted to be the presidential candidate in the 2012 presidential election, but he supported Mitt Romney and did not contest the 2012 presidential election. Trump had put his desire to become President before everyone during the 2000 and 2012 elections. But at that time, Trump's desire to become President was not taken very seriously by anyone. But Trump always wanted to be America's President.

But Trump had made up his mind to be the candidate for the 2016 presidential election. In 2015, when he got the ticket from the Republican Party, he declared himself as the Presidential candidate of the 2016 presidential election. But his campaign was not taken seriously by political analysts. Most people thought that Trump would not be able to win this election because his opponent was Hillary Clinton. Hillary Clinton is the wife of 42nd US President Bill Clinton. And everyone thought that Hillary Clinton would be America's new President.

But Donald Trump proved everyone wrong. He defeated Hillary Clinton in a strong contest and became America's new President. He became the 45th President of America. Trump again nominated himself for the 2020 presidential election against Joe Biden, but Trump could not win in this election, and Joe Biden became the new President of America after defeating Donald trump in the 2020 presidential election. So, in this way, Donald Trump was the President of America from the year 2017 to 2021. Donald Trump has done a lot for America in these 4 years, but some decisions for Donald Trump were also quite controversial.

Marriage and children

First Marriage

If we talk about Donald Trump's marriage, he has done three marriages. Donald Trump had his first marriage in the year 1977. His first wife's name was Ivana Marie Trump. By profession, Ivana Trump is a businesswoman, media personality, fashion designer, author, and former model. Donald Trump and Ivana Trump divorced in 1991-1992. Donald Trump and Ivana Trump have three children. His first child's name is Donald John Trump Jr. He was born in 1977, by profession; he is an author, businessman, American political activist, and former television presenter. His second child's name is Ivanka Trump. She was born in 198, by profession she is a businesswoman. And his third child's name is Eric Frederick Trump, and he is a businessman, activist, and former reality television presenter. He was born in 1984.

Second Marriage

Donald Trump was Divorced from Ivana Trump in 1992, and in the year 1993, he had his second marriage with Marla Ann Maples. She is a singer, model, and television personality. Donald Trump has a daughter from this marriage named Tiffany Trump. Tiffany Trump was born in the year 1993. But this marriage also did not last long, and they decided to separate from each other. And after only 7 years of his marriage to Marla Ann Maples, in 1999, Donald Trump got divorced from Marla Ann Maples.

Third Marriage

After divorcing Marla Ann Maples in 1999, Donald Trump had his third marriage with Melania Knauss in 2005. Melania Knauss is more famous with the name Melania Trump. By profession, she is a model and businesswoman, and she is the first wife of Donald Trump, who from 2017 to 2021 served as the first lady of the United States of America. Donald Trump has a son from this marriage named Barron William Trump. Barron Trump was born in 2006. So, in total, Donald Trump has 3 wives and 5 children; apart from this, the story of many affairs of Donald Trump also keeps coming into the public.

Conclusion

Donald Trump is a very positive person. Even though he was born in a rich family, he has achieved great heights in his life due to his hard work. When he was the President of America, he did a lot for the development of America, and he is considered the richest President in the history of America.
