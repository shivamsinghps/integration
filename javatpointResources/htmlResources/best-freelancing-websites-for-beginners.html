Best Freelancing Websites for Beginners

Freelancing is a form of self-employment. It is an online portal where you provide services for a fee. Since you do not work only for a specific business, you can work anywhere with a dedicated internet connection. There is the liberty to stay anywhere. As a freelancer, your major goal is to deliver high-quality services on time.

Many freelancing websites are available where you can get projects and work from home easily. You can earn money through freelancing whether you are a student, a working professional, or anyone who wants to earn extra income.

The necessary thing is that if you want to start freelancing, then you must have a skill. You will also need determination and self-motivation. This article discusses more than 25 freelancing websites that can help you earn extra money online.

Following is the list of best freelancing websites for beginners:

1. Fiverr

Fiverr is a marketplace that offers freelance services. It is a site where you can sell or buy services. If you want to gain additional income, you can offer your skills and expertise to people worldwide.

Whether you are a graphic designer, lawyer, painter, teacher, entrepreneur, writer, or just someone who is good at something, then Fiverr could be for you. You can also begin your online business on Fiverr.

Key points of Fiverr:

Following are the some key features or points of Fiverr:

  • It was launched 12 years back in 2010 by Micha Kaufman and Shai Wininger.
  • It provides jobs for Logo Design, UX Design, Icon Design, Illustration, Pattern Design, App Design, Social Media Marketing, SEO, Guest Posting, Video Marketing, Music Promotion, Email Marketing, Resumes Writing, Script Writing, Proofreading, Animation, Audio Editing, Beat Making, Podcast Production, WordPress Developers, Game Development, Web Developer, User Testing, Sales, Financial Consulting, Project Management, Virtual Assistant, Online Tutoring, Dance Lessons, Cooking Lessons, Data Entry, Data Engineering, Data Processing, and many more.
  • Freelancers have already earned more than $2 billion on this platform.
  • It has 400 million registered employers.
  • Its payment options are PayPal, Credit/Debit Card, Google Pay, Apple Pay, Giro Pay, Wire Transfer, iDEAL, Apple Pay, Sofort, OXXO, and Boleta.

2. Freelancer

Freelancer is one of the biggest freelancing websites on which you can sell your services. This website provides jobs like Software Engineering, Education & Training, Translation, Data Entry, Website Design, Package Design, Logo Design, Sales & Marketing, Mobile App Development, 3D Modeling, Product Design, Graphic Design, Illustration, WordPress Developer, etc.

You can do freelancing through this website and earn extra income. If you are a fresher, then you can search for freelance jobs on this marketplace and start your career. It is a reliable and safe marketplace. It provides 24/7 support to customers.

Key points of Freelancer:

  • It was founded 13 years ago in 2009 by Matt Barrie.
  • There are more than 1800 categories of jobs available here.
  • It provides jobs for Graphic Designers, PHP Developers, App Developers, Logo Design, Content Writing, Photo Editing, Video Editing, Game Development, Software Engineering, Product Manager, Digital Marketing Managers, Customer Service, and many more.
  • There are over 63 million users registered on this platform.
  • More than 22 million jobs have been posted on this platform.
  • Its payment options are PayPal, Credit Card, Bank Deposit, or Skrill.

3. Upwork

Upwork is a global freelancing platform where businesses and freelancers from all around the world can connect and collaborate on certain jobs. The company's vision is to connect great talent to work without limits.

You can earn money from this platform if you have any skills. It is open to all firms, agencies, professionals, and freelancers. It is a platform with no boundaries, as clients can post a job, and freelancers can bid on it from anywhere in the world.

Key points of Upwork:

  • It was founded seven years ago in 2015 by Srini Anumolu, Beerud Sheth, Sanjay Noronha, Stratis Karamanlakis, and Odysseas Tsatalos.
  • It is a top-ranked platform, and more people are joining it daily.
  • It has jobs for more than 10,000 skills in 90+ categories.
  • It provides freelancing jobs for SEO, SEM, Social Media Managers, Email Marketing, PHP Developers, Java Developers, JavaScript Developers, Data Scientists, Graphic Designing, UX/UI Designing, Animation, Web Designing, Translators, Content Writing, Data Entry, Virtual Assistants, Accountants, Engineers, and more.
  • Its payment options are Discover, Visa, Diners Club International, Diners Club, American Express, and Master Card.

Toptal is a platform that hires developers and programmers from among the most qualified freelancers. The company emphasizes quality over quantity in its available coders. They allow only the most talented applicants to be offered a job opportunity. Toptal has become one of the best marketplaces for developers and coders. Once selected by Toptal, people from different companies provide you with work that you can do from anywhere and deliver on time.

Key points of Toptal:

  • It was founded 12 years ago in 2010 by Taso Du Val and Breanden Beneschott.
