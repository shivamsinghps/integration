Advantages of iPhones

The advantages of iPhones are as follows:

  • iPhones are user friendly
  • Due to its best features and consistent performance, it is the best smart phone.
  • In respect of performance, all the iPhones work the same in contrary to other smart phones that work differently.
  • The software and hardware of the iPhones are better than other smart phones.
  • iPhones have an iOS operating system which is smoother and faster than other smart phones. One of the major advantages of this operating system is we can update it contrary to other smart phones that do not provide this facility.
  • The security levels of iPhones are also very high as apple ensures its security, and it is not easy for hackers to access its data.
  • The connectivity and networks of iPhones are also faster and smoother than other smart phones.
  • Further, apple ensures the battery life of iPhones as it works longer than any other smarts phone's battery.
  • Its screen size also makes it a better choice than other smart phones.

Disadvantages of iPhones

  • The iPhones camera's megapixels have lower than its equivalent smart phones.
  • Its small speaker affects its quality.
  • High cost
  • The memory of iPhones is not expandable.
