Guru Gobind Singh Indraprastha University

Guru Gobind Singh Indraprastha University (GGSIPU), also known as I.P. University or IPU, is ranked 77 among the top universities in India by the NIRF 2022 rankings. IPU has UGC approval and NAAC accreditation with a grade of "A." I.P. University offers a wide variety of U.G., P.G., and research-based programs. The University provides top-notch instruction for a very affordable price, with I.P. University BBA Fees being INR 1.84 Lakhs. Professional courses are highly sought-after and well-liked by students. The BCA program at I.P. University is also well-liked by the students. In addition to these, the University offers a variety of additional courses, including an MBA, LLB, and many others.

All courses are open to applicants who have received an adequately qualified score on either the national entrance exam or the University's entrance exam, the IPU CET. Students who want to enroll at GGSIPU must get the minimum marks required by the University's IPU CET Cutoff score. Students interested in being admitted to I.P. University must go to the University's official website because only there will an online application be made available. The selection procedure is merit-based for a few courses.

The placement cell at GGSIPU is effective and well-run and works hard to connect job seekers with employers in their professions and industries. During the hiring drive, top companies visit the University. Companies like Oracle, Facebook, Google, and Microsoft give internships and jobs to top university students. The greatest CTC was available for 32 LPA (INR). Prestigious companies hire 94% or so of the students.

IPU offers scholarships to deserving students from underprivileged classes with vital academic records. The University assists students who fall under the racial or physical disability category. In addition to scholarships, the institution provides facilities for the students' comfort, including housing options, a library, an auditorium, a seminar room, a medical facility, and much more.

About IPU

Following the terms of the Guru Gobind Singh Indraprastha University Act 1998, as amended in 1999, Guru Gobind Singh Indraprastha University (GGSIPU) was the first University to be created. The University is accredited by the University Grants Commission (UGC) of India following provision 12B of the UGC Act. The explicit goals of this teaching and affiliated University are to "achieve excellence in these and related fields as well as other matters connected in addition to that or incidental to that" and to facilitate and promote "studies, research, and extension work in emerging areas of higher education with a focus on professional education, for example, engineering, technology, management studies, medicine, pharmacy, nursing, education, etc. The University formed its vision, mission, and quality policy statements to fulfill the broad objectives for which it was founded. They are as follows:

Vision

To "stimulate both the hearts and the minds of academics, enable them to contribute to society's welfare generally, train them to adapt to the changing demands of the economy, and urge them for cultural leadership to secure peace, harmony, and prosperity for all,"

Mission

Guru Gobind Singh Indraprastha University will put a lot of effort into offering a market-oriented professional education to the student community of India in general and of Delhi in particular by encouraging the establishment of colleges and Schools of Studies as Centers of Excellence in emerging areas of education with a focus on professional education in disciplines of engineering.

IPU Courses & Details

The Guru Gobind Singh Indraprastha University provides several courses in various subject areas. Below are the details of the best techniques:

  • Admissions to Tech and B.Pharm are determined by JEE Main Score, followed by online GGSIPU Counseling.
  • Admissions to BHMCT will be based on NCHMCT JEE results.
  • Candidates who have completed NATA training may apply for the Arch. Final admission is determined by a 50% weighted average of NATA and qualifying exam scores.
  • Admissions to MBBS, BDS, BAMS, and BHMS are made through the All India Level NEET UG exam, which is followed by counseling.
  • BA-L.L.B Admission to BA-LL.B programs will be based on CLAT scores.
  • Admission to the Sc. (Nursing) and B.Sc. (MLT), BA, B.Com, BCA, B.Voc, B.Ed, and BJMC programs require a passing score on the IPU CET exam.
  • GATE is the admissions process for M.Tech.
  • NEET PG scores determine admission to M.D. and M.S. programs, whereas NEET SS scores and counseling determine access to D.M. and Ch programs.
  • IPU CET and GGSIPU Counseling are the basis for Sc., M.Sc. (Nursing), MPT, M.Ed, MOT, MA, and MPH admissions.
  • A passing score on the AYUSH-conducted AIA-PGET exam is required for the PGAC (Ayurveda)
  • Candidates are chosen for Ph.D. programs based on their performance on the CSIR NET, UGC NET, or JRF. The final selection is made after a round of interviews and counseling.
  • There are seats set aside for applicants from specific categories, such as migrants from Kashmir, people with disabilities, and applicants under the management quota. In the case of standard mode, the institution solely offers GATE scholarships.

IPU Application Process

By going to the GGSIPU website, you can fill out online applications for all courses. Online, and adhere to the directions provided.

  • Visit I.P. University's official website.
  • Complete the application by entering your contact information, professional information, and payment information.
  • Upload the desired documents' scanned copies.
  • Check the Declaration box after you have verified the information you have entered on the form.
  • Application form submitted; make a note of User ID and Password.
  • Pay the INR 1,000 application fee with a credit/debit card or net banking.
  • The course information and transaction I.D. will be presented after receiving payment.

Colleges Associated with IPU

More than 120 institutions are affiliated with IPU, 83 of which are self-financed, and 35 are owned and run by the Government of the NCT of Delhi. The University provides 472 courses to about 22,000 students through its associated colleges a year. IPU's main campus is in Delhi's Dwarka neighborhood. It has 14 colleges and universities under its control.

IPU Placements

A single, centrally located career counseling and placement unit manage all placement-related activities at IPU. The University has a placement rate of about 94%. Every year, prestigious businesses from several industries participate in the IPU placement drive. Out of 949 graduates from various postgraduate and undergraduate programs, 500 B.Tech students received a placement, according to the most recent NIRF data. IPU Facilities

  • Library: The University's library houses a vast collection of books. It includes 2,500 volumes of scholarly publications, 338 print journals, a sizable group of electronic resources, subscriptions to 20 newspapers, and many other things.
