The Walt Disney Company

About Walt Disney Company

For over 90 years, the Walt Disney Company has set the standard in providing family entertainment across Europe, the Middle East, and Africa (EMEA). To increase local attachment and trust for the business, its brands, and its personalities, it is concentrated on providing its fans with outstanding entertainment experiences.

The Walt Disney Company carries the Creativity, Innovation, and International Growth worldwide strategy. It provides the finest entertainment with local relevance to all fans and customers where, when, and how they want it. Via a physical presence in 30 countries and more than 7,000 employees (Disneyland Paris employs an extra 16,000 people), The Walt Disney Company EMEA reaches children and families in 133 countries with various channels.

  • Headquarters: Burbank, California.
  • Category: Public Company.

Leadership:

The Walt Disney Company executive team oversees some of the most reputable and well-liked businesses, developing modern content and experiences while embracing new technology and breaking into international markets.

Leadership in EMEA (Europe, the Middle East, and Africa)

A dedication to quality, innovation, and originality. Millions of families worldwide are delighted by the tales, characters, and experiences that The Walt Disney Company executive team's vision and strategic direction create.

The Walt Disney Company Businesses:

Innovating entertainment experiences for kids and families is a top priority for The Walt Disney Company, a leading global family entertainment and media organization, along with its subsidiaries and affiliates. The Disney brand is experienced in the country in various ways, such as through its direct-to-consumer service Disney+, in theatres, on stages, online, on TV screens, and through a large selection of consumer goods offered at mass market shops and through shopDisney. The Disney name is also present in Disneyland Paris, which debuted in 1992 and is the most popular tourist attraction in Europe.

Movies:

Live Entertainment:

Our Live Events company provides consumers with the most immersive, high-quality entertainment, from time-honored productions like The Lion King to fresh experiences like Frozen and Beauty and the Beast.

Media:

In EMEA (Europe, the Middle East, and Africa), the media networks section of our company operates a wide range of direct-to-consumer, digital, and broadcast companies.

Resorts And Parks:

With more than 320 million visitors since it first opened in 1992 and more than 15,000 staff members on-site, Disneyland Paris is the most popular tourist attraction in Europe. Disneyland Paris is the top single-site employer in France and the top private employer in its home area (Ile de France). In France, its operations support 56,000 direct and indirect employees.

Licensing And Retail:

The Walt Disney Company EMEA creates cutting-edge physical products and captivating digital experiences to bring our tales and characters to life, from toys and fashion to music, apps, and games.

The Disney EMEA History:

Consider a business that looks backward more than The Walt Disney Company. Disney proudly carries on its legacy of producing top-notch tales and experiences for every family member, starting with its modest origins as a cartoon company in the 1920s and growing to become the most recognizable name in the entertainment business today.

The strategy of The Walt Disney Company:

  • The Walt Disney Company thinks acting ethically toward families is also ethical toward the company. The Walt Disney Company brand is built on ethical behavior.
  • A market with many options increases the attraction of Disney products. It deepens the relationship with customers who want nothing less from The Walt Disney Company. It raises the value of working for our organization and enables The Walt Disney Company to attract the best and the brightest to join its international cast. It creates positive links among the areas where The Walt Disney Company works. The Walt Disney Company continues to expand and succeed due to all these factors.
  • Six areas focus on The Walt Disney Company's global commitment to conduct business and produce goods responsibly and ethically. These areas are ethical conduct, responsible content, environmental stewardship, community engagement, civic engagement, and respectful workplaces.
  • The Walt Disney Company continues to make significant progress toward its citizenship objectives. It sets new goals for itself regularly to make sure it is always looking to do better.

1. Ethical Behavior:

We conduct our company with honesty, integrity, and conformity with the law everywhere we operate.

Visit the international website of The Walt Disney Company and the Standards of Business Conduct, which describes the position on anti-bribery, anti-corruption, and other business behavior subjects, for additional details on the worldwide strategy and policies on ethical conduct.

2. Responsible Content:

Some of the most loved characters and beloved stories in the history of the world have called The Walt Disney Company home for more than 90 years. Because Disney's themes and characters are inclusive, approachable, and relevant to everyone, the company has a particular place in the hearts of millions of people. The Disney brand has always been welcoming, with stories demonstrating tolerance and acceptance and celebrating the diversity that makes the Walt Disney Company characters unique and individual.

3. Supply Chain Responsible:

The Walt Disney Company EMEA (Europe, the Middle East, and Africa) team is a part of a worldwide organization dedicated to assuring the ethical sourcing of goods bearing the Disney mark as part of our broader corporate citizenship initiatives. By striving to improve working conditions at manufacturing facilities, screening products for safety and integrity, and looking into methods to lessen the environmental impact of our supply chain, we support the ethical manufacture of Disney-branded goods.

4. Stories Matter:

Stories influence how we perceive the world, ourselves, and others. The Walt Disney Company can represent the variety of voices in our world as storytellers, uplifting and inspiring others. The Walt Disney Company is currently reviewing our collection and adding cautions to literature that involves unfavorable representations or exploitation of people or cultures as part of our continued commitment to diversity and inclusion. Instead of eliminating this information, The Walt Disney Company sees a chance to initiate a discourse about history that impacts all of us.

2021 Gender Pay Report From The Walt Disney Company In The UK:

To address and ensure gender equality in The Walt Disney Company workforce, The Walt Disney Company adopts a comprehensive strategy. The Walt Disney Company is proud of the proportion of women it employs across the organization and the fact that it pays and promotes employees under their job duties, experience, and performance.

The Walt Disney Company Commitment:

The Walt Disney Company is dedicated to motivating children to lead better lifestyles by promoting a healthy diet and regular exercise among families using The Walt Disney Company's creative characters and stories. The Walt Disney Company global Healthy Living Commitment, established by the first media firm and linked into The Walt Disney Company EMEA business with European Nutritional Guidelines launched in 2014, was founded in 2006.

These provide families with nutrition choices for regular food and drink while limiting sugar, salt, fats, and portion sizes. The Walt Disney Company seeks to educate and inspire kids about healthy eating through The Walt Disney Company content and products. The Walt Disney Company utilizes Disney storytelling to encourage the family to be more active.

1. Protecting The Environment Commitment:

The Walt Disney Company is dedicated to conserving resources and safeguarding the environment as they run and expand their company. Disney fosters a lifetime appreciation for conservation among kids and families by using the narrative power of volunteerism and local community initiatives.

2. Commitment To Healthy Living:

The Walt Disney Company is dedicated to growing generations with better health.

The Disney Kitchen:

  1. Disney's everyday food and beverage range is organized under one recognizable brand by the package design known as Disney Kitchen.
  2. Disney designed this line of goods to be enjoyable for kids, delicious, and economical. Additionally, items must conform to Disney's requirements to wear the Disney Kitchen design, including the daily dietary recommendations, Disney Kitchen product quality and integrity inspections, and Disney Kitchen international labor standards.
  3. Disney's Healthy Citizenship initiative delivers help beyond motivating and empowering families. Disney provides their time, money, and storytelling in collaboration with regional charities and organizations to help make a difference.

Inclusion And Diversity:

The Walt Disney Company has influenced how people think, which is why it is significant today. Walt Disney's most ambitious goal was to develop a theme park where visitors could experience the wonder of animation and immerse themselves in their favorite films. Disney has evolved to blend in with society to retain a popular franchise, yet it will always be the same business founded with only $20 and a dream.

To provide guests, audiences, viewers, and customers worldwide with unmatched entertainment experiences, Disney employees all around the world put in tremendous effort. The Walt Disney Company is firmly committed to encouraging a vibrant range of views, viewpoints, and life experiences?both on screen and within The Walt Disney Company team.
