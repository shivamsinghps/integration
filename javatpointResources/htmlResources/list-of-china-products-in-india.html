List of China Products in India

The trades between India and China have been going on for more than 2000 years. This trade usually consisted of essentials and goods required for each nation. Talking about the current scenario, the increase in tension between the two nations has led India to boycott many Chinese products. Still, India is dependent on China for various goods.

India imports around 16 % of its import products from China. In 2020, India's import from China was said to be worth $58.71 billion. Major import from China includes raw materials, Iron and Steel, Machinery, Medical instruments, nuclear machines, etc.

The list of major products imported from China are as follows:

  1. Electrical Products and Equipment
  2. Boilers, Nuclear Reactors, and Machinery
  3. Organic Chemicals
  4. Plastic products and plastic
  5. Fertilizers
  6. Optical Apparatus
  7. Photo, Technical, medical Apparatus
  8. Chemical products
  9. Spare parts of Vehicles (cars and motorcycles)
  10. Iron and Steel
  11. Toys
  12. Other accessories

India is a big market for China as India imports a lot of goods from them. What would happen if these products' imports are stopped?

Well, one can imagine that manufacturing would cost more, so one will face inflation on such products. Although India has started its "made in India movement" it would take a considerable amount of time to be completely independent. There will still be some raw products that have to be imported from China because of natural reasons.
