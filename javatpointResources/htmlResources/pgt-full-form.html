  • Ed. or equivalent qualification from a reputable university.
  • Knowledge of Hindi and English on a basic level.
  • The age restriction is 21 years of age.

Post-Graduate Teacher (PGT) Exams

  1. To be chosen for a government school, you must pass several PGT tests. After passing these tests, you'll be able to get employment with a respectable wage and working conditions. Certain qualifying requirements apply to these tests too.
