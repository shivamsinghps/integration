Best Oil for Hair Growth

While you may be unable to do anything about the environmental pollution, dust or sun, you have certain things in your control that can help you prevent hair loss, hair damage, and scalp problems. For instance, using onion hair oil is a tried-and-true Indian treatment that can make your hair grow healthily. Indian Ayurveda has regarded onion oil or coconut oil as the key to thick, healthy hair growth. In addition, some hair care methods, including hair massage, and regular oiling in your hair care routine, may help you resolve most of your hair and scalp problems.

Read on to explore the top hair oil for growth in India. And pick one that best suits your hair type.

1. Coconut Oil

Can you avoid the popular coconut oil if you reside in India? Well, certainly not!

Coconut oil is recommended by doctors for hair growth and is famous, especially among people in the south. It can be found in all country areas, from the north to the south. The oil is high in fatty acids, penetrating deeply into the hair follicles and nourishing the hairline. It's also high in fatty acids, vitamins, and minerals, which are important for hair growth. Coconut oil is excellent for growing hair, making them silky and shiny, and protecting it from heat damage.

2. Olive Oil

Everyone loves olive oil, versatile oil that is beautiful and useful in the kitchen. A blend of vitamin E, antioxidants, and oleic acid is a boon to your hair. Olive oil rejuvenates the hair, nourishes the roots, relaxes the hair, and provides a smooth texture and shine. Thus, making it a fantastic oil for hair growth. Furthermore, it helps to fight dandruff when coupled with lemon juice.

3. Argan Oil

Argan oil is a fantastic option if you are searching for an oil that encourages hair growth, fixes split ends and repairs heat damage. Argan oil is commonly referred to as "Morocco's liquid gold,". Manufacturers produce this oil from nuts of the argan tree, and it has become incredibly popular in the cosmetics industry. This oil has high amounts of antioxidants and vitamin E, which gives this oil ability to improve the health of your scalp and minimize breakage, leading to healthy hair growth. The oil undergoes minimal processing, making argan oil wonderful for hair growth.

4. Tea Tree Oil

Tea tree oil, one of the most well-known essential oils, is now used as an ingredient in various body, skin, and hair products. This hair oil is known for its excellent antibacterial and cleaning characteristics. Furthermore, this is a fantastic oil for growing hair and maintaining thickness since it helps the hair absorb it efficiently and awakens dormant hair follicles. Tea tree oil can help with hair growth and concerns like dryness and dandruff when used regularly.

5. TNW Natural Wash Onion Hair Oil

TNW Natural Wash Onion Hair Oil is chemical-free and does not include mineral oils. Through hair shaft moisturization, the oil deeply nourishes your scalp. It cures split ends and makes your hair frizz-free, reducing hair breakage and fall. Also, the oil is non-sticky and gets absorbed quickly into your scalp. This oil further aids in strengthening, conditioning, and nourishing the hair and scalp. Each of these things helps to make TNW Natural Wash Onion Hair Oil even better for hair growth.

Onion oil contains antioxidants that protect hair from environmental damage and postpones hair ageing, which may help reduce hair fall. Antioxidants also help you get the most out of your hair growth cycle. Fatty acids, minerals, and vitamins in this natural oil help to strengthen hair strands. Furthermore, Vitamin E in flaxseed oil feeds your hair strands and aids in improved anchoring.

6. Men's Red Onion Oil by QRAA

Men's Red Onion Oil by QRAA combines onion oil, other essential oils, and several powerful herbal ingredients that give men a hair boost. The oil has a non-sticky texture. Its natural ingredients promise to nourish your hair and help eliminate dandruff and other scalp problems.

Red Onion Oil and Amla Extract help build healthy, lustrous hair by nourishing the scalp, strengthening the hair roots, reducing dandruff and lice, and acting as a hair conditioner. Reetha Extract, another oil ingredient, helps balance the "tri dosha," which helps prevent dandruff and stimulate hair development. Argan oil hydrates your hair and scalp by reducing hair fall and repairing split ends. And also protects it from the harmful pollutants to which hair is exposed daily.

7. Mamaearth Onion Hair Oil

Mamaearth Onion Hair Oil has updated its formula with this oil, which now maintains the smoothness of your hair and makes it stronger and shinier. This onion-rich hair oil has sulfur, potassium, and antioxidants which may help decrease hair loss and promote hair growth. This oil contains natural ingredients and no toxins or harmful additives. It has a pleasant scent and is non-sticky. Furthermore, the oil is free of silicones, minerals, and colours, making it suitable for both coloured and chemically treated hair.

Onion seed oil may have all it needs to encourage hair growth and minimize hair fall. Its ingredients increase blood supply to hair follicles. Its ingredients and benefits include;

Redensyl: Boosts blood circulation on the scalp and may help to encourage hair growth.

Almond Oil: Nourishes your hair by providing Omega-3 fatty acids, phospholipids, Vitamin E, and magnesium.

Castor Oil: Locks moisture inside the scalp and aids blood circulation, thereby thoroughly nourishing the scalp using proteins, vitamins, and antioxidants.

Amla and Bhringraj: Bhringraj has indeed been known to Ayurveda for increasing blood flow to the scalp, helping to minimize hair fall, boosting hair growth, profoundly moisturizing, and acting as a healthy conditioner for your tresses for a long time. At the same time, Amla helps in premature greying, dandruff and fungal/ viral infections.

8. Beardo Onion Oil

Beardo Onion Oil Concentrate contains a high concentration of red onion oil. The strong onion oil may help with hair development, hair loss, and hair nutrition and add moisture to your hair. This leads to good hair growth. The oil's antibacterial, anti-inflammatory, and antiseptic qualities may help with dandruff and aid in maintaining your scalp's health.

Red Onion Oil Extract: Aids in hair loss prevention and maintains scalp's pH balance.

Sunflower Oil (Sunflower Seed Oil): Its vitamin E with sesamol content helps to nourish the hair from roots to the tips.

9. Kesh King Ayurvedic Onion Oil

Ayurvedic Onion Oil by Kesh King may aid in producing healthy hair growth and reducing hair fall. Onion oil can help with hair loss and thinning and promote a healthy hair growth cycle, thanks to its antioxidants. Besides onion oil, Kesh King Vedic Onion Hair Oil contains 21 unique Ayurvedic herbs. It is cruelty-free and devoid of parabens and silicone. You can use it regularly to help minimize hair loss, dandruff and frizz. Regular application keeps your hair silky and frizz-free.

Bhringraj: Promotes hair growth by activating hair follicles and maintaining hair's natural colour.

Methi: Acts as a healthy conditioner and provides nutrients to your hair.

10. Ningen Chamomile Hair Serum infused with Onion Oil

This hair care serum is produced with excellent Japanese ingredients. Chamomile, Onion, Amla, Rosemary, and Argan extracts are found in Ningen Chamomile Hair Serum. These substances provide a protective coating for your hair, keeping it shinier and more resistant to environmental damage and pollutants. When hair does not damage, they grow better.

Chamomile: Nourishes the hair, keeps them moisturized and frizz-free, and minimizes hair fall.

Rosemary: Helpful for hair health because of its anti-inflammatory effects.

Argan oil: Moisturizes the scalp and hair, protects it from everyday damage, reduces split ends and breakage, and acts as a start-leaving conditioner and de-tangler.

Benefits of Onion Hair Oil for Hair Growth

  • Onion oil contains various compounds that help specific enzymes perform better. In some circumstances, these enzymes work together to minimize hair loss. Your hair growth cycle is effectively optimized as a result of this.
  • Onions contain flavonoids such as kaempferol and quercetin. These include anti-inflammatory, antioxidant, and vasodilatory (blood vessel widening) characteristics, aiding blood flow to the scalp. This aids in the treatment of dry, damaged hair.
  • Onion oil's anti-inflammatory qualities aid in calming your scalp. Moisturization aids in the regulation of scalp dryness, hence minimizing itching.
  • Malassezia furfur is the yeast that causes dandruff. Onion oil's antibacterial and antifungal qualities may aid in reducing its colony and dandruff. So, it is effective in treating dandruff or other scalp and hair disorders.

Things to Keep in Mind Before Buying Hair Oil

  • Clarity about materials is a litmus test for a company's honesty. It's best to buy well-known brands and models or read reviews carefully for newer ones. You can choose from a variety of hair oils. It isn't easy to choose one because each one promises to have unique advantages.
  • Please review the components, ingredients and roles in each one and match them up with your requirements. Check for allergens and adverse effects; avoid mineral oil, parabens, and other potentially harmful chemicals.
  • It would be best to examine the effects and other benefits of oil before "zeroing" in on your choice. Hair oil with active components is more suitable for regular hair problems - such as dryness, hair loss, or curly hair.
  • Prioritize your requirements, not the price. This is when product reviews come in handy. You can read the reviews of the people who have used it and judge the product on that basis.
