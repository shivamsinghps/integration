Emoji Meaning

While texting someone in our daily lives, we use several emojis or emoticons to show our emotions or express ourselves. Emoji offers us an innovative way to express our emotions. We have an emoji for almost every emotion, let see how many we have and what they mean and where we can use them.

What is Emoji?

Emoji are used while "texting" or messaging someone, usually for an informal cause. They are pictograms or smiley mostly used to add emotions to the conversation. They are too similar to the emoticons but are available in various genres, few represent various facial expressions and a few represent everyday items, places, etc.

Origin of Emoji

Emoji word is taken from the Japanese word "E-moh-jee," which means "picture character", it was added to the Japanese keyboard in 1997 but got popular worldwide in 2010. Meanwhile, emoticons were added to the keyword dictionary in 1990 by a Japanese company.

Benefits of Emoji

  • Emojis are used to add emotions to a conversation.
  • Many emojis are added in the keyboard library with a social message.
  • Sometimes, when you don't get words to say, emoji's help express your emotions.
  • It saves your time and does not take much space.

Drawbacks of Emoji

Emoji is misunderstood so many times. Even sometimes, people use them in the wrong mean and sometimes understood in the wrong way, which may cause annoyance among people. Some common drawbacks of emoji are as follows;

  • It may look unprofessional if you use an emoji in your message.
  • It may not fit with the reputation of a brand.
  • It may not render properly, e.g., instead of seeing a heart, the receiver could see a black box or a question mark.
  • It may deteriorate vocabulary, e.g., kids won't learn new words if they overuse emoji in their messages.
  • Some emoji are hard to judge and they may have multiple meanings.
