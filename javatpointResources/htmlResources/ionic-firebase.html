Ionic Firebase

Firebase is a popular tool for mobile and web app development platform. It provides many services to help you in building fast and high-quality apps without managing infrastructure. Initially, this platform was developed by Firebase, Inc. in 2011, and then it was acquired by Google in 2014. It is a powerful Database as a Service (DBaaS) solution, which provides a scalable cloud database to store and sync data for client and server-side development. Firebase is a type of freemium model, not an open-source model. However, you can use its services free until you don't pass the limits of its free tier.
