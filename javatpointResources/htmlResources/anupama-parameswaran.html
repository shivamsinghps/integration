Anupama Parameswaran

Anupama Parameswaran is an Indian actress born in Irinjalakuda in the Thrissur district in Kerala. Anupama's nickname is Anu. She mainly works in the Tamil, Malayalam, and Telugu industries. The actress is well known for her charming looks and impressive acting skills. Moreover, her performance in Telugu movies has earned her fame. Thus, making her very popular in south India. She is famous for her debut role of Mary George in the Malayalam movie Premam, a romantic drama directed by Alphonse Puthran in 2015.

She has played many roles in Southern movies, but there are very few movies in which Anupama played the main role. Some of the movies in which she played the lead roles include;

  • Vunnadhi Okate Zindagi is an Indian Telugu movie directed by Kishore Tirumala in 2017.
  • Hello guru Prema kosame is also an Indian Telugu language movie directed by Trinadha Rao Nakkina in 2018.
  • Natasaarvabhowma is an Indian Kannada language Horror movie directed by Pavan Wadeyar in 2019.
  • Rakshasudu is an Indian Telugu language psychological movie directed by Ramesh Varma in 2019.

Birth Place

Anupama was born in Irinjalakuda in Thrissur district in Kerala on 18 February 1996.

Family Details

There are three members in Anupama's family: mother, father, and brother. Her father's name is Parameswaran Irekkath, and her mother is Sunita Parameswaran. Apart from her parents, Anupama has a younger brother named Akshay Parameswara Anupama loves her brother very much. She often shares photos with her brother on social media platforms.

Educational Qualifications

Anupama completed her schooling at Don Bosco Higher Secondary School in Kerala. After that, she pursued her graduation from CMS College Kottayam in Kerala in Communicative English. However, she later dropped out of college and started her career in acting.

Marital Status

Anupama is not married yet. She gained attention when she expressed her desire to marry Indian cricketer Jasprit Bumrah in 2021. She was in a romantic relationship with him. However, she is currently dating Chiranjiv Makwana, a movie director.

Early Life

Anupama is 26 years old actor. She was born on 16 February 1996. While pursuing her college degree in Communicative English, she realized that she was better at acting and wanted to grow in the acting field. So, she dropped out of college and started her career in acting.
