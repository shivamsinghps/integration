Best Gaming Phones

Here you can argue that almost all smartphones offer gaming features. But if you want to enjoy a better gaming experience on your mobile on which the game runs smoothly without interruption. For this, you can wish for a high processor, physical-style buttons and display having a high refresh rate in your phone.

Thanks to some of the best android games, which are widely played by youths in India and love to enjoy them over high graphic mobile phones. This also forces smartphone makers and chipset providers to build and bring gaming smartphones to the market.

Keeping the requirement of gamers, chipset manufacturers started manufacturing processors that fit for the gaming experience. Some of the current processors that make a better gaming experience are Snapdragon, Dimensity, etc. Not only a processor in a phone that gives all possible gaming experience. To enjoy the better gaming experience users even wish for a high mah battery, high refresh rate screen, device cooling feature, dual stereo speakers, etc. that take the gaming experience to the next level.

A few years ago, the price of gaming phones in India was very high; however, it is available in every price range in the market in today's era. Here, we will discuss the best gaming phone with all price range that you can buy for a better gaming experience.

If you want to buy some low or mid budget gaming phone then lookout best gaming phones under Rs 20000.

  • Asus ROG Phone 5
  • Apple iPhone 12 Pro Max

  • OnePlus 9 Pro

Asus ROG Phone 5

We pick the Asus ROG Phone 5 smartphone as the best gaming phone right now. This phone is uniquely designed, mainly by keeping gamers in mind. It is a full-featured phone that will make your gaming experience excellent. Some of its top-rated features include Snapdragon 888 chipset processor, Adreno 660 PGU, 144Hz screen refresh rate, a new reshaped vapor chamber, and extensive graphite sheets for heat control. The phone also includes a long-life battery with 6,000mAh, 18GB of RAM, and 512GB internal storage.

The Asus ROG Phone 5 comes in different models as ROG Phone 5, ROG Phone 5 Pro, and ROG Phone 5 Ultimate, these all phones differ in their specifications. The general ROG Phone 5 model comes with a ROG light at the back, the ROG Phone 5 Pro offers ROG Vision Color PMOLED display, ROG Phone 5 Ultimate offers ROG Vision monochrome PMOLED display. However, these three phones come with the same front screen and AirTriggers 5.

Apple iPhone 12 Pro Max

As we are talking about the best gaming phone, how can we forget Apple iPhone 12 Pro Max? This smartphone is the latest iPhone model from Apple that gives excellent performance, fantastic camera quality, and lots more. This smartphone comes with a large 6.7 inch OLED XDR display, and the fastest processor Apple A14 Bionic. Currently, Apple A14 Bionic is the latest and fastest chipset (at the time of writing the article) in the smartphone industry. These features make iPhone 12 Pro Max as one of the best gaming phone compilations.

Apple iPhone 12 Pro Max is available with up to 512GB of internal storage along with fast battery charging support, stereo speaker, excellent contrast ratio at 2,000,000: 1, and lots more.

If you are looking for an iPhone that gives the best battery life, you can go for it. However, this device might be expensive; you can also consider its other models such as iPhone 12 and iPhone 12 Pro. iPhone 12 and iPhone 12 Pro both smartphones offer 6.1 inches of OLED display with the same processor A14 Bionic.

OnePlus 9 Pro is undoubtedly one of the best Android smartphones for all-purpose use. The OnePlus 9 Pro smartphone combined great hardware and software, such as a 6.7-inch WQHD display with a 120Hz screen refresh rate. It also contains 10-bit color depth, P3 color gamut, Hyper Touch, and HDR10+ certification.

The OnePlus 9 Pro handset deserves to be one of the best gaming phones, which is powered by Qualcomm Snapdragon 888 processor. The OnePlus 9 Pro phone comes with a 120Hz AMOLED screen display and up to 12GB of RAM, and 256GB of UFS 3.1 storage space. The handset packs also give a 65W wired charging and 50W wireless charging support. However, its battery capacity is one of its weak points. It has 4500 mAh capacity while talking as a gaming phone.

Xiaomi Black Shark 3 series

Xiaomi Black Shark 3 series smartphones are well known for their gaming features. This series includes general Black Shark 3 and Black Shark 3 Pro smartphones. Black Shark 3 handset has 6.67 inches of AMOLED display with 270 Hz touch refresh rate. Shark 3 phones contain Qualcomm Snapdragon 865 processor chipset. Its octa-core CPU and Adreno 650 GPU give excellent gaming experience, browsing, streaming, and many more. Its triple primary camera 64 MP+13MP+5MP and 20MP front camera captures quality pictures. This phone is available in two different variants: 8GB / 12GB RAM and 128GB / 256GB of storage space.

Handset Black Shark 3 Pro offers a better gaming experience as compared to the Black Shark 3 phone. Its Pro device comes with a larger display which has a 7.1 inches AMOLED display. This handset also contains a Snapdragon 865 chipset processor and Adreno 650 GPU. The device contains 8GB of LPDDR5 RAM and 256GB of internal storage.

The main difference between these two handsets devices is that the Pro device comes with a more prominent screen display and a slightly bigger battery. And we can say that Black Shark 3 Pro version is better than the general Black Shark 3 device. One more thing that you might love about Black Shark 3 Pro is its physical shoulder buttons, making it one of the best gaming phones.

iQoo 7 Legend series

iQoo 7 once again came back to India after a year and launched the iQoo 7 series with the name iQoo 7 Legend, which is primarily a China edition. It offers all the possible features that a user wants in a gaming phone. It comes with a Snapdragon 888 chipset processor along with Adreno 660 GPU. It has a 6.62 inch AMOLED display having full HD+ resolution, 120Hz screen refresh rate, and 300Hz touch sampling rate. It also offers a 4096mmsq vapor chamber to maintain the device heating effect and keep it cool.

The Indian version and its manufacturer misses out 125W fast charging and offers a 66W flash charger. Manufacturers claim that its 4,000mAh battery gets fully charged in about 22 minutes. The handset is available in two different variants of RAM as 8GB and 12GB. While buying a smartphone, we consider every single feature of the device. One such feature of this device is its camera. iQoo 7 Legend provides a 48MP primary camera and 13MP ultra-wide lens, and a 13MP portrait lens with a focal length of 50mm, which captures an excellent picture. Its selfies (or front) camera has 16MP.
