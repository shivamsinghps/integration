Top 10 Colleges in Tamil Nadu

Tamil Nadu is among the most literate states in the country of India. This state's average literacy rate is 80.33% in the year 2011, which surpasses the national average rate. Tamil Nadu has been making tremendous achievements in higher education. The Gross Enrolment Ratio (GER) of the state was above the national average ratio, as stated by Higher Education Minister K.P. Anbalagan.

In accordance with the National Institutional Ranking Framework (NIRF), as many as 21 universities and 35 institutions or colleges from Tamil Nadu ranked among the top 100 in the colleges and universities classifications respectively. Following are some best colleges in the state of Tamil Nadu:-

1. IIT Madras

IIT Madras is regarded as one of India's top engineering institutions. The Indian Institute of Technology Madras is recognized both nationally and globally for excellence and competence in technical education, fundamental and applied research, innovation, entrepreneurship, and consultancy in the industrial areas.

The NIRF organization has ranked it first in engineering and also overall. In the QS World Ranking, IIT Madras is rated 94th. The 16 departments, several labs, and research centers of IIT Madras are accessible to both undergraduate and graduate students. Admission to this college's engineering program will be based on the results of the JEE Advanced entrance exam. It is a public technical institute in Chennai, Tamil Nadu founded in the year 1959.

Courses

  • Architecture and planning
  • Banking, Finance, and Insurance
  • Business and Management studies
  • Engineering
  • Humanities and social sciences
  • IT and Software
  • Science

Placement- The highest package in the placements of the year 2022 was INR 1.89 CPA and the average package lay between INR 33 LPA TO INR 50 LPA.

Some of the biggest recruiters like Qualcomm, Texas Instruments, Microsoft, Goldman Sachs, Honeywell, and many more companies participated in the placement drive of the year 2021.

Campus and Infrastructure

A beautiful campus with an area of 620 acres. The Guindy National Park was used to cover a large portion of the campus, which is now home to various species that are uncommon to spot in a busy metropolis like Chennai.

About 300 different tree and plant varieties, a majority of which are native wild species specific to the tropical, dry evergreen flora, may be found on the IIT campus. This institute is a paradise for bird watchers in the city as well.

Other exciting facilities such as-

  • OAT- Open Air Theatre
  • CLT- Central Lecture Theatre
  • Hostels, with all possible facilities, 20 in number where 5 are for girls and the rest for boys.
  • The Students' Activities Center offers first-rate amenities including an Olympic-standard pool for swimming, a fully-stocked gym, the outstanding IIT-Chemplast cricket pitch, a spacious stadium, and a high-tech wood-floored multifunctional indoor stadium. Apart from this, there are many other things that this institute provides under sports culture.
  • 3 temples for worship.

Notable Alumni

Alumni include various names such as Anant Agarwal, Atul Chokshi, Raju Narayana Swamy, Ramesh Govindan, and many more accomplished personalities.

2. VIT - Vellore Institute of Technology, Chennai

It was created under Section three of the UGC- University Grants Commission Act, 1956, and it was launched in the year 1984 as a self-sustained college named the Vellore Engineering College. The Union Ministry of HR Development granted University status to Vellore Engineering College in the year 2001.

The Institution is led by its founder and Chief Executive, Dr. G. Viswanathan, a veteran Parliament member & Minister in the Government of Tamil Nadu state. In acknowledgment of his contribution to India in providing quality education, he was granted an honorary degree by USA, West Virginia University. Vellore Institute of Technology, Chennai was formed in 2010.

VIT was formed with the purpose of offering excellent advanced education learning on par with worldwide benchmarks. It consistently explores and employs new techniques to enhance the quality of university education on a regular basis. The campus boasts an international ambiance with students from around the world. Experienced and knowledgeable instructors are greatly urged to foster the learners. The worldwide standards established at VIT in the area of teaching and research push them on in their persistent quest for success.

Courses

VIT offers both postgraduate and undergraduate programs in full-time mode

At present, in the year 2021-22, this institute offers a total of 39 programs including BBA, BCA, B.Tech., B.Sc, B.Sc in Agricultural Hons., B.Des in Industrial Design, and other programs under UG programs and these courses are of 4 years. Under PG programs institute offers MCA, M.Des in Industrial Design, and M.Tech. and other programs as well, and the duration for these courses is two years.

Placement- This institute has brilliant placement records by offering its students various opportunities to fulfill their career aspirations.

The highest package of the last placement drive was INR 10 LPA and the average stood at INR 6.05 LPA.

Some of the prominent recruiters who attended were Qualcomm, Nokia, Titan, NetApp, PayPal, Philips, VMware, Intel, Media Tek, Danfoss, and many more.

Campus and Infrastructure

VIT campus is stretched over a huge area of 192 acres. A notable aspect of VIT is the well-designed and extensive infrastructure offered for both faculty and students. Fully furnished and well-equipped laboratories embellish every building in the Institution with servers to handle all the data flows. Moreover, the institution is gifted with sufficient elements that enable all sorts of events - Auditoriums, sumptuous open spaces, external stages, and a lot more.

A notable aspect of VIT is the thoughtfully designed and extensive infrastructure offered to students and professors. There are 2 academic blocks in VIT that are particularly specialized in their own certain discipline, namely- AB1 and AB2. Various auditoriums and conference facilities are also available, the main one is the Mahatma Gandhi Auditorium with a seating capacity of 2500+ people.

3. NIT Trichy- National Institute of Technology Tiruchirappalli

NIT Tiruchirappalli, also known as NIT Trichy, is a public research and technical institution in Tamil Nadu, India, not far from the city of Tiruchirappalli. Both governments of India and Tamil Nadu established this institution in the year 1964 as the Regional Engineering College Tiruchirappalli, which was affiliated with the University of Madras.

With the cooperation of the UGC- University Grants Commission, the Indian Government, and the All India Council for Technical Education (AICTE), the college received Deemed University status in 2003. It changed its name to National Institute of Technology (NIT) Tiruchirappalli. The institute is among the institutes of the National Institutes of Technology (NITs) system, a collection of leading Indian technical institutions governed by the Council of NITSER, and has been designated as an Institute of National Importance by the Indian Govt. under the "NITSER" Act, 2007- "National Institutes of Technology, Science Education and Research."

Courses

This institute offers various courses, including almost all streams and majors. With its 17 academic departments, the institution provides 17 doctorate programs, 40 master's programs, and ten bachelor's programs, and it awards more than 1900 degrees yearly.

Admissions are done on the basis of the results of entrance exams such as JEE, CAT, UGC NET, GATE, etc., and counseling rounds are also conducted for certain courses.

Placement- The median package was INR 10.8 LPA last year. The overall percentage was 90 for the UG level and 92 for the PG level. The top recruiters who were involved in NIT Trichy placements in recent preceding years include Oracle, Amazon, JP Morgan, Samsung, Schneider Electric, Bosch, Sapient, Infosys, and many others as well.

Campus and Infrastructure

With a campus area of 800 acres being one of the biggest academic campuses in India, the facilities that this institute provides are-

  • The Orion, lecture hall complex comprises 24 large lecture halls.
  • Central Library
  • Octagon center- a computer central area
  • Hostels- 22 boys hostels and 5 girls hostels, with all necessities available.

Notable Alumni

A few of them are as follows- Natarajan Chandrasekaran (who is a Chairman of the board at Tata Sons and Tata Group), Rajesh Gopinathan (CEO and MD of Tata Consultancy Services), Satya Prabhakar (CEO of Sulekha), and many significant others.

4. CMC- Christian Medical College, Vellore

Christian Medical College, Vellore, sometimes referred to as CMC, Vellore, is a privately owned medical college, hospital, and research facility run by the local Christian community. In and around Vellore, Tamil Nadu, India, this Institute has a network of basic, secondary, and tertiary care facilities.

Dr. M.G.R. Medical University is associated with the institute's constituent college. The first College of Nursing was established at CMC Vellore in 1946, the first successful open heart surgery was performed in India in 1961, the first kidney transplant was carried out in India in 1971, the first bone marrow transplant was carried out in India in 1986, and the first reconstructive surgery for leprosy was performed in the world in 1948. CMC Vellore was founded in 1900 by an American missionary named Dr. Ida S. Scudder (2009).

Courses

The college provides courses at four levels: diploma, ug, pg, and doctorate. Christian Medical College Vellore's academic list comprises 61 courses in all. The institution provides pharmacy, sciences, medical & allied sciences stream programs. A few of the CMC Vellore courses include a Diploma in Clinical Pathology, M.B.B.S., M.D. Generic Medicine, D.M. Nephrology, M.Ch. Pediatric Surgery etc. CMC Vellore courses tuition fees vary from Rs. 1,53,000 to Rs. 11,15,000. The term of the M.B.B.S. course is 5 years. Christian Medical College Vellore PG courses length is 3 years. The college provides all its courses on a full-time basis.

Admission- The institution accepts National Eligibility cum Entrance Test (NEET) results for admission to the M.B.B.S. degree. Christian Medical College Vellore entrance to diploma and pg programs is done obased on marks achieved by applicants in NEET PG.

The institution also accepts NEET PG results for admission to M.Ch Neurosurgery Six Years course. Candidates willing to apply for CMC Vellore admission need to visit the official website of the institution and complete the online application.

Placement- The placement percentage is 70%. The median package was of INR 6 LPA for a student of M.B.B.S. in the year 2020-21. Internship opportunities are quite good here.

Campus and Infrastructure

With a 19- acre campus area, the institution provides a huge variety of amenities to the students. CMC Vellore facilities are contemporary and updated. The institution includes residential facilities for the students. There are separate male and female hostels at the university.

Christian Medical College Vellore amenities include a library, modern gym, canteen, auditorium, etc. The institution includes indoor and outdoor athletic facilities for the students. CMC Vellore campus includes wifi access for the students. A medical facility is also situated at the site. CMC Vellore's amenities list also includes labs, transportation facilities, IT infrastructure, and parking spaces. The amenities create a pleasant atmosphere for children.

Notable Alumni

Some of them are Dipti Gurdasani (British epidemiologist), Mahendra Bhandari (Padma Shri awardee), Nagarur Gopinath (cardiothoracic surgeon and Padma Shri awardee), K.A Abraham (interventional cardiologist and Padma Shri awardee)

10. Tamil Nadu Agricultural University (TNAU)

The Tamil Nadu Agricultural University (TNAU) has its birth from the creation of an educational Agricultural School at Saidapet, Madras, in the state of Tamil Nadu, in the year 1868, and it was subsequently transferred to Coimbatore.

TNAU gained complete duties of education of Agricultural and its research and assisted the State Agricultural Department by providing research findings. The Agricultural College and Research Institute, Coimbatore, was the only institute for education related to agriculture for the entire South India.

In 1958, it was designated as a leading educational center in Post-graduation to Master's degrees and Doctoral degrees. The Agricultural College and Research Institute, Madurai was founded in the year 1965. These two institutions constituted the foundation of the Tamil Nadu Agricultural University when it was formed in 1971. There are almost 16 constituent colleges and 29 affiliated colleges with this university.

In total this university offers around 30+ courses under PG programs.

Under UG programs this university provides 10+ courses and 27+ doctoral programs.
