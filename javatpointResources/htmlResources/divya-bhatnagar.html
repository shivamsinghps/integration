Divya Bhatnagar

Everyone's life is filled with unpredictability, even the lives of famous performers and wealthy businessmen. No matter how important or protected we are, nothing can prepare us for the unpredictability of life, and anything may happen to anybody.

Everything, from unexpected injuries to unexpected deaths, is an unavoidable and essential component of life since it encourages us to develop and teaches us to continue gaining knowledge along the way. These things also show that materialism has no basis and that the circle of life is always moving. Change is the only thing that stays the same in our lives. As a result, we need to make the most of every minute we have and always be prepared for the unexpected.

You must be wondering why all of a sudden, today, we have taken such a philosophical tone. There is a reason, and we will explain it to you. We recently heard about the death of a Bollywood star, Divya Bhatnagar, who was extremely young when she passed away. This motivated us to understand life better. Continue reading if you are curious about the identity of this actress.

Early life

Divya Bhatnagar was a famous Indian actress born in New Delhi, India. She was born on Monday, September 15th, 1986. She was raised by her father, late Vinay Kumar Bhatnagar, and mother, Dolly Bhatnagar. Divya mainly focused on working as an entertainer. However, she was also a creation supervisor. Nowadays, she is well-known for her work in the acting industry as well as in production management. It is said that Divya was a devout follower of the Hindu religion and spent her life mostly in India.

Divya Bhatnagar was primarily famous for her work on TV network shows such as "Sethji", "Shrimaan Shrimati Phir Se", and "Sawaare Sabke Sapne", among others. She started her career with the television program "Chand Ke Paar Chalo" in 2006. She has since appeared in Preeto, Yeh Rishta Kya Kehlata Hai, and many other television shows.

In addition to TV shows, she has also made appearances in Bollywood movies such as "99" and "Sapne Sakar Karo".

Divya Bhatnagar's Career

She made her debut in the acting world with roles in Bollywood films such as "99" and "Sapne Sakar Karo". Although she started with the movies, she later decided to work on television shows. She moved to Hindi TV serials because she was getting unimportant and small roles in the movies. She began her TV career with "Ye Rishta Kya Kehlata Hai", which has been dominating the world of Hindi TV serials for the past couple of decades. The first episode aired on January 12, 2009, and the show is still going strong after more than 3238 episodes have been produced.

The television program, Ye Rishta Kya Kehlata Hai, can be seen on Star Plus. The duo, Jai Kalra and Ram Pandey, are credited with directing this television series that Rajan Shahi produced for Director's Kut Productions.

Her (Divya) outstanding work in the show earned her a role in another show produced by Endemol Productions called "Geet Hui Sabse Parai". This show was broadcast on Star One and was met with praise from the entertainment industry. It was the story of a girl named Geet, who got married because her family wanted her to. However, her husband left her and took all her things, leaving her pregnant.

Divya later relocated to Delhi from Mumbai, where she met the man she would marry at her place of employment. She left the serial "Geet" later because of scheduling problems, and Khushboo Thakkar took her place.

After some time, she appeared in the television shows "Sanskar-Dharohar Apnon Ki" and "Sawaare Sabke Sapne Preeto", both broadcasted on Zee TV. Her work in both these serials was praised as she played it well.

How did Divya Bhatnagar die?

At the SevenHills Hospital in Mumbai, one of the most well-known television actresses, Divya Bhatnagar, exhaled her last breath. The shocking announcement sent shockwaves across the whole of the entertainment industry.

The reports suggest that Divya Bhatnagar was suffering from COVID-19 at the time of her death and that she was 34 years old. Divya has been experiencing symptoms of both COVID-19 and high blood pressure since she became infected with the dangerous virus a week earlier from her death. When it became clear that her health worsened further, she was placed on a ventilator.

At the time she was placed on the ventilator, her condition was exceedingly serious and had reached a critical stage. Divya's fight against death was ultimately unsuccessful, and she left the world at such a little age.

Divya Bhatnagar's Husband & Controversy

Divya Bhatnagar's brother, Devashish, discovered a handwritten letter stating that her husband, Gagan Gabru, was having a violent relationship with his wife. The letter that Divya's family discovered claims that "Gagan Gabru began abusing Divya physically and psychologically shortly after the marriage." Divya's family discovered this information after finding the message from her. She had written this message about how he hit her so violently anytime he wanted.

According to her brother, she also went to the police station in her hometown to report that she had been the victim of domestic abuse. In addition to it, she submitted an NC (Non-cognizable offence) report. Following the traumatic event, her brother encouraged her to maintain her calm and to recover quickly after being admitted to the hospital.

Later, on December 7, 2020, she died due to the effects of Covid and left her family and friends behind. It has been said that the actress' family was always unhappy with Divya Bhatnagar's husband as their marriage was intercaste, and they never considered him the kind of person who would look after Divya. As time passed, it was proved right when Divya's message about her unhappy marriage was made public.

The clarification given by Gagan Gabru

Divya Bhatnagar's husband, Gagan Gabru, who was accused of domestic abuse by the actress' family and Devoleena Bhattacharjee (Divya Bhatnagar's friend), has rubbished all claims through his videos on the internet.

Gagan released the footage of Divya having a good time with him, enjoying dancing and laughing with him. In a separate video, Gagan disproved each and every one of Devoleena's statements, including that Gagan intentionally led Divya to mental abuse, which ultimately caused her to get ill. Due to this, she got weak, felt overthrown, and lost her fight against Covid-19. Gagan later said that he is still shocked by Divya's sudden death, adding that Devoleena Bhattacharjee is just trying to get publicity.

Gagan made it clear that he and Divya had acknowledged and accepted one another's past. He stated that Devoleena is no one to come between them. He stood by her side through all of the challenging moments, during which he disclosed that Divya had acquired financial obligations on account of her family and had pledged her jewellery as collateral for a loan.

Gagan wondered why her family and Devoleena were not there to help when she needed them. He said that her family was living in his home without his permission and threatened to expel him. Gagan continued by saying that no one from her family gave him permission to see Divya when she was in the hospital with them. He disclosed that he had all of the proof necessary to demonstrate his innocence at this time.

  • She was not only a well-known face on television but was also quite famous on the video-sharing platform TikTok before it was banned in India.
  • On the 22nd of December 2019, she married her long-time partner Gagan in a private ceremony held at a Gurudwara. Apart from these two, no one knew about the marriage.
  • While she was brought to the hospital after testing positive for COVID-19, her mother made severe charges against her husband, Gagan. These allegations were made while she was infected with COVID-19.
  • After a few days passed, her husband uploaded a video on his social media account in which he displayed his happy life with Divya to defend himself.
