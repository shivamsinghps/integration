Top 10 Engineering College For Computer Science In Bangalore

Engineering in Computer science is a field that combines the principles and techniques of computer science with those of engineering. It involves the application of computer science concepts such as programming, data structures, and algorithms to the design, development, and maintenance of software and systems in engineering fields like aerospace, automotive, electrical, and mechanical engineering. The goal is to create efficient and effective solutions to engineering challenges through the integration of computer science and engineering principles. Here is the list of the top 10 engineering colleges for computer science in Bangalore:

1. Ramaiah Institute of Technology

  • Established in the year 1962.
  • Campus Area: 85 acres
  • Average package - ₹ 7.66 LPA
  • Fees: ₹ 3-4 Lakh

M.S. Ramaiah Institute of Technology (MSRIT) was founded in 1962 by the late Dr. M S Ramaiah. Dr. Ramaiah was a well-known visionary, philanthropist, and pioneer in developing several significant infrastructure projects in India. Among all VTU-affiliated engineering colleges, Ramaiah Institute of Technology placed at the top of the list. RIT Bangalore is listed as the 67th top engineering school overall in the NIRF rankings for 2022.

At the undergraduate, graduate, and doctorate levels, Ramaiah Institute of Technology provides specialties in Engineering, Architecture, Biotechnology, Management, and Technology. At the UG and PG levels, Ramaiah Institute of Technology provides B.E., B.Arch., MTech, MBA, and MCA programs. Admission to research programs at the Ramaiah Institute of Technology, including M.Sc. and PhD degrees, is also offered in 17 departments.

By completing an online application form on the institute's website, qualified applicants can apply to any course. Various entrance tests, including those accepted by the institution, such as KCET/COMEDK/NATA/GATE/PGCET/CMAT/MAT, are used to determine admission to programs at RIT. Around 1007 students were placed during the placement drive in 2021, according to the NIRF placement report from 2022. The average salary was INR 7.66 LPA, while the biggest salary package available was INR 50 LPA. Today, MSRIT is one of India's leading institutions for engineering education.

2. International Institute of Information Technology University (IIIT)

  • Established in the year 1998.
  • Campus Area: 9 acres
  • Average package - ₹ 20-25 LPA
  • Fees - ₹ 2 lakh approx

The first IIIT in India to be established is the International Institute of Information Technology, Bangalore, an independent institution that was established in 1998 as a not-for-profit public-private partnership (N-PPP). Strong research programs in several fields have been developed by the institution throughout the years, focusing on technology and applied research for business and society. The institute promotes seamless knowledge transfer and multidisciplinary research. The research portfolio of IIITH includes several centres of excellence with a global reputation. With an industry reach including key national and international enterprises, it has built several joint partnerships and co-innovation models.

One of the best universities for technical professional courses is the International Institute of Information Technology-Bangalore, which received a rating of 81 in the engineering category in the 2022 NIRF Ranking and managed to remain in the top 70 institutions for three consecutive year.

The IIIT-B provides post-graduate courses at the levels of MTech in CSE, MTech in ECE, M.Sc. in Digital Society, M.Sc. by Research/Ph.D., etc. The Integrated MTech, a 5-year program that is offered to the students finishing their 12th grade the chance to obtain a master's level education, is the most recently established and one of the institute's most popular courses.

The program culminates in the awarding of two degrees, a B.Tech and an M.Tech. In addition to this, it offers research-based degrees like the Ph.D. and Master of Science, and additional programs like Diplomas, Certificate Programs, Short Term Programs, etc.

The institute offers pupils a well-rounded atmosphere that fosters self-governance, societal contributions, athletics, and the arts and cultures in addition to academics. Even lower-level students are given the opportunity to take part in current research and technological development. National entrance exams like JEE Mains and GATE are used to determine admission to the programs. The college has a good track record for placement, with the highest reported package being INR 27 LPA and the lowest being INR 7 LPA.

3. B.M.S. College of Engineering (BMSCE)

  • Established in the year 1946.
  • Campus Area: 15 acres
  • Average package - ₹ 8-9 LPA
  • Fees- ₹ 9 Lakh

Founded in 1946 by Late Sri B. M. Sreenivasaiah, a brilliant visionary and philanthropist, and supported by his distinguished son Late Sri B. S. Narayan, the B.M.S. College of Engineering (BMSCE), has a long history. The BMSCE is India's first private-sector effort in engineering education. The school has produced more than 40,000 engineers and leaders during the last 74 years of its storied history. These individuals have improved the globe via their enormous achievements for mankind.

The B.M.S. College of Engineering (BMSCE), an independent engineering school in Bangalore, Karnataka, is listed by the NIRF as the 83rd-best engineering school in the nation. Undergraduate, postgraduate, and doctoral degrees in engineering, management, computer science, and architecture are offered by the institution, which is associated with Visvesvaraya Technological University (VTU) in Belgaum. A management quota or the results of qualifying exams are used to determine admission to these programmes. BMSCE provides five to six LPA placements in well-known and reputed international corporations like Epicor, Wipro, Westline, Volvo, TCS, and others. A more recent concentration in the BE program at BMS College of Engineering is artificial intelligence and machine learning.

4. RV College of Engineering (RVCE)

  • Established in the year 1963.
  • Campus Area: 17 acres
  • Average package - ₹ 8.63 LPA
  • Fees- ₹ 11 Lakh

RV College of Engineering was Established in 1963 with just three engineering branches-civil, electrical, and mechanical. RV College of Engineering (RVCE) is a self-financing engineering college and is certified by AICTE. The RCVE provides 16 specialties for master's degree programs and doctoral studies, as well as 12 specializations for undergraduate engineering programs. Over 1200 students can enrol at RCVE each year. The RCVE is renowned for its high-quality instruction, competence in technical studies, cutting-edge instructional strategies, and consultation and research via an outcome-based curriculum.

In the engineering category of the NIRF Rankings 2022, RVCE is ranked 89th. The flagship MCA program at the postgraduate level is also offered by RCVE. The merit achieved by the students in the entrance exams is used to determine admission to the various engineering programmes. GATE, K PGCET/KMAT, or admission examinations with an equivalent are approved by RCVE. On the official website, an application form for B.E., MTech., and MCA (Management Quota) is presently accessible.

5. PES University Bangalore

  • Established in the year 1998.
  • Campus Area: 30 acres
  • Average package - ₹ 10-11 LPA
  • Fees- ₹ 8.80 Lakh

PES University Bangalore (commonly known as PESU) was founded in 1972 in Bangalore, India. The institution concentrates on five major fields of study: engineering, management, law, medicine, and life sciences. The University is spread over four campuses: PESU Medical Campus, PESU Hanumanthanagar, PESU Electronic City, and PESU Ring Road. Additionally, it has Amaatra Academy, PES Public School, PES Polytechnic, and PES PU College.

An extensive selection of engineering diploma courses is available at the PES Polytechnic. PES University received ranks of 5 in the Outlook-ICARE India University Rankings 2020 and 100 in the NIRF 2022 for Engineering. There are 12 departments in all, and they offer 97 courses in 11 streams.

Admissions are available for engineering, electronics, marketing, computer application, architecture, and law at PES University (PESU), Bangalore. For admission to the PES University, Bangalore courses, students must take the PESSAT. Along with applying to PES's numerous programs, several national and state-level exams are also considered.

Research and thesis projects are required for the course, and they will be evaluated during each individual semester, regardless of the student's major. The PES University (PESU), located in Bangalore, is highly recognized for its technical expertise and offers students the opportunity to receive the greatest training available on the globe while also gaining a conceptual grasp.

6. New Horizon Colleges of Engineering (NHCE)

  • Established in the year 2001.
  • Campus Area: 12 acres
  • Average package - ₹ 11 LPA
  • Fees - ₹ 10 Lakh

The New Horizon Colleges of Engineering (NHCE) was founded in 1970. With a vision and objective to provide comprehensive education to all its students, NHCE has a distinguished history of offering innovative education. NHEI, which is well-located in Bengaluru, India's famed IT centre, has experienced tremendous growth over the past few decades.

New Horizon College of Engineering (NHCE) is a private, independent university in Bangalore and it is affiliated with Visvesvaraya Technological University (VTU). According to NIRF Ranking 2022, the institution is placed at the 121st engineering college in India.

There are five courses available at NHCE in Bangalore, each with a particular focus. It offers four more postgraduate programmes in addition to one undergraduate course. All these courses have entry requirements for enrolment. Additionally, the college provides PhD degrees.

The New Horizon College of Engineering offers a variety of excellent placement options. Top recruitment firms like Wipro, Cognizant, TCS, and Amazon frequently visit the campus. Additionally, the college provides several scholarships to worthy students. The outstanding faculty chair at the institute includes more than 20 PhD holders and three MBA holders.

7. Jain University

  • Established in the year 2008.
  • Campus Area: 576 acres
  • Average package - ₹ 6-7 LPA
  • Fees- ₹ 6-7 Lakh

Jain University, Bangalore is a private Deemed to be University that was founded in Bangalore in 1990. It is one of the best institutions in India. It is regarded as a cerebral destination for students from all over the world, and Bangalore in particular, for its distinguished history of fostering genius.

A supportive environment is provided for studying at the university, which is in Bangalore, India's Silicon Valley, whether it be through coursework or extracurricular pursuits. JAIN (Deemed-to-be University) is renowned for emphasising education, entrepreneurship, research, and sports. It boasts some of the top brains in educational and research sectors, as well as centres that encourage entrepreneurship and ground-breaking work to simplify and manage life better.

The undergraduate, graduate and doctoral programs offered by Jain University are in the fields of arts, humanities, and social sciences, computer application and IT, animation and design, media, mass communication, and journalism, as well as in the fields of medicine and allied sciences, hospitality, and tourism. Full-time and online education options are available through Jain University.

For admission to its undergraduate and postgraduate programs, Jain University administers the Jain Entrance Test (JET). A placement cell at the institution is in charge of the recruitment effort at Jain University. In addition to many other facilities, the university features a hostel, library, canteen, auditorium, classrooms, and labs.

8. CMR Institute of Technology

  • Established in the year 2000.
  • Campus Area: 8 acres
  • Average package - ₹ 6.3LPA
  • Fees- ₹ 10 Lakh

The CMR Institute of Technology was founded in 2000 and offered high-quality, real-world education in engineering and management to aid students in sharpening their analytical skills and expanding their capacity for creative thought. By fostering young minds in a cutting-edge and forward-thinking learning environment, CMRIT responds to the industry's increasing demand for technology specialists. The CMRIT campus in Whitefield, Bangalore, is situated in a location that is highly linked and accessible since it is placed right in the middle of South India's IT corridor.

The Bangalore-based Chikka Muniyappa Reddy Institution of Technology (CMRIT), also known as the CMR Institute of Technology is run by the CMR Jnanadhara Trust. CMR Institute of Technology is a private engineering college located in Bengaluru, Karnataka, India. The college offers undergraduate and postgraduate courses in engineering, technology, and management. It is affiliated with Visvesvaraya Technological University and is approved by the All-India Council for Technical Education (AICTE).

Institute strongly focuses on research and innovation. It provides students with various facilities and resources to support their studies, including state-of-the-art labs, a library, and a computer centre. Recently it placed 1,767 students in reputable business companies with salaries ranging from INR 4.87 LPA on the low end to INR 11.76 LPA on the high end.

In the field of Engineering, Management, and Science, CMRIT provides a comprehensive range of undergraduate and graduate programs, with admission based on both merit and entrance examinations. Karnataka CET/COMED-K/JEE scores are used to determine undergraduate admission to the different academic programmes at CMRIT. In contrast, PGCET/GATE/CMAT/CAT scores are used to determine postgraduate admission to various academic programmes. Every year, the CMRIT Bangalore Cut-off for COMED-K, PGCET, and KCET changes.

9. The Dr Ambedkar Institute of Technology (Dr AIT)

  • Established in the year 1980.
  • Campus Area: 23 acres
  • Average package - ₹ 5-6 LPA
  • Fees- ₹ 2 Lakh

In 1980, M.H. Jayaprakash Narayan created the Dr Ambedkar Institute of Technology (Dr AIT). The institute, which bears Dr. B.R. Ambedkar's name is affiliated with Visvesvaraya Technological University (VTU), Belagavi, and has received accreditation from the NBA and recognition from the AICTE. The school has received an "A" grade from NAAC for accreditation. In the National Institutional Ranking Framework, the institution has a 163 rank (NIRF).

The KCET or COMEDK UGET entrance test result determines admission to ambedkar College Bangalore for BE programs in 2021. Additionally, PGCET is used as the foundation for admission to PG programmes, including the MBA and MCA at AIT Bangalore. MTech admission to Dr Ambedkar Institute of Technology in 2021 is based on GATE results.

Performance on the UGC NET / CSIR NET test is a requirement for admission to Ambedkar College Bangalore's doctoral-level and research programs. The Training and Placement Cell of Ambedkar College in Bangalore organizes seminars, written exams, pre-placement discussions, and other placement-related events for students. AIT Bangalore's top employers include well-known businesses like Amazon, IBM, TCS, and HP.

10. University of Visvesvaraya College of Engineering (UVCE)

  • Established in the year 1917.
  • Campus Area: 50 acres
  • Average package - ₹ 6-7 LPA
  • Fees- ₹ 1 Lakh

Sir M. Visvesvaraya, a Bharat Ratna, founded the University of Visvesvaraya College of Engineering (UVCE) in 1917. The College offers seven engineering courses: civil engineering, electronics and communication, electrical and electronics, mechanical, information science, and computer science. The College is currently affiliated with Bangalore University.

The main UVCE Bangalore campus is located near KR Circle, close to Vidhana Soudha, while the other campus is spread out over the 50-acre Jnana Bharathi Campus. There are 2517 students enrolled in the University Visvesvaraya College of Engineering, and there are 93 faculty members overall. The All-India Council of Technical Education of the Indian Government recognises UVCE Bangalore (AICTE). Government Engineering College was the name given to the institution when it was founded, and it is funded by the World Bank's Technical Education Quality Improvement Program (TEQIP).

UVCE Bangalore offers 33 courses in total. The list of university courses offered by Visvesvaraya College of Engineering includes B.Tech, B.Arch, M.Tech, etc. Engineering, architecture, computer applications, IT, and sciences are the fields in which UVCE Bangalore specialises. Candidates must satisfy the qualifying requirements before submitting an application for admission to UVCE Bangalore.
