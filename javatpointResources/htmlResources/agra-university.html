Agra University

Agra University, now known as Dr Bhimrao Ambedkar University, is an open university in the Indian state of Uttar Pradesh. The institution bears Bhimrao Ambedkar, an Indian scholar, social reformer, and creator of the Indian Constitution.

About

1st July 1927 saw the establishment of Dr Bhimrao Ambedkar University, thanks to the tireless efforts of a group of committed educators, including Rev. Canon A.W. Davis, Munshi Narain Prasad Asthana, Dr L.P. Mathur, Lala Dewan Chand, Rai Bahadur Anand Swaroop, and Dr Brajendra Swaroop, University's original jurisdiction entailed United Provinces of Agra, Central India, and Rajputana with 14 affiliated faculties and 2530 college students of which, 1475 college students belonged to United Provinces.

Admission and Courses

The courses at Dr Bhimrao Ambedkar University are available at various levels, including undergraduate (BBA, B.Ed., B.Sc. in physical science, etc.), postgraduate (M.Sc. in mathematics, computer applications; MBA, M.Com. etc.), and doctoral levels. The institution also recognizes a variety of national-level entry tests, including UPCET and UPTU. Merit and admission examinations are used to determine who gets into the University.

BA: This institution offers BA programs in history, psychology, mathematics, and English. Students who complete 10+2 with an average score of 50% pursue it. Any reputable board of education must be the source of the education.

B.Voc: Students have the option of pursuing a B.Voc degree, which is also available in a variety of subjects. Additionally, passing the 12th-grade exam with at least 50% is required.

B.Sc.: A bachelor's degree in science, or B.Sc., is available in various specializations, including agriculture, agronomy, chemistry, botany, and many others. The 12th board exam in the science stream must have been passed by students who wish to enrol in this course.

MBA: Passing an entrance test like the CAT, MAT, or CMAT with a satisfactory score is one of the requirements for admission. A BBA or an equivalent degree with an average GPA of 55% (or 50% for SC/ST) is required for access.

MA: This University offers merit-based Master of Arts admissions for various fields. To graduate, a student must earn at least 50% of the maximum score in their bachelor's or comparable degree.

MCA: Dr BR Ambedkar University offers the postgraduate course MCA (Master of Computer) Applications. Candidates can apply for enrollment in this course if they have received their bachelor's degree or similar in a relevant stream. One of the most professional programs requires at least two years to complete.

Placements

The University also has a placement cell that functions appropriately to improve the learners. The cell plans activities to ensure students' progress on a routine basis. The team organizes pep speeches, mock tests, ability enhancement, and the technical and soft talents of the students and brings industry-ready qualities. The cell sends invitations to several elite organizations & groups to participate in the recruitment drives.

The employers plan a pre-placement event where they provide the students with an overview of the company's operating structure and job description and take questions from Dr Bhimrao Ambedkar University students based on the employer and industries in which the employers function.

Top companies, TCS, Wipro, and Microsoft, hire qualified college students. The maximum remuneration package was provided at INR 15 LPA, and the average CTC was presented at INR 8LPA.

Facilities and Infrastructure

The teachers and students at Dr Bhim Rao Ambedkar University in Agra have access to various facilities. DBRAU Agra offers big classrooms. A library with a sizable collection of books, periodicals, and other study materials is part of the infrastructure at Dr Bhim Rao Ambedkar University in Agra. Students can use the library's study materials and other resources to learn their course information.

The auditorium at Dr Bhim Rao Ambedkar University in Agra serves as the location for all of the University's academic and extracurricular events. At Dr Bhim Rao Ambedkar University, Agra, there are numerous, well-equipped laboratories for every topic. Both boys and girls can live on campus; there is a separate dorm for each gender. There is a cafeteria on the campus that serves sanitary and healthful meals to the teachers and students. Students at DBRAU Agra can access sports, medical, and Wi-Fi services.

Residential Accommodations: The College delivers housing services with Wi-Fi centres for 24hrs, a warden, and eighty rooms.

Boys Hostel: The University offers facilities for boys staying in hostels. The dormitory rooms are nicely furnished and offer the students all the amenities they require daily.

Girls Hostel: The University offers a separate hostel for girls. The dormitory rooms are nicely decorated and equipped with all the necessities.

Gym: The University offers both male and female students access to a modern gym. The gym is equipped with all the latest equipment.

Library: DBRAU students have access to the University's central library, which offers a good selection of books and other study aids. The library provides an interactive facility to the student. Students can access the data, reports, and other study materials housed at the library for information on their courses.

Sports: The University offers facilities for a wide range of sports, including basketball, football, cricket, and many others. For these sports, DBRAU Agra has grounds and courts.

Cafeteria: A kitchen on the site serves clean and nutritious meals to the instructors and learners. The Dr Bhim Rao Ambedkar University, Agra students, are served wholesome, hygienic cuisine in the well-equipped canteen.

Auditorium: The University infrastructure provides an arena which is the platform for all the academic and cultural events of Dr Bhim Rao Ambedkar University. The halls have excellent audiovisual capabilities.

Medical/Hospital: The University provides 24/7 medical support to the students.

Wi-Fi: Students, academic staff, and workers have access to a Wi-Fi network available around the clock at the University of Agra.

IT Infrastructure: Outstanding IT facilities are available to operate the Agra University campus.

Laboratories: A number of the University's laboratories are available to the students. Students can conduct experiments and obtain practical information for their laboratory courses.

Convenience Store: There is a store on the university campus where students can buy supplies for their everyday needs, including stationery. The shop also has a photocopying service and offers students a postal service.

Alumni Associations: The alum association can network effectively at the University.

Guest Room: For visiting faculty, student, and students' family members and guardians, the University offers a guest house.

Banks Facilities: Two bank branches are located on the University's campus and ATM terminals.
