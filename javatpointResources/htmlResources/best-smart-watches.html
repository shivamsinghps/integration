Best Smart Watches

To improve our lives, we require more technological instruments. Human ingenuity and inventiveness know no bounds, from mobile phones to smartphones to smartwatches. What exactly is a smartwatch? It's a form of a wearable computer that sits on our wrists like a watch, but it's different in that it has additional smart digital functions.

These days, smartwatches are a wonderful companion device for keeping track of not only time but also health. Most smartwatches make our lives easier because they include numerous technological capabilities such as heart rate monitoring, while others are just fantastic at counting and measuring for workouts.

Smartwatches provide the following advantages:

  1. They allow easy accessibility to some smartphone applications and can provide sensor data such as sleep records and heart rates that are not offered on smartphones.
  2. Some smartwatches can connect with our smartphones and display incoming calls, weather forecasts, and maps in real-time.
  3. Smart watches act as an extension of our smartphones, allowing us to access some content without having to reach for our phones. It is also much more convenient to check notifications from our smart watches when driving a car or riding a bike.
  4. Some smartwatches now include waterproof technology, making it easier for athletes, such as swimmers, to track their swim time, heart rate, and distance.
  5. Some smartwatches offer a 'Find my phone option, which makes it much easier to locate your misplaced phone, even if it is in silent mode because our smartwatches will cause our phones to beep loudly.

There are a lot of smartwatches on the market, which makes it tough to pick the finest one with all of the necessary technical characteristics. So, let's familiarize ourselves with a look at some of the best smartwatches one by one:

1. Mi smartwatch- D20

If you are genuinely searching for a low-budget, nice-looking smartwatch with some of the most crucial smartwatch-like functions, then this one is for you as it comes in at a very budget-friendly price of just 799 rupees.

The item weighs roughly 80 grams and comes in a variety of colors, including pink, green, yellow, and white, though the black one looks sleek and on-trend. This smartwatch has the following features:

  • Keeping track of daily activity
  • It can display calls and notifications.
  • After installing the Fitpro fitness tracker software, this smartwatch syncs with all of the apps on our phones.
  • This gadget comes with a one-year warranty and features superb Bluetooth connectivity as well as being water-resistant.

The sole disadvantage of this smartwatch is that it may occasionally deliver inaccurate values.

2. Apple Watch Series 7

If you are planning to buy a smartwatch, then the Apple Watch Series 7 would be the best purchase option for you. As of now, this is the most productive Apple Watch.

The Apple Watch, like the iPhone, has a quality design and a long list of practicality. Though there isn't much of a difference in terms of functionality when compared to the previous Apple Watch 6 model, the Series 7 model does have a broader display screen than the previous one. This Apple smartwatch is available in two versions, one of which is 41 mm and the other of which is 45 mm with a bigger screen. This version of the Apple Watch comes with a QWERTY keyboard, and a variety of watch faces to match the occasions.

Its health and wellness monitoring features are the finest among all smart watches' brands, and its GPS functionality is likewise excellent. The native sleep tracking stats are not that great, but one can utilize a third-party sleep monitoring app if one needs more information. Along with an 18-hour battery life, the device also offers fast charging.

If you are looking for a smartwatch to be worn while you are working out at the gym or want to answer calls on the go, this is the smartwatch for you. It should be noted, however, that the Apple Watch Series 7 is exclusively compatible with iPhones, the same as its predecessors.

3. Samsung Galaxy Watch 4

Samsung Galaxy Watch 4, which has used Tizen as its operating system in recent versions, is one of the top-class products that has created a lot of hype in the market. Samsung dropped the 'Active' moniker in its current range, thereby promoting the Galaxy Watch 4 as the company's flagship device and a 'Classic' variant that retains elegant features such as the manual rotating bezel. Quite notably, they are the only two Wear OS 3 watches on the market right now, which allows the device immediate access to a variety of apps.

Samsung Galaxy Watch 4, which is available in a range of sizes and colors, has a lot to offer.

The Galaxy Watch 4 is developed for sports enthusiasts, while the Classic model has a more pleasing design that makes it more adaptive. Both serve a range of functions, including health and activity surveillance, smart reminders, and personalization.

The Galaxy Watch 4's 3-in-1 health sensor monitors heart rate, records ECGs, and analyses body composition. There are, unfortunately, certain downsides to consider. The Galaxy Watch 4 has a number of flaws, including irregular battery life, delayed charging, and the lack of Google Assistant connectivity. Since certain functionalities are exclusive to Samsung customers, not all Android smartphone users can gain the benefits of the Galaxy Watch 4's capabilities.

4. Fire-Boltt SpO2 smartwatch

Another firm in India with a strong reputation in the field of smartwatches is the Fire-Boltt brand. This brand offers a 1.4-inch full-touch smartwatch with 8-day battery life, among other features. Virat Kohli, a cricketer, is the brand ambassador for this company.

Fire-Boltt SpO2 smartwatch is equipped with SpO2 technology to assure our happiness and well health. It can keep track of our stride and count exercises. It features a great water-resistance function that allows us to wear our watch without anxiety while sweating, swimming, or in the event of any water contact, such as rain. The following are some of the best features of this smartwatch:

  • It offers an HD display that is both bright and sensitive.
  • It's the first wearable with a SpO2 capability on the market.
  • It's a smartwatch with a full touch screen and a beautifully designed metal casing.
  • It has an IPX7 waterproof rating.

The biggest benefit of owning this smartwatch is that it is cost-effective, as it costs only 2499 rupees and includes all of its smart capabilities. It calculates heart rate and blood oxygen levels, as well as tracks other sports, sleep, and fitness. The gadget weighs roughly 80 grams and comes with a one-year warranty. The sole disadvantage of this smartwatch is that it occasionally causes connection difficulty.

5. boAt Xtend smartwatch

In this day of super technology, there cannot be anyone who has not heard of boAt's name, a company that specializes in supplying the best technical equipment. It includes smartwatches in the list as well, to diversify the market with options, from its superb earbuds to headphones and others.

Boat Xtend Smartwatch has a 1.69" HD display with a big square color LCD and a round dial which offers a consistent touch-sensitive experience that makes it simple to seize control. The smart watch's most revolutionary feature is the inclusion of Alexa as an inbuilt speech assistant. This voice assistant, like the ones on our phones, can help us set notifications and timers and receive answers to questions like the weather, cricket results, and so on. This smartwatch also has a number of face-altering options. To enhance your everyday outfit. The light source display on this watch dynamically adjusts the brightness to complement the environment. This item costs 3499 rupees and weighs 54 grams. It also comes with a one-year warranty. Polycarbonate is used to make the crystal material.

This smartwatch has the following features:

  • Built-in Alexa technology.
  • It comes with a huge square LCD display
  • It determines the SpO2 and heart rate.

The benefits of this product are its minimal weight and IP68 waterproof resistance. It is also better than its competitors in terms of battery performance and offers interchangeable straps. The sole disadvantage is that it occasionally displays erroneous SpO2 readings.

6. Honor Magic Watch 2

If you're a fitness enthusiast who likes to run often, the Honor Magic Watch 2 is one of the coolest wearables you can have. It is inexpensive, light, and has a broad list of outstanding functionalities.

The Honor Magic Watch 2 analyzes a range of athletic disciplines and can even suggest inputs on workout regimens and feedback for particular physical tasks. In a nutshell, it's great for people who desire to be in a good physique.

The watch's battery life is also impressive, and considering what model you have, you won't need to charge it for a week or two. In the sphere of aesthetics, it is quite elegant.

The watch has some flaws, such as the fact that it doesn't have many features other than fitness tracking. Also, its layout attracts dust very often and needs to be cleaned frequently, and many of its fitness functions aren't compatible with iOS. Aside from these flaws, the watch appears to be a good option among other smartwatches.

7. Fossil Gen 6

The Fossil Gen 6 is certainly among the most appealing smartwatches available on the market. Fossil utilizes its expertise in creating traditional premium watches and implements it to the Gen 6 smartwatch with finesse. The Fossil Gen 6 provides users with a fashionable alternative for a smartwatch courtesy of its all-metal design.

Fossil employs a unique approach while designing its watches, and the Gen 6 follows that tradition. It has a round display, a metal body with a leather strap, and physical controls.

The watch has a round AMOLED display that measures 1.28 inches in diameter and has a 416 x 416pixel resolution. The bezels are effectively disguised. On the right side of the digital display, there are two buttons. It turns up the display or sends you to the app list when pressed. The button above the crown shows one's health stats, including steps walked, calories burnt, latest heart rate, and oxygen saturation, while the control underneath it allows users to use their Google Pay cards to make Contactless payments. Any WearOS app can be accessed using the two buttons.

A mic and speaker are also included to support Google Assistant. Receiving and making calls with the watch is always convenient.

The Gen 6 has a battery life that lasts for a day. If you monitor your sleep at night or switch on GPS, your battery performance suffers. Continuous heart rate monitoring depletes battery power at a higher rate. Fortunately, the watch supports fast charging and can charge up to 80% in half an hour.

Another fantastic feature of the Fossil Gen 6 is that it works with both Android and iOS devices.

This smartwatch, which starts at Rs. 23,995 seems to be more economical than the Apple Watch SE and approximately cost similar when compared with the Samsung Galaxy Watch 4. So, if you're seeking a smartwatch that is affordable along with a traditional look, then Gen 6 is a good option to consider.

8. Noise Colorfit Ultra Bezel-less smartwatch

Like other companies, Noise smartwatches are valued in the market for their high quality. This colorfit Ultra bezel-less watch comes in a selection of strap patterns to complement any outfit. This wristwatch is a complete premium smartwatch with a variety of customization options that you may modify to suit your requirements. There are 60 distinct sports settings included within the smartwatch. Apart from just that, one can monitor all essential activity stats with the Noise Fit app.

This smartwatch offers a 1.75-inch HD TruView display, as well as functions like heart rate monitoring, stress and sleep level tracking, call and text messaging rapid reply, SPo2 level reporting, and stock market information. This gadget weighs roughly 47 grams and costs approximately 1799 rupees on Amazon's online store, with a one-year warranty.

This device also has the following features:

  • It has a nine-day battery life when fully charged.
  • It has an IPX68 water resistance rating of four stars.
  • It is compatible with both Android and Apple devices.

This product's advantages include the fact that it is backed by sensory technology. The sole disadvantage is that, given the availability of competing items on the market that offer a wide range of features at low rates, this brand's performance may have been better in capturing the consumer's attention.

9. OnePlus Watch

OnePlus has debuted in the smartwatch segment with its OnePlus Watch, a wearable highly anticipated by android users.

The OnePlus watch has a round dial and is made of a stainless-steel body. The watch is available in two colors: Midnight Black and Moonlight Silver, and the look and feel of the watch are superior.

The 1.39-inch AMOLED display on the OnePlus Watch is surrounded by narrow bezels that are difficult to notice. The OnePlus Watch is dirt and water-resistant to IP68 standards, and the manufacturer claims it can withstand the pressure of up to 5ATM.

Much to everyone's astonishment, it doesn't run on WearOS but on its own Real-Time Operating System. It features SpO2 as well as sleep and heart rate monitors like all of its major competitors. It also has a 4 GB storage option, allowing you to store a few soundtracks for offline listening on the OnePlus Watch.

The watch's user interface is straightforward and simple to use. The OnePlus Watch is proactive with battery saving. Once you are finished looking at it, the display will instantly turn off. It also comes with an auto-brightness adjustment mode.

With WhatsApp messages activated and indoor activities being monitored, the OnePlus Watch's battery life was excellent, lasting roughly 12 days on a single charge. Also, it supports fast charging. However, with SpO2, heart rate tracking, and the always-on feature enabled, the battery may drain at a faster rate.

The OnePlus Watch is compatible with Android smartphones running on android 6.0 and higher, while it doesn't support iPhones.

Available for Rs 14,999 in India, the OnePlus watch is overall a decent option for people looking for an inexpensive smartwatch with great battery life and a number of fitness and health features.

10. Titan Smartwatch

The Indian Watch brand Titan has launched its top product in the smartwatch segment, Titan Smart Pro. Titan Smart Pro Watch sports a 1.19-inch AMOLED touchscreen display packed in an aluminium frame and costs Rs 14,999.

The display is efficient, and the watch has a round-shaped dial, which should attract those who want a somewhat classic watch look. It has a silicone wristband that is wearable for long periods of time.

There are two controls on the watch. The top one can be enabled to access all of the functions, such as daily targets, tracking heart rate, sleep, and body temperature, while the button on the bottom allows you to quickly browse your activity record as well as all of the other athletic modes.

The watch claims to be 5 ATM water-resistant; hence it can be worn while swimming. It ought to resist most drips and splashes.

One advantage of the watch is that it works with both Android and iOS devices and displays a variety of notifications, including SMS, WhatsApp, and Gmail. The large display makes the notifications properly visible. There is, unfortunately, no way of responding to anything that appears on the watch.

The Titan Smart Pro is said to have a battery capacity of up to a week or so on a single battery charge. Putting on the Always-on screen mode and raising the brightness to the maximum, on the other hand, will deplete the battery far more quickly.

It does, however, have some drawbacks:

  • The step counter on the watch is a little inaccurate.
  • The performance of the watch is slightly disappointing in terms of speed.
  • It must be synchronized with your device on a daily basis, or your data may be lost.

The Titan Smart Pro-Watch is unquestionably loaded with functions, and it delivers on a lot of its promises. You can surely give it a try considering its affordable price.

Conclusion

Finally, we can claim that smartwatches are gradually becoming mainstream products in India. This list of ours contains some of the top smartwatches and is intended to provide you with some guidance in selecting the best-value smart watches and narrowing down your options. All of the smartwatches listed above are available for purchase on Amazon or Flipkart, and if any of them appear to be unsuitable for your needs, we recommend double-checking their characteristics before making any judgments.
