Udemy Alternatives

Udemy is one of the best, popular, user-friendly, standard looking, worldwide online eLearning platform in the industry. It is used to learn and acquire knowledge. It offers more than 100,000 online high-quality courses in 65 languages. It allows us to easily create, upload, and sell online courses at a very reasonable amount. Currently, it is used by more than 34 million students. It perfectly works on various devices like Windows, macOS, Linux, Browser, iOS, and Android.

Click on the given link to know more about the Udemy learning platform - https://www.udemy.com/

1. Coursera

Coursera is the world's best education (online course) delivery platform, founded in 2012. It provides impressive, free, high-quality content each time. Coursera platform helps us to learn the latest skills such as business analytics, graphics design, Python, Machine learning, Artificial intelligence, Data Science, language learning, and more. It offers peer interaction that helps us to improve our learning capability by interacting with other students.

Pros of Coursera

A list of Pros of Coursera is given below -

  • Coursers allow us to interact with professionals during the course.
  • It is a more suitable platform for various fields like Artificial intelligence, Machine Learning, web development, cloud engineering, and more.
  • It provides self-placed quizzes and hands-on projects.
  • We can access Coursera on Android, iOS, and Browser.
  • It comes in more than 30 languages, including Spanish, English, French, Russian, Chinese, and more.
  • It offers certificates for paid courses.

Cons of Coursera

  • Need to provide a better description of what they offer.
  • We can't take full courses for free.
  • Fewer courses are available as compared to Udemy.

Pricing

Coursera 7 days of free trial is available for some courses. Its specialization plans start at $39-$79 per month, and online degree courses are available at $15,000 to $25,000.

Website

Click on the given link to know more about Coursera and take a course with Coursers: https://www.coursera.org/

It is the best site for Entrepreneurs (Business Professionals). This site mainly provides content in different fields like Technology, Marketing, Business & Leadership, and Data Analytics. All courses are available in the form of videos, audio, and presentations (slides).

Pros of Learning.ly is as follows -

  • ly provides in-depth syllabus for each lesson as well as topics.
  • It offers various discounts and coupon codes.
  • 100% refund guarantee if we are not satisfied with the course content.
  • It offers lifetime access to any purchased course.
  • Its interface is easy for experts to use.
  • Rich media support.

Cons of Learning.ly
