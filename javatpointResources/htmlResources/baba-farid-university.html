Baba Farid University

Baba Farid University of Health Sciences (B.F.U.H.S.) is a public university with its main campus in Faridkot, Punjab. It was founded following a law enacted by the state's legislature in July 1998. The American Heart Association's international training center is now at Baba Farid University of Health Sciences. Numerous U.G., P.G., Diploma, P.G. Diploma, Certificate, M.Ch. And Ph.D. programs are available at B.F.U.H.S. Faridkot. The B.F.U.H.S. will administer entrance exams for some courses, while scores from external entrance exams will be considered for most of them.

Admission to U.G. and Diploma Programs at Baba Farid University

Admissions to U.G. and Diploma programs are based on a candidate's performance on the necessary Entrance Exam and in the 10+2 or equivalent (in the applicable subject). In contrast, admittance to diploma programs is determined on merit. An applicant for a B.Sc. program must have completed the 10+2 program with the issues of Physics, Chemistry, Biology, and English on their own, as well as 40% in Physics, Chemistry, and Biology combined.

  • Minimum 45% on the Pharmacy Diploma (for B.Pharm (Lateral).
  • Valid Score for the NEET UG Entrance Exam (for M.B.B.S. and BDS.).
  • Examining P.M.E.T. Entrance - Valid Score (for B.Sc., B.P.T., BAMS, B.H.M.S., and B.M.L.T.).

The allotment of seats through counseling by the relevant counseling agencies is the basis for final selections to U.G. programs. While admissions to diploma courses and B.Pharm. (Lateral) are based on merit.

P.G. & P.G. Diploma Admission at Baba Farid University

Admissions to graduate and post-graduate diploma programs are based on a candidate's performance on their bachelor's degree examination or a comparable test in a related field, and entrance testing is necessary.

  • Minimum 50% in the relevant stream of a bachelor's degree program.
  • Valid Score for the NEET PG Entrance Exam (for M.D. and M.S.).
  • Correct Score for the NEET MDS Entrance Exam (for M.D.S.).
  • G.E.T. Entrance Exam - Acceptable Mark (for M.Sc., M.P.T., and P.G. Diploma).

The allocation of seats through counseling undertaken by the relevant counseling authority determines the final selection of the programs. While M.Pharm. admissions are determined by merit.

Admission to M.Ch. and Ph.D. programmes at Baba Farid University

Candidates holding a Master's Degree or M.Phil. in a relevant subject and a minimum cumulative G.P.A. of 55% are eligible for a three-year full-time M.Ch. and Ph.D. programmes at BFUHS. The criteria used for final applicant selection are as follows:

  • Valid Score for P.H.D.E.T. Entrance Exam (for Ph.D.)
  • Good Score for Entrance Exam and Personal Interview (for M.Ch.).
  • Exams for the C.S.I.R. (J.R.F.) and U.G.C. (N.E.T.) (for Ph.D.).
  • Holders of S.L.E.T., GATE, and Teacher Fellowship (for Ph.D.).
  • Direct Fellowship Award from DST (INSPIRE), I.C.M.R., or another National Agency (Ph.D.).
  • Any other equivalent test.

Baba Farid University Facilities

  • Technical Campus: As we move forward in time, Baba Farid College has acknowledged the critical necessity to make use of technological advancements for improving educational inputs. It is the first institution in Punjab to have digital smart boards in the classrooms. For effective teaching, colleges have multi-media rooms, and more and more emphasis is being placed on offering audio-visual stimuli to improve learning.
  • Library: Any civilized society's foundation and the center of any academic institution are its libraries. A periodical area, spacious reference section, and reading hall make up the modern design of the exquisite, centrally air-conditioned library. It is well-stocked and has many books relating to the course syllabus and various topics of general interest. It subscribes to reputable national and international newspapers, publications, and journals across multiple subject areas. Additionally, it offers internet access, which the college constantly works to improve and expand upon the current supply.
  • Hostel: The college features separate, air-conditioned dorms for boys and girls with all the conveniences, including completely furnished rooms with attached bathrooms and private balconies, a specific area well-equipped with cutting-edge equipment, a clean mess, and a 24-hour power supply. Students have access to a gym and Wi-Fi Internet access, among other amenities. The boarders have access to newspapers, periodicals, television, and music systems for entertainment. A medical facility and suitable security arrangements are provided for the student's protection.
  • Cafeteria: The cafeteria at the campus is attractively built and well-maintained, and it boasts the coziest sitting configuration. The workers and students have access to the highest-quality, healthiest snacks, juice, and soft drinks, routinely inspected by staff members. The food is offered at discounted prices.
  • Transport: The institution has a well-organized bus network that provides transportation to and from all the nearby cities and villages, keeping in mind our goal of offering quality education to students from far and near locations.

Baba Farid University Placement

Baba Farid College has set up a training and placement department to offer regular training sessions in soft skills, computer skills, and personality development. This department's primary goal is to improve Baba Farid College students' employability and skill development to meet employer expectations. The general growth of the students is the main focus. The department is making an effort to offer pieces of training on various topics.

All students undergo a student evaluation to determine what they have learned and what areas need the most improvement to meet industry demand in addition to receiving their respective degrees.
