Allu Arjun

The Telugu film industry is one of the largest and most significant entertainment sectors. Thousands of films are being released, and the audiences love the characters and the stories the actors are presenting to them. Films by Telegu film actors are well-liked by the audience as they bring a lot of uniqueness to every role they play. Thus, we can find many actors in Tollywood who are getting a lovely audience from all across the country. In this article, we will look at one such actor having a huge fan following not only from South India but the entire country.

About

Allu Arjun is an actor, producer, director, and dancer who works in the South Indian cinema industry. When it comes to South Indian cinema, he is one of the first names that come to mind. His films are consistently superhits and are known among the greatest Telugu films. He is on the list of the highest-earning actors in the Tollywood industry.

With back-to-back box office successes, the actor has gotten attention, and as per Forbes India, Allu Arjun is one of the top 100 celebrities in the country. He has also been given a few nicknames based on the roles he played in his movies. Nicknames given to hin include "Bunny" and a "stylish star".

Interested in knowing more about this megastar?

Then, read on to know about his complete net worth, income sources, automobile collection, brand endorsements, lifestyle, and other aspects.

Eary Life

Allu Arjun, a well-known Telugu actor, was born on April 8, 1983, in Chennai, India, and will be 39 years old in 2022. A South Indian filmmaker and co-owner of the Indian Super League FC, "Allu Aravind," is his father, while Nirmala, the actor's mother, is a politician in India. In addition to his mom and dad, Allu also has a small brother, Allu Sirish, who works as an actor, but has no fame like Allu Arjun yet. When it comes to Allu Arjun's relationship status, he is married, and his wife's name is Sneha Reddy.

Career

Allu Arjun began his acting career in 2003 with the hit movie "Gangotri," although he had already worked as a child artist back in 1985. He acted in several films like 'Desamuduru', 'Happy', Rudhramadevi' 'Parugu', Lucky The Racer, and others. He was also awarded a Filmfare Award for Best Male Debut for his work in this film. Allu then featured in an immensely successful movie "Arya" in 2004. In addition, he was honored with the Nandi Award for Best Actor. Then another movie, "Bunny," featuring Allu, was released in 2005 and got the audience's intense love and gave him the nickname "Bunny". Recently a movie named "Pushpa" got released, and people loved it.

Allu Arjun Top Movies

1. Arya(2004)

Despite the success of Allu Arjun's first film Gangotri, his appearance has been heavily criticized. On the other hand, Allu Arjun accepted the criticism positively and reshaped himself in the film Arya. The film provided an ideal platform for Allu Arjun. Arya's thought was incredible, and it caught everyone's imagination. One-sided love is a unique theme, and young people immediately identified with the film's message. Allu Arjun's dance abilities and performance were excellent. At the time, Arya was one of the most successful characters. On the other hand, Devi Sri Prasad's music deserves special notes.

2. Desamuduru (2007)-( Ek Jwalamukhi )

Allu Arjun's journey took a new direction with the movie Desamuduru. It is Bunny's first big-budget film. Allu Arjun's career has taken a positive turn thanks to the film. Hansika makes her acting debut in Desamuduru. The movie was made more accessible to fans by the popular songs and Ali's comedic element. Allu Arjun has once again been stunned with his battles and dancing.

3. Parugu(2008) -(Veerta The Power)

Following Desamuduru, a huge film shifted his focus to a total elegant entertainer. Veerta The Power, the Hindi dub of the film, is accessible on Goldmines Telefilms' YouTube account. The film shows the father's heartbreak when his daughter runs away for love. The movie's songs, in particular, are significant. For his acting performance, he received both Nandi and Filmfare awards.

4. Vedam(2010) -(Antim Faisla)

In some films, every hero acquires a lot of admiration. Allu Arjun has achieved a great deal of respect from his movie Vedam. In this film, he gives an outstanding and perfect performance. In the character of Cable Raju, a middle-class man with huge goals, Allu Arjun is incredibly realistic. Bunny's performance in the hospital scene, as well as the conclusion, is well-deserving of appreciation. He got the Filmfare Award for Best Actor in a leading role. Also, among Allu Arjun's performances, his performance in this film is considered one of the finest. Antim Faisla, the Hindi dub of the film, is accessible on Goldmines Telefilms' YouTube account.

5. Julayi(2012)-(Dangerous Khiladi)

Julayi is among the most entertaining films in the Telegu industry, with a remarkably successful theatrical run. The role of Ravindra Nayaran, a man with higher intelligence and no life goals, is played effectively by Allu Arjun. The story's drama is about "one night" when Allu Arjun and Sonu Sood's lives were changed forever. Julayi is the main figure in Allu Arjun's career and unquestionably one of the greatest. Trivikram's punches and Devi Sri Prasad's soundtrack added to the enjoyment of the film. It's a movie that'll keep you entertained throughout.

6. Race Gurram(2014)-(Lucky The Racer)

Iddarammailatho, directed by Puri Jagannath, didn't fare well after Julayi's prominence. As a result, Bunny thought of recovering and collaborated with Surender Reddy, hoping Race Gurram would undoubtedly rank among Allu Arjun's best three blockbusters. Furthermore, Race Gurram was one of the finest entertainment of the decade thanks to the brothers' bonding, the heroine's special character, foot-tapping music, whistle-worthy dances, rib-tickling humor, blood rush emotions, and stylish photography, and excellent production qualities. It satisfies all segments of the audience. The Down Down Dupa steps of Allu Arjun could not be forgotten that easily. Allu Arjun even earned a Filmfare award for his performance in the film.

7. S/o Satyamurthy(2015)

Again for the second time in his career, Allu Arjun has collaborated with Trivikram Srinivas, the master of words. S/o Satyamurthy is a father's dedication to his kid. The film is intensely emotional. Allu Arjun has given one of his most impressive performances yet. The plot of S/o Satyamurthy is somewhat outstanding. Trivikram Srinivas has woven an emotional narrative for Allu Arjun this time. It is one of Bunny's distinct characters.

8. Sarrainodu (2016)

Sarrainodu is a Telugu film. Boyapati Srinu directed the film, produced by Allu Aravind and released in 2016. Allu Arjun, Aadhi Pinisetty, Srikanth, Rakul Preet Singh, Pradeep Rawat, Catherine Tresa, Jayaprakash, Suman, Sai Kumar, Brahmanandam, and Adarsh Balakrishna are among the cast members of Sarrainodu. It was a huge box office hit, the second highest-grossing Telugu film of 2016. This box office hit film gained critical praise and was a breakout hit.

9. Rudhramadevi (2015)

Allu Arjun has made one among his greatest judgments. He performed Gona Ganna Reddy's role in the film Rudhramadevi. His involvement has drastically transformed the film's structure. Allu Arjun is a fantastic actor. His character is really strong and well-defined. Despite his minimal screen time, he has gained greater attention. The Nandi Award for Best Character went to the stylish star. This film is considered to be one of the best of Allu Arjun.

10. Ala Vaikunthapurramuloo (2020)

Allu Arjun's best film seems to have been Ala Vaikunthapurramulo. His madness increased many times over and had no limits. His third collaboration with Trivikram Srinivas has proven to be a success. Sarileru Neekevvaru, starring Mahesh Babu, was a major competitor for the film. Despite this, it has become the highest-grossing picture of 2020. Allu Arjun has performed admirably in the character of Bantu. The film's primary highlight is the finale combat sequence that plays in the song's backdrop.

11. Pushpa: The Rise (2022)

The Tamil, Malayalam, Hindi, and Kannada versions of the Telugu action drama are also available. Tollywood superstars Allu Arjun and Rashmika Mandanna appear in the film. This movie has surpassed over Rs 300 crore globally in box-office collection. Despite the different reviews, the film's presentation is outstanding. Despite facing strong competition from Ranveer Singh's 83 and Spider-Man: No Way Home, the film was a commercial hit.

Marriage And Children

On March 6, 2011, Allu Arjun married Sneha Reddy. Allu Ayaan, his 7 years old son, and Allu Arha's 4 years old daughter are their children. Allu Arjun enjoys with his children and other family members, especially his father, and spends a lot of time with them. It can usually be seen on his social media handle, where he shares his emotions and feeling with captions attached to the post.

Net Worth

Allu Arjun's net worth in Indian rupees is 354 crore. He's one of the highest-paid actors in the film industry in the South. Every month, Allu Arjun makes 3 crores. Movies are where he makes the most of his money. The movie fees for Allu Arjun vary from 12 to 15 crores. Besides movies, brand sponsorships are also a source of income for him. He charges 1.9 crores per commercial ad. The annual profits of Allu Arjun are thought to be in the millions of rupees. In Hyderabad, he owns a nightclub. Every year, around a fifth of his net worth increases. Furthermore, the actor donates many of his profits to philanthropic organizations.

Besides this, Allu Arjun also has a variety of real estate holdings in several parts of India..

Favorites

Allu is a huge fan of Chiranjeevi, the South Indian legendary actor, while Bollywood actress Rani Mukherjee is his favorite actress. He was indeed a great fan of gymnastics and martial arts in school, and he holds a back belt in martial arts. He enjoys visiting vintage places and doing photography and painting. He's a jovial guy who enjoys making others happy.
