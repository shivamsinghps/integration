25 Best Tourist Places in Vizag

Visakhapatnam, which is popularly known as Vizag, is the right place where one can plan their summer vacation trip if they are confused about where they should spend their summer vacations. Vizag is a perfect spot for all those people who are planning to spend their summer vacations in a coastal paradise with the beauty of nature. Visakhapatnam, the city of Andhra Pradesh, is popular for its picturesque beaches and serene landscapes, and this city is one of the oldest port establishments in Indian history. Located in the heart of Andhra Pradesh, Vizag has some of the best places to visit during a summer vacation trip, and all these places hold a great amount of significance to the city's cultural heritage. There are many tourist spots in Vizag that one can visit during their trip to this city, and these places will let them know about the cultural and heritage history of Andhra Pradesh. All these tourist places of Vizag will surely make one's trip to Andhra Pradesh more meaningful as well as memorable. This article provides a complete list of all popular tourist places of Vizag that one can visit during their trip to Andhra Pradesh to learn about the heritage and culture of the state.

Following a complete list of all major tourist spots in Visakhapatnam is provided with their speciality and time to visit these places:

(1) TU 142 Aircraft Museum

If one is very passionate to know about the history of the Indian military, the TU 142 Aircraft Museum of Vishakhapatnam is one of the best and finest places to visit. This place will surely help in understanding the history of the Indian military and its functioning, and it is one of the major tourist attractions of Vizag. The Tupolev Tu-142, the aircraft which had served the Indian Naval system for more than 29 years, is preserved in this museum, and the name of this museum is taken from this Tupolev Tu-142 aircraft only. There is a VR room in this museum wherein one can surely get a more exciting experience, and it also has an exhibition hall that showcases many important defence equipments, including anti-submarine missiles, sonar, data recorder, propeller, an engine, and many more. TU 142 Aircraft Museum is positioned at Dr NTR Beach Rd, opposite the Kursura Submarine Museum, in the north campus of Andhra University of Vishakhapatnam city.

Popularly known for - Defense equipments, and Tu-142 Indian Naval Aircraft

Fee to enter the museum - INR 40 for kids, whereas INR 70 charged for adults

Timings of the museum - Weekdays: 2:00 PM to 8:30 PM

Weekends: 10:00 AM to 8:30 PM

(2) Mudasarlova Park

The Mudasarlova Park, which was completed in the year 1902, is a perfect picnic spot for all the visitors who are planning a trip to Vizag, and it is spread over an area of 20 acres. This park is counted among one of the best places to visit in Vizag, and if one is looking to spend time with verdant hills, lush greenery, and palm trees, then this park is the perfect tourist spot for them. Nestled in the picturesque valley, Mudasarlova Park has much more to offer as one can enjoy a quiet time while sitting in this park which is positioned next to the Mudasarlova Cheruvu Lake. One can refresh their mind by visiting this park and spending some time with nature, and that's why this park is counted among one of the most boosted places located in Vizag. This park is positioned at the Mudasarlova Road of the Mudasarlova area in the Hanumanthavaka town of Vizag.

Popularly known for - its natural beauty, and a place for having some quality time

Fee to enter the park - INR 10 charged for kids and for adults as well

Timings of the park - Weekdays: 8:00 PM to 5:30 PM

Weekends: 8:00 AM to 5:30 PM

(3) Thotlakonda Buddhist Complex

Positioned on a hillock 15 KM far from the main city of Vizag, the Thotlakonda Buddhist Complex is one of the finest and most interesting places to visit in Visakhapatnam while having a trip to this city. This tourist place holds rich cultural importance, and there are a number of rock-cut inscriptions hemmed into the hill rock where this complex is located. Since the hill area of Thotlakonda Buddhist Complex is located near the sea, this tourist place has become an ideal place and a major attraction for monks who built the monastery complex because of its sub-tropical climate.Positioned on a hillock 15 KM far from the main city of Vizag, the Thotlakonda Buddhist Complex is one of the finest and most interesting places to visit in Visakhapatnam while having a trip to this city. This tourist place holds rich cultural importance, and there are a number of rock-cut inscriptions hemmed into the hill rock where this complex is located. Since the hill area of Thotlakonda Buddhist Complex is located near the sea, this tourist place has become an ideal place and a major attraction for monks who built the monastery complex because of its sub-tropical climate.

Popularly known for - its Sub-tropical climate and monastery complex

Fee to enter the complex - No entry fee is charged from kids as well as from adults

Timings of the complex - Weekdays: 8:00 PM to 6:00 PM

Weekends: 8:00 AM to 6:30 PM

(4) Sagar Nagar Beach

Sagar Nagar Beach, which is counted among one of the best places to visit while having a trip to Andhra Pradesh, is positioned on the coast of the Bay of Bengal. This beach, which is included in the list of tourist places in Vizag, gives anyone serene vibes when they sit and soak on this beach. To get the ultimate experience from this tourist place, one should make sure that they are visiting this beach in the wee hours of the morning. This place is the perfect spot for tourists to try out adventurous sports, and it is popular for its golden sands, natural beauty, and picturesque sunset view.

Popularly known for - its natural beauty, adventurous sports, golden sands, and picturesque sunset view

Fee to enter the Beach - No entry fee is charged from kids as well as from adults

Timings of the Beach - Weekdays: Open 24 Hours

Weekends: Open 24 Hours

(5) Ross Hill Church

Located in the Port Area of Visakhapatnam, Rose Hill Church is counted among the popular tourist places of Vizag, and one should make sure that they have included this place in their list while having a trip to this city. One can observe completely inside this Church, and not only religious followers but also other tourists are attracted to this place because of its beautiful architecture. Being in one of the best and most popular churches in Andhra Pradesh, one can explore and attend prayers while having a visit to this church. Other than religious followers, Ross Hill Church is also commonly visited by nature lovers and photographers as this allows them to have a picture-perfect view. This church is one of the few churches in the state which one can climb to witness splendid views of the church and the natural beauty around it.

Popularly known for - its natural beauty, morning prayers, and splendid view

Fee to enter the Church - No entry fee is charged from kids as well as from adults

Timings of the Church - Weekdays: 9:00 AM - 6:00 PM

Weekends: 9:00 AM - 6:00 PM

(6) Bojjannakonda

Located in the Rebaka region of Visakhapatnam, Bojjannakonda is one of the most beautiful and popular places that should be included on everyone's list when having a trip to this city. The origin of this place can be dated back to the 4th century or beyond because of the group of six rock-cut caves, as these caves map a relationship with the time when Buddhism found its way into the region. Many Buddhist devotees and other tourists as well are attracted to this place because of the intricately carved statues of Gautam Buddha. Many tourists come to this place to unwind amidst nature, whereas some Buddhist monks travel here to meditate under the caves.

Popularly known for - its peaceful environment, natural beauty, and Buddhist early connection

Entry Fee - No entry fee is charged from kids as well as from adults

Timings - Weekdays: 9:00 AM - 6:00 PM

Weekends: 8:00 AM - 6:30 PM

(7) Visakha Museum

Visakha Museum is situated on Beach Road, opposite Palm Beach Hotel, Dutch Layout, Kirlampudi Layout, Chinna Waltair, Pedda Waltair, Visakhapatnam and is also known as Corporation Museum. This museum is for a person who loves to research the history of a new place and is an ideal location to acquire detailed knowledge about Vizag. It is home to some outstanding pieces of art, armour suits, life-size portraits, guns, spears, and other prehistoric weapons. This museum also shows a 250 - lb bomb from World War 2 that was dropped by Japan but didn't detonate. Similarly, there are various things that have been conserved and are now exhibited in Visakha Museum.

Fee to enter the museum - INR 10 per adult and INR 5 per child (5-12 years)

Timings of the museum - 11:00 am to 7:00 pm (weekdays, closed on Friday), 12:00 pm to 8:00 pm (weekends)

(8) Kambalakonda Wildlife Sanctuary

Kambalakonda Wildlife Sanctuary is spread out in an area of 71 square km and is the next thing to watch after exploring the flora of Vizag. This sanctuary received its name from a nearly located mountain name Kambalakonda. Previously, the land was the property of the Maharaja of Vizianagaram till 1970, but presently it is supervised by the Andhra Pradesh Forest Department. Many endangered species are preserved in the sanctuary, such as Indian Leopard, Russell's viper, Indian Muntjac, and Asian Paradise - flycatcher. Undoubtedly, this is one of the best places for wildlife admirers to explore in Vizag.

Fee to enter the sanctuary - INR 10 per adult, INR 5 per kid, and INR 15 per person for trekking

Timings of the sanctuary - 9:00 am to 4:30 pm

(9) Araku Valley

As far as one's eyes can see, they can spot that every inch of Araku Valley's land is masked with greenery, and without any question, it is a perfect spot for nature admirers to explore in Vizag. It is situated in Araku - Visakhapatnam Road, Araku, Visakhapatnam. Furthermore, the scenic view of sky-high mountains across the coffee farm can enchant visitors to the core and leave them in awe for sure. If visitors want to spot the Visakhapatnam's natural beauty, then they should definitely head to this location and capture some alluring landscapes to cherish for a lifetime.

Fee to enter the valley - No entry fee is charged from kids as well as from adults

Timings of the valley - always open

(10) Rama Krishna Mission Beach

Rama Krishna Mission Beach is considered amongst the most famous and uplifted locations by Visakhapatnam tourism, where tourist rush is mostly spotted. This beach is named after the Rama Krishna Mission ashram that is placed just across the beach; it is perfect for a relaxing time. One can absorb the warmth of the sun and wander with their partner here. Visitors can also spot the famous Kurusura Submarine Museum positioned on the shore. Beyond a shadow of a doubt, this is amongst the most amicable locations near Visakhapatnam.

Fee to enter the beach - INR 50 is charged for boat rides

Timings of the beach - 5:00 am to 12:00 am

(11) Gangavaram Beach

Gangavaram Beach is listed amongst the most alluring beaches in Vizag, with dramatic views make up of breathtaking palm trees. This location is also famous for getting featured in numerous films and television shows. Freshened air and soft sands make it an ideal sit back for all the visitors after travelling for a long day. Rocky structures on the beach are a major attraction for all visitors.

Fee to enter the beach - No entry fee is charged from kids as well as from adults

Timings of the beach - Always open

(12) Lawson's Bay Beach

Lawson's Bay Beach is an expansion of Ramakrishnan Beach and is untouched as well as relaxing. This is included on the list of best destinations to explore in Vizag, and this isolated Beach is placed within beautiful sands and lush greenery. Visitors love to explore this beach and spend their time surfing, swimming, and sunbathing. If one wants to move closer to nature and loosen up, this location ought to be at the top of the list.

Fee to enter the beach - No entry fee is charged from kids as well as from adults

Timings of the beach - Always open

(13) Sea War Memorial

Sea War Memorial is located at 31 Beach Road, Kirlampudi Layout, Chinna Waltair, Pedda Waltair, Visakhapatnam, and travellers who are on their way to the beach generally come here as this is amongst the Vizag's best touring spots. In the year 1996, this monument was erected to pay tribute to Eastern Naval Command sailors and the Indian Navy, who all served in Indo-Pak War. Visitors will find a pyre in the memorial that has been illuminated for all time to pay gratitude and honour to the martyrs of India. Tankers, missiles, and fighter plane models are exhibited in the memorial.

Fee to enter the memorial - No entry fee is charged from kids as well as from adults

Timings of the memorial - Always open

(14) Bheemili Beach

Bheemili Beach is located on Beach Road, Visakhapatnam, and is placed where the Gosthani River and Bay of Bengal unite. Visitors can spot the leftovers of fort and graveyards that were established in the 17th century and demonstrates the Dutch era. Due to the aggressive waves and strong winds, most of the beaches in Visakhapatnam are unsafe. Even so, Bheemili Beach is popular for its calm waves and consoling surroundings that are an absolute haven for visitors from chaotic city life.

Fee to enter the beach - No entry fee is charged from kids as well as from adults

Timings of the beach - Always open

(15) Rishikonda Beach

Rishikonda Beach is located in Beach Road, Rushikonda, Bheemili, Visakhapatnam, and is a majestic beach with see-through water. Placed on the coast of the Bay of Bengal and due to its unmatched charm, this beach is considered the 'Jewel of East Coast. This beach is positioned in the midst of lush greenery and is overlooked by the magnificent Rishikonda hillock. Travellers who are fond of water sports should explore this beach undoubtedly. This beach is easy to reach as it is placed at a distance of 8 kilometres from Vizag port.

Fee to enter the beach - No entry fee is charged from kids as well as from adults

Timings of the beach - Always open

(16) Simhachalam Temple

Located on the Simhachalam Road of the Simhachalam region of Vizag, this temple strikes a perfect balance between a historic place, and a religious site, like the Vizag city. One can go to Visakhapatnam anytime, and they won't be disappointed for sure; Simhachalam Temple is one of those tourist attractions of this city, which solidify the statement given above. Simhachalam Temple, which is counted as the most popular tourist place in Vizag, is a temple dedicated to Lord Narsimha. This Hindu housing temple, which is highly decorated with elephant idols on the boundaries and a horse-drawn chariot made of stone at the front, is another attraction that draws a huge number of travellers to the city. These are the reasons which make this temple a must-visit tourist place when having a trip to Vizag.

Popularly known for - its natural beauty, morning & evening prayers, and splendid view

Fee to enter the temple - INR 100 as the entry fee is charged from kids as well as from adults

Timings of the temple - Morning: 7:00 AM - 4:00 PM

Evening: 6:00 PM - 9:00 PM

(17) Dolphin's Nose

Located on the Gangavaram port of Vizag, Dolphin's Nose is a popular tourist place in the city, and it offers spectacular views of the busy city at one end and a fascinating viewpoint for visitors. The name of this place is named after the large rock viewpoint that resembles a shape similar to Dolphin's nose shape, and this place also provides breathtaking views of the coastline on the other end of the rock. Counted among one of the most amazing and beautiful tourist places in the city, Dolphin's Nose attracts tourists from all over India.

Popularly known for - its spectacular views, fascinating viewpoint, and natural beauty

Fee to enter the spot - No entry fee is charged from kids as well as from adults

Timings of the place - Weekdays: 3:00 PM - 5:00 PM

Weekend: 3:00 PM - 6:00 PM

(18) VUDA Park

VUDA Park, which is positioned on the Beach Rd Kailasagiri Arch of Chinna Waltair (Jalari Peta) region of the city, is known for containing more than 2500 trees that are spread across an area of 37 acres of land. The greenery of this park elevates the spirits of nature lovers and makes sure that the visitors will mesmerize by the place. This tourist place is the perfect spot for gyms, children's play areas, and fitness centres, and all these places keep the tourists and local residents occupied. If one is looking for a quiet tourist place while having a trip to Vizag, then one should visit this park.

Fee to enter the park - INR 20 is charged as the entry fee is charged from kids as well as from adults between 8:30 AM - 2:00 PM

whereas INR 60 is charged as an entry fee is charged from kids as well as adults between 2 PM - 8:30 PM

Timings of the park - Weekdays: 8:30 AM - 8:30 PM

Weekend: 8:30 AM - 8:30 PM

(19) Indira Gandhi Zoological Park

Indira Gandhi Zoological Park is located near a dairy farm, Yendada, Visakhapatnam, and is a must-visit tourist attraction for the travel fanatic who admires wildlife. This zoological park was set up in 1977 and is home to around 100 species of birds, reptiles, and mammals. Positioned in the midst of dense Kambalakonda reserve forest, this is a worth watching location for people on their tour to Vizag.

Fee to enter the zoological park - INR 10 for kids and INR 20 for adults

Timings of the park - 9:00 am to 5:00 pm

(20) Matsyadarshini Aquarium

Matsyadarshini Aquarium is located in Beach Road, Pandurangapuram, Chinna Waltair, Pandurangapuram, Visakhapatnam, Andhra Pradesh and is an initiative begun by the municipal corporation of Visakhapatnam. The aquarium conserves and houses innumerable species of freshwater and saltwater aquatic life. Placed on the alluring shore of Ramakrishna Beach, this aquarium has numerous marine diversities such as lionfish, damsels, and squirrel fish, over and above, it offers a compelling experience to the visitors.

Fee to enter the aquarium - INR 20 for kids and INR 30 for adults

Timings of the aquarium - 9:00 AM - 9:00 PM

(21) Submarine Museum

Submarine Museum is located on RK Beach Road, Kirlampudi Layout, Chinna Waltair, Pandurangapuram, Visakhapatnam, and as a port city, it tops the list of sightseeing sites in Vizag. The museum is basically set up inside the submarine called INS Kurusura. Placed on the shores of Rushikonda Beach, this museum contains artefacts and pictures that display the life of warriors. Taking people through the life of soldiers, unquestionably, this is among the great place to explore in Vizag.

Fee to enter the museum - INR 25 per individual

Timings of the museum - 2:00 pm to 8:30 pm

(22) Borra Caves

Placed in the midst of the Ananthagiri Hills in Araku Valley, Borra Caves is a haven for history lovers. It is located at an elevation of around 705 meters and is believed to be one of India's largest caves. In 1807 the caves were founded, and ever since, they've turned into a famous travelling spot.

Made of karstic limestone, tourists can see the exquisiteness of these structures during the time natural skylight covers its way in. Visitors can also praise the mystifying charm of scenic landscapes, evergreen forests, and awe-inspiring views from which the Borra Caves are surrounded.

Fee to enter the caves - INR 45 for kids and INR 60 for adults

Timings of the caves - 10:00 am to 5:00 pm

(23) Kailasagiri

Kailasagiri is located in Hill Top Road, Kailasagiri, Visakhapatnam, and is mostly recommended by the local residents to the tourists who are on a trip to Vizag. This most popular hill station is positioned in the heart of Visakhapatnam and is settled at the height of 360 ft and proffers striking views of the forests, city, and incredible beaches. Believed to be a famous spot because of the huge idols of Shiva and Parvathi, this beautiful place offers a large number of fun activities, pilgrimage sites, and electrifying experiences to tourists.

Fee to enter the Kailasagiri - counts on what you do and how you travel here

Timings of the Kailasagiri - 6:00 am to 7:30 pm

(24) Katiki Waterfalls

The location of Katiki Waterfalls is Katiki Waterfalls Roads, Visakhapatnam, Andhra Pradesh, and this spot should be definitely mentioned on one's Vizag sightseeing list. This scenic waterfall is encompassed by lush greenery in Araku Valley and measures around 50 ft. The main source of Katiki Waterfalls is the Gosthani River. It is considered amongst the most attractive waterfalls nearby Visakhapatnam and is a retreat for adventurous travellers where they can enjoy trekking in the region nearby the waterfall.

Fee to enter the waterfalls - No entry charges

Timings of the waterfalls - 6:00 am to 7:00 pm

(25) Yarada Beach

Yarada Beach is a must-visit tourist attraction that one should not miss exploring during their trip to Visakhapatnam. On one side, the beach is covered by the Bay of Bengal, and the other three sides are enveloped by the splendid hillock. This is a perfect spot to get through the day, witnessing the appealing views of the sunrise and sunset.

Fee to enter the beach - No entry fee. But INR 30 is the fee for entering the Arigold property and shopping there

Timings of the beach - Always open
