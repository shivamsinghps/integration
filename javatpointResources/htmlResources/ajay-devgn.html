Ajay Devgn

Introduction

The Bollywood industry has been entertaining people for many years, and almost every Indian loves to watch Bollywood movies. Not only this, some people who do not live in India also like to watch Bollywood movies. Apart from children, older people are also a fan of Bollywood movies.

There have been many legends in the Bollywood industry. Whether it is the field of singing or acting, there are many great artists in the Bollywood industry. The artists who work in the Bollywood industry present their art in front of everyone and entertain people. Today, in this article, we will talk about one of the greatest actors, a superstar of Bollywood. This article discusses superstar Ajay Devgn.

Ajay Devgn is a big name in the Bollywood industry, who has made everyone crazy through his action and acting. Today there is hardly anyone who does not know Ajay Devgn. Ajay Devgn is considered one of the most successful actors in the Bollywood industry today. He is also called Singham of Bollywood because he worked in the Singham movie, and this movie is one of the most successful movies of Ajay Devgn's career. He started his acting career with Phool Aur Kaante, and since then, he has worked in more than 120 films.

Ajay Devgn is considered one of the most successful actors in the Bollywood industry today. He is not only a successful actor but also a successful film producer and director. He does all kinds of roles very easily and has appeared in more than 100 Bollywood films. Ajay Devgn has also been honoured with Padma Shri by the Government of India, and two of his films have also received the National Award.

Early Life

Ajay Devgn was born in New Delhi, the capital of India. He was born on 2nd April 1969 in a Punjabi family. His father's name is Veeru Devgan, and his mother's name is Veena Devgan. Ajay Devgn is originally from Amritsar, Punjab. You will be surprised to know that Ajay Devgn's real name is not Ajay Devgn but Vishal Veeru Devgan. He changed his name to Ajay before appearing in films. Ajay Devgn's parents have four children (Ajay Devgn, Neelam Devgan Gandhi, Kavita Devgan Vaswani, Anil Devgn). Ajay Devgn is the eldest son of his parents.

His father was a well-known person in the Bollywood industry. He was a famous stunt choreographer, and along with this, he was also an actor and film director. He had worked in more than 200 films. Moreover, his mother is a film producer. Ajay Devgan's brother Anil Devgan was a screenwriter and filmmaker, but unfortunately, he died in 2020 due to cardiac arrest. Ajay Devgan studied at Silver Beach High School, Juhu, Mumbai. After this, to continue his further studies, he took admission in Mithibai College, Mumbai, and completed his graduation.

Filmy Career

Ajay Devgn is one of the most successful actors of Bollywood in today's time. He is indeed one of the best actors in Bollywood today, but he had dreamt of being a director, not an actor. His father, Veeru Devgan, wanted his son to become an actor because Veeru Devgan himself wanted to be an actor, but for some reason, he could not become an actor, and then he decided that he would make his son an actor.

Today Ajay Devgn is such a big actor only and only because of his father. Ajay Devgan's father has a huge contribution in making him so successful. Ajay Devgan's father gave him a lot of training to become an actor. Ajay Devgan has appeared as a child artist in some films in his childhood. Ajay Devgan's first film as a child actor was Pyari Behna, and in this film, he played the childhood role of Mithun Chakraborty's character.

But Ajay Devgan entered the film world with the 1991 film Phool Aur Kaante. But this film was earlier supposed to be done by Akshay Kumar, but for some reason, Akshay Kumar did not do this film, and Ajay Devgn got a chance to act in this film as a lead actor.

Phool Aur Kaante proved to be a super hit film, and Ajay Devgn became many people's favourite overnight because of this film. Ajay Devgn also received the Filmfare Award for Best male Actor Debut for this film. No doubt he did a great performance in his first film, and because of this, he kept getting films one after the other. His stunts on two motorcycles in this film remain a topic of discussion even today.

After the success of the film Phool Aur Kaante, Ajay's second film, Jigar, was released in 1992. Karishma Kapoor also acted with Ajay Devgn in this film, and this film was equally successful as the film Phool or Kaante. The two consecutive successful films made Ajay Devgan a superstar. After this, Ajay Devgn became popular and started getting numerous offers for films.

After watching Ajay Devgn's acting in the film Dilwale, released in 1994, it was confirmed that Ajay Devgan is the upcoming Bollywood superstar. Ajay Devgan played the character of Arun Saxena in this film.

Ajay Devgan's film Zakhm which was released in 1998, proved to be a great film of his acting career, and for this film, he also won the national film award in the best actor category.

The very next year, in 1999, another film by Ajay Devgan was released, whose name was Hum Dil De Chuke Sanam. Apart from Ajay Devgan, Salman Khan and Aishwarya Rai were also in this film. Ajay played the role of Aishwarya's husband in this film, and this film proved to be one of the best films of his life; he also got a lot of praise for this film.

After this, Ajay Devgn worked in many films; Ajay Devgan has worked in romantic, action, comedy, and thriller films.

Dilwale, Phool Aur Kaante, Drishyam, Taanaji: The Unsung Warrior, Ishq, Singham, Omkara, Golmaal, The Legend of Bhagat Singh, Company, Once Upon a Time in Zakhm, Mumbaai, Raid, Gangaajal, and are some of the best films of Ajay Devgn's career. Apart from this, he has also done guest Apparence in many films.

Ajay Devgan has appeared in more than 100 films to date. Apart from films, Ajay Devgan has also acted in web series. The web series Rudra released in the year 2022 was the first web series by Ajay Devgan, and it was well-liked by people.

Apart from this, Ajay Devgan will also be seen in many big films in the coming years, such as RRR, Runway 34, Drishyam 2, Maidaan, Singham 3, Thank God, and many more.

Production and VFX company

Apart from acting in films, Ajay Devgn has also produced many films. He has his film distribution and production company named Ajay Devgn Films. This company was established by Ajay Devgn's father, Veeru Devgan, and Ajay Devgn himself in 2000 and the same year, Raju Chacha, ADFF's first film, was released. Ajay Devgn and Kajol were in the lead roles in this film.

After the film Raju chacha ADFF released many films such as U Me Aur Hum, All the Best: Fun Begins, Singham Returns, Shivaay, Total Dhamaal, and many more.

After the production company's success (Ajay Devgn Films), Ajay Devgn established his Visual Effects (VFX) company in 2015. The name of this VFX company is NY VFXWAALA. The name of this company is named after Ajay Devgan's kids, Nysa and Yug. NY VFXWAALA has worked in many films famous Bollywood films such as Tamasha, Shivaay, Simmba, Dilwale, Prem Ratan Dhan Payo, Bajirao Mastani, Force 2, and Mersal.

Marriage and Children

Ajay Devgn married Kajol Mukherjee on 24 February 1999. Kajol Devgn, better known as Kajol, is one of the famous Bollywood actresses. Kajol has given stellar performances in films like Bazzzigar, Dilwale Dulhania Le Jayenge, Tanhaji, etc. Ajay Devgn and Kajol have been dating each other since 1995 and later married in 1999.

It is said that Ajay Devgn was in a relationship with Karisma Kapoor before in a relationship with Kajol. However, they ended their relationship in 1995, and after this, the relationship between Ajay Devgn and Kajol started. Ajay Devgn and Kajol Devgn have two children, a boy, and a girl. The daughter's name is Nysa, born on 20 April 2003, and the son's name is Yug, born on 13 September 2010.
