Bharathidasan University

Bharathidasan University Online has received approval from the Distance Education Bureau (D.E.B.), the University Grants Commission (U.G.C.), N.A.A.C., and NIRF. In honor of the celebrated Indian revolutionary Tamil poet Kanagasabai Subbu, commonly known as Bharathidasan (29 April 1891 - 21 April 1964), the University was named Bharathidasan University in February 1982. We will construct a brave new world as the motto of the Bharathidasan University in Palkalaiperur, Tiruchirappalli, Tamil Nadu, India. Bharathidasan inspired the poetic sentence. Tiruchirappalli, Tamil Nadu, India, is "We will create a bold new world." Bharathidasan inspired the poetic line. The University aspires to live up to such a lofty ideal by ushering in a brand new world of academic innovation for social change in the area.

B.D.U. is one of Tamil Nadu, India's oldest institutions. The original main Campus of Bharathidasan University was situated in Palkalaiperur, a vast region of more than 1000 acres. How You Can Apply

Candidates who wish to apply for admission to Bharathidasan University may do so online using the University's official website by registering and submitting the necessary paperwork and the application fee. The following are the steps required to apply:

  • Check out Bharathidasan University webpage.
  • Choose the program of study you want to enroll in.
  • Provide all the information requested in the form.
  • Upload all required documents as requested.
  • Pay the 200 INR application cost using debit, credit, or net banking.
  • Remove the application form's printout and save it for offline use.

Scholarships at the University of Bharathidasan

The students of Bharathidasan University receive financial aid in the form of loans and scholarships. The many sorts of scholarships offered by Bharathidasan University to deserving and needy students are listed below:

  • Scholarships for post-matriculation are available to BC MBC students with a minimum attendance rate of 75% and whose family income does not exceed 1 lakh per year.
  • Special Loan Scholarship: This scholarship is offered to S.C. and S.T. students residing in the University'sUniversity's dorms whose family income does not exceed $50,000.
  • Scholarship offered by the Indian Government: To be eligible, parents in the S.C. and S.T. segments must earn a combined maximum of INR 1,00,000 and INR 1,08,000 each year. Students must also have fulfilled at least 75% of their required attendance.

Facilities at Bharathidasan University

  • Fitness Center: Under the direction of certified fitness instructors, Bharathidasan University offers facilities at its fitness center to its students, staff, and visitors. For ladies only, there is a separate fitness center. The primary goals of the fitness center are physical fitness, weight loss, and bodybuilding. The exercise center is outfitted with all the amenities required for enhancing cardio fitness, such as a treadmill, bicycle, ergometer, and rolling machine.
  • University Hospital: Both teaching personnel and non-teaching staff have access to hospital services. Staff members and students must present their smart cards to the medical officer to receive medical consultation. They must also enter their information into a register available in the health center. In addition, a fully functional upgraded medical campus with full-time doctor service and a new ambulance for patients' emergency requirements is also constructed.
  • Bank: Bharathidasan University offers its students banking services through the Bank of India, with 750 Bank of India locations across the country. The bank offers savings accounts, current accounts, foreign exchange services, and a multi-branch banking system. The bank also plans to supply an A.T.M. machine. Student fees may also be paid at the bank's extension counter.
  • Canteen: The University also has two canteens where students, staff, parents, and guests may get clean, wholesome cuisine. Vegetarian and occasionally non-vegetarian food products are served at these two canteens as snacks.
  • University Library: The University Library is a separate facility on the main Campus with a beautiful multi-story design that houses all kinds of resources, including e-library materials. The library includes different Halls for keeping books, journals, magazines, and reference materials, in addition to an extensive and tastefully decorated reading room.
    Regarding educational materials, the library subscribes to several journals and has a sizable collection of books and electronic books. The library has D.E.L.N.E.T. and INFLIBNET connections. Thanks to the U.G.C. Infonet service, thousands of e-journals and more than 10,000 other e-resources are accessible. Students and teachers can access over 5,000 old issues and 7,000 electronic books through the University's digital library. The library provides reprographic services for the advantage of researchers and students. Except for five national holidays, the library is open daily from 8 am to 8 pm. Students, academics, and teaching staff who utilize the library can access its resources freely. The departments also have their libraries in addition to the aforementioned common facilities.
  • Green Energy from a Solar Power Plant: A grid-interactive 500 kWp Solar P.V. as part of the University's green initiatives, a power plant was built and placed into operation at the Palkalaiperur campus of Bharathidasan University for a total cost of Rs. 4.85 crores.
