Zoom Alternatives

Zoom is one of the leading cloud-based, reliable, and easy video conferencing applications, founded by Eric Yuan. Currently, it becomes an essential part for everyone and is used by all small, medium, as well as large organizations. It allows users to meet with partners, employees, and family members with or without video and with or without audio. It also allows us to record sessions, collaborate on projects, share screen, voice, and many more.

Most Commonly used Zoom Alternatives

A list of most commonly used Zoom Alternatives is given below -

  1. GoTo Meeting
  2. Google Hangouts Meet
  3. Cisco Webex Meeting
  4. BlueJeans Meeting
  5. Zoho Meeting
  6. Microsoft Term
  7. Skype
  8. Eyeson
  9. Join.me
  10. Discord
  11. Jitsi Meet
  12. Facebook Messenger Rooms

1. GoTo Meeting

GoTo Meeting is a reliable, web-based, and user-friendly online video conferencing platform that is everyday used by millions of users. It allows users to meet face to face, share opinions, presentations, and more with each other. The main feature of GoTo Meetings is that it allows users to schedule meetings in advance and also allows them to record meetings. Its basic plan supports up to 150 participants at once for the meeting. 3

Pros of GoTo Meeting

A list of Pros of GoTo Meeting is given below -

  • GoTo Meeting is an easy to navigate platform.
  • We do not require much training to use GoTo Meeting.
  • GoTo Meeting is a fast, reliable, and user-friendly platform.
  • To provide security, it uses various encryption techniques.
  • It easily integrates with Office 365.
  • It contains file sharing and document collaboration tools.

Cons of GoTo Meeting

  • GoTo Meeting is more expensive as compared to Zoom.
  • Uses higher bandwidth.
  • Need to pay separately to use GoToWebinar tools.

Pricing

GoTo Meeting paid plans start at the cost of $12/ organizer per month.

Website:

Click on the given link to know more about GoTo Meeting: https://www.gotomeeting.com/en-in

2. Google Hangouts Meet

Google Hangout is a free, decent, well-known, reliable, and widely used Zoom alternatives. It allows us to add up to 10 participants at once to make video conferencing. Its free trial available at the end of 2021.

Pros of Google Hangouts Meet

A list of Pros of Google Hangouts Meet is given below -

  • Google Hangouts Meet is easier to setup.
  • It is easy to create and join video calls.
  • It allows users to share documents, links, pictures, and more during the meeting.
  • It is easy to add more users to the same meeting.
  • It is the best choice for medium-sized businesses.
  • The screen sharing feature is available.
  • It supports browsers like Firefox, Edge, Chrome, and Safari.

Cons of Google Hangouts Meet

  • It is not a suitable platform for large meetings.
  • It can crash on some Mac devices.
  • It can't be able to send multimedia documents.
  • Not good support for video collaboration.

Pricing

Due to Covid 19, its free plans are available at the end of 2021. Its paid plans start at $10 per user per month.

Website

Click on the given link to signup with Google Hangouts Meet: https://meet.google.com/

3. Cisco Webex Meeting

Cisco Webex Meeting is one of the best Zoom alternatives. It is a modern video conferencing platform in the World. It allows users to make HD video calling, including screen sharing, cloud storage, and video recording options.

Pros of Cisco Webex Meeting

A list of Pros of Cisco Webex Meeting is given below -

  • Cisco Webex is a secure, flexible, and seamless video conferencing (meeting) platform.
  • It supports up to 100 participants.
  • Unlimited usage with no time restriction.
  • Ease of setting up meetings.
  • It allows us to share chat history with new members.

Cons of Cisco Webex Meeting

  • Issues related to connecting via audio.
  • It is complicated to navigate Cisco Webex as compared to other alternatives.
  • Lack of good backgrounds.

Pricing

Cisco Webex meeting paid plans to start at $13.50 per host per month with 50 participants.

Cisco Webex meeting plus plan starts at $17.97 per month with 100 participants.

Cisco Webex meeting business plan starts at $26.95.

Website:

Click on the given link to learn more about the Cisco Webex Meeting: https://www.webex.com/

4. BlueJeans Meeting

BlueJeans is an excellent cloud-based video, audio, and web conferencing platform that allows us to make high-quality video calling securely. It allows us to quickly and securely hold live video calls, webinars, meetings, and conference calls. It is capable to connect with other video conferencing platforms such as Cisco Jabber, Microsoft Lync, and Google Hangouts.

Pros of BlueJeans Meeting

A list of Pros of BlueJeans Meeting is given below -

  • BlueJeans Meeting is a simple-to-use and fast meeting platform for users.
  • It is a more secure meeting platform as compared to other zoom alternatives.
  • It is a fast cloud-based service that allows us to connect desktops, mobile devices, and more in one video meeting.
  • It is easy to create a new account in the BlueJeans meeting.
  • Unlimited recording options are available.
  • It easily integrates with Slack, HipChat, and Skype.

Cons of BlueJeans Meeting

  • BlueJeans Meetings is more expensive.
  • A high-speed internet connection is required. Otherwise, the system got crashed.
  • It does not run fast as compared to other alternatives such as Zoom and Cisco Webex.
  • It is quite a buggy platform.

Pricing

BlueJeans Meeting basic plan starts at $19.98 per month for 50 participants.

Website:

Click on the given link to make a video conferencing using BlueJeans Meeting https://www.bluejeans.com/

5. Zoho Meeting

Zoho Meeting is one the best, open-source, and more powerful zoom alternatives. It is an open-source video conferencing platform that allows us to host and record meetings securely using an advanced encryption technique.

Pros of Zoho Meeting

A list of pros of Zoho Meeting is given below -

  • Zoho uses end-to-end encryption technology to provide more security.
  • It easily integrates with applications such as Zoho CRM, Projects, and Bookings.
  • It is a user-friendly platform supported by various platforms, including desktop, Android, and iOS.
  • It allows us to view and download webinar reports.
  • Participants do not need to sign up to use the Zoho meeting. They can directly join meetings from the browsers.

Cons of Zoho Meeting

  • The screen sharing feature is not good.
  • A high-speed internet connection is required.
  • It is difficult to manage meetings.
  • Sometimes integration with Hubspot doesn't work properly.

Pricing

Zoho meeting paid version starts at $10 per host per month for 100 participants.

Website

Click on the given link to signup with Zoho Meeting https://www.zoho.com/meeting/

6. Microsoft Term

Microsoft Term is a cloud-based, most popular built-in platform for video conferencing. It is specially designed for Office 365 users. Currently, it is used by huge companies. It allows us to share our screen for detailed explanations. It allows us to make one on one and group video calls, screen sharing, support online training sessions, webinars, and more.

Pros of Microsoft Term

A list of Pros of Microsoft Term is given below -

  • We do need to download Microsoft Term to attend the video conference.
  • It is supported by various platforms such as Andriod, iOS, Linux, as well as desktop.
  • It supports various file formats such as CSV, XLS, and DOCX.
  • It offers 1 TB of OneDrive storage, unlimited app integration, including Adobe, Trello, and more.
  • It is suitable for both small as well as large organizations.

Cons of Microsoft Term

  • Sometimes the structure of files confuses users.
  • Users face challenges regarding the permission setting.

Pricing

Microsoft Term is available for both versions free as well as paid. Its free trial available for 6 months. and its paid plan starts at $5 per user per month.

Website

Click on the given link to learn more about Microsoft Terms https://www.microsoft.com/en-in/microsoft-365/microsoft-teams/group-chat-software

7. Skype

Skype is a well-known and widely used video conferencing platform used to make and receive free voice and video calls over the internet. It is one of the popular zoom alternatives. It allows users to make free video calls, one-to-one voice calls, group calls, and more. It is suitable for mobile, computer, as well as tablets.

Pros of Skype

There are the following Pros of Skype -

  • Skype is free and easy to download.
  • It allows us to quickly share ideas.
  • No signups are required to use Skype.
  • It allows us to make HD voice video calling.
  • It allows us to do a video call to multiple users at the same time.
  • It offers end-to-end encryption techniques.
  • It is a secure & confidential video conferencing platform.

Cons of Skype

  • Sometimes users suffer problems related to PC overloading during the call.
  • Difficult to share large files.
  • On the Windows platform, it takes a lot of time to load and start.
  • It requires a high-speed internet connection.
  • We are unable to receive SMS on Skype.

Pricing

Skype is usually free to use. Its paid online plan starts at $2.99 per month, and the Office 365 Business Premium plan starts at $12.50 user per month.

Website

Click on the below link to sign up with Skype - https://www.skype.com/en/

8. Eyeson

Eyeson is one of the most popular cloud-based zoom alternatives. It is suitable for all sized businesses. It is similar to collaboration (check) products like slack, Jira, Freshdesk, Zendesk, YouTube, and Facebook. It offers 100% safe meeting rooms and becomes Europe's 1st meeting solution platform.

Pros of Eyeson

A list of Pros of Eyeson is given below -

  • No need to download Eyeson. Participants directly join meetings through their browser.
  • You need to click only one link to join the meetings.
  • It allows users to record conferences.
  • It allows us to share PDFs and images with attendees.
  • You can check past video calling history.
  • It offers good quality audio and video.

Cons of Eyeson

  • Free plan is not available.
  • No whiteboard feature for video collaboration.
  • It does not allow us to share a screen and camera at the same time.

Pricing

Eyeson basic plan starts at $9 per host per month.

Website

Click on the below link to learn more about the Eyeson - https://www.eyeson.com/

9. Join.me

Join.me is also the best online meeting and team collaboration platform similar to Zoom. It is more suitable for small businesses. It also acts as TeamViewer and skype. It also allows other team members to control the mouse and keyboard.

Pros of Join.me

A list of Pros of Join.me is given below -

  • me is suitable for platforms like Desktop, Android, as well as iOS.
  • It supports various languages such as German, English, French, Italian, Portuguese, and Spanish.
  • It is a simple and reliable video conferencing platform.
  • No time limit for video calling.
  • It is the combination of both Skype as well as TeamViewer.
  • Easy to navigate.
  • Smooth video conferencing.

Cons of Join.me

  • Video quality decreases with a large number of participants.
  • Sound quality is not as good as compared to other alternatives.
  • It does not integrate with Outlook.
  • It is quite difficult for beginners.

Pricing

Join.me free plan available for 14 days. Its lite plan starts at $10 per host per month, the Pro plan available at $20 per host per month, and the business plan available at $30 per host per month.

Website

Click on the given link to know more about Join.me. - https://www.join.me/

10. Discord

Discord is one of the strongest and popular zoom alternatives. It is the best group-chatting platform launched in 2015. It allows gamers to connect up to 50 attendees at once. It allows us to communicate with our team through text, voice, and video calling. Its free app is available for Android, iOS, Linux, macOS, and Windows platforms.

Pros of Discord

A list of Pros of Discord is given below -

  • Discord is an excellent platform that provides free chat and video call services.
  • It easily integrates with Xbox account.
  • You can use Discord on platforms like Android, iOS, Linux, macOS, and Windows.
  • It offers private as well as public servers.
  • Discord provides an easy-to-use interface and group chatting tools.
  • It is free to download.
  • It is the best, suitable, and more flexible platform for gamers.

Cons of Discord

  • 8MB limit for free users.
  • It is quite hard to learn.
  • The search option is not good.
  • It is only used by gamers.

Pricing

Discord basic plan starts at $5 per month or $50 per year. Its standard plan starts at $10 per month or $100 per year.

Website

Click on the below link to learn more about Discord: https://discord.com/

11. Jitsi Meet

Jitsi Meet is one of the great zoom alternatives. It is a secure, flexible, well-known, free, and open-source video conferencing platform. To use and join meetings on Jitsi Meet, we don't need to create an account. It is supported by platforms like Windows, Mac, and Linux. It allows participants to share their screen, send text messages during the video call (conference) for better presentation.

Pros of Jitsi Meet

There are the following Pros of Jitsi Meet -

  • Jitsi Meet provides a user-friendly as well as developer-friendly interface.
  • It easily integrates with various chat applications to provide better video meeting facilities.
  • No signup or download is required to use the Jitsi Meet platform.
  • It is a completely free video conferencing platform.
  • It provides better security using a password, keeps conversations private, and end to end encryption techniques.
  • It has no limit to join users.
  • It easily integrates with Slack, Google Calendar, and Office 365.

Cons of Jitsi Meet

  • Less than 50 people can join a meeting.
  • Sometimes, we face problems related to the audio delay.
  • Its beta version can blur the background.
  • It is a new entrant in the market.
  • It provides inflexible screen sharing options.

Pricing

Jitsi Meet is completely free to use.

Website

Click on the given link to know more about Jitsi Meet: https://meet.jit.si/

12. Facebook Messenger Rooms

Facebook Messenger Rooms is one of the most popular Zoom alternatives. It allows Facebook members to create public as well as private video chat rooms. It is commonly used to hold up to 50 participants for unlimited time access and 100 attendees for 40 minutes. The best part of the Facebook Messanger Room is that we don't need to login into a Facebook account to use messenger rooms.

Pros of Facebook Messenger Rooms

A list of Pros of Facebook Messenger Rooms is given below -

  • Facebook Messenger Room provides a fast and user-friendly platform.
  • We don't need a Facebook account to join meetings.
  • It is a simple and easy-to-use platform.
  • It provides an ability to share room availability with friends and partners.
  • No time limit for video conferences.
  • It also allows us to invite those participants who are not our Facebook friends.

Cons of Facebook Messenger Rooms

  • Facebook Messengers Room is not suitable for business.
  • It is difficult to provide complete privacy.
  • It can quickly drain the battery of your device.
  • It takes a lot of space.

Pricing

Facebook Messenger Room is a free video-conferencing platform.

Website

Click on the given link to learn more about Facebook Messenger Rooms: https://www.messenger.com/rooms
