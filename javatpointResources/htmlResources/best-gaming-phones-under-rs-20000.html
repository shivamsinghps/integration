Best Gaming Phones under Rs. 20000

Mobile games are one of the exciting ways to spend your spare time. Playing games on a smartphone is always entertaining and enjoyable. Some of the popular games that gamers love to play are newly launched mobile games like PUBG Mobile, FreeFire, Fortnite, and a lot more. Keeping the requirement of gamers, chipset manufacturers started manufacturing processors that fit for the gaming experience. Other than the processor, gamers also wish for a high power battery, high refresh rate screen, device cooling feature, dual stereo speakers, etc., that also take the gaming experience to the next level.

Some of the best gaming phones with a high budget are Asus ROG Phone 5, iPhone 12 Pro Max, OnePlus 9 Pro, etc. But some mid-range phones also provide a quality gaming experience.

Affordable mid-range phones are suitable for playing the game on them. They also offer capable enough chipset processors such as Qualcomm and MediaTek that handle games smoothly. In this article, we will see some of the best gaming smartphones that come under Rs 20,000.

If the budget doesn't matter for you and you want to buy all price range gaming phones, then lookout best gaming phones.

Poco is a sub-brand of Xiaomi that launches the Poco X3 Pro as a gaming phone that runs on the Snapdragon 860 chipset processor, octa-core CPU, and Adreno 640 GPU. It has a 6.67-inch IPS LCD full HD+ display with screen resolution 1080 x 2400 pixels. Its display features offer a 120Hz screen refresh rate that runs programs smoother as silk. In addition, Poco X3 Pro is topped with water-resistant Gorilla Glass. Poco X3 Pro comes with two different RAM and internal memory variants: 6GB / 8GB RAM and 128GB / 256GB storage space. The camera feature is also good, it has a 48MP primary sensor, 8MP wide-angle sensor, 2MP macro sensor, and a 2MP depth sensor and its selfie camera offers a 20MP sensor.
