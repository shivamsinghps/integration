What is WazirX App?

WazirX is a cryptocurrency exchange platform or application. You can trade various cryptocurrencies and coins by using the national currency. WazirX is one of the older crypto trading apps and launched on March 18.

WazirX has become the largest crypto exchange in India and has a significant role in making crypto trading to India's public.
