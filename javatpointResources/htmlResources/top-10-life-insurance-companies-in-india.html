What is Life Insurance?

A contract between a person and an insurance company is known as life insurance, states that the latter will guarantee to pay a certain amount (the death benefit) to the policyholder's family in the unfortunate circumstance of a sudden demise.

When the policy reaches its maturity, in the absence of death, a maturity benefit-a sum assured-is granted. For critical diseases, several insurance providers additionally provide optional coverage. There are now 24 insurance firms in India that provide life insurance coverage.
