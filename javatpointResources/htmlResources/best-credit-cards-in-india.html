Best Credit Cards in India

It might be challenging to choose credit cards that fit your requirements and lifestyle among the various possibilities available. Most credit card issuers provide a range of cards, each with its features, criteria, and fees. So, if you're looking for a credit card that's appropriate for you, this article discussed the most popular credit cards from India's leading credit card issuers in 2022, with unmatched benefits like cashback, rewards, and discounts on shopping, petrol, and travel.

What is a Credit Card?

A credit card is a small plastic card issued by a bank, building society, or other financial organization that enables the cardholder to make purchases on credit. If you're new to credit cards, here are the advantages and disadvantages of a Credit card.

Advantages of Credit Cards

  • Credit cards offer special discounts and incentives to encourage the use of the card. These may be in cashback, discounts, or points, which can be redeemed for airline miles and used to pay off the balance.
  • If you want to make a purchase but don't have the cash on hand, you can pay it from your credit card. You may also pay for your item using a credit card in equal monthly instalments rather than a lump payment. It is more economical to pay for an asset with EMIs than a loan.
  • Credit cards keep note of every transaction made with the card, and your monthly credit card statement includes a detailed summary. This may monitor and determine your expenditures and purchases, which is useful when making a budget or paying taxes.

Disadvantages of Credit Cards

  • Invisible costs: A credit card has a variety of hidden fees that may mount up over time. Taxes and fees apply to credit cards, such as late fees, membership fees, subscription fees, and processing fees. Missing a card payment may lead to penalties, and making many late payments may decrease your credit limit, lowering your credit score and threatening your future credit chances.
  • The interest rates on credit cards are rather high. If you do not pay your bills by the due date, the balance will be carried forward, and interests will be imposed. On purchases made beyond the interest-free period, this interest accumulates over time.
  • Credit card fraud may happen to everyone, even if it is infrequent. It is now feasible to clone a card and obtain access to confidential information, enabling another person or organization to make transactions on your card. Check your bank records for odd investments and notify them right away if you suspect card theft. Typically, banks may eliminate fees if a copy is produced, so you will not be responsible for the robber's transactions.

SBI credit card is the finest credit card for online or e-commerce shopping in India. For individuals searching for the most widely recognized credit card in India for shopping, this credit card offers incentives and bonuses. This is a wonderful credit card for online shoppers.

Customers gave five stars rating to this credit card. The joining price is INR 499, and after the second year, you'll have to pay INR 499 as an annual payment to keep your account active. Your annual fees are waived when you spend more than INR 1 lakh per year.
