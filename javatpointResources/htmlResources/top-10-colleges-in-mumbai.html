Top 10 Colleges in Mumbai

Mumbai is a significant central point when it comes to education provided in India. Mumbai is renowned and recognized for providing its students with the best quality education.

University of Mumbai

The university was established in the year 1857, in the month of July, approximately 165 years back which makes the university one of the ancient/ oldest universities in India. This university comes among the first five universities in India.

Initially, the university was named "University of Bombay"as the name of the city 'Bombay' got changed to 'Mumbai' the university also changed its name to "University of Mumbai" in the year 1996.

When it comes to ranking, this university in the year 2001 was granted 5-star status, and in the year 2012, in the month of April "The National Assessment and Accreditation Council" or NAAC gave 'A' grade status to the university.

Further, it is one of the largest universities in our country India which is associated with almost more than 710 colleges. A few of them are as follows:

1. Narsee Monjee College of Commerce and Economics

About

A private college, generally known as NM College. One of the most accomplished, top-notch quality colleges in India, especially in economics and commerce, is associated with Mumbai University. Shri Vile Parle Kelavani Mandal established this college in the year 1964, and the college was awarded an 'A' grade and a GPA of 3.42 by NAAC.

Courses and More

Narsee Monjee College offers many quality courses for Bachelor, Master, and many professional courses as well. Apart from courses, this college is immensely active in extracurricular activities and has more than 20 clubs and societies which include a debate club, WDC, Enactus- a philanthropic organization, and many more.

The fees of courses vary accordingly. It has a capacity of more than 6000 students.

Further, it also organizes regular IV (industrial visits) for students for exposure to the real practical world, and how corporate life functions.

Placement - Median package of the undergraduate courses is INR 4 LPA.

Notable Alumni

Famous Alumni of the college- Amir Khan, Dilip Joshi, Paresh Rawal, Amaal Malik, Divyank Turakhia, etc.

2. St. Xavier's College

About

A private Catholic college founded in the year 1869 in the month of January by the Jesuits- The Society of Jesus. St. Xavier's College is an eponym for Francis Xavier, a 16th-century Jesuit saint who is Spanish. Autonomy was granted first to Xavier College by Mumbai University in the year 2010. This college also has an 'A+' Grade from NAAC.

COURSES and More

This college offers several Arts courses, Commerce and Management courses, and Science courses as well. St. Xavier College has courses for Junior college, Undergraduate, Postgraduate, Diploma, and Certification as well. Admissions are usually based on the marks obtained in the entrance tests/ exams. The placements history of the college is remarkable, the highest CTC was INR 15.7 LPA and the average CTC was 5.6 LPA.

Campus

St. Xavier College covers an area of 2.94 acres or in other words 11,900 m2 in South Mumbai. The main area of interest- is the central library with its huge collection of more than 135500 books, another place i.e. 'The Woods', a leisure space that includes plants, large trees, a sitting area, and more. If we talk about the look and structure, building, etc. of the college there is no doubt in saying beauty at its peak whether it is the architecture or the vintage vibe it possesses.

College also has a hostel only for boys. This college was always been famous for film shooting. Famous film shoots that took place in the college were Kucch to Hai, Main Hoon Na, Paa, Hichki, Kabir Singh, Student of the Year 2, and many more.

Notable Alumni

Some of them are - Zeenat Aman, Mukesh Ambani, Sanjeev Bagai, Zakir Hussain, Sunil Gavaskar, Anil Kapoor, Salman Khan, Vinod Mehra, and many more renowned personalities in different fields.

3. Bharati Vidyapeeth College of Hotel and Tourism Management Studies

About

This is a private college also called BVCHTMS, located in New Mumbai. It was established in the year 1992. This college is also associated with Mumbai University and was also awarded the grading of 'A' by NAAC and AIU & ACU also approved this college. In other remarkable rankings i.e. Outlook ICARE of 2021, this college was ranked 12 among the top 30 private H&M Institutes of India.

COURSES and More

Eight add-on courses and two UG courses are offered by this college to students all across Hotel and Hospitality Management. The placement percentage of the college is quite good. In the year, 2020-21 the college achieved a placement percentage of 92% and the average package was approx. INR 3 LPA.

Objectives

The main objectives of the college are:

  • To facilitate quality education and training in the area of hospitality with industrial-oriented exposure.
  • To infuse and promote Indian culture, values, and essence or spirit in the students.
  • To inculcate a common bond, team spirit, and fellowship among students which is required in this field.
  • To prosper the passion of students in learning hospitality services.
  • And lastly, to provide the industry with a cream layer of skilled students, the future of the world.

Infrastructure

When it comes to the Hotel Management course the college must have the best infrastructure that would fulfill the requirements of the students and must provide a quality education. And BVCHTMS does come with the best infra related to this, whether be it a training kitchen, advanced technology, bakery, dining room and restaurants for training are also well equipped with contemporary culinary equipment. Information tech. lab and library are also there that are updated from time to time on any modern stuff related to this.

4. IIT Bombay- Indian Institute of Technology Bombay

About

IITB is a government-owned autonomous public technical and research institute in Powai, city Mumbai, state Maharashtra which is internationally approved. IIT Bombay was founded in the year 1958.

IIT Bombay is regarded as being among the top engineering and science institutions in Asia, and the most prestigious and challenging institution in India to get admission into, and always been the first option or choice of top-rank holders in the engineering and science fields.

Courses and More

IIT Bombay offers various differentiated programs based on the year, such as 2-year, 4-year, and 5-year programs. To take admission to these programs one has to clear the entrance exams i.e., JAM - Joint Admission Test for Masters, GATE- Graduate Aptitude Test in Engineering, and JEE - Joint Entrance Examinations.

The main focus of IIT Bombay is engineering but IITB also provides various other programs such as physical sciences, courses related to design, arts & social science (psychology, philosophy, management studies, etc.) Over 1000 students are awarded educational Degrees which include degrees of B.S. - Bachelor of Sciences, B.Tech., MSc., M.Tech, and Ph.D.

Placement- IIT Bombay has always been remarkable with its placements. As per the placement report for the year 2022 almost 325 companies participated in the placement drive and approx. 210 pre-placement offers were made. Various MNCs also gave their offers. The highest CTC was INR 2.1 CPA this was an international package. And INR 1.8 CPA was the highest national package. The average package was INR 22.7 LPA.

Campus

IITB covers an area of approximately 550 acres wherein the buildings of the campus are separated into sections according to the various departments. The main building is occupied majorly by the academic department and a number of departmental annexes and auditoriums. A hallway called Infinite Corridor connects each department's annexes. Most of the dorms are located further than the Convocation Hall. The institute has a total of 18 hostels, two of which- Hostels 11 and 10 as well as a section in the newly built hostel- Hostel 15 are specifically allocated for female students. Further, the institute also has the luxury of 2 swimming pools, and grounds for the sports like cricket, football, hockey, and courts for squash, tennis, and many other sports too.

Notable Alumni

In Arts- Jag Mundhra, Mansoor Khan, Nitesh Tiwari, Satyendra Pakhale, etc.

In Business- Anil Kumar, Bharat Desai, Nandan Nilekani, Parag Agarwal, etc.

In Science- Narendra Karmarkar, Sarita Adve, Pratim Biswas,Subhash Khot, etc., and many others in different fields.
