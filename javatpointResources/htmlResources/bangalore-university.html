Bangalore University

You may find it in Bangalore, Karnataka, also known as the "Silicon Valley" of India. The University Grants Commission (U.G.C.) has approved Bangalore University (B.U.) to operate as a public state university. The National Assessment and accreditation council have awarded it an "A" rating. According to the National Institutional Ranking Framework 2022 (NIRF), Bangalore University is rated 64th among the best institutions in India. The number of postgraduate departments at Bangalore University is 48, and the University also maintains a postgraduate centre at Kolar, as well as 4 constituent colleges and 684 associated institutions. Moreover, it serves as the parent organization for several specialized research centre and academic administrations.

Most of the professors at Bangalore University have doctoral degrees in the areas in which they teach, which contributes to the institution's reputation for having exceptionally competent faculty members. The instructors are knowledgeable, kind, and inspirational and assist their students.

In addition to being affiliated with the University Grants Commission, the University is a member of the Association of Indian Universities (A.I.U.) and the Association of Commonwealth Universities (A.C.U.). Bengaluru City University and Bengaluru North University were established as separate institutions after the division of Bangalore University.

Various Undergraduate, Graduate, Postgraduate, Doctoral, Integrated, Diploma, and Certificate programs are available to students at Bangalore University. The classes are offered in many subject areas, including the arts, sciences, engineering, management, law, and teaching. For admission to the different programs, the University would also accept marks from national and state-level entrance examinations such as K.C.E.T., Karnataka P.G.C.E.T., GATE, and UGC NET/CSIR NET. The candidate's overall performance in the qualifying examination is considered when determining whether or not they will be admitted to Bangalore University.

The placement rate at Bangalore University hovers around between 80 and 90 percent each year. The institution provides a range of pay packages, the largest of which is worth INR 15 lakh per annum, while the average package was worth INR 5 lakh per annum. Companies like Microsoft, Infosys, Dell, Tata consultancy services, SAP, Accenture, and Royal Enfield are among those that have visited Bangalore University in recent years.

Curriculum Available from the University of Bangalore

Undergraduate Courses

Classes are available to students interested in various subjects at Bangalore University. Courses leading to the Bachelor of Arts degree, the Bachelor of Commerce degree, the Bachelor of Engineering or Technology degree, the Bachelor of Education degree, and the Bachelor of Library Science are among the undergraduate programs offered. Additionally, I.Sc., B.M.M., B.Sc., B.Voc., B.B.A., B.B.A. LL.B., B.C.A., B.H.M., B.P.A., B.V.A., and D.Ed. are some of the other courses available.

Bachelor's degree programs in Information Science & Engineering, Civil Engineering, Mechanical, Computer Science & Engineering, and Electronics Engineering are offered full-time at Bangalore University. These programs take place over four years. Generally, the undergraduate degree programs at Bangalore University take four to five years to complete.

Postgraduate Courses

The Bangalore university's postgraduate curriculum includes the following degrees and programs: MBA/PGDM, LL.B, LL.M, M.A., M.Arch, M.Com, M.E./M. Tech, M.Ed, M.J.M.C., and M.Lib, etc. Over two years, students pursuing a Master of Engineering may focus their studies on one of twelve areas, including Software Engineering, Computer Science and Engineering, Information Technology, Web Tech, Thermal Science and Engineering, Machine Design, Environmental Engineering, Structural Engineering, Geotech Engineering, and Structural and Geo-environmental Engineering.

A Master of Science degree can be earned in chemistry, math, botany, biochemistry, life science, statistics, biotechnology, and microbiology for 18,090 Indian Rupees (INR). The program lasts for a total of two years. Specializations in Economics, Journalism & Mass Communication, Political Science, Philosophy, Sociology, French, and Rural Development are offered via the Master of Arts program. The master's and doctoral programs offered by Bangalore University typically last for two years.

Ph.D. Course

Full-time, three-year Ph.D. programs are available in various fields, including engineering and computer science, management studies, English, mechanical engineering, economics, electrical engineering, life science, commerce, environmental science, and botany, among others, at Bangalore University.

The Doctor of Philosophy degree is based on a thesis created and presented for the purpose. Candidates may submit themselves for it at Bangalore University, governed by the Karnataka State Universities Act, 2000. The candidate must have conducted the original research included in the thesis while working in a Postgraduate Department of Studies or an Institution recognized by the University as a research centre. The candidate must have done this work under the supervision of a Research Supervisor or Supervisors approved by the University.

The University of Bangalore's Placement Opportunities

The University has formed a Centralized Placement Cell in the Canara Bank School of Management Studies, located in Central College. The pre-placement training and recruiting requirements of postgraduate students attending Jnana Bharathi and Central College Campus will be fulfilled by the Centre.

The University has a reputation for producing successful graduates in the working world. To better serve its students, Bangalore University's Placement Cell cultivates partnerships with local and national businesses, which it subsequently utilizes to secure internship and employment opportunities for its graduates. Students get pre-placement training from the cell, which increases their chances of finding work on campus via various recruitment initiatives. The placement cell will do practice interviews for the students to prepare them to tackle the real deal without anxiety. This university student may get up to 16 lakhs per year as the highest possible wage package from the organizations interested in hiring them. The organizations often provide graduates with a starting wage package worth a minimum of 5 lakh Indian rupees each year.

The Placement Process at Bangalore University is Outlined Below: -

  • Companies interested in participating in the recruitment campaign at Bangalore University are encouraged to visit the campus by the Placement Cell.
  • Once the placement cell has confirmed the student's enrolment with the firms, they will share crucial information, such as the specifics of the available positions and other relevant facts.
  • Applicants interested in attending a specific interview can register their names with the placement cell and provide their resumes to the same location.
  • The resumes will be sent to the company by the placement cell. By reviewing the applicants' resumes, organizations may compile a list of qualified students for consideration.
  • Each employer has its placement standards; on the day of the recruiting drive, those standards will be used to determine which students will be chosen.
  • This is the last phase in the placement cell's process, in which students are notified of their status.

Infrastructure at Bangalore University

The campus has an area of around 3,000 square feet and features well-established classrooms that are completely air-conditioned and equipped with internet access. The University has been divided into two campus heads, one of which is the management college, and the other is the law college; this has resulted in the institution being much more specialized and offering a greater variety of educational opportunities.

Since its founding, there have been around 1200 faculty, and 10000 students enrolled in its courses. The members of the faculties have been selected based on their experience level in the relevant fields; both campuses have professional management that is always working well. Below, you'll find further information on the campus of Bangalore University.

Library

The Jnana Bharathi and Central College campuses are locations where the Bangalore University Library serves the University's students and faculty. The whole collection of the university library consists of more than 3.3 lakh books, 175 current monthly publications, and 8,554 theses and dissertations. Under the UGC-INFONET Programme, the B.U. The library has access to 7,500 digital journals.

Health Centre

The health centres are situated on Jnana Bharathi and Central College campuses. Two senior doctors and support staff administer it, and it caters to the professionals and students of the institution to meet their fundamental medical requirements. It is managed by specialists who administer medicine, take electrocardiograms, and use glucometers to determine glucose levels in the patient's blood. In addition, Bangalore University has skilled Registered Nurses on staff who can care for patients in the case of an unexpected medical emergency. Moreover, it offers a bed for temporarily monitoring blood pressure, chest pain, and other difficulties before the patient being transported to other institutions. The health centre also has an outpatient ward, general laboratory, and X-ray laboratory for its patients' convenience. According to the arrangement, there will be a visit every other week from a pair of highly qualified medical professionals.

Canteen

Through the healthy meal plan, students are offered a lunch that is both delicious and nourishing at a much lower cost. It allows students to continue their academic and personal development goals while remaining on campus. Hundreds of students use this facility daily.

Other conveniences that may be found on the University Campus include a postal service, a bank, a swimming pool, both indoor and outdoor gaming facilities, a food and grocery centre, a childcare centre, and broadband, among other things. In addition, an R.O. drinking water plant with a capacity of 2,000 litres has been installed on the campus to provide a clean and sanitary source of drinking water. The city Campus is home to several meeting spaces, such as the senate hall, seminar hall, board room, etc., and the state-of-the-art Jnana Jyothi Auditorium.

The Application Procedure at Bangalore University

The application procedure for the University of Bangalore may be completed entirely online. Applicants interested in enrolling at Bangalore University must submit their materials via the institution's main website. A detailed explanation of how to do so is provided in the following steps:

  • To access the Student's Corner, go to the University's official website.
  • To continue, go to the "Admission"tab and choose the appropriate course level from the drop-down menu. If applications are still being accepted, a link to the online application form will be included at the bottom of this page.
  • To become a new user, you will first need to register by providing the essential information, which includes your name and contact information.
  • After logging in with the appropriate credentials, complete the application form.
  • Once an application has been received, prospective students must check the University's website for the merit list. Candidates must pay the fee to ensure their seats if their names are included on the list.

Qualifications Required for Undergraduate Study at Bangalore University

The eligibility requirements for enrolling in a bachelor's degree program at Bangalore University are further outlined in the following information.

  • Applicants for theBachelor of Engineering program (B.E.) should have completed class 12 or its equivalent and earned at least 45% of the minimum score in both mathematics and physics. During the counselling process, eligible candidates must present a valid score from the Karnataka Common Entrance Test (CET).
  • A candidate for the Bachelor of Science degree should have earned a 10+2 or equivalent degree from a state's Pre-University Education Board. To be eligible for consideration as a new entry candidate, the applicant must be at least 17 years old.
  • To be eligible for admission to the B.A. program at the University of Bangalore, applicants must have completed two years of the Pre-University exam administered by the Karnataka Pre-University Education Board.
  • Candidates interested in Bharatanatyam, Carnatic music (vocal), or Western music (instrumental) are expected to complete two years of training at an authorized music or dance school and a bridging course of one year in fine art. Meanwhile, those who want to pursue drawing and painting must have completed at least three years of training.
  • LLB Candidates must have completed the 10+2 with an aggregate score of at least 50% or 45% in the case of reservation categories in related disciplines.
  • To be eligible for the B.Lib.Sc course, applicants must have earned an aggregate grade point average of at least 50%. Candidates from the Socially and Educationally Disadvantaged (SC/ST) categories are eligible for a 5% mark reduction.
  • To be eligible for the B.PEd program, candidates must have qualified for their 10+2 examination with a minimum of 50% marks from any recognized board.
  • Arch. Candidates must have completed grades 10th and 12th in relevant subjects and received an overall grade of at least 50% or 45% if they fall into a reserved category. The selection procedure is mostly based on merit; however, to be considered for some programs, such as B.E. and B.Arch., applicants need to be able to pass an entrance exam, such as KCET, which KEA administers.
  • To be eligible for admission to the following undergraduate programs: BBA, BBM, BCA, BFA, SW, B.Com, B.Ed, and BCA, applicants must have earned a minimum of 50% on their 10+2 exams (45% for reserved categories) in courses related to their subject.

Qualifications for Admission to Postgraduate Study at Bangalore University

To be eligible to apply for postgraduate programs, applicants must first possess a bachelor's degree with an overall grade point average of at least 50% or 45% for those who fall into a reserved category and a grade point average of at least 50% in the subject area being pursued.

Students applying for admission to M.A. programs must have a Bachelor's degree with a minimum of 40% overall and 50% in the relevant topic.

  • Sponsored applicants seeking admission to the Master of Science program in Fashion and Apparel Design are required to have a Bachelor's Degree with a minimum of 50% marks in the aggregate of their chosen courses, as well as be currently employed in a reputable institution or organization and have a minimum of three years of experience working in the field of apparel, textile, or fashion design.
  • In the Master of Science program in Environmental Science, fifty percent of the available seats are set aside for students whose undergraduate degrees include Environmental Science or a related field. When students pursuing a career in Environmental Science are limited, applicants majoring in other relevant fields are considered.
  • To meet the LLM eligibility criterion, applicants for an LLM degree need to have completed either 3 or 5 years of an LLB program. To be granted admission, the candidates who are qualified and who have taken the Common Entrance Test (CET) must have a score that is considered legitimate. The candidates must have obtained 50% of the marks in the CET and have secured 45 or 40% of the marks in the LLB course. The LLM selection process is based on a merit list that the admission committee will prepare by the criteria set, and the candidates are required to appear for the counselling process.
  • To qualify for MPEd admission at Bangalore University, applicants must have earned a Bachelor of Physical Education (BPEd) degree with a minimum of 50%. The applicants are needed to provide a medical fitness certificate given by a medical officer of at least the level of district surgeon.
  • To be eligible for the M.Com program at Bangalore University, applicants for the M.Com program need to have completed a three-year B.Com or BBM degree program or an equivalent with an aggregate mark total of at least 50% (or 45% for SC/ST/Category candidates).
  • To be eligible for the Master of Architecture program, applicants must have obtained a valid score in either the Postgraduate Common Entrance Test (PGCET) or the Graduate Aptitude test in engineering (GATE) and graduated with a Bachelor of Architecture degree from an institution of higher education that is acknowledged.
  • The final admission will be based on the merit list that will be announced according to the marks received in the most recent qualifying examination and a list that will be subdivided by category. The final admission is based on course-by-course rank lists.

Scholarship Opportunities Available at Bangalore University

Scholarship programs are available to students at Bangalore University. These programs not only serve as a source of a morale boost for the student body, but they also provide financial assistance to students from financially disadvantaged families. Bangalore University provides the following scholarships to students who demonstrate financial need:

  • Students from Karnataka's Scheduled Castes and Tribes whose parents' yearly income is less than or equivalent to INR 2.5 Lacs are eligible for the Government of India's Post-matric Scholarship.
  • Minority students whose families have an annual income of less than INR 44,500 are eligible for the Minority Scholarship Program's Encouragement Fund/Fees Reimbursement. This program serves Muslims, Buddhists, Sikhs, Anglo-Indians, and Jains. The Department of Welfare for Minorities is in charge of coordinating this program.
  • The University Grants Commission is in charge of administrating the Postgraduate Indira Gandhi Scholarship (UGC). This privilege will be extended to those female students who are the only girl offspring in their families. The applicant will get 500 INR per month for ten months each year.
  • To recognize and reward academic excellence, the University awards one scholarship in each subject to the student who achieved the best score on the most recent qualifying test. In addition to receiving money equal to 600 INR, they come with a free ship, which relieves the recipient from paying tuition and lab expenses.
  • For students who score in the top five but whose families earn less than the poverty threshold set by the government of Karnataka, the "Hon'ble Vice Chancellor's Merit Scholarship" was established. A scholarship of Rs. 500/- will be awarded to each department of Bangalore University for ten months yearly. Students who fall into the payment group responsible for their finance and who have been accepted into a second postgraduate program are ineligible for this scholarship. Students who have already received funding from one or more other scholarships are not ineligible for this particular scholarship.
  • Special Scholarships are Scholarships for the Children of Government Employees or University Employees who Pass Away While on the Job. They are Administered by the Director of Collegiate Education or the University, Depending on the Circumstances.
  • In addition to these opportunities, the Government of India offers a scholarship known as the India National Merit Scholarship to students who have achieved exceptional academic success.

Distant Education at Bangalore University

Online registration is required for admission to the Bangalore University Distance Education program, which offers a variety of undergraduate and graduate degrees. The admissions procedure at Bangalore University is supervised by the Directorate of Correspondence Courses & Distance Education.

The Directorate of Distance Education is now authorized to provide various educational programs leading to undergraduate degrees, graduate degrees, professional degrees, postgraduate degrees, and diplomas. This authorization came from the University Grants Commission (UGC).

Application Procedure for the DDE at the University of Bangalore

Offline and online methods are available for submitting a DDE application at Bangalore University. The following is a step-by-step guide to the process of downloading and submitting the DDE application form:

Mode of Form Submission: Offline

  • Applicants may get an application from State Bank of India branches or Bangalore One Centres after depositing 750 Indian Rupees (INR).
  • The application form may also be received from the DDE office of Bangalore University for a fee of INR 750 by submitting a demand draught made out to "Finance Officer, Bangalore University" and payable at Bangalore.

Mode of Form Submission: Online

  • The University's official website allows students to download the application form. The application cost of INR 750 may be paid using a demand draught and then sent to the DDE address.
  • Applicants may also acquire the application form by mail by submitting a self-addressed envelope and a demand draught for INR 750 along with INR 200 in postage stamps to the DDE address.
