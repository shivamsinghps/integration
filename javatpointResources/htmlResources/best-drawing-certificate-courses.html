Best Drawing Certificate Courses

Drawing is a form of art; if you are an artist, you can draw using a pen, paper, or pencil. You can express your feelings through drawing. It is an effective way to communicate with other people worldwide because drawings do not require any language to understand. You can communicate only through shapes and objects.

In today's time, everything is online, and you can also create a digital painting on a tablet. Many people are fond of drawing and sketching and can also make a career in creative arts or learn drawing skills for fun.

In this article, we will discuss various courses of drawing, such as figure drawing, perspective drawing, animal drawing, pencil color drawing, digital painting, and many more.

The following courses are for beginners, intermediate as well as experienced people.

1. How to Draw from Beginner to Master (Available on Udemy)

It is an online course available online on Udemy. Joseph Patric Daniels has created this course and will be your tutor for this course. If you want to learn drawing from scratch, this course will teach you to draw from the beginner level and take you to the master level.

In this course, you will learn the basics of drawing, the use of different types of pencil lead weights, simplifying objects to make them easier to draw, shading techniques, how to light portraits, how to create realism in your drawings, how to draw axis lines for drawing easily, drawing hair, visualization & simplification techniques, drawing planes, cueball line drawing, line weight management, and much more.

Anyone who does not know how to draw can also enroll in this course and learn to draw from the beginning.

Key points:

  • This course has got a rating of 4.8 out of 5.
  • It is a beginner-friendly course.
  • You will require a pencil, ruler, paper, etc.
  • The length of this course is 8 hours and 12 minutes.
  • Learn how to draw and shade from basics to photorealism.
  • Provides certificate after completion.

2. The Art & Science of Drawing / BASIC SKILLS (Available on Udemy)

This course is accessible online on Udemy. It is created by Brent Eviston, who will be the instructor for this course. If you want to learn basic drawing skills, then this course is the right choice for you as it is created for intermediate and beginner-level artists.

In this course, you will learn how to hold a pencil, draw shapes like circles, & ovals, break down objects into basic shapes to make them easier to draw, use light lines, adding details & textures to drawings to make them look realistic, draw straight lines, and many more.

In "The Art and Science of Drawing" series, Basic Skills is the first course. There are other courses in this series, such as DYNAMIC MARK MARKING, CONTOURS, MEASURING & PROPORTION, FORM & SPACE, and more. You can learn drawing from basic to professional through this series.

Key points:

  • The rating of this course is 4.8 out of 5.
  • This course is for both beginners and experienced.
  • The length of this course is 4 hours and 18 minutes.
  • All you need is a pencil and paper to practice the drawing skills you will learn.
  • Learn at your own pace from home.
  • Earn a certificate after completing the course.

6. Improve Your Drawing Skills (Available on LinkedIn)

"Improve your drawing skills" is an online learning path on LinkedIn Learning. There are nine courses under this path from which you will learn drawing from the basic to advanced level. If you want to work in a creative role, then you must possess good drawing skills. This learning path of drawing is the best choice for you. The instructors for these courses are Will Kemp, Von Glitschka, and Amy Wayne.

Following are the nine courses:

Course 1: Drawing Foundations: Fundamentals: In this course, you will learn the theory behind the drawing, the use of lines, the relationship between objects, value & tone, light, shadows, etc.

Course 2: Drawing Foundations: Figure: In this course, you will learn an introduction to figure drawing, recognizing simple structural landmarks, drawing 3D volumes in space, etc.

Course 3: Drawing Foundations: Light and Shadow: In this course, you will learn the principles of Chiaroscuro, the basics of light, compositional patterns, three-tone masses, etc.

Course 4: Drawing 2-Point Perspective: In this course, you will learn perspective basics, creating street scenes, the illusion of depth, 3D cubes, creating interior space, etc.

Course 5: Drawing Foundations: Urban Sketching: In this course, you will learn about urban sketching, landscape sketching, early morning light, restaurant scene, cafés scene, bar scenes, street furniture, monuments, etc.

Course 6: Drawing Foundations: Sketching the Landscape: In this course, you will learn proportion & scale, light & shadow, composition, water & reflections, sky & clouds, building & houses, trees & foliage, rocks & mountains, etc.

Course 7: 21-Day Drawing Challenge: In this course, you will learn about drawing challenges, the science of drawing, inspiring drawers, etc.

Course 8: Drawing Vector Graphics: Isometric Illustration: In this course, you will learn about isometric illustration, vector illustration methods, inspiring illustrator, isometric challenge, etc.

Course 9: Drawing Vector Graphics: Color and Detail: In this course, you will learn about the color wheel, lighting, gradient blend detailing, hot spots & deep shadows, color resources, etc.

Key points:

  • These courses are for beginners or anyone in the creative field.
  • There are nine courses in this learning path.
  • It provides 28 hours of content.
  • You will learn everything from the basics.
  • Provides certificates after completion of each course.
