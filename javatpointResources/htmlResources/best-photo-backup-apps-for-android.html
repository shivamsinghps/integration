Best Photo backup apps for Android

The backup of photos has become very important in today's life. There are many apps available on the Play Store that allow you to back up your photos and videos on the phone. For this list, we will take a look at the best photo backup apps on android phones.

Top 5 best photo backup apps for Android

There are several photo backup applications available in the Play Store. Some of them are described below:

  1. Google Photos
  2. Dropbox
  3. Amazon Photos
  4. MEGA
  5. Microsoft OneDrive

Below are a little more details about each app and a direct link to download the app.

1. Google Photos

Google Photos is a mobile application in which users can store or backup their photos and videos. It is a very popular and feature-rich application. This application was developed by Google in 2015. This app is available on Android as well as the iOS platform.

In this application, the user gets up to 15 GB of free storage. After that, the user has to pay 130 rupees per month for 100 GB storage.

Features of Google Photos app

There are the following features in this application.

  1. Free storage: This application provides up to 15 GB of free storage to back up photos and videos.
  2. Create automatic albums: It automatically creates an album after an event or trip occurs.
  3. Share photos instantly: It lets you share photos instantly with any contact, e-mail, or phone number.
  4. Powerful searching: In this application, your photos can be searched by people, places, and things.
  5. Advanced editing: It provides advanced editing features like filters, color adjustment, and more.

Price: Free to download

Required Android version: 4.1+

Rating on Play Store: 4.5/5

Download Link from Play Store: https://play.google.com/store/apps/details?id=com.google.android.apps.photos

2. Dropbox

Dropbox is the cloud storage system. It is an alternative to cloud storage like iCloud, one drive, google drive, etc. This application was developed by Drew Houston in 2007. It stores various types of data like photos, videos, audio, PDF files on its servers. It lets users save space and share files faster. In addition, it allows the user to move the files from their computer to the server with one click. This app is available on Android as well as the iOS platform.

In this application, the user gets up to 2 GB of free storage. After that, the user can buy the premium plan according to his need.

Features of the Dropbox

There are the following features in this application.

  1. Free storage: This application provides up to 2 GB of free storage to back up photos, videos, and other files.
  2. Simple upload files: It uploads the photos and videos automatically from the camera roll.
  3. Share photos instantly: It lets you share photos and videos instantly with any contact, e-mail, or phone number.
  4. Scanning Capability: You can scan ID, documents, bills, and much more in this application.

Price: Free to download

Required Android version: 6.0+

Rating on Play Store: 4.2/5

Download Link from Play Store: https://play.google.com/store/apps/details?id=com.dropbox.android&hl=en

3. Amazon Photos

Amazon Photos is online storage for backup the photos and videos. This application is available on both Android and iOS platforms. Amazon Mobile LLC developed it. It automatically organizes the photos or videos into folders that mirror the folders you have on your mobile.

In this application, the user gets up to 5 GB of free storage. After that, the user has to pay 800 rupees per year for 100 GB storage.

Features of Amazon Photos app

There are the following features in this application.

  1. Auto-backup: It automatically back-up photos and videos from the phone.
  2. Share photos instantly: It shares photos and videos via WhatsApp, E-mail, Facebook, and other apps in just one click.
  3. Simple interface: It provides a very eco-friendly interface.
  4. Searching: The prime members of this application can search for photos and videos by keyword, place, and thing.

Price: Free to download

Required Android version: 5.0+

Rating on Play Store: 4.6/5

Download Link from Play Store: https://play.google.com/store/apps/details?id=com.amazon.clouddrive.photos&hl=en_IN&gl=US

4. MEGA

MEGA is a mobile application that provides the online storage services of the user. It is an alternative to cloud storage like iCloud, one drive, google drive, Dropbox, etc. It was developed by Kim Dotcom on 19 January 2013. The MEGA app is available on both Android and iOS platforms. It stores user data in an encrypted form so that the data is secure here.

In this application, the user gets up to 50 GB of free storage. After that, the user has to pay 445 rupees per month for 400 GB storage.

Features of MAGE app

There are the following features in this application.

  1. Free storage: It provides up to 50 GB of free storage to back up photos, videos, and files.
  2. Secure interface: It provides the two-factor authentication user interface.
  3. Share photos instantly: It shares photos and videos via WhatsApp, E-mail, Facebook, and other apps in just one click.

Price: Free to download

Required Android version: 5.0+

Rating on Play Store: 3.6/5

Download Link from Play Store: https://play.google.com/store/apps/details?id=mega.privacy.android.app&hl=en

5. Microsoft OneDrive

Microsoft OneDrive is another decent mobile backup app. It is a feature-rich application. This application was developed by Microsoft Corporation in 2007. It stores user data in an encrypted form so that the data remains secure. This app is available on Android as well as the iOS platform.

In this application, the user gets up to 5 GB of free storage. After that, the user can buy the premium plan according to his need. If you buy the premium plan of this app, you get a free subscription to Office Suite.

Features of MAGE app

There are the following features in this application.

  1. Free storage: It provides up to 5 GB of free storage to back up photos, videos, and files.
  2. Security: It stores user data in an encrypted form so that the data remains secure.
  3. Searching: In this application, the photos can be searched by people's names.
  4. Scanning Capability: In this application, users can scan and store their ID, documents, bills, and much more.

Price: Free to download

Required Android version: 6.0+

Rating on Play Store: 4.5/5

Download Link from Play Store: https://play.google.com/store/apps/details?id=com.microsoft.skydrive&hl=en
