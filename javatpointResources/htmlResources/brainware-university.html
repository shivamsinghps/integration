Brainware University

Brainware University, a private state university with UGC recognition founded in West Bengal in 2016, provides Ph.D., P.G., U.G., Diploma, and Skills programs to more than 10,000 students on its cutting-edge, expansive, green, ragging-free campus. Brainware University Kolkata, a 33-year-old leading education group, Brainware of West Bengal, was founded to assist nation-building through research, innovation, and high-quality education.

Mr. Phalguni Mookhopadhayay created it, the University's hands-on chancellor, whose insight and vision in just five years have made Brainware University one of the top learning environments.

Education has continued unabated throughout the pandemic. The University has a well-stocked library with both domestic and foreign journals, giving students enough resources for their academic endeavors. Sports and leisure activities are essential to education. Additionally, Brainware hosts lectures, workshops, and festivals to encourage students' creativity. Students might also join several groups for communication, technology, photography, and other hobbies. The pupils' education could be continued uninterrupted thanks to online classes.

The hallmarks of this institution of excellence include top-notch infrastructure, contemporary labs, and workshops, a focus on research and extension activities, a team of highly qualified Faculty, extensive pre-placement grooming, soft skills training, an effective placement cell, a pan-Indian network of customers and developers, excellent placements that aim for a mystery figure of 100%, and an equal increased focus on academic studies and discipline.

Mission

Brainware University's mission is to develop responsible technologists, leaders, and innovators who will drive society forward and improve the quality of life for all. They are working towards a better tomorrow by creating a positive environment that fosters creativity, innovation, and entrepreneurship on their campus.

Brainware University is a state-of-the-art university offering a Multi-Disciplinary and Global Integrated curriculum. Its vision is to provide education of international standards that are relevant and excellent in all aspects to students, Faculty, and staff to meet the demands of a globally connected world.

Vision

The vision comprises of three components: - Educational; - Research; - Innovation. It provides superior quality education with community involvement, job placement, and lifelong learning opportunities on various fronts - Academics, Research, Innovation (AIIMS), Industry Interface Programmes (IIC), Outstanding Student Awards Scheme (OSAS), Technology Village (T.V.), Student Scholarship Programme (SCP), etc.

Campus

It is situated in Barasat, the administrative center for the North 24 Parganas district. It has excellent connections to the major city. Barasat is the closest railroad station (2 km away). Champadali, the closest bus depot, is 2 km from the campus. Students can use the usual auto or toto service from the train station or bus depot to get to campus. The distance to Netaji Subhas Chandra Bose International Airport is one hour.

Courses Offered by Brainware University

Brainware University offers 55+ courses in a wide range of subjects. The courses help students gain the knowledge and skills required for their chosen career path.

The courses offered by Brainware university can be taken online or offline, depending on the student's preference and convenience. As an online course provider, Brainware university allows students to complete their studies anywhere and anytime. This is especially useful for students who have busy schedules and cannot attend classes during conventional times.

Students can choose among many subjects such as accounting, computer science, health care management, Internet marketing, etc. Each issue has been designed to provide students with a wide range of learning opportunities to help them develop their careers in various fields.

Q. How many courses are at Brainware University?

The number of courses at Brainware University is quite large, which makes it a desirable option for students. The classes are designed to help you learn new skills and develop your existing ones. The courses are available on demand, so you can start studying whenever possible. You can also pause and resume anytime, making it easy to fit your studies into your busy schedule.

Q. Is Brainware a Recognized University? Is it recognized by the University Grants Commission (UGC)?

As a private institution that was formed by an act of the West Bengal government, Brainware University has the authority to confer degrees on its main campus in a regular way following Section 22 of the UGC Act. According to Section 2(f) of the UGC Act, 1956, the University's name is included among the universities it maintains.

The Government of West Bengal passed Act XXXI of 2015, which founded Brainware University. The West Bengal Legislative Assembly approved "The Brainware University Act, 2015," and on January 27, 2016, it was published in the Kolkata Gazette, Extraordinary. The Government of West Bengal issued a notification (No. 165-Edn (U)/I.U. - 125/2014) on February 24, 2016, putting the principles of the Brainware University Act, 2015, into effect as of February 25, 2016.
