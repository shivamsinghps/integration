Best Android TV Boxes

Nowadays, there are numerous options to stream content on your television, and one of them is an Android TV box. Android TV box is one of the best possible ways to get entertained on television (smart TV). It will change your ordinary TV experience into a smart TV. You will possibly enjoy a fast and seamless gameplay experience.

Android TV boxes facilitate you to access your favorite videos, music apps, as well as some lightweight games. Android TV boxes have become one of the necessary household sources of entertainment.

In this article, we will discuss some of the best Android TV boxes that help you to find a suitable Android TV box.

Before buying an Android TV box, we should consider some of the primary factors of an Android TV box. These primary factors are:

  • Processor: The processing power of an Android TV box is one of the core factors. A good processor will protect your Android box from run slowly and execute all tasks easily. It is recommended to choose an Android TV box with a5 GHz Quad-Core CPU or higher than this (octa-core preferred).
  • Storage: It is also an important factor that affects performance. Most Android TV boxes come in the range of 8GB-64GB. Users should choose according to their requirements; higher storage space is recommended for gamers.
  • Connectivity: The connectivity option allows you to connect your device with other devices for file transfer or content sharing. Ensure that your Android TV box should have at least Bluetooth ports, HDMI ports and 2 USB
  • Memory: Greater memory size devices give better performance. The device OS needs memory to load channels quickly and perform tasks faster. Select a device that has at least 4 GB RAM. Less than 4 GB may cause a problem for loading programs or games.
  • Operating System version: Choose an Android TV box that is running on the operating system Android 5.0 or higher. Try not to buy the older version of the device. It is recommended to use an Android TV box that supports Android 6.0 or higher
  • Warranty: It is better to buy an Android TV box that offers a warranty period. Most of the device comes with a range of 3 months to 1-year
  • Price: Checking the device's price list is important if you want to buy a TV box under budget. The cost of such devices ranges from Rs. 3,000-5,000.
