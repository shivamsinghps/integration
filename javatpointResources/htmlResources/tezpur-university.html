Tezpur University

In 1994, a Parliamentary Act created Tezpur University, an Indian Central University, in Tezpur, Assam. It is located in the gorgeous region of Napaam, bordered by many florae, historical sites, and tourist attractions. It provides employment-oriented and multidisciplinary courses in engineering, humanities, business administration, science, and many other fields to address the regional and national goals and the growth of the state of Assam and India. Additionally, it encourages study in the burgeoning fields of science and technology and fields of particular and immediate relevance to the region.

Tezpur university is a public university located in Tezpur, Assam, India, and is one of the oldest & largest universities in the northeastern area of India. The university was Started in 1994 and offered graduate, undergraduate, and doctoral packages in various fields.

The university already has a variety of cutting-edge labs, computer facilities, internet access, a proper power supply system, and a very small but well-stocked library with connections to many digital libraries. Twelve well-designed dormitories are available for students, but several residential buildings have been constructed to house teachers and non-teaching employees. To meet the diverse demands of the university community, additional essential services such as central water supply, campus safety, guest room, cafeteria, gym, indoor & outdoor sports facilities with flood lights, post office, banks with ATMs, schools, etc. are also accessible on campus. The university has consistently been ranked within the top one hundred universities in India by the countrywide Institutional Ranking Framework (NIRF) from 2017 to 2019, peaking at 77th in 2019.

Campus

The university's main campus is located at Napaam, 15 kilometres east of Tezpur, the Assamese district capital for Sonitpur. Urban Napaam is surrounded by people of many languages, castes, and religions. The 262 acres (1.06 km2) of land that makes up the Napaam campus are enclosed by pucca walls. A PWD road connects Napaam to National Highway No. 37A roughly in the middle of the distance between the Kalia-Bhomora bridge and Misson Chariali. Road and rail connections connect Tezpur to the rest of the state and the nation, between Kolkata and Tezpur, there are flights three times every week.

Transportation Facilities

The primary form of public transportation in and around Tezpur is the e-rickshaw, which connects Tezpur University and Tezpur City. Between the university and the ASTC bus stop, which is situated in the centre of Tezpur city next to the State Bank of India, there is an ASTC bus service (Main Branch). Every day, ASTC and private buses connect Tezpur with several important locations, including Guwahati (ISBT, Lokhra), Bongaigaon, North Lakhimpur, Dibrugarh, Tinsukia, etc. Twenty-seater taxis run between Tezpur and Guwahati's Paltan Bazaar. The Dekargaon Railway Station provides access to different areas of Assam and Tezpur. Additionally, travelers can fly to and from Tezpur, where the closest airport, Tezpur Airport, offers air service to Silchar and Kolkata.

Library

The centre of the Tezpur University user community is the Central Library. Scholars throughout the nation utilize the library's resources as they serve the educational and research needs of the academic community. Since 1994, the Central Library has been operational. Currently, there are more than 83200 books, 10,000 journals (print and online), online databases, 7848 back issues of journals, and C.D.s in the library. The library is a part of the DELNET, DeLCON, and e-ShodhSindhu Consortiums.

From any terminal on the university campus, library users can access book databases, journal databases, e-journals, institutional repositories, electronic theses and dissertations, and other e-resources. A library offers reading opportunities every day of the year. The library is constantly working to implement the best practices in all its endeavors. The library has gone completely computerized with the Libsys software, an integrated multi-user library automation system that supports all internal library operations.
