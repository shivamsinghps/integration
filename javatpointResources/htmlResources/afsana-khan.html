Afsana Khan

Singing and listening to music is a universal pastime. We listen to certain songs repeatedly because they're so fantastic. The singer is one of the most important factors in the song's success. The power of a singer's voice is what keeps a song fresh. Throughout history, there have been many talented vocalists. When a talented singer performs a song, it has the potential to become an all-time hit. In this article, we'll talk about Afsana Khan, a singer with a magical voice who has made millions of people go crazy with his music.

  • Profession: Singer, Actress
  • Date of Birth: June 13 1994

  • Religion: Islam/Muslim religion

Early Life

Born on June 13, 1994, Afsana Khan is an Indian singer. Sheera Khan is her father's name, while Asha Begum is her mother's. Her father died when she was a child. She has three older sisters and a younger brother. Her father and grandfather were both singers, so she comes from a musically inclined family. When Afsana was a child, she began her singing career to help her family financially.

Afsana Khan's Family, Cousins, and Friends

Father's Name: Sheera Khan (Singer)

Mother's Name: Asha Begum

Brother's Name: Khuda Baksh (Singer)

Sister Name: Raftaar Kaur, Raman Khan, Razia Sultan and Neetu Khan

She was raised in a Muslim household by parents who converted to Islam when she was a little child. Khuda Baksh, her brother, is also a singer and is best known for his appearance on Indian Idol 9. The singers Razia Sultan (sister), Raman Khan (Sister), Raftaar Kaur (sister), and Neetu Khan (sister) all share her bloodline (singer). Saajz, a singer, was the subject of her affections at the time. On February 27, 2021, she married the artist Saajz.

The Journey of Afsana Khan

As a contestant on Bigg Boss 15, Afsana Khan made her name as a singer, actress, and songwriter in the Indian Punjabi community. The songs Titliaan, Dhakka, and Jodaa were all sung by her. She was educated at Sri Muktsar Sahib's Government Senior Secondary School. After this, she earned a master's degree from Chandigarh University. Afsana, despite being born into a low-income family, refused to give up on her dreams.

Having lost his father at an early age, he took on the responsibility of supporting his family by singing from an early age. Her grandfather was a singer, as was her father, and she inherited that love of singing from them both. Despite having no formal training in music, her voice has a lovely tone and is enjoyable to listen to. After singing throughout her village and the neighbouring regions, she became a local sensation, thanks to her strong vocals.

Following this, she was offered the option to take part in the third season of The Voice of Punjab. Afsana's participation in the program and successful performance earned her applause from the panel of judges and the audience. The audience responded well to the songs she sang on the show; as a result, she was able to place among the top five contestants at the event.

Afsana didn't require an introduction in Punjab once she returned from the event; everyone knew and recognized her. In 2015, Afsana accepted an invitation to compete in the first season of the singing competition program "The Rising Star" on Hindi television; before auditioning for the program, she had no prior knowledge or experience with Bollywood music. Only after she had completed this practice session with Jag Suna Suna Lage could she complete the audition and earn a place on the show. Based on his impressive performance, she made it to the top 7 of the show.

After that, she performed a slew of concerts around Punjab that became Internet sensations. Afsana Khan's songs are extremely popular on YouTube, and her followers seem to agree. Her involvement in Bigg Boss 15, which was broadcast on Colors TV in 2021, was a competition in which she faced off against several other aspiring actors and actresses.

Body Measurement & Physical Stats

She is a Muslim Punjabi woman of Indian descent, and her age is 28 years. Her horoscope sign is Sagittarius. She is 165 cm tall and weighs around 75 kg. A quick look at her measurements reveals a 40-34-38-inch frame. She has brown hair and brown eyes. Travelling and playing music are two of her favourite pastimes. Her right hand also has a tattoo of a dragon.

Afsana Khan Salary, Earnings, and Wealth

When discussing Afsana Khan's entire wealth, her net worth is somewhere in the neighbourhood of $20 million. Performing as a singer is her primary source of income. The songs she sings are well-liked by the public, and her videos on YouTube have amassed millions of views. She has had the opportunity to work with some of the biggest names in the industry.

She is a well-known Punjabi artist, and her songs become quite popular almost as soon as they are published, which brings in a significant amount of money for her. In addition, she performs on other occasions throughout the country and charges up to 2 lakh rupees for each performance.

The Afsana Khan Controversy

The debate now includes Afsana as well. She sparked outrage when she sang her hit song Dhakka at her former Govt Higher Secondary School for the first time after becoming famous. She received a lot of flak and was harassed on social media due to the controversy. At the police station, they filed a complaint against them.
