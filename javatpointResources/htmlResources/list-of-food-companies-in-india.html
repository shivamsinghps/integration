List of Food Companies in India

Food companies produce and sell the food products. The food company produces dairy products, frozen foods, snacks, beverages, candy, bakery products, etc. Most popular Indian food companies are described below with their food products.

1. Amul

It is a food processing industry in India. It is famous for milk products. Tribhuvandas Patel was the founder of Amul and was founded in 1946. Amul's headquarter is in Anand, Gujarat. Amul is serving its products worldwide. Amul has a revenue of Rs 386 billion. Banas Dairy and Dudhsagar Dairy are the divisions of Amul.

Amul produces the following food products:

  • Milk and dairy products produced by the Amul are UHT milk, lactose-free milk, Amul camel milk, Amul butter, processed cheese, Emmental cheese, gouda cheese, malai panner, fruit dahi, mishti doi, Masti dahi, flavored milk, Amul lassi, Amul Masti buttermilk, ice cream, cow ghee, whipped cream, high aroma cow ghee, Amul sour cream.
  • Amul sauces and spreads include pizza sauce, peanut spread crunchy, jalapeno sauce, Mexican salsa sauce, peanut spread creamy, choco buttery spread, Amul lite bread spread, delicious fat spread,
  • Other products produced by the Amul are chocolates, Amul filled bars, Amul syrups, Amul wafer chocolates, fresh cream, mithai mate, aloo Tikki, french fries, hash brown, butter cookies, Amul rusk, bread, pav, bun, pizza base, Amul puffles, shrikhand, Amul gulab jamun, Amul basundi, roti softener, Amul panchamrit, Amul cattle feed.

2. Bisleri

It is the beverage industry in India that produces the drink products. Jayantilal Chauhan was the founder of Bisleri and was founded in 1984. Bisleri's headquarter is in Mumbai. Bisleri is serving its products in India and the United Arab Emirates. Zainab Chauhan and Jayanti Chauhan are the directors of Bisleri. Bisleri has a revenue of 210 million USD.

Drink products produced by the Bisleri company are Bisleri soda and Bisleri mineral water.

Bisleri is available in multiple pack sizes:

  • Bisleri 20 liter water dispencer.
  • Bisleri Rockstar 300 ml water bottles.
  • Bisleri 1 liter and 2 liter water bottle.
  • Bisleri 5 liter, 10 liter and 20 liter water can.
  • Bisleri Vedica 750 ml glass water bottle.
  • Bisleri Vedica 500 ml and 1 liter water bottle.
  • 750 ml Bisleri club soda.
