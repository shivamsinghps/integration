Sana Khan

Sana khan is a former Indian actress, figure, and dancer who became prominent and well-known in 2005 at the age of 17. She was born on 27 August 1988. Her zodiac sign is Leo. She did her education in the schools of Mumbai and also tried some small skincare courses. She worked in films in different languages like Tamil, Hindi, and Telugu, which are seen and praised today.

Her talent and enthusiasm for modeling were glimpsed in various advertisements, films, and television shows. Her exit from the entertainment industry sent shockwaves to her fans and people who are in the industry.

Physical Appearance

She has a fair skin complexion with dark brown color orbs. Her height is about 5 feet 3 inches, and her hair strands are brown. Her weight is 53 kgs.

Earlier life of Sana khan

Sana khan was born and educated in Mumbai (Dharavi). Her father and mother share different regions. Her father was a Muslim from Malayali, while her mother was from Mumbai. Even though she was born into a middle-class family, she didn't allow this situation to hamper her acting dreams and decided to try her hands at it after school at a local school situated near her home.

Career and livelihood

Sana khan worked in print and television commercials. She began her profession by working in low-budget films, which gave her a deep acting experience. She kept her acting spirit dominant from 2005 to 2020.

Despite being rejected in a Tamil movie named Kettavan, she never gave her hopes and kept her pace. Her dreams led her to debut with the Tamil movie Silambattam with the same actor who declined her part in Kettavan.

Having noticed the presence of Shahrukh khan with Sana khan, Silambarasan was impressed and phoned her for a female lead part.

Sana khan stated in Hindi that it was tough for her to achieve a role in the Tamil film industry, but she was lucky to be selected by the actor while looking for a new face for his upcoming movie.

Barely did Sana know that this movie would eventually grab the interest of the viewers. She received a lot of praise from the public and won an award for best new actress in Singapore.

She worked on doing further movies in the Tamil industry in 2010, like Thambikku Indha Ooru. She also worked in a movie that portrayed a hijack situation and worked on such a theme.

Keeping her acting spirit alive, She entered various Canadian movies like Kool sukkah hot Maga. In 2012, she was signed to her sixth Tamil film Thalaivan, produced by Big Ocean pictures. This year, she worked to make a debut in a Malayalam movie that was based on the Hindi film, the dirty picture.

She even landed the role of antagonist's daughter in a 2014 movie named Jai ho. Her special Appearance was when she received a chance to work in Toilet - Ek prem Katha starring as Akshay Kumar's girlfriend.

Her famous role till today is Kavita Singh.

TV Commercials

In commercials and advertisements, too, she had given her best acting. She acted in 50 advertisements; one of them included a cosmetic ad directed by the Shirish Kunder in July 2007. She also presented herself in deodorant ads, Xbox gaming, and yatra.com. She also worked in an advertisement for the product Amul macho, which turned out to be highly controversial. It was an underwear brand that provoked controversy and was banned by the Government of India, and the attracted affronts from the public. The company rehired Khan and gave her a part in some other theme to prevent further disagreements. She never let public opinions interfere with her career and prepared herself to ignore the controversies completely, as creative people dominated her acting.

She worked with several superstars in her television ads, including Salman Khan, Shahrukh Khan, Hrithik Roshan, Mahendra Singh Dhoni, Yuvraj Singh, etc. She endorsed many popular and well-known brands with these actors during her acting period.

Big boss and other television shows

She also took part in the Indian edition of Big Boss Season 6 in 2014. She was nervous at that time and wanted to make people see her real personality. True to her belief, she was able to make out and remain in the show for long. The most unexpected was when she was chosen in the list of top three, which she scarcely believed would happen. She earned a lot of popularity from this event.

She was rumored to be in close connection with Salman Khan and was linked with several big boss actors, one of which is named Aashka Goradia.

In many of the upcoming seasons of the famous show big boss, she was chosen as the guest. In 2015, she competed for Fear factor: "Khatron ke Khiladi" and outdid herself to 9th position. It was a great achievement in reality games on the life that exhibited her power and strength. She also grabbed her part in kitchen champion 5 in 2019. She also played her role in many television serials.

In February 2019, Khan demonstrated her relationship with choreographer Melvin Louis to the public. They were close to each other and even shared pictures and videos of them dancing. She even shared screenshots of her on social media talking about her ending relationship with his boyfriend. In the past, she was also in a relationship with Ismail khan.

In 2020, Khan posted on Instagram that she wished to serve humanity and quit the film industry. She even deleted some of her posts on Instagram. This happened when she broke up with her boyfriend, charging him with abusing him physically.

Khan went on to marry Islamic scholar Mufti Anas Sayied in 2020. She was pleased by the goodness and modesty of the Anas, and her marriage was not a decision of haste. She cleared any suspicions about her marriage by praising Anas as a man of her imagination in an interview with Bombay Times. She even posted about her wedding on Instagram by a post and captioned a poem on their wedding, which poured out her true feelings for Anas and her views on this marriage.
