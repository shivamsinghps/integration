Best Movies

In the world, every year, many movies are made or produced, and some of them get the tag of blockbuster or superhit. Everyone likes to watch movies, from children to older people; everyone spends some time watching movies. This article is also about movies; in this article, we will discuss about some best movies ever made or produced. So that you do not have to face any problems in choosing the best movies to watch and you enjoy some best movies. Here is the list of some Best Movies one by one: -

1. Mughal-E-Azam

Mughal-e-Azam is one of the most successful films in the history of Indian cinema. This movie was released in 1960, and legendary actors and actresses like Dilip Kumar, Madhubala, Prithviraj Kapoor, and Durga Khote were part of this historical drama film. The story of this movie revolves around Salim and Anarkali. They are both in love with one another, but Salim's father, Akbar, is against this relationship and orders Salim to break this relationship, but Salim doesn't do this. So to know what is happening next, you have to watch this all-time blockbuster movie.

IMDB Rating: 8.1 /10

Cast: Prithviraj Kapoor, Dilip Kumar, Durga Khote, Madhubala, and many more.

Director: K. Asif

Original Language: Hindi

Release Date: 5 August 1960

Country: India

2. The Shawshank Redemption

It is an American drama film and is considered one of the best movies in the world. This movie was released in the year 1994, and this movie is based on Rita Hayworth and Shawshank Redemption, a novella whose author is Stephen King. This movie is about a man who is sentenced to life imprisonment for the murder of his wife, but in reality, this person is innocent; if you want to know more about this person and what he does next, then you have to watch this movie.

IMDB Rating: 9.3/10

Cast: Tim Robbins, Morgan Freeman, Bob Gunton, William Sadler, Clancy Brown, Gil Bellows, James Whitmore, and many more.

Director: Frank Darabont

Original Language: English

Release Dates: September 10 (TIFF), September 23(United States), 1994

Country: United States

3. Titanic

Titanic is an American romantic film released in the year 1997. If you like watching movies, then you must have seen Titanic at least once, and if you have not seen it, then you must have heard its name. If you like to watch romantic movies, then this movie is one of the best options for you. Leonardo DiCaprio and Kate Winslet are this movie's lead actors and actresses. In this movie, a boy and a girl fall in love on a ship, and this movie focuses on this boy and girl. If you like to watch a romantic movie, then don't miss this masterpiece.

IMDB Rating: 7.9/10

Cast: Leonardo DiCaprio, Kate Winslet, Billy Zane, Kathy Bates, Frances Fisher, Bernard Hill, Jonathan Hyde, Danny Nucci, David Warner, Bill Paxton, and many more.

Director: James Cameron

Original Language: English

Release Dates:

  • November 1, 1997 (Tokyo)

  • December 20, 1997 (South Africa)

Country: United States

4. Sholay

Sholay is the most popular and blockbuster movie in the history of Indian cinema. Every movie lover likes this movie; this movie was released in the year 1975, and it is an action-adventure film. The story of this film focuses on a brutal dacoit who tortures villagers, and Jai and Veeru come to escape villagers from this atrocity. The film stars Dharmendra, Amitabh Bachchan, Sanjeev Kumar, Amjad Khan, Hema Malini and Jai Bahaduri in lead roles. This film is considered one of the best movies in India ever made, and all the kids and older people love it. If you haven't seen this movie till now, then what are you waiting for, go and watch this masterpiece on an urgent basis.

IMDB Rating: 8.1/10

Cast: Dharmendra, Amitabh Bachchan, Sanjeev Kumar, Amjad Khan, Hema Malini Jaya Bahaduri and many more.

Director: Ramesh Sippy

Original Language: Hindi

Release Dates: 15 August 1975

Country: India

This movie was released in the year 1995, and it is considered one of the best romantic movies in India. This movie made Shah Rukh Khan an overnight superstar and ran on the big screen for 20 years at the Maratha Mandir Theater in Mumbai. Shahrukh Khan and Kajol were in the lead role in this film, and in this film, Raj (SRK) and Simran (Kajol) are in love with one another, but Simran's Father fixed her marriage with some other man to know what happened next, you have to watch the movies.

Country: India

If you call yourself a movie lover and have not listened to the name of this movie or not watched it till now, then you are not a movie lover. If you like action and thrilling movies, then you should watch The Dark Knight. This is a superhero film which is released in the year 2008. This movie is based on the superhero Batman. The Dark Knight is the Sequel to the 2005 film Batman Begins. In this movie, Batman wants to end crime in his city, but a mastermind named Joker escapes every time he commits a crime. You have to watch the movie to see if Batman catches the Joker or not. This is a movie in which the hero, as well as the villain's acting, was highly praised, and it would not be wrong to say that in this movie, the Joker is ahead of Batman. The second movie in this series came in 1974, and the third in 1990. These films won many awards, and the acting done by the star cast in these series of movies is at another level.

Through this film, an attempt has been made to tell us that some students study a subject other than their interest due to the pressure of their parents. This film won many awards in India, and all the actors did brilliant acting in this film. If you have not watched this movie till now, then you are missing some great movies.

13. 12 Angry Men

12 Angry Men was released in the year 1957, and even after more than 50 years after its release, this movie is one of the best movies in the world. This is an American courtroom drama film which is produced by Henry Fonda and Reginald Rose. This movie is based on the teleplay of the name Twelve Angry Men by Reginald Rose, and the movie name is also taken from the same teleplay. The story of this movie is based on a boy who is accused of killing his father. And to investigate this murder, the court formed a team of 12 people so that it could find out whether the boy was guilty or not. This is one of the best courtroom drama films and also worth watching the film.

IMDB Rating: 9 /10

Cast: Henry Fonda, Lee J. Cobb, Ed Begley, E.G. Marshall, Jack Warden, and many more.

Director: Sidney Lumet.

Original Language: English
