Best Hard Drive for Gaming

A hard drive is an essential thing for a perfect gaming experience. The user may have the best gaming monitor, mouse, or keyboard, but all these things are useless if there is no space in the user's computer to install his favorite game. To overcome the problem of space, a dedicated hard drive is needed. The hard drive provides the space to install the games and reduces the load time and game lagging.

List of Best Gaming Hard Drive

However, there are thousands of gaming hard drives available in the market, out of which some are given below:

1. Samsung 860 Evo (Best internal SSD Hard Drive)

Samsung 860 Evo provides the incredible speed of SSD that attract gamers. However, it is the specialty of all the SSDs, but Samsung hard drives come with the latest V-NAND technology that differs from all other SSDs. This hard drive can read at the speed of 550MB/s and write at 520MB/s, which is a very higher rate than normal hard drives.

This hard drive can also improve the performance by 51% as compared to the Samsung 850 Evo. This hard drive has a self-encrypting drive technology that provides data protection without effectively affecting the drive's performance.

When we upgrade to a new hard drive, we have to reinstall the operating system, and then we can move all the data to the new drive. Still, Samsung 860 Evo comes with Data migration software that will automatically transfer all the data into it.

The user can also maintain or monitor the SSD by downloading the Samsung Magician Software. This drive comes with five years of warranty and available in sizes ranging from 250 GB to 4 TB.

Advantages of Samsung 860 Evo

  • This hard drive provides excellent speed.
  • This hard drive protects all your data.
  • The user can easily monitor it through Samsung Magician software.

Disadvantages of Samsung 860 Evo

  • Sometimes data migration software stop working.

2. WD Black (Best Internal Laptop Hard Disk)

Western Digital is one of the best companies in the field of hard drive manufacturing. The WD Black provides the space for all your games and data. The WD Black has a dual-core processor that provides the read and write speed of 200MB/s. This hard drive also consists of 64 MB of cache that enhances the load and access time.

This hard drive comes with some protection features such as No Touch Ramp Load Technology and Corruption Protection Technology. This hard drive has silica gel on the top of its plastic wrapper to protect it from moisture.

Advantages of WD Black

  • This hard drive provides a very fast speed.
  • This hard drive comes in multiple sizes.
  • This hard drive also comes with a data protection mechanism.

Disadvantages of WD Black

  • This drive makes noise while performing the tasks.

3. Seagate FireCuda SSHD (Best hybrid hard disk)

The Seagate FireCuda Hard disk is specially designed for gamers to enhance their gaming experience. This hard drive is available in only 1TB and 2 TB version because any lesser capacity is not very good for the modern gaming experience.

This hard drive comes with 7200 RPM, 64 MB of cache, and SATA 6GB/s connection. The most attractive feature of this hard drive is its hybrid architecture.

This hard drive is a combination of SSD and HDD because it provides the speed of an SSD and storage capabilities of an HDD. This hard drive also memorizes the accessed games and saves them to the flash memory, which reduces the access time.

This FireCuda 3.5'' provides higher performance than the FireCuda 2.5'', but its bigger size takes more space for the rotating disk.

Advantages of Seagate FireCuda SSHD

  • The user can easily erase the data from the hard drive without facing any hassle.
  • This hard drive comes with self-encrypting technology that protects the user's data.
  • A 2-years warranty is also provided.

Disadvantages of Seagate FireCuda SSHD

  • The user can not manually select what to store in flash memory.

4. Seagate FireCuda Gaming (Best Internal Laptop Hard Disk)

The Seagate FireCuda Gaming is very convenient to use. It comes with a plug and play setup with which the game takes a few seconds to load when the Xbox recognizes the hard disk. This hard drive also takes very less load time than the system's internal hard drive.

This drive is also used to store movies and other media files such as videos, audios pictures, etc., that can be directly played with the Xbox console's help.

Advantages of Seagate FireCuda Gaming

  • This hard drive comes with an in-built plug and play set up.
  • This hard drive comes with fast USB 3.0 ports.
  • This hard drive has 4TB of space and comes with two years of warranty.

Disadvantages of Seagate FireCuda Gaming

  • The user can not play media and games both at the same time.

5. Seagate BarraCuda (Best External Hard Drive)

The Seagate BarraCuda is considered the best external hard drive. This hard drive is used to add more space to the system without removing the existing hard drive.

This hard drive is very efficient for those who used to play games on laptops. It is available in multiple sizes, starting from 1 TB to 8TB. This drive is compatible with both the 3.0 ports and 2.0 ports.

This hard drive helps the gamers to store their games when they are not in use by providing an extra 4TB space. The users can play their games whenever they want, or they doesn't have to delete their games when they needs extra storage.

Advantages of Seagate BarraCuda

  • This hard drive provides enough space for gamers.
  • The users can easily set up this hard drive to their system.
  • This hard drive comes with a fast USB with a 3.0 interface.

Disadvantages of Seagate BarraCuda

  • This hard drive is a little bit bulky.

6. WB Blue (Best Budget Hard Drive)

This hard drive is considered one of the best budget-friendly hard drive. This hard drive is best for those who have a low budget, or their game library is not so big.

This hard drive comes with a rotation speed of 7200 RPM and a cache size of 8 MB. This drive provides a very fast load and access time.

The user has to compromise on the number of games and size of games, but by comparing its price to other hard drives, this is the best drive. It will provide a two-year warranty, and also the user can return the product for a refund.

Advantages of WB Blue

  • This hard drive comes in a very affordable price range.
  • This hard drive provides a very fast load time.
  • The user can get a refund while returning it, which does not apply to other hard drives.

Disadvantages of WB Blue

  • The user can play games of limited size.

7. Toshiba P300 (Best Hard Drive for PC and Desktops)

The Toshiba P300 is considered as one of the best Hard Drive for PCs and Desktops. This hard drive comes with a rotation speed of 7200 RPM, a cache size of 64 MB, and 2 TB of storage space. This hard drive comes with the caching technology that allocates the cache space during the read and writes cycles, which results in faster access to the games. Toshiba has also taken the necessary measures that protect the data.

This Toshiba hard drive is available in multiple sizes ranging from 500GB to 3 TB and comes with two years of warranty.

Advantages of Toshiba P300

  • This hard drive provides a great loading time.
  • This hard drive protects the user's data.
  • This hard drive does not produce noise.
  • This hard drive is very easy to install.

Disadvantages of P300

  • There are some reported cases of premature failure.
