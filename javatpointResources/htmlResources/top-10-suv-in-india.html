Top 10 SUV In India

The word SUV stands for Sport Utility Vehicle. A sport utility vehicle (SUV) is an automobile class that incorporates characteristics of a passenger car with off-road vehicle attributes such as greater ground clearance and four-wheel drive. There is no universally accepted definition of an SUV, and the term is used differently in different nations. SUVs are huge vehicles having seats for at least five people.

Compact SUVs, moderate SUVs, and full-length SUVs are the three main kinds of SUVs. Buyers with relatively large families and for extended travel favor SUVs. It might be challenging to select the ideal SUV at times. Finding a nice automobile that fits your budget may be quite challenging because body style is one of the most important factors to consider when purchasing a car. 106 SUVs are now available for purchase from several manufacturers, with prices beginning at 4.54 lakh. BMW XM is the newest SUV to be released.

The least costly vehicle is the Nissan Magnite, and the most expensive SUV is the Rolls-Royce Cullinan. The Toyota Fortuner (Rs. 32.59 - 50.49 Lakh), Mahindra Thar (Rs. 13.59 - 16.29 Lakh), and Mahindra XUV700 (Rs. 13.45 - 24.95 Lakh) are the most popular vehicles in this price range. Maruti Suzuki, Hyundai, Tata, Renault, Mahindra, and Kia are the leading manufacturers. The giants of the road are SUV vehicles. You will undoubtedly have a comfortable drive whether you are on an urban road or an off-road route.

People are now exploring this booming vehicle market outside of sedans and hatchbacks. While previously, due to their exorbitant price, not everyone could buy SUVs. The emergence of tiny and compact SUVs at a comparably lower price point, however, has elevated them to the list of best deals. SUVs are growing more and more common, particularly in India. Indian and international businesses have taken notice of the Full-Size pickup or Sports UV type.

Even the pandemic's effects on this population have been significantly less severe than on others. The justification is easy to comprehend. SUV demand has increased recently due to a global trend. Therefore, it should come as no surprise that the majority of passenger car makers have recognized this potential and are working to either create new SUV models or increase the current SUV portfolio. Following are the top ten SUVs in India:

1. Hyundai Creta

The Hyundai Creta is available with one diesel engine and two petrol engines. The diesel engine has a displacement of 1493 cc, while the petrol engines have displacements of 1497 cc and 1353 cc. It comes with both manual and automatic gearbox options. The Creta has a mileage of 16.8 kmpl, depending on the version and fuel type.

The Creta is a five-seater four-cylinder vehicle with a length of 4300mm, a width of 1790mm, and a wheelbase of 2610mm. Panoramic sunroof: Increases cabin airiness. Apple CarPlay and Android Auto with split-screen multiplayer features are available on the 10.25-inch touchscreen. Front-seat ventilation is a lifesaver in a tropical climate like ours.

Despite fresh introductions in its sector, the appeal of the Hyundai Creta SUV in the Indian SUV marketplace has remained robust, with stable sales statistics. Hyundai sold 11,880 Creta SUVs in India in October 2022, a rise of more than 84% year on year.

2. Maruti Suzuki Brezza

The new Brezza is only available with a 1.5L naturally aspirated petrol engine that produces 103 PS and 137Nm of torque. The engine is mated to either a 5-speed manual or a 6-speed torque converter automatic transmission. The SHVS mild-hybrid technology that Maruti has introduced on its small SUV will be available on the Brezza. The new Brezza offers a stated mileage of up to 20.15 km in manual form and 19.80 km in automated form.

The SUV offers Up to 6 airbags, along with ESP, ABS, and EBD. It does come with an Electric sunroof, Cruise control and an Automatic climate control system. A 360-degree camera, Hill hold assist, and a 9-inch touchscreen with an infotainment display adds to the features of the SUV. An Arkamys sound system with an additional Heads-up display makes it stand at the top list.

The Maruti Suzuki Vitara Brezza facelift includes projector headlights with LED DRLs, a revised grille, new 16-inch dual-tone alloy wheels, LED taillights, and an updated rear bumper. New LED taillights and a redesigned rear bumper are added to the Vitara Brezza's rear end. The recently introduced Maruti Suzuki Brezza slid to the 4th rank in October 2022, despite the continuing Christmas season. This might be owing to the long wait time and persistent chip scarcity. Maruti Suzuki sold 9,941 units of the Brezza SUV in October 2022, representing a 23.77% YoY sales growth.

3. Kia Seltos

One diesel engine and two gasoline engines are available for the Kia Seltos. The petrol engine is 1497 cc, and the diesel engine is 1493 cc, respectively. Both a manual and automatic gearbox are offered. The mileage of the Seltos varies depending on the model and fuel type. The Seltos is a four-cylinder, five-seat vehicle with dimensions of 4315 mm in length, 1800 mm in width, and 2610 mm in wheelbase.

The industry's first built-in air filter with continuous air quality monitoring is a smart air purifier. The most luxurious 8-speaker sound system in its class is the Bose system. None of the Seltos' competitors offers a touchscreen this size, which measures 10.25 inches. The overall system experience should improve as a result.

For the South Korean carmaker, the Kia Seltos continues to generate strong sales results. Additionally, Kia's 5-seat SUV is presently the most popular model in the nation. Kia sold 9,777 Seltos SUVs in India in October 2022. YoY sales data did, however, decline by roughly 7%.

4. Mahindra Scorpio

There are three diesel engines available for the Mahindra Scorpio 2523, 2179, and 1997 cubic centimeters that make up the diesel engine. Both a manual and automatic gearbox are offered. Scorpio's mileage varies according on the model and fuel type, and its ground clearance is 180mm. The Scorpio is a four-cylinder, seven-seat vehicle with dimensions of 4456 mm in length, 1820 mm in width, and 2680 mm in wheelbase.

While the other models have a five-speed manual transmission, the top-of-the-line S7 vehicle gets a new six-speed manual transmission. The company asserts that the new simple shift 6-Speed gearbox and sixth-generation Borg Warner turbocharger optimize performance, cruise on highways in overdrive, and conserve petroleum.

One of India's best SUVs for logging miles is the Mahindra Scorpio. The Mahindra 7-seater SUV performs admirably and has a dominating driving posture. India continues to favour Mahindra Scorpio as a preferred option. But if the manufacturer could boost production numbers, the sales numbers may increase significantly. According to sales data, Mahindra sold 7,438 Scorpio SUVs in India during the month of October 2022, more than doubling its YoY sales.

5. Tata Nexon

Sales of the sub-compact SUV from Tata Motors have increased. Nexon's sales have climbed by almost 68% since January of last year, when 8,225 units were sold. Tata sold 12,899 Nexon devices in December. The Tata Nexon is powered by both gasoline and diesel. The 1.2-liter turbocharged petrol engine produces 170Nm of torque at 1,750 rpm and 118bhp at 5,500 rpm. The 1.5-liter diesel engine, on the other hand, generates 260 Nm of torque at 1,500 rpm and 108 horsepower at 4,000 revolutions per minute.

A six-speed manual and an automated manual transmission are available transmissions (AMT). The dashboard of Tata Nexon is uncomplicated and clear. The tiny SUV has a redesigned steering wheel with a shorter horn pad and controls for the multimedia system and cruise control that seem luxurious. The Nexon is svelte, with a chiseled profile, unlike the previous model's muscular and fuller look.

New dual-barrel projector headlamps with built-in tri-arrow LED DRLs are now installed on the small SUV. A huge "tri-arrow" screen has been constructed, and the bottom air dam has been made larger. The car has been equipped with contemporary alloy wheels that measure 16 inches in diameter. Compact SUVs are in demand, and the Tata Nexon is among the finest models in its class.

6. Mahindra XUV700

There are so many goodies in the new Mahindra XUV700. The Advanced Driver Assistance Systems (ADAS) are the components of the 7-seater SUV that get the greatest attention. There are two diesel engines and one petrol engine available for the Mahindra XUV700. A 2.0-liter turbocharged engine with 197 horsepower powers the petrol engine.

While both diesel engines are 2.2 liters in size. The more powerful model produces 182 horsepower, while the other model manages 153 bhp. All other models come with an option of either a 6-speed manual transmission or a 6-speed automatic gearbox, with the exception of the diesel engine with a lower power output. The XUV700 boasts a black grille frame, big headlights, and dog-shaped LED daytime running lights.

On the exterior, it boasts futuristic flush door handles, and the ORVMs can be powered and have turn indicators integrated. The AX5 and AX7 versions have 17-inch diamond-cut alloy wheels and 18-inch diamond-cut alloy wheels, respectively, while the MX Series and AX3 have 17-inch steel wheels. Arrow-shaped LED taillights highlight the back. The Mahindra XUV700 is offered in 28 variations. In addition, the Mahindra XUV700 is the only SUV in its class with an all-wheel-drive configuration.

7. Toyota Fortuner

The Toyota Fortuner is well-known throughout India. In India, the Toyota Fortuner doesn't require an introduction. Almost every Indian politician has a 7-seater SUV, which has a significant cult following in the nation. In addition, the Toyota Fortuner is among the greatest off-road vehicles in the nation. The Toyota Fortuner measures 4,795mm in length, 1,855mm in width, 1,835mm in height, and 2,745mm in wheelbase.

A 2.7-liter petrol engine and a 2.8-liter diesel engine are the two powertrain choices for the Toyota Fortuner. While the latter generates 201 horsepower and 420 Newton metres of torque, the former generates 164 horsepower and 245 Newton metres (500Nm with AT). Both a six-speed manual and a six-speed automatic gearbox are available. The Toyota Fortuner has a wireless charger, an eight-inch smart play infotainment system with Apple CarPlay and Android Auto, a cooled glove box, commute modes, electronically controlled front seats, heated seats, and cruise control.

It also has automatic climate control and ventilated front seats. Seven airbags, ABS with EBD, parking sensors, a seatbelt reminder system, a speed alert system, VSC with brake assistance, traction control, and a park assist feature are among the model's safety features. The Toyota Fortuner gets a new grille, outside taillights, 18-inch alloy wheels, reworked bumpers, and LED headlights with LED DRLs. The SUV's Price at the Showroom is ?31.39 - ?43.43 Lakh.

8. Mahindra Thar

The brand-new Mahindra Thar combines cutting-edge innovation with vintage grace. Other than that, it's among the greatest off-road vehicles now available. Like no other automobile in India, the new Mahindra Thar successfully combines cutting-edge tech with classic appeal. Aside from that, it is unquestionably among the top off-road vehicles now on the market in the nation. A brand-new 1,997cc turbocharged petrol engine and either a torque converter automatic gearbox or a six-speed manual transmission power this brand-new Thar.

While the automatic transmission generates 320Nm between 1,500 and 3,000 rpm, it produces 150bhp at 5,000 rpm and 300Nm of torque between 1,250 and 3,000 rpm. The most recent model of the reliable mHawk has a 2,184cc diesel engine and a six-speed manual or automatic transmission. There are two available trims for the Thar: optional AX and LX. Mahindra offers complicated top and convertible top choices that are factory fitted. Additionally, the AX option has 16-inch alloy wheels while the LX grade has 18-inch alloy wheels.

For extreme off-roading, the two doors may be removed. Mahindra completely renovated Thar's interior, making it washable and drainable. The Thar boasts a contemporary interior, similar to a chic hatchback or sedan. An infotainment system with a touchscreen and smartphone connection is present. Additionally, a manual gearstick is used to handle the four-wheel-drive system.

9. TATA Harrier

The Tata Harrier drives more like a legitimate SUV than a hulking-up 5-seater, offering one of the greatest rides in its class. The Tata Harrier boasts a strong diesel engine, much charisma on the road, and outstanding mileage capabilities. The Tata Harrier has one of the nicest rides in its class and drives more like an actual SUV than a hulking-up 5-seater vehicle.

The Tata Harrier has a powerful diesel engine, excellent mile-eating skills, and a dominating road presence. There are two different types of transmissions: a 6-speed manual and a 6-speed automatic.

The Harrier sports stylish high-mounted DRLs and low-mounted headlights. The Harrier has a distinctive profile made up of sweeping arches, a sloping roof, and a floating appearance on the D-pillar. Its tailgate is curved, and the LED taillamps are modern and precise.

The 1.5-liter diesel engine that powers the Mahindra Bolero produces 75PS and 210Nm.
