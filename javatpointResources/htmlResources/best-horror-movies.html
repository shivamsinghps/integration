Best Horror Movies

Watching movies has been considered a means of entertainment for the last many years. People of all ages like to watch movies. Nowadays, many types of movies are made, such as horror movies, comedy movies, action movies, romantic movies, and many more. Some like to watch comedy movies and some like action movies; everyone has their one choice and interest. Today in this article, we list some best horror movies for horror movie lovers so they do not have to face many problems selecting the best ones to watch. This article discusses some Best Horror Movies to watch.

1. The Conjuring

IMDB Rating: 7.5/10

Movie Cast: Vera Farmiga, Patrick Wilson, Ron Livingston, Lili Taylor, and many more.

Directed By: James Wan

Original Language: English

Release Dates:

  • July 15, 2013 (Cinerama Dome)
  • July 19, 2013 (United States)

Country: United States

Many horror movies must have been made until today, but The Conjuring is considered the best horror movie of all time. This movie was released in the year 2013, and it is a supernatural horror film. It is believed that this film is based on a true incident. In this movie, a family moves to a new house, and after some days, strange things start happening in that house; why did this happen, and what did that family do? To know this, you have to watch this movie. If you want to see one perfect horror movie, then The Conjuring is for you.

Apart from The Conjuring, two more parts of The Conjuring have been released, The Conjuring 2 and The Conjuring 3 (The Devil Made Me Do It), in 2016 and 2021.

2. Annabelle

IMDB Rating: 5.4/10

Movie Cast: Annabelle Wallis, Ward Horton, Alfre Woodard, and many more.

Directed By: John R. Leonetti

Original Language: English

Release Dates:

  • September 29, 2014 (TCL Chinese Theatre)
  • October 3, 2014 (United States)

Country: United States

Annabelle is also one of the best horror movies, and millions of horror movie lovers consider this film their favorite horror film. This is a supernatural horror film released in 2014, and the director of this movie is John R. Leonetti. It is a precursor to the 2013 horror movie The Conjuring and is the second entry in the Conjuring Universe series. The story of this film is centered on a doll, whose name is Annabelle.

This film is believed to be based on a true incident, and a similar doll is actually found. Like The conjuring three-part, there is also three-part available in Annabelle Movies series. The first part was released in 2014, the second (Annabelle: Creation) was released in 2017, and the third (Annabelle Comes Home) in 2019. What are you waiting for if you have not watched this movie yet?

3. The Nun

IMDB Rating: 5.3/10

Movie Cast: Demián Bichir, Taissa Farmiga, Jonas Bloquet, and many more.

Directed By: Corin Hardy

Original Language: English

Release Dates:

  • September 4, 2018 (TCL Chinese Theatre)
  • September 7, 2018 (United States)

Country: United States

In the year 2018, the film The Nun was released and liked by many people and is today considered among the best horror movies in the world. The director of this movie is Corin Hardy, and it is an American gothic supernatural horror film. The story of this movie is based on Nun, who lives in Saint Cartha's monastery, and some unseen evil force attacked them. To know what happens next, you have to watch this awesome movie.

4. The Exorcist

IMDB Rating: 8.1/10

Movie Cast: Ellen Burstyn, Max von Sydow, Lee J. Cobb, Kitty Winn, Jack MacGowran, Jason Miller, Linda Blair, and many more.

Directed By: William Friedkin

Original Language: English

Release Dates: December 26, 1973 (United States)

Country: United States

Recently, many horror films have been released, which are the favorite of millions of people, but some horror films which have been released before more than 30 years from today are also favorites of many people and also considered as one of the best horror movies ever made. The Exorcist is also a horror film that was released in the year 1973 and is considered among the best horror movies ever made. This movie is based on a horror novel written by William Peter Blatty in 1971, and the name of the film is also taken from the name of the novel.

This film tells the story of a girl who is haunted by ghosts and demons, and what has to be done by her mother to get rid of that ghosts and demons is beautifully shown in this movie. If you have not watched this perfect horror movie yet, watch this awesome movie as soon as possible.

5. Hereditary

IMDB Rating: 7.3/10

Movie Cast: Toni Collette, Alex Wolff, Milly Shapiro, Ann Dowd, Gabriel Byrne, and many more.

Directed By: Ari Aster

Original Language: English

Release Dates:

  • January 21, 2018 (Sundance)
  • June 8, 2018 (United States)

Country: United States

In the year 2018, a supernatural psychological horror film was released whose name was Hereditary. Today this film is the first choice for many horror movie lovers. This story revolves around Charlie and the maternal grandmother of this girl who dies in a suspicious condition; since then, the shadow or soul of that woman gets attached to that girl, but the girl's mother tries to find this hidden secret between her baby girl and her maternal grandmother, but she comes across some truth about the family that leaves her stunned. After which, the story has many interesting and scary twists that can make you go crazy.

6. The Ring

IMDB Rating: 7.1/10

Movie Cast: Naomi Watts, Martin Henderson, David Dorfman, Jane Alexander, Brian Cox, and many more.

Directed By: Gore Verbinski

Original Language: English

Release Dates: October 18, 2002

Country: United States

If you like to watch a horror movie and have not heard about this movie or watched it until now, this cannot be possible. The Ring is a perfect horror movie for the horror movie lover. It is a supernatural horror film released in the year 2002, and the director of this movie is Gore Verbinski. This is the remake of the film Ring, which is a Japanese horror film based on the novel Ring. Even after more than 20 years of release, the film is counted among one of the best horror films of all time. After this movie, two more part of this movie was released in the year 2005 and 2017; in 2005, The Ring Two was released, and in 2017 Rings was released.

At the beginning of the film, two friends are shown, Katie Embry and Becca Kotler, and they both discuss a videotape; one of the friends told that after watching this videotape person will die on the 7th day and to know what happens next you have to watch this film.

7. Insidious

IMDB Rating: 6.8/10

Movie Cast: Patrick Wilson, Rose Byrne, Barbara Hershey, and many more.

Directed By: James Wan

Original Language: English

Release Dates:

  • September 14, 2010 (TIFF)
  • April 1, 2011 (United States)

Country: United States, and Canada

Although most horror films have 2 to 3 parts, but only a few horror films are such that all the parts are very good. Similarly, there is a horror film named Insidious; it is a series of horror films, of which 4 parts have been released so far. The secret behind the success of Insidious is that all its parts are well-liked by the audience and horror movie lovers and for this reason, this film series has been named one of the best horror movie series ever made.

Insidious is a supernatural horror film that was released in 2010, and this film was the first film of the Insidious movie series. After the success of this film, its different parts were released in the year 2013 (Insidious: Chapter 2), then in 2015 (Insidious: Chapter 3), and again in 2018 (Insidious: The Last Key). The first part of this film is about a family who shifts to a new house, and their son goes into a coma after a few days. Even after 3 months of treatment, they do not feel any improvement in his health, then they shift their child from the hospital to the same house, but the boy's parents say that there is a shadow of a monster or a demon in this house, to know what happens next, you should watch the film.

8. Sinister

IMDB Rating: 6.8/10

Movie Cast: Ethan Hawke, Juliet Rylance, Fred Thompson, James Ransone, Clare Foley, Michael Hall D'Addario, and many more.

Directed By: Scott Derrickson

Original Language: English

Release Dates:

  • March 11, 2012 (SXSW)
  • October 12, 2012 (United States)

Country: The United Kingdom and The United States

The film Sinister, released in the year 2012, is a supernatural horror film considered one of the scariest horror films. At the beginning of this film, 4 people are shown hanging on one tree, and an invisible force is cutting that tree's branch; due to this, they all die. After some days, one struggling writer shifted to that house with their family for some realistic story, so he becomes successful overnight, but soon after shifting to a new house with his family, his son facing some hidden supernatural power, to know what happened next, you have to watch this awesome movie. After the success of the first part second part of this movie is also released in the year 2015 (Sinister 2).

9. The Shining

IMDB Rating: 8.4/10

Movie Cast: Jack Nicholson, Shelley Duvall, Scatman Crothers, Danny Lloyd, and many more.

Directed By: Stanley Kubrick

Original Language: English

Release Dates:

  • May 23, 1980 (United States)
  • October 2, 1980 (United Kingdom)

Country: The United Kingdom and The United States

So many psychological horror films must have been made till now, but The Shining is considered among one of the best psychological horror films till now. This movie was released in the year 1980, and the director of this film was Stanley Kubrick. This movie is also based on the novel, which is written by Stephen Edwin King, and the name of this movie is also taken from the same novel name.

At the beginning of this film, a writer goes to work in a hotel, and that writer finds a secluded room for writing in the hotel, but the manager of that hotel advises that writer not to stay in that room due to some reason. Will that writer stay in it, and if he stays, then something bad will happen to him or not? To know this, you have to watch this amazing movie.

10. The Evil Dead

IMDB Rating: 7.4/10

Movie Cast: Bruce Campbell, Ellen Sandweiss, Richard DeManincor, Betsy Baker, Theresa Tilly, and many more.

Directed By: Sam Raimi

Original Language: English

Release Dates: October 15, 1981

Country: The United States

The Evil Dead was released in the year 1981, and the director of this movie was Sam Raimi, and it is a supernatural horror film. This movie is one of the most popular horror movies, even after many years of release. Every scene of this movie will make you think and engage you until the movie is not finished.

The film focuses on five college friends who go to a secluded place to vacation during their vacation. Meanwhile, they get an audio tape, and after playing it, something extremely scary and painful occurs. To know what happens after listening to that audio tape, you must watch this one of the scariest horror movies.

11. The Thing

IMDB Rating: 8.2/10

Movie Cast: Kurt Russell, R.J. MacReady, A. Wilford Brimley, T. K. Carter, David Clennon, Keith David, Richard Dysart, Charles Hallahan, Peter Maloney, Richard Masur, Thomas G. Waites, and many more.

Directed By: John Carpenter

Original Language: English

Release Dates: June 25, 1982

Country: The United States

No matter how many science fiction horror films you must have seen but if you haven't seen The Thing yet, then you are missing one of the best science fiction horror films. Who Goes There? is the novella on which the story of The Thing is based, and the writer of this novella was John W. Campbell Jr., and this novella was published in the year 1938. This film is based on some researchers who want to do some missions in Antarctica, and after starting the mission, they face big problems; how they handle it, you know, after watching this awesome movie.
