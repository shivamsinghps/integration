Neil Bhatt

Neil Bhatt is an actor and a model. He is one of the finest actors in the entertainment industry. Generally, it was said by people that, in a lot of instances, small-town boys could not dream about bigger things, but Neil Bhatt broke that stereotype making a big success in his career. He was first introduced as a dance and choreographer on the screen. Later he strived hard, and eventually, he got many offers on Television and films. He became famous for his role in Roop Mard Ka Naya Swaroop and as Virat Chavan in the Tv series. In the TV series 'Diya Aur Bati Hum, he played Zakir Siddiqui, an IPS officer.' This role made a benchmark in his career.

'Ghum Hai Kisikey Pyaar Mein' has bought him success after Roop Mard Ka Naya Swaroop. He has a huge social media following. Nearly three lakh seventy thousand people follow him on Facebook, and eight lakhs twenty-nine thousand people on Instagram. His youtube subscribers count eighty-two thousand and still counting. He often makes headlines in famous national news articles like Times Of India and The Hindu.

Neil Bhatt, before entering the entertainment industry:

Neil Bhatt was born on 4 August 1987 in Vadodara, Gujarat. Bhatt schooling has happened at Jasudben M.L. School in Mumbai. Himanshu Bhatt is Neil Bhatt's father, and he is a lawyer. His mother is Sunita Bhatt. Neil maintains a lowkey about his family. He and his wife, Aishwarya, frequently share their pictures on social media. He has one sibling, and her name is Shikha. He is a professional dancer, and he entered as a dancer initially.

Then he won a popular dance show and later became an actor. When Neil Bhatt was asked what he would be doing if he was not an actor, Neil said that he had always wanted to be a lawyer. So that he would have been a good lawyer. Bhatt studied Law at Jitendra Chauhan College of Law, Mumbai, and later chose to become an actor. He once said in one of the famous
magazines, 'The Times of India', that he wanted to complete his graduation anyways and then move into acting. He also learned gymnastics and wanted to participate nationally, but that couldn't happen because of his issues.

Physical Appearance:

Neil Bhatt's height is five feet and nine inches. Neil Bhatt is a fair-skinned person with seventy kg of weight and is browned eyed man with black hair. Since he is a fitness freak, his physical appearance has been a great plus to his fan base and following.

He is a guy with desirable physical features, and in a way that dragged him to grab more television series and films.

Personal Life and Interests:

Neil Bhatt is a fitness freak. If one observes his social media, most of his content will be on his fitness and gym videos. While he was shooting for 'Ghum Hai Kisikey Pyaar Meiin', unfortunately, he tested positive for the coronavirus. He then shared the process he had gone through to cure himself of the coronavirus was hard, and his body metabolism has helped him so much. He is an animal lover, and his social media is full of dog pictures. Neil was also open to doing films, and in fact, he was waiting for opportunities. Since he is from Gujarat, he has always
wanted to work in Gujarati films. After 'Ghum Hai Kisikey Pyaar Mein', he was offered many films, shows and TV series. In one of the interviews, he said that he is ready to act in any genre despite the director's chosen genres.

Neil Bhatt Favorites:

His favourite actor is supposedly Aamir Khan, and Bhatt's favourite actress is Madhuri Dixit. And his favourite food seems to be Daal, south Indian, and anything his mom makes. He is a vegetarian by birth since he comes from a brahmin family. Because of his work demand, sources say that he had to become an eggetarian. But after a while, Neil decided to say NO to eggs in one of the events. A few of his co-actors also praised and supported him for that decision. His favourite colours are white and red. His favourite channels are National Geography and History Tv. He says he loves watching series like Big Bang Theory, Twist Of Fate, Ancient Aliens and Highway On My Plate.

Journey of His Dance Career:

He started his career in 2008 by participating in a dance reality show called Kaboom and won the title. He was a good dancer before he entered the industry. He enrolled himself in a dance reality show for that reason. Later he participated in Boogie Woogie, a popular dance show in the early 2000s, and he gained a bit of popularity through it as he won the show. One of the biggest Dance shows used to be hosted by Saroj Khan called 'Nach Le Ve' in 2011. He has participated as a contestant in the 'Smart Jodi' dance show.

Television Career:

In 2009, he acted as Veer in a TV series called 'Jo Ishq Ki Marzi Woh Rab Ki Marzia. In 2008, he acted in a serial called 'Arslaan', which got telecasted on the Sony channel. He earned fame with '12/24 Karol Bagh', an Indian Television Series.

Later in the serial 'Gulaal', he acted as Kesar, where he got many fans. He played Lord Ram's brother Lakshman in the serial 'Ramayan.' His character as Lakshman in Ramayan was well-liked globally, and it became popular within no time. It got so famous that it was telecasted in Indonesia. The whole cast of Ramayan was once invited to perform the play in Jakarta. Bhatt later acted in 'Diya Aur Baati Hum' as Zakir, Bhushan Pethawala in 'Tum Hi Ho Bandhu Sakha Tumhi.' He also acted in a few popular serials like 'Pyar Tune Kya Kiya' and Zindagi Wins. He once starred in an Indian crime series 'Gumra End of Innocence' in 2012. In 2013 he appeared in the National crime show 'Savdhaan India' followed by 'Yeh Hai Aashiqui' as Pratyush in 2014. In 2018, he acted in 'Laal Ishq' as Aditya. He got a break for the series' Roop-Mard ka Naya Swaroop' and 'Ghum Hai Kisikey Pyaar Mein', where he recorded the highest TRPs in his career. That TV series was directed by Jaydeep Sen, Jafar Shaik and Sidhartha Vankar. 'Ghum Hai Kisikey Pyaar Meiin' was placed in second place among the other Indian Television series for several months.

Film Career:

Neil Bhatt's film career started with the film 'Gulaal.' His big-screen entry was quite a good choice. Later in 2009, he played a dancer in the movie 'Fast Forward.' In 2017, Neil acted in a film called 'Bhanwar', where he played a folk puppeteer. People recognize him much in the film 'Bhanwar' since his performance in that movie is fabulous. Sources say he has yet to sign some of the movies in the coming years.

Awards:

In 2010, Neil won the Zee Rishtey Award under the favourite Naya Sadasya category for '12/24 Karol Bagh.' He gained enormous fame and award nominations for his devotional series 'Ramayan.'

  • In 2018, he won Gujarati Iconic Film Award in the Best Male Debut category for the movie 'Bhanwar.'
  • In 2020, Bhatt won an award for best choreographer for 'Bhanwar.'
  • In 2021, he won the Internation Iconic Award under the 'Versatile Actor in the Indian Television' category for the serial 'Ghum Hai Kisikey Pyaar Mein.'
  • In the same year of 2021, he again got an award for best Jodi of Indian Television for 'Ghum Hai Kisikey Pyaar Mein' along with Ayesha Singh. In one of his interviews, he said his serial 'Ghum Hai Kisikey Pyaar Mein' made him survive in the Television industry for so long.
  • His film 'Bhanwar' got six awards in various categories. The Gujarat government gave the award.

Relationships:

While Neil was acting in 'Ghum Hai Kisikey Pyaar Mein', he knew Aishwarya Sharma as his co-actor. They know each other by the sets of that serial. Aishwarya was playing a role called 'Pakhi' in the same serial. The audience connected to the on-screen pair so much, and they started adorning them with fan pages on social media too. They eventually fell in love and married, a delight to watch for their fans.

Neil Bhatt and Aishwarya Sharma are considered the power couple in the Television industry. Neil's wife, Aishwarya, says that she had many trust issues with other people in her life until she met Neil. She also added that marrying Neil changed her life in many ways in a better way. They made their announcement public in 2021 and later got married by the end of that year. In the dance show 'Smart Jodi', nine other Jodis were competing with them. When conducting a poll on the audience's favourite couple, Neil and Aishwarya won first place. Neil and Aishwarya got eliminated, which made headlines and was heartbreaking to many of their Jodi fans. Aishwarya and Neil often make headlines about their chemistry by posting social media pictures and being told as made-for-other.
