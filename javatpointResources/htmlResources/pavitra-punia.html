Pavitra Punia

Pavitra Punia is an actress and a model from India. She was born on 27 April 1987 in the state of Uttar Pradesh. Gained popularity from reality shows like MTV Splitsvilla (2009) and Big Boss Season 14(2014), and she was on the list of top three finalists. The show Love You Zindagi was her biggest career achievement in a lead role. She played the famous character of Geet Dhillon in this Star plus show, and later she also played the role of an evil queen character named Timnasa in the Sony SAB's fantasy series BaalVeer Returns.

Career

Pavitra Punia, also known as Neha Singh at her hometown where she was born. She came to Mumbai to try her luck in Bollywood. In the year 2010, she made her acting debut in the show Geet- Huyi Sabse Paraai, in which she played the role of Dalljiet. After earning fame from her debut show, she got the very first lead role in the show Love You Zindagi with co-actor Sidharth Shukla. Later, she was also a part of Welcome- Baazi Mehman Nawazi Ki of Life ok's reality show.

Soon she started her journey as an actress on Big Screen for the movie Siddhartha- Love, Lust, Peace, in which the other stars were Mahesh Bhatt, Shivam Bhargava, and Shazahn Padamsee. She has also been a part of Jeele Ye Pal, MTV Making the Cut 2, Hongey Juda Na Hum, Sawaare Sabke Sapne Preeto, and Darr Sabko Lagta Hai.

Star's plus show Yeh Hai Mohabbatein in which she played the antagonist and also played the character of Karuna in Tv's Gangaa. For the maha episode in Zee Tv's of Kaleerein, she portrayed the character of the snake who changes his forms. Then she was also part of the supernatural show on Colors Tv's Naagin3, and she also played the character of an evil queen named Timnasa fantasy series of BaalVeer Returns on Sony Sab.

In the year 2020, she entered the Bigg Boss Season 14 as a contestant in the house and remained in the house for 57 days, and one of the three finalists is she.

Similar to numerous other well-known Bollywood and TV performers, Pavitra favors using a separate stage name. The actress changed both her first and last names. Neha Singh, in full, is her name.

Before Splitsvilla, Pavitra had a lot of aspirations. You might be surprised to know that the Naagin 3 girl originally intended to work as an IPS officer before pursuing a career in filmmaking. In reality, she was preparing for it when Spiltsvilla reached her. The reality television series Splitsvilla 3 on MTV launched the 32-year-career old's in 2009. 4-The rumored romance between her and Siddharth Shukla

According to rumors, Sidharth Shukla, the Bigg Boss 13 winner, dated Pavitra. In the television program Love You Zindagi, which took its influence from the movie Jab We Met, the two co-starred. When asked if this was true, Sidharth responded to Times Of India, "I'm very much single. I'm not interested in starting a relationship right now.
