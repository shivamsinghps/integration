It makes the first impression on the particular employer and gives a unique look among the crowd. There are a variety of styles like professional, classic, plain, etc. to create templates.

You can create your resume and templates with different backgrounds, colors, texture, etc. according to the requirements.

Difference between resume and CV (Curriculum Vitae)?

A resume is a summary of your skills and experience, while CV is more detailed and stretched version. The resume is of one or two pages, while CV is beyond the two pages. The resume is the most common type of document, while CV includes extensive information on the background of your academics.
