Top 10 female billionaires

Further every year, Forbes magazine also published the list of female billionaires in the world. According to its 2021 survey, there are a total of 328 women billionaires in the world, in which Francoise Bettencourt Meyers from France holds the 1st position. Now I am going to give the list of the top 10 female billionaires in the world that is given below:

1. Francoise Bettencourt Meyers & Family

Francoise Meyers, a board member of L'Oreal from France, holds the top position in the world billionaire's ranking. Her total wealth is $73.6 billion.

2. Alice Walton

Alice Walton from the United States holds the 2ND rank in the world's ranking. Her total wealth is $61.8 billion, and the source of income is Walmart.

3. Mackenzie Scott

Mackenzie Scott, also from the United States, is at 3rd rank in the world's ranking. Her total wealth is $53 billion. She is the ex-wife of Amazon founder Jeff Bezos. After separation, she became a philanthropist during covid19 and also funded several non-profit organizations in the United States. However, she married again with Dan Jewett, a science teacher.

4. Julia Koch & Family

Julia Koch, one of the boards of directors of Koch Industries in the United States, is at 4th position in the world's ranking. Her total wealth is $46.4 billion.

5. Miriam Adelson

Miriam Adelson from the United States holds the 5th rank in the world ranking. Her total wealth is $38.2 billion. Her source of income is the casinos of Las Vegas Sands. However, during the pandemic, the company faced a big loss and announced its selling.

6. Jacqueline Mars

Jacqueline Mars from the United States is at 6th rank in the world's ranking. Her total wealth is $31.3 billion. Her source of income is Candy, Pet food, and Food Companies.

7. Yang Huiyan & Family

Yang Huiyan, a real estate businesswoman from China, holds the 7th rank in the world ranking. Her total wealth is $29.6 billion.

8. Susanne Klatten

Susanne Klatten from Germany holds the 8th rank in the world's ranking. Her total wealth is $27.7 billion. Her source of income is BMW, one of the luxurious car companies and Pharmaceuticals.

9. Gina Rinehart

Gina Rinehart, a chairperson of mining and Agriculture Company of Australia, is at 9th position in the world's ranking. Her total wealth is $23.6 billion. She is also the richest person in Australia.

10. Iris Fontbona & Family

Iris Fontbona from Chile holds the 10th rank in the world's ranking. Her total wealth is $23.3 billion. Her source of income is Antofagasta, a mining company in Chile.

List of 10 billionaires in India

1. Mukesh Ambani

Mukesh Ambani (from Mumbai), the chairman and managing director of Reliance industries, is at 1st position in the list of billionaires in India. His total wealth is $84.5 billion. He is the richest person in the Asia continent as well as at 10th rank in the world's billionaires ranking.

2. Gautam Adani

4. Radhakishan Damani

Radhakishan Damani (from Mumbai), a retailer and investor as well as the founder of avenue supermarket, holds number 4th in the list of billionaires in India. His total wealth is $16.5 billion. In the world's ranking, he holds the 117th position.

5. Uday Kotak

Uday Kotak (from Mumbai), the founder of Kotak Mahindra bank, holds the 5th rank in the list of billionaires in India. He is the richest banker in India. His total wealth is $15.9 billion. In the world's billionaires ranking, his rank is 121st.

6. Lakshmi Mittal

Lakshmi Mittal (NRI, residence in London), the executive chairman of steel company Arcelor Mittal, holds the 6th rank in the list of billionaires in India. His total wealth is $14.9 billion. In the world's ranking, he holds the 140th position.

7. Kumar Birla

Kumar Birla (from Mumbai), who has a commodities empire, holds the 7th rank in the list of billionaires in India. His total wealth is $12.8 billion. In the world's ranking of billionaires, he is at 169th position.

8. Cyrus Poonawalla

Cyrus Poonawalla (from Pune), chairman of Serum Institute of India, is at 8th rank in the list of billionaires in India. His total wealth is $12.7 billion. In the world's billionaires ranking, he holds 169th position.

9. Dilip Shanghvi

Dilip Shanghvi (from Mumbai), the founder of Sun Pharmaceuticals, is at 9th rank in the list of billionaires in India. His total wealth is $10.9 billion. He holds the 203rd position in the world's ranking of billionaires.

10. Sunil Mittal & Family

Sunil Mittal (from Delhi), leader of Bharti Airtel, is in the 10th position in the list of billionaires in India. His total wealth is $10.5 billion. In the world's ranking, he holds the rank 213. Mittal Telecome company is at number second after the Jio company of Ambani.
