Thapar University

The Thapar Institute of Engineering and Technology (TIET) is one of India's oldest and best educational institutions. It was founded in 1956, and the 250-acre Patiala campus is where it is located. TIET is renowned as a pioneer in engineering education, research, and innovation. The college has an A+ NAAC accreditation and is ranked 20th out of India's top 100 engineering schools. Six schools, eight departments, and eight centre make up TIET and provide a variety of undergraduate, graduate, and doctoral programs. The TIET centre are there to fulfil the university's academic requirements. Patiala takes tremendous pride in Thapar University.

Thapar Institute of Engineering & Technology, Patiala, was its last name. Lala Karam Chand of the renowned Thapar Group founded the Institute sometime in or around 1955. The Institute is famous for demanding engineering studies, high discipline, quality education, 100% campus placement, illustrious alums, expansive hostel accommodations, expansive green areas, ultra-modern annual fests, open-air theatres, and many other things.

It is rumored that the exam questions for the various engineering semesters are either more challenging than even IITs or were created by IITs. The university has a strong reputation both domestically and abroad. Thapar University is one of the most prominent regional universities in the nation, covering a relatively sizable 265 acres. The campus, which occupies a 250-acre plot of ground in the centre of Patiala, is home to six former academic buildings-A, B, C, D, E, and F-as well as the two more recent ones, TAN and H. The original Thapar Polytechnic College structure has been transformed into the G academic block.

Three new structures, including a lecture hall complex, a computer facility, and a new library, were inaugurated by the Institute in 2018. A new five-floor Nava Nalanda Library, a six-floor computer complex with labs and faculty areas, and a six-floor lecture complex with lecture halls ranging from 200 to 550 students each and activity rooms with capacities of 200 students each were among the completed expansions. Modern hostels with a combined capacity of more than 8,000 students can be found on the campus, including A, B, C, D, H, J, K, L, M, and O for boys and E, F, G, I, and N for girls.

Facilities

Thapar University's facilities and infrastructure have been modernized. The facilities at the Thapar Institute of Engineering and Technology guarantee that students will have the best possible collegiate experience. Additionally, TIET Patiala's infrastructure and facilities are set up for extracurricular activities to foster students' holistic development. The university features a gym, cafeteria, library, and housing options. Students at Thapar Institute of Engineering and Technology can participate in indoor and outdoor sports on the school's campus. Students have access to Wi-Fi across the campus. Additional Thapar Institute of Engineering and Management amenities include laboratories, a convenience store, a medical centre, an auditorium, and cutting-edge I.T. infrastructure.

  • Male Hostel: The university includes dorms for boys where students can stay. It provides students with a comfortable living environment. Boys' hostel rooms are tastefully designed and equipped with all the necessities.
  • Female Hostel: All the essential amenities are accessible on campus for the convenience of the students and to ensure their comfort during their stay. Students can stay in big and nicely furnished rooms in girls' hostels. The females' dormitories at Thapar Institute of Engineering and Technology are separate.
  • Gym: The TIET Patiala features a state-of-the-art gym for students. It features tools for weightlifting, aerobics, and other exercises.
  • Sports: The Thapar Institute of Engineering and Technology strongly emphasizes students' holistic development. On campus, you can play a wide range of sports indoors and outdoors, including cricket, football, basketball, hockey, table tennis, volleyball, lawn tennis, and badminton.
  • Cafeteria: A cafeteria, a coffee shop, and a juice stand on the Thapar Institute of Engineering and Technology campus. Additionally, a multi-cuisine restaurant nearby offers students excellent and nutritious food.

Auditorium: To organize events on campus, the university boasts a modern auditorium and an outdoor theatre. The arena hosts a variety of varsity academic and social events.

  • Laboratories: More than 100 department-based laboratories are available to students at TIET Patiala. Modern technology in every lab enables students to conduct practical and experiments on campus.
  • Library: There are thousands of books in the university's Nava Nalanda Central Library, which also contains digital learning aids. Students can browse the library's active website to search for books, view new releases, and access other services.
  • Wi-Fi: The Thapar Institute of Engineering and Technology has Wi-Fi access available in every campus area to guarantee that students can access the internet.
  • Infrastructure for I.T.: The campus of TIET Patiala is equipped with a cutting-edge and improved I.T. infrastructure to guarantee that students have the most excellent learning environment possible.
  • Transportation: The Thapar Institute of Engineering and Technology offers transportation services to students, employees, and faculty members.
  • Convenience Shop: To help students, the university has a food and department store with all the necessary amenities. The department shop contains all the essential items to ensure students have a comfortable stay on campus.
  • Alumni Associations for Alumni: The alum association of the university has good networking opportunities.

Ranking

According to the 2020 and 2021 editions of the Times Higher Education World University Rankings, Thapar Institute of Engineering and Technology was rated 601-800 globally and 144 in Asia. It was ranked 1001-1200 globally in the Q.S. World University Rankings of 2022 and 261-270 in Asia in the Q.S. World University Rankings of 2020. In India, the university was ranked 27 by Outlook India in 2019 and 23 among engineering institutes by India Today in 2020. In 2020, the National Institutional Ranking Framework placed engineering institutions 29th, universities 31st, and all other institutions 51st.

Why Choose Thapar Institute of Engineering and Technology?

The dynamics of the education sector have altered exponentially in the current environment. Most institutions are still working to meet the requirements for infrastructure and curriculum. Exposing the next generation to appropriate practical knowledge is more important than academic information. One institution that has improved its faculty, curriculum, and infrastructure to prepare students for the workforce is the Thapar Institute of Engineering and Technology or TIET. The university has much more to offer than educating future engineers and encouraging young minds.

Rankings and Accreditations for TIET

Higher Education Institutions (HEI) in India are evaluated and accredited by the National Assessment and Accreditation Council (NAAC). Thapar Institute of Engineering and Technology recently received certification from an independent authority, receiving an A+ for five years. Similarly, the TIET Patiala institute is ranked among the finest globally in 2019 THE World University Rankings, coming in at number 22.

TIET Admissions Requirements

TIET added new admission requirements before opening the application period for BTech Admissions 2019. This Institute permits applicants to apply using their 10+2 (PCM) marks, unlike other colleges where you should only do so based on your JEE Mains result. JEE Mains and 10+2 (PCM) each receive 70% and 30% of the available seats, respectively.

TIET Staff

Any academic institution's faculty should be of the highest caliber. The Thapar Institute takes pride in having some of the nation's and the world's most gifted and committed thinking leaders. The majority of the faculty at TIET has doctoral degrees, making them among the top academically and professionally. Additionally, TIET hosts an incredible number of visiting academics and guest lecturers thanks to effective partnerships with other prestigious universities from India and outside, thereby boosting the caliber of information provided to the students.

Infrastructure TIET

The campus and infrastructure at TIET Patiala are made to offer students the highest possible quality of life and comfort. The campus also contains a composting and vermicompost unit, a biogas plant, and a playhouse for large-scale production of the blue-green algae biofertilizer, in addition to well-equipped labs, temperature-controlled rooms, and award-winning lodging facilities.

International Exposure of TIET

Thapar Institute routinely partners with other overseas colleges to provide cutting-edge education at pace with shifting market needs.

According to higher education experts worldwide, the most excellent approach to obtaining a quality education with global exposure is through an international engineering program that includes credits from another reputable university abroad. Aside from gaining valuable engineering credits, being immersed in contemporary society helps one learn a new culture and language. They help mold a person's personality and foster growth by preparing them to collaborate with a global team and audience.

To train engineering aspirants worldwide, the premier engineering college Thapar Institute of Engineering and Technology in India offers a 2+2 engineering program with overseas schools like Trinity University Dublin, Tel Aviv University Israel, and many more.

Learn About a New Culture

The most excellent approach to understanding engineering works is to experience it from a different angle. This is possible by first learning the fundamentals at a reputable Indian university, in this example, T.I., and then obtaining exposure abroad by receiving international credit from a different university. Since there won't be many opportunities for vacations, you'll need to become used to your new environment before you can deepen your understanding and learn about the unique educational culture.

Be ready to collaborate with a global workforce: The TIET BE-IEP Program 2019 opens students' minds to new concepts and increases their ability to retain further engineering information. They are more dependable and more competitive in the worldwide market. Anyone who can claim on their resume that they have studied overseas can take advantage of improved work prospects, says a renowned expert. It's essential to have experience in the business sector. It will provide you with a topic to discuss in an interview on a fundamental level. However, it will also demonstrate to your possible employer that you are resourceful and independent, can adapt to new situations, and can stand on your own two feet.

Placements in TIET

With an average placement rate of 92% or higher over the last ten years, Thapar Institute has assisted more than 9000 students in landing their ideal position. Every year, 320+ top organizations from around the world conduct recruiting drives on the TIET campus, including well-known brands like Deloitte, HCL, Whirlpool, HSBC, IBM, Infosys, JP Morgan, and many more. In addition to helping students find their ideal employers, the TIET CPIL placement unit runs various programs (internships, industry-institute engagement, summer training) to prepare students for the workforce.

Character Development

In addition to offering top-notch education and in-demand employment, the Institute creates an environment perfect for preparing students for much more. Every student at TIET has access to more than 50 cultural societies, providing many opportunities to expand their horizons and learn a few new skills.
