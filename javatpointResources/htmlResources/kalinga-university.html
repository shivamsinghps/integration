Kalinga University

The Institution has become the centre of excellence for higher education in India. It is situated in Raipur, Chhattisgarh.

Kalinga University came into existence in 2013 and has admitted more than 8000 students in a brief period. Students from more than twenty foreign nations (mainly Afghanistan, Uganda, South Korea, Nepal, Bangladesh, and Nigeria) and deserving students from our country have sought admission into Kalinga University.

University comes in the list among the top 101-150 universities nationwide. Kalinga University provides Undergraduate, Post- Graduate, and Doctoral Courses in Journalism and Mass Communication, Engineering and Technology, Arts and Humanities, Law, Science, Information Technology, Education, Pharmacy, and Design. Kalinga University accepts admission of international and national students. Students are admitted to Kalinga University through KALSEE. Pearson Vue conducts it. The application process is available, and the application charge is INR 1000.

Engineering

The University admits engineering students and provides specialization in B.Tech. The University also offers M.Tech for the postgraduate level.

Minimum Requirement

  • 10+2 (Minimum 45% marks) and 40% seat is reserved for special category student
  • For a PG degree, the minimum requirement is a Bachelor's degree in the concerned subject

MBA

Eligibility

  • For the management program at the undergraduate, the least requirement is a 10+2 level and English. The minimum requirement at the PG level is Graduation.

Law Courses

Students are also admitted for BBA + L.L.B / BA+L.L.B at the UG level and LLM at the PG level.

Minimum Requirement

For BBA+L.L.B/ BA+L.L.B, the student should have cleared 12th minimum of 45% marks, and 40% of the seats are reserved for students belonging special status, and for LLB, 45% in any bachelor's degree and 40% seats are reserved for the special category students.

University Admissions

Admission to the University is conducted based on a merit system. Students should have secured minimum eligibility for applying to the University:

Admission Rules are:

  • Forms will be available on the University's official website, i.e., www.kalingauniversity.ac.in. Candidates are also provided with an option to fill out their forms in person by visiting the administration office of Kalinga University. Candidates need to register themselves for the process.
  • Deposit the application charge online/offline in the name of Kalinga University.
  • Students should submit all the required documents with a self-attested form.
  • Kalinga University declares its results in May- June.

Infrastructure

Kalinga University takes pride in having world-class infrastructure and student facilities. The University comprises more than 75 laboratories and workshops fully equipped with state-of-the-art technology and apparatus. Special attention has been paid to improving communication skills with the help of the language lab. University has made more than 1200 computers available to students for their use. The University library has more than 79,000 books and provides Digital content using the membership of DELNET, NPTEL, and National Digital Library. Journals and Magazines are present for the use of students and faculty members.

University infrastructure consists of Student Hangout Area, Sports Complex, Music, Recreation activities, a Fully Wi-Fi Campus, ATM, Mini-market, Gymnasium, and Food Mess.

Leadership and Industry oriented: Interaction with the industries is a common feature of the university curriculum, and Industry visit is an integral part of their routine. The University has made a superb connection with Industries associations like the Indian Importers Chamber of Commerce and Industry, the Federation of Indian Chamber of Commerce and Industry, and the Ph.D. Chamber of Commerce and Industry, and the Confederation of Indian Industries.

Medical Facilities

The University offers basic medical care to the students and workers. There may be a full-time doctor, an ambulance, and four beds on campus. For critical conditions, consultation, and care, the University has collaborated with a three-hundred-bed equipped hospital and Medical college through the Raipur Institute of Medical Sciences.
