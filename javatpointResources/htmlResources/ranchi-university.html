Ranchi University

Ranchi, Jharkhand, India, has a public state institution called Ranchi University. A law passed by the Bihar legislature in 1960 created it. There are many degrees available such as undergraduate, graduate, M.Phil, and doctoral. You can do courses from the above list from Ranchi University.

About

Before the founding of Ranchi University, Patna University served as the associate institution for all of the degree-granting institutions in what is now Jharkhand. Ranchi University was established as an academic and affiliated university on July 12, 1960. All degree-granting institutions in the modern state of Jharkhand fell within Ranchi University's purview when it was first founded. Bishnudeo Narayan Singh was the institution's initial vice-chancellor.

Academics

Like most universities in India, the state governor serves as the university's chancellor. The state's chief executive was Bihar's governor before Jharkhand's formation on November 15, 2000. The Vice-Chancellor serves as the university's principal executive.

Affiliations

As an affiliating university, Ranchi University controls the five districts of Jharkhand: Ranchi, Gumla, Khunti, Simdega, and Lohardaga. This university has responsibility for over 29 associate colleges and 15 constituent colleges. These colleges comprise medical schools, nursing schools, colleges of engineering and law, as well as management and psychiatric institutes.

Traditional and distance education

The university and its affiliated colleges and institutions create undergraduate and graduate courses in various subject areas, including medicine, nursing, engineering, liberal arts, etc. While vocational and liberal undergraduate degree courses are given through affiliated schools and institutes, Ranchi University often offers postgraduate liberal arts courses on its campus.

The linked institutions provide professional postgraduate courses such as M.Tech., M.D., and M.s. The outcome of the higher secondary (10+2) examination is the primary basis for admission to undergraduate liberal arts degree courses. However, one must pass an entrance exam by national organizations to be admitted to undergraduate courses in professional areas like engineering and medicine (NEET UG or JEE).

The university or a national organization may offer results from the undergraduate degree test and performance on the entrance exam, which are used to determine admission to postgraduate degrees. Candidates for research institutions must take an admission exam and then interview. Additionally, Ranchi University maintains a Directorate of Distance Education for online postgraduate coursework.

A central library at Ranchi University contains more than 100,000 books. The ethnographic collections of the Andaman and Nicobar Islands and central Indian states are among the exhibits in the museum run by Ranchi University's anthropology department.

Admission

  • Admission to all courses is either based on merit or entrance-based requirements.
  • The CAT/MAT/CMAT/ATMA exam scores determine admission to the M.B.A. program.
  • MBBS and B.D.S. admissions require NEET-UG exam scores, while entrance to M.D. and M.S. is done via the NEET-PG exam score.
  • MBBS and B.D.S. admissions need NEET-UG exam scores, while entrance to M.D. and M.S. is done via the NEET-PG exam score.
  • Candidates are admitted to B.Sc. and M.Sc. nursing courses based on their performance on the qualifying exam.
  • Candidates must provide a valid JCECE or JEE Main test score to qualify for B.Tech admission.
  • Candidates must pass the entrance exam given by the university to be admitted to the B.Ed. Course.
  • Admission to the L.L.B. course is based on performance on the prior qualifying exam.
  • According to the Q.S. rankings 2020, Ranchi University is now ranked 95 for total university performance. Ranchi University graduates are hired by organizations like T.C.S., Infosys, ICICI Bank, HDFC Bank, Tech Mahindra, and others.

Faculties and departments

Ranchi University divided its departments into three faculties.

1. Faculty of Science

This faculty comprises the departments of Mathematics, Physics, Chemistry, Geology, Botany, and Zoology.

2. Faculty of Humanities

Mathematics, Physics, Chemistry, Geology, Botany, and Zoology are some of the departments that make up this faculty.

3. Faculty of Social Sciences and Commerce

Departments in this faculty include History, Geography, Anthropology, Political Science, Economics, Home Science, Psychology, Sociology, and Business.

Ranking and accreditations

The U.G.C. Act's Section 12B recognizes Ranchi University. Ranchi University received a "B+" grade from the National Assessment and Accreditation Council (NAAC) in 2017.

Facilities

On 75 acres of land, Ranchi University's campus belongs to a massive, impressive building. For a better educational experience, it provides students with cutting-edge facilities. A library, computer centre, sports facilities, and other facilities are available on campus. Below are further specifics about Ranchi University's infrastructure:

  • Infrastructural IT
  • A library
  • A hostel
  • A computer centre
  • Sports facilities

1. Library

There are more than 350 PhD theses, 10,000 reference books, and 9,000 textbooks in this library at Ranchi University. Additionally, it offers online access to over 4200 international and national journals. By enrolling in the institution, students get access to these numerous resources.

2. I.T. infrastructure

Ranchi University has top-notch hardware and software computing resources. It offers many systems with different combinations. Most of these are Core2Duo systems (i3 and i5 Intel processors). It has 22 distribution switches and one core switch with dedicated computing resources like Wi-Fi and LAN.

For annual pattern exams and software for semester pattern exams, MECON India Ltd.-built proprietary software is used in the examination process. Under the NME-ICT project, all college departments and additional offices, including the library, academic staff college, and dorms, are connected to N.K.N., offering a 1 Gbps connectivity speed.

3. Hostel

Students who prefer to reside on campus have housing options at the Ranchi University Hostel. Two categories have been established for hostels:

  1. Boys Hostel: A student may select a single room or a shared occupancy, depending on the availability of rooms. There are 210 beds available.
  2. Girls Hostel: Students may select a single room or a shared occupancy, depending on their preferences. There are 60 beds available.

4. Computer Center

Ranchi University's computer centre was created to provide the best resources. There are 25 computer nodes connected to the internet and INFLIBNET.

5. Sports facilities include

  • A Gymnasium with all the equipment
  • A playground.
  • One court for volleyball

6. Facilities at Ranchi University include

  • A seminar hall
  • Auditorium
  • Multipurpose room
  • Banking facilities
  • A post office
  • Book stores
  • Medical facilities

Review

1. Course Curriculum Overview

The course curriculum is almost perfect. The campus placement procedure is limited, and very few employers visit this college for campus placements. No firms visited this university for placement as a result of COVID-19. Therefore, I think this university placement is not good, but the instructors are knowledgeable, and the exams are finished on time. Faculty members are highly helpful and sympathetic to the challenges that students face. They work to adapt their teaching style so that it would better aid students in comprehending the subject. Additionally, they offer advice on how students can quickly learn and understand.

2. Remarks

Both online and offline methods are used to process admission. In the event of an offline admissions procedure, applicants must complete the online application form found on the university's official website. Candidates applying offline must come to the campus to buy and fill out the form. The institution offers admission based on a valid score on the last qualifying exam and a good score on the course-specific entrance exam.
