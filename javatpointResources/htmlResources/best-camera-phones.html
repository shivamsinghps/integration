Best Camera Phones

The best camera phones are about more than just the ability to take great photographs. They need to be intuitive and have the right features for a particular user's needs. With the rise of smartphone cameras, we've seen many great devices come to market in recent years. Some have had fantastic cameras, others have been about the selfie experience, and others have focused on combining the two.

The best camera phone does it all: it can take great photos, capture fantastic videos, and amaze your friends with its specs. To help you choose the right camera phone, we've rounded up some of the best camera phones in the world.

1. Apple iPhone 14 Pro Max

The iPhone XS Max is a powerful smartphone that can efficiently perform various tasks. This device has a dual rear and front-facing camera that can capture full HD videos and photos. The phone comes with 4K video recording capabilities and 1080p slow-motion video recording capabilities.

The Apple iPhone 14 Pro Max is one of the best camera phones in the market as it has a dual-lens camera setup with 16 megapixels and 12 megapixels. The rear lens is an f/1.8 aperture that can capture more light than other smartphones. One of the most notable features of this smartphone is its Portrait mode which makes it possible to take portraits without blurring backgrounds. It also has a Quad-LED True Tone flash for better quality images in low light conditions.

2. Apple iPhone 13 Pro Max

The Apple iPhone 13 Pro Max is an impressive device made with the latest technology, and it's now one of the best camera phones available. Apple has always been a company that takes great care to ensure its products are built to last. That's why it can be so frustrating when Apple fails to deliver on its promises in this area, as we've seen with the iPhone XR and other devices like the iPhone SE.

The iPhone 13 Pro Max is different. The front camera has an 8MP sensor, while the rear unit has an 8MP sensor with an f/1.8 aperture lens and OIS support. It's a great smartphone with a solid camera that will last years. The camera on this flagship device can shoot 4K video at 60fps and 1080p at 240fps. It also allows you to record slow-motion video at 720p at 240fps, 1080p at 480fps, or 1440p at 960fps. Other features include face tracking autofocus, optical image stabilization, 4K HDR video recording, and more.

3. Vivo X80 Pro 5G

The Vivo X80 Pro is one of the best camera phones on the market. With a superb notch display, a 10-megapixel selfie camera, and a massive battery, it's no wonder the phone has become so popular.

It has a triple rear camera setup, with one 20MP sensor, another 12MP sensor, and a third 13MP sensor. The front camera has a 10MP sensor and a front-facing selfie cam for when you want to take pictures of yourself. The Vivo X80 Pro 5G is IP68 water and dust-resistant, so it can handle being submerged in up to 1 meter of water for 30 minutes without any problems.

4. Google Pixel 7 Pro 5G

The Google Pixel 7 Pro 5G is the best camera phone you can buy right now. It has a camera that's better than the ones on most flagship phones, and it will even give the iPhone XS Max a run for its money, primarily if you use it with the latest software updates.

The Pixel 7 Pro 5G has a fantastic camera but has many other great features, like wireless charging and water resistance. You can get this phone on Amazon or directly from Google's website.

5. OnePlus 10 Pro

The OnePlus 10 Pro has the best camera in a smartphone, and it's not even close. It's got a triple camera setup with an ultra-wide-angle lens, a telephoto lens, and a regular lens that can be used for portrait or landscape shots. The OnePlus 10 Pro has all the specs you want in a phone, including 6GB of RAM and 128GB of storage. It also has a screen larger than most smartphones by about 6 inches, making it easier to see content on the screen without having to squint. If you're looking for the best camera phone, look no further than the OnePlus 10 Pro.

6. OnePlus Nord CE 2 5G

OnePlus is the only company that has managed to create a genuinely affordable flagship device while offering great specs. The OnePlus 7 Pro does this with its notch-less design and provides a fantastic camera experience in every single shot you take. The OnePlus Nord CE 2 5G is a great camera phone with a super high-end dual rear camera. It has a 16MP + 20MP sensor with f/1.7 aperture and can capture photos at up to 4K resolution.

The phone's battery life is good enough because it has a 3,700 mAh battery and supports fast charging. The best camera phones can produce high-quality photos with excellent color reproduction and sharpness. A good camera phone should handle low light situations without ruining any details or making them look blurry.

7. Vivo V25 Pro 5G

The Vivo V25 Pro 5G phone has dual rear and front-facing cameras. It also has a 64-bit octa-core processor and 6GB RAM. It supports 4K video recording at 60fps, which is great for shooting videos with slow-motion effects. Other features include AI photography, portrait mode, face unlock, and more.

The Vivo V25 Pro 5G comes in two variants: 6GB RAM/64GB storage or 8GB RAM/128GB storage. Both have an aperture of f/1.8 on the primary and secondary rear cameras, which means excellent low-light performance.

8. Samsung Galaxy S22 Ultra3

The camera on this phone is the best in its class. It has a 12MP sensor with an aperture of f/1.9, which means it can capture more light than other phones with a smaller sensor. The primary camera also has an autofocus system that allows it to focus quickly and accurately on subjects moving close to it. The rear camera also has optical image stabilization (OIS) for better low-light shooting and a 7x digital zoom feature that lets you zoom into faraway subjects without losing any detail.

The Samsung Galaxy S22 Ultra3 has several other features, including a 16MP selfie cam, 4G LTE connectivity, and a removable battery that lasts up to 1,000 hours on standby mode.

9. Google Pixel 6a

Google Pixel 6a and Pixel 6 are the smartphones that can be seen in the top best camera phones list. These phones have a dual camera setup on the back, known as the "dual-pixel zoom" feature. That feature lets you zoom into your photos while maintaining their sharpness and clarity.

The Google Pixel 6a also has a portrait mode with artificial intelligence (AI) that helps you take better selfies. The AI on this phone takes care of all the settings, such as lighting conditions, background blur, and beauty level, to get a natural-looking portrait.

Other features include the new Motion Eye mode, which lets you capture fast-moving action scenes in slow motion. It can also capture slow-motion videos at 960 fps for 1/15th of a second, which is impressive for its price range!

10. Oppo Reno 8 Pro

The Oppo Reno 8 Pro is a perfect camera phone, with a large 13 MP sensor and great software features. However, its battery life leaves something to be desired. It's not the best camera phone on the market, but it's certainly not bad either. The 13 MP camera comes with a dual LED flash and an f/2.0 aperture, while the back camera has an f/2.0 aperture as well.

The front camera has also been upgraded from 8 MP to 13 MP, which makes it better than most other smartphones in this price range. If you're looking for a quality smartphone with a good camera and software features, this is one option you should consider.

11. Xiaomi Mi 11 Ultra

Xiaomi Mi 11 Ultra is a camera phone with a dual-camera setup and an all-screen display. Xiaomi offers the device in two variants, one with 6GB RAM, 128GB storage, and Snapdragon 710 chipset and another with 8GB RAM, 512GB storage, and Snapdragon 710 chipset. The Xiaomi Mi 11 Ultra is the first smartphone from the Chinese manufacturer to feature a triple camera setup on the back. The rear cameras are arranged in a vertical configuration, so you can effectively use them to create bokeh effects on your photos.

Xiaomi has included Face Unlock technology in this phone, which uses your face as an unlock mechanism instead of having to type in your PIN or password every time you want to unlock your device. The Xiaomi Mi 11 Ultra has a 6.47-inch IPS LCD with 1080 x 2220 pixels resolution and 403 PPI pixel density. It has an aspect ratio of 18:9, which makes it slightly taller than most smartphones these days but still reasonably compact for one with such a large screen size (5.91 inches). The panel is protected by Corning Gorilla Glass 3 for added durability.

12. Realme GT 2 Pro

The Realme GT 2 Pro is a camera phone with a dual camera setup at the back and an 8-megapixel front camera. The Realme GT 2 Pro has an 18:9 aspect ratio, which means it has a taller screen than most other phones. The phone comes with a 5.8-inch screen with Full HD+ resolution (2160 x 1080 pixels) and an aspect ratio of 19:9. It also has an Octa-core processor clocked at 2GHz and works with 4GB of RAM to run apps smoothly.

The Realme GT 2 Pro also has 128GB of internal storage, which can be expanded up to 512GB via a microSD card slot. It runs on the Android 9 Pie operating system powered by MediaTek Helio P60 octa-core processor with a 3GB/4GB RAM option, respectively. The rear camera has two lenses with a 16MP + 2MP sensor combination for the front and rear shooting, respectively. It has dual rear cameras for better image quality in low light conditions and monochrome mode for black & white images.

13. IQOO Z6 Lite 5G

The Z6 Lite 5G is a new smartphone from iQOO, making smartphones since 2011. The company was founded by two former employees of Huawei, one of the leading global smartphone manufacturers. The Z6 Lite 5G comes with a 5G modem and supports all major wireless carriers, including AT&T, Sprint, T-Mobile, and Verizon. The device is available in black or gold and has a 6.2-inch display with a resolution of 2160 x 1080 pixels. It also has an 18:9 aspect ratio screen that makes it easier to watch videos on the phone without zooming in or out.

The Z6 Lite 5G runs Android 9 Pie out of the box and features dual rear cameras that can shoot up to 120fps slow motion video at 1080p or 720p resolution. The front camera can take photos up to 2160 x 1080 pixels with f/2 aperture for better low-light performance.

14. POCO X4 Pro

POCO X4 Pro is a great camera phone. It has a 6.39-inch WQHD+ (1440 x 3120 pixels) Super AMOLED display with an 18:9 aspect ratio and a 19.5:9 aspect ratio. The device has a triple rear camera setup at 20+8+6 MP with its primary sensor of Sony IMX586 and secondary sensors of Samsung S5K3M2 and Samsung S5K4E8. The front camera has an 8MP sensor with 1080p HD recording capability. The phone runs on Android 9 Pie OS out of the box with Xiaomi's MIUI 11.

15. Motorola Edge 30

The Motorola Edge 30 has a 12MP camera and a 5MP selfie camera, which is one of the best features of this phone. The camera has dual-tone LED flash and autofocus capabilities, so you can take pictures even in low light. The front camera also has an 8MP sensor that can take good selfies in lighting conditions.

The Edge 30 comes with a 5.9-inch full HD+ display with an 18:9 aspect ratio and Gorilla Glass 3 protection for durability. It also has an octa-core processor, 4GB RAM, 64GB storage capacity, and a MicroSD slot for extra memory expansion. The Motorola Edge 30 is available in black or silver variants with a fingerprint scanner on the back panel for added security. Other features include Bluetooth 4.2, Wi-Fi 802.11ac, NFC, GPS, and dual SIM functionality. This list of the best camera phones for taking photos of objects and people. There are a lot of great camera phones out on the market at the moment, so you'll be able to find one that meets your needs.
