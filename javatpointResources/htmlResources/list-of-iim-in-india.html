List of IIM in India

Nowadays management degree is also as important as engineering. For management courses, students prefer IIM colleges more than other management colleges in India. IIM means the Indian Institute of Management. IIMs are the Public Business Schools established in 1961 by the Ministry of Education, Government of India. IIMs institutions are located in 20 cities of India. The medium of teaching in the colleges is English. The institutions were declared the institutions of National Importance by the Ministry of Human Resources Development (MHRD) under the Indian Institute of Management act, 2017. The planning commission of India suggested to Jawaharlal Lal Nehru to establish IIM, and he had initiated IIM'S establishment in India.

Courses offered by IIM colleges in India

IIMs mainly offer undergraduate, postgraduate, doctoral, and executive education programmes. Some programmes offered by all IIMs are similar. However, some IIMs offer unique programmes for specialized purposes. IIM colleges in India basically offer two years diploma courses that are called PGDM. PGDM is equivalent to (MBA) Masters of Business Administration.

Procedure for getting admission in IIM colleges

Firstly, for getting admission to IIM College, we have to qualify for the CAT Common Aptitude test Examination. After that, candidates were called for the written test, (GD) Group Discussion and interview. And there, the deserving candidates are selected for admission.

What are the qualifying marks for IIM colleges?

The qualifying marks vary in the category like; for General category, the cutoff is 99-100 percentiles whereas for others reserved category its 97-98 percentiles.

Fees of IIM colleges in India

The fee structure of IIM colleges varies in India as it starts from 12 lakh, and the highest is 23 lakh. The top and old IIM colleges like IIM Ahmedabad, IIM Calcutta and IIM Bangalore have the highest 23 lakh fee for two years diploma courses, whereas the new and the youngest IIM colleges like IIM Bodh Gaya and IIM Ranchi have the lowest fee as of 12 lakh for two-year diploma courses.

National Institutional Ranking Framework (NIRF) released every year the ranking of all institutions across the country. In the ranking of NIRF 2020 total of 14 IIM colleges of India hold a place in the top 50 management and engineering colleges. But due to COVID-19, the 2021 ranking is not released yet as it is expected to be released soon. So here, I am giving the list of IIM institutions and their ranking according to NIRF 2020.

List of IIM colleges

1. IIM Ahmadabad:

Indian Institute of Management Ahmadabad ranked 1st in the survey of NIFT 2020. It is also known as (WIMWI) Well Known Institute of Management in Western India. The institute was established on 11th December 1961 in Ahmadabad, Gujarat. It is the 2nd IIM college of India. The Chairman of the institute is Kumar Mangalam Birla, and the Director is Errol D'souza. The motto of the institute is Progress through the Application of Knowledge, and the website is www.iima.ac.in.

2. IIM Bangalore:

Indian Institute of Management Bangalore, also known as IIMB, ranked 2nd in the survey. It was established in 1973, and it is the 3rd IIM college of India.
