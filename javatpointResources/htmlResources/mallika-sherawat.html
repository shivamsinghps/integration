Mallika Sherawat

Mallika Sherawat, her real name is Reena-Lamba, is an Indian actress and a model. Born on 24 October 1976 in Hisar in Haryana, she mainly works in Hindi language movies. She is a well-talented actress. Mallika is one of the few Bollywood celebrities attempting to enter Hollywood. Mallika, a beauty icon and one of the most well-liked superstars in Bollywood, is known for her outspoken on-screen personality in movies like Khwahish in 2003 and Murder in 2004. She also made a successful comedy film performance in Pyaar Ke Side Effects in 2006, for which she received high reviews from critics.

When she started her career, she said that she was from a smaller, conservative town family and had to face many difficulties from them to pursue her career in acting. Her family did not support her acting career, and later, she created her own story, after that, she married a Jet airways Pilot and her husband's name is Karan Singh Gill.

Since then, she has made appearances in blockbusters like Double Dhamaal and Aap Ka Suroor; both were released in 2007, which was her biggest commercial achievement in 2011. With movies like Hisss in 2010 and Politics of Love, she also got the chance to work in Hollywood.

Birth and Family:

Mallika was born on 24 October 1976 in the Hisar district of Haryana. But now she lives in Mumbai, Maharashtra. Mallika was born into a Jaat family. Her father's name is Mukesh Kumar Lamba, and her mother's name is Santosh Sherawat, her brother's name is Vikram Lamba.

Education Details:

Mallika completed her schooling at Delhi Public School in Mathura road of Haryana. After that, she pursued her graduation from Miranda House College of the University of Delhi (Miranda House College is one of the top-ranking colleges at Delhi University).

Marriage-Status:

In 1997, Sherawat married Delhi-based jet airways pilot Karan Singh Gill during her starting career as an air hostess. Later, she got divorced. And she hide their divorced news from the media because it would have stopped her from succeeding in Bollywood, according to the media. In 2017, she dated French property agent Cyrille Auxenfans.

Early-life:

Mallika was born in the family of Seth Chhaju Ram, a leading Jaat leader. She changed her name from Reena to "Mallika" she chose the screen name "Mallika," which means "empress.", to avoid confusion with other actresses named Reema. She used her mother's surname "Sherawat" because her mother supported her very much in her struggling face; then, she claimed that Mallika uses her mother's surname.

Mallika's family denied her decision to work in the movie industry, but when she started working in films and when her parents saw her as a good and reputed actress. Then they accepted her in their career choice and supported her.

Mallika completed her schooling at Delhi public school in Mathura road of Haryana. After that, she pursued her graduation from Miranda House College of the University of Delhi with a degree in philosophy.

Career:

She started her career acting as a Model in television commercial ads. Mallika first participated in some TV commercials with Amitabh Bachchan for BPL (an electronic manufacturing company) and Shahrukh Khan for Hyundai Santro, a car advertisement. She also participated in a music album by Nirmal Pandey and Surjit Bindrakhia. She debuted in the film Jeena Sirf Merre Liye in 2002, along with Tusshar Kapoor and Kareena Kapoor.

After the success of many movies, Mallika played her lead role in the movie Khwahish in 2003 and Murder in 2004; the audience loved the murder movie because of her acting and amazing songs. After this movie, she got a nomination for Best Actress at the Zee Cine Awards for the movie Murder and was appreciated by the audience very much. It was one of the biggest hits of the year.

Movies of Mallika Sherawat:

2002:

Mallika Sherawat started her acting career in 2002. Her first movie was Jeena Sirf Mere Liye, she played her role as Seema, and the budget of this movie was 10 crore, but this movie flopped at the box office.

2003:

In 2003 Mallika worked in the movie Khwahish, and she played the role of Lekha Khorzuvekar. The budget of this movie was around 2.5 crore, and at the box, office movie collected more than 5 crores, but this movie also flopped at the box office.

2004:

In 2004, she did another movie, Kis Kis Ki Kismet and in this movie, she played her role as Meena Madhok. The budget and box office collection information is not available.

In the same year, her another new movie Murder was released, she played her role as Simran Saigal, and this movie was a hit at the box office and very much appreciated by the audience because of the romantic songs of this movie and Emraan Hashmi also played a lead role in this movie and on box office it collected more than 25 crores on worldwide.

2005:

In 2005, she worked in Bachke Rehna re Baba (a comedy-drama movie) directed by Govind Vasantha. She played her role as Meena Madhok, and at the box office, this movie collected more than 5 crore rupees.

2006:

After so many successful movies, Mallika was offered a role in the Hollywood movie The Myth, directed by Stanley Tong, and she played her role as Samantha. The budget for this movie was around 10 crores. At the box office, this movie collected more than 20 crores. After the success of this movie, she worked in another Bollywood movie, Pyaar Kai Side Effect, and she played the role of Trisha. The budget for this movie was around 50 lakhs. The box office collection of this movie was more than 7 crores.

After the success of pyar kai side effect, she worked in another movie in the same year, Shaadi sai Pehle, and in this movie, she played her role as Sania. The budget of this movie was around 5 crore, and at the box office, this movie collected more than 16 crores worldwide.

In 2006 she also worked in a horror-drama movie Darna Zarruri Hai, and she played her role as Riya. The budget of this movie was around 2 crores, and at the box office, this movie collected more than 8 crores worldwide, but this movie flopped.

2007:

In 2007 Mallika worked in another Bollywood movie, Guru, directed by Mani Ratnam. She played her role as Champa and worked with Abhishek Bachan and Aishwarya Rai. And the budget for this movie was around 20 crores. At the box office, this movie collected more than 80 crores worldwide and became a hit on the box office. After some time in the same year, another movie, Aap ka Suroor A real love story, was launched. She played her role as Ruby James, and this movie collected more than 25 crores at the box office.

After the success of AAP ka Suroor, her other movie Welcome was released in the same year, and she played her role as Ishika along with Akshay Kumar, Anil Kapoor and Katrina Kaif. This movie collected more than 122 crores at the box office, and this movie was a super hit at the box office.

2008:

Mallika's welcome movie was a superhit at the box office. After this movie, she worked in a Telugu movie in 2008, Dasavatharam, and she played her role as Jasmin in this movie. This movie collected over 200 crores at the box office and was a super hit.

After the success of Dasavatharam, she worked in another Bollywood movie Ugly or Pagli. She played her role as Kuhumini and this movie collected more than 7 crores, but this movie flopped at the box office. In the same year, she worked in another movie Maan Gaye Mughal a Azam. It was a comedy-drama movie, and she played her role as Shabnam. This movie collected more than 25 crores worldwide.

2010:

In 2010, she came back with a Hollywood movie Hisss and she played her role as a Naagin in this movie, and this movie collected more than 30 crores at the box office but the budget of the movie is more than 28 crore and this movie flopped in Bollywood.

2011:

In 2011 she worked back-to-back in five movies. The first one was Thank-You wherein she performed her role in an item song Razia and her performance in this song was very much appreciated by the audience, after that she worked in another movie as an item girl in the movie Bin Bulaaye Baarati and she played her role as Shalu.

Later, she did Double Dhammal wherein she played her role as Kamini Nayakk along with Sanjay Dutt, and at the box office, this movie collected more than 71 crores. After some time another Hollywood movie, Politics of Love was launched, and she played her role as Aretha Gupta. This movie collected more than 1.4 lakh USD at the box. After that, she performed in a Tamil movie as an item girl in the movie Osthe.

2012:

In 2012 she worked with Ajay Devgan in the movie Tezz as an item girl Laila and after that, she worked in the movie Kismat Love Paisa Dilli wherein she played her role as Lovina and at the box office this movie collected more than 3 crores, but this movie flopped at the box office.

2015:

In 2015, she worked in the movie Dirty Politics, a crime-drama movie and she played her role as Anokhi Devi. At the box office, this movie collected more than 4 crores but flopped.

In 2016 she worked in a Chinese movie Time Raiders and she played her role as a snake empress, at the box office this movie collected more than 100 crores worldwide. In 2019, she worked in a horror web series Boo Sabki Phategi and she played her role as Haseena, a walking ghost.

Recently Mallika performed in another web series Rkay and in this series she played her role as Gulaabo/Neha.

Television Roles:

In 2005 she performed a role in the television serial Sarabhai vs Sarabhai, she played her character as Sunehri in season 1 episode 29.

In 2013, Mallika started a reality dating show platform based on The Bachelorette called Bachelorette India. Mallika finds a partner for her- Mere Khayalon ki Mallika to find the ideal man for herself.

In 2014 she performed in the American action series Hawaii Five-0 in the role of Farah Khan in season 4 of episode 24.

Activism

Sherawat has participated in women's rights activism, and in 2014, she spoke about women's issues at the 65th DPI/NGO Conference of the United Nations. Sherawat spent a full day in a cage at the 2018 Cannes Film Festival to raise money for the Free a Girl India NGO.

Awards and Nominations:

Here is the list of awards that Mallika won:

In 2004 Mallika was nominated for the zee cinema award in the category of best actress female role in the movie murder and she was also nominated for the Stardust award in the category of superstar of tomorrow in 2005.

In 2007 Mallika was nominated for the Stardust award in the category of Star of the Year Female in the movie Pyaar Kai Side Effects, and in the same year she also nominated for the Annual Central Europeans Bollywood Awards

In 2008, she won the 16th annual diversity award in the category of resistance artist award and the year 2009, she was nominated for another screen award in the category of the best screen award for the best comedian.

In 2011, Mallika won the award in the Gotham screen film festival and screenplay contest in the category of best actress in the Hollywood movie Hiss.

In 2015, she won another award at the 23rd kalakaar awards festival in the category of international youth icon award, and in the same year, she was nominated for Indian film festival Melbourne in the category of best actress in the movie dirty politics and she was also nominated for another screen award in the category of Best actress popular choice.
