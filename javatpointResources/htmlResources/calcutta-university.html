Calcutta University

In the year 1857, the city of Kolkata became home to a public state university that came to be known as the University of Calcutta. Calcutta University is another name that may refer to the institution. The University of Calcutta provides undergraduate, graduate, and doctorate students with a wide variety of educational options.

The institution has received authorization to function as a university from the University Grants Commission (UGC) and the National Assessment and Accreditation Council (NAAC). College Street in Kolkata is where you'll find the University of Calcutta.

The fields of engineering and architecture, computer application and information technology, medicine, sciences, pharmacy, and allied sciences, arts, humanities, and social sciences are only a few of Calcutta University's academic programs.

According to the National Institutional Ranking Framework (NIRF) for 2021, Calcutta University is now ranked fourth among universities. The university was given the 11th spot in the overall ranking in the NIRF 2021 competition. Additionally, the institution has achieved the 33rd position in the research category of the NIRF rating for 2021.

University of Calcutta programs

At Calcutta University, admissions are offered to various programs at various educational levels, including diploma, undergraduate, postgraduate, and doctorate. The University of Calcutta has a total of 67 distinct types of classes available for students to choose from.

The University of Calcutta provides a wide variety of academic programs, including Master in Commerce, M.sc, Bachelor of Arts in Law, Bachelor of Engineering, Bachelor of Computer Application, and more. Students may choose from a wide range of technical and non-technical professional courses that the institution offers.

The Eligibility Requirements for an Undergraduate Degree at Calcutta University

  • To be eligible for admission into BA (General), B.Sc. (General), B.A. + B.Li.Sc, or B.Sc + B.Li.Sc, applicants are required to have passed the 10+2 examination.
  • Candidates must have a 10+2 certification with English as a required subject to be considered for admission into the Bachelor of Commerce program.
  • Candidates must have passed the 10+2 examination with a chemistry grade of at least fifty percent to be considered for admission into the B.Sc. (Hons.) program in Food and Nutrition.
  • To be eligible for admission to the B.Sc. (Hons.) degree in Human Development, applicants must have completed 10+2 with a minimum aggregate GPA of 50% in the subjects of Sociology, Psychology, Education, Home Management, Physics, Chemistry, and Biology.
  • To be eligible for admission to the B.A. (H) program, applicants must have earned a cumulative grade point average (GPA) of at least 50% on their 10+2 examinations.
  • To be eligible for admission to the B.A.LLB program, applicants' cumulative scores on their 10+2 examinations must be at least 45%.

Eligibility Criteria for Postgraduate Courses at Calcutta University

  • Candidates want to take admitted into the (M.A./M.Sc) / MA/M.Sc / M.Sc (Ag.) programs require a Graduate Degree in a subject area relevant to the program.
  • To be eligible for admission into the MSc program in Chemistry or Environmental Science, applicants must first have completed and received a B.Sc. (H) degree in Chemistry with a minimum grade of 55 percent.
  • The minimum educational requirement for entry into the Master of Science in Biotechnology program is a Bachelor of Science (Honours) degree in life sciences, chemistry, physics, agriculture, biotechnology, or medicine.
  • Candidates must have at least 60 percent marks on average in Biology, Physics, Chemistry, and Mathematics or its equivalent to be considered for admission into the Master of Science in Genetics program. They must also have at least 50 percent marks in their Bachelor of Science with Honours degree.
  • To be eligible for admission into the master's degree program in Atmospheric Science or Biophysics and Molecular Biology, students must have a minimum of 60 percent overall in their 10+2 grade point average.
  • To be eligible for admission into the master's degree program in Bioinformatics and Biophysics, candidates must have scored at least a 55 percent honours grade in the subject that is relevant to the program, as well as a 10+2 grade average of at least 60 percent in each of Physics, Chemistry, Mathematics, and Biology.
  • For admission into the M.A. (Education) program, applicants must score a minimum of 40 percent in their undergraduate degree.
  • Applicants for the M.A. in French must have either a B.A. with Honours or a score of at least 45 percent on the Pre-MA exam.
  • Candidates seeking entry into the Master of Commerce program are required to have a Bachelor of Commerce degree or to have graduated from a distance education program offered by IGNOU, NSOU, or another university in the state of West Bengal.
  • For admission into LL.M, applicants must have passed B.A. L.L.B (Hons.) / B.A. L.L.B. / L.L.B (Hons.) / L.L.B.
  • To get into M.Li.Sc, you must have a B.Ed, B.Li.Sc, BA-B.Ed, B.Li.Sc, BA-B.Ed, B.Sc-B.Ed, or a degree with the same name from a recognized university with a total of 55%. OR a D.El.Ed. or D.Ed. with honours in a BA, B.Sc., or B.Com. With a total score of 55%. OR a Special Bachelor of Education degree with an overall grade average of at least 55 percent. OR Degree with a Regular MA, M.Sc., or M.Com.
  • Candidates must have a graduate degree to be considered for admission into the Integrated MLIS program.
  • To be eligible for admission into the M.Ed program, applicants must have a B.Ed and an average grade point of at least 50 in their undergraduate degree or a B.Sc-B.Ed, BA-B.Ed, or B.El.Ed with an aggregate grade point average of at least 55 percent.

Calcutta University Partnerships

The University of Turin in Italy, The University of Tokyo in Japan, Chungbuk University in Korea, The Palm Oil Research Institute in Malaysia, The Chinese University of Hongkong in China, The International Rice Research Institute in the Philippines, and The ISAS National University of Singapore in Singapore are just a few examples of the many institutions outside of India that Calcutta University has associated with.

Students at the University of Calcutta have access to comprehensive resources designed to enhance their academic experience. Students at Calcutta University (CU) are encouraged to participate in various co-curricular and extracurricular activities.

Facilities Available to Students at Calcutta University

Students at the University of Calcutta have access to facilities of world-class calibre, which enhances their educational experience. The campus is home to a library designed specifically to store manuscripts. Over the years, the library has amassed a collection of more than 42,000 manuscripts, including 20,000 manuscripts written in Sanskrit and more than 12,000 in Bengali and Tibetan combined. There are a few of these manuscripts that are written on palm leaves. In addition, the library of Calcutta University has many manuscripts written in Arabic and Persian.

In addition, the institution has 15 dorms, of which 8 are reserved exclusively for female students, and 7 are reserved exclusively for male students. The student residence halls of the University of Calcutta are kept up to date with amenities such as internet access and a medical centre. In addition to that, there are plenty of food facilities at the hostel, and the whole atmosphere is quite clean.

University of Calcutta's Digital Resources

The University of Calcutta has prioritized providing its academic community with the finest learning materials to support its teaching, learning, and research activities. To meet the long-term educational requirements of its students, the University's cutting-edge library system holds an impressive collection of books, periodicals, theses, and reports.

The University has been adopting digital technologies to make all of its resources more available to a wider audience to keep up with the rapid technological advancements. The work done by the University to preserve learning materials in digital form will increase the efficient use of resources and streamline access to information for university stakeholders.

One of Calcutta University's primary missions is to curate and organize the University's e-learning materials to enhance the quality of the online learning experience. They have made an INFLIBNET facility so that their research scholars can send in their theses to be checked for similarities and so that the academic community can maintain academic integrity.

Students can discover a variety of resources by using the Calcutta University Digital Library. These resources include online catalogue of library resources, full-text collections of theses, ebooks, e-journals, digitized resources, and many more. Students can access all of these resources by using the library's website. Their goal with this digital project is to make education more approachable, inexpensive, motivating, and entertaining for as many people as possible.

Placements at the University of Calcutta

During the annual Calcutta University placement drive, several well-known companies travel to the University from nearby cities. Calcutta University placements are open to students in technical, non-technical, professional, and non-professional programs.

Students at Calcutta University are given, first and foremost, the opportunity to participate in training sessions via the placement cell at the University. The placement cell at the University of Calcutta hosts mock tests, interviews, and group discussions and seminars.

Students at Calcutta University will have the opportunity to get industry experience and placement services thanks to the efforts of the University's Placement Cell, which is responsible for organizing Industry-Institute Interaction Programmes.

According to the placement record for the academic year 2018-19 kept by Calcutta University, out of 302 eligible students enrolled in four-year undergraduate programs, 265 received employment offers from reputable firms, while 37 students decided to pursue further education instead.

For postgraduate programs, there were a total of 5838 students who were eligible for placements; of them, 4216 received placement offer letters, while 1622 chose to pursue further education instead. The average salary provided to students for placement at Calcutta University was 6.24 lakhs for undergraduate programs that were four years long and 8.34 lakhs for graduate programs that were two years long.

The West Bengal Power Development Corp. Ltd., Satyam Computer Service Ltd., VXL Technologies Ltd., Tega Industries Ltd., Nelco Ltd., Tata Refecat, Berger Paints, Jubliant Organisation Ltd., Accenture India, etc. are just some of the notable companies that hire from Calcutta University.

Extracurricular Activities

The institution maintains a field that is now used for various sporting events. In addition to this, competitions between different colleges and universities are conducted in sports such as football, archery, basketball, and hockey. Rabindra Sarobar was the location of the very first meeting of the University's rowing club, which took place in the year 1983. The students' panel at Calcutta University is known as the Calcutta University Students' Union and is responsible for organizing various social and cultural events. These events include blood donation camps, environmental awareness programs, relief fund gatherings, teachers' day celebrations, Saraswati puja, and other similar occasions.

How to Apply for Admission to the University of Calcutta?

  • Interested candidates must submit an online application and supporting documents to be considered for admission.
  • The first thing you should do is check out the webpage of the educational institution.
  • After you have finished this, go ahead and click on the link for admission.
  • Next, you need to choose the course to which you want to be admitted and click the button that says "apply today."
  • Once this step is complete, you will be required to input every single piece of data separately.
  • After this, please be sure you upload all of the required files.
  • Applicants are required to make a financial contribution to be considered.
  • After doing so, proceed to the next step of the registration procedure: paying the required application cost. The application cost is 500 Indian Rupees (INR) for students in the general category and 170 Indian Rupees (INR) for students in the reserved categories (SC/ST).
  • Before submitting your application for admission, double-check that all of the necessary information has been included. Please hand in the admission form, which should be completed in its entirety, and any required certifications and mark sheets. On the admission status page, the current status of the application for admission will be shown. Applicants must visit the admissions site regularly to monitor the progression of their admission form
