Top 10 MBA Colleges in India

MBA stands for Master of Business Administration. It is one of the most popular postgraduate study programs not only in India but throughout the world. MBA is one of the best programs for all individuals who want to ramp up their early-career or mid-career. Another thing that attracts a large number of aspirants towards an MBA is that a person from any academic background, whether engineering, commerce, law, arts, etc., can pursue an MBA to open the doors of success in the future. The MBA program is a perfect combination of theoretical, practical, and peer learning. At present, there are 20 IIMs and more than 6,000 business schools running in the country.

The available courses, infrastructure, facilities, faculty level, etc., may vary in these various MBA institutions, but their admission process is almost similar. The process includes the five steps:

  1. Register and apply for MBA entrance exams such as CAT/GMAT/MAT/XAT/NMAT and others.
  2. Appear in the exam.
  3. Shortlisting by the college on a merit basis.
  4. Appear for the final selection round conducted by the college, which may include Group Discussion, Personal Interviews, Written Aptitude Tests, etc.
  5. In the end, the final aggregate score is calculated by including some other measures like work experience, school board, marks in graduation and 12th, etc. to select the most suitable candidates.
