University of Waterloo

It is a renowned university in Canada. According to Macleans 2022, it is the most innovative University. It has more than 100 buildings and six faculties. The University of Waterloo has 42,000 students (Full time and part-time). Four thousand five hundred students come from other countries. The cost of studying at the University of Waterloo is 25-39 lakhs, including books and other supplies. The Institution also gets funds over 64 million from Government and Industry. University funding for research stands at 247.7 million, making the University one of Canada's top and best research institutes. International students have the option to select close to 200 graduate programs and 100 undergraduate courses. Some of the most popular courses offered at the University at Waterloo are Master of Artificial Intelligence and Data Science.

History

The University of Waterloo's existence starts with Waterloo College, presently Wilfrid Laurier University. When the college's presidency was transferred to Gerald Hagey, he was determined to expand the Institution. He also made necessary arrangements to acquire the necessary funds. Provincial Government remained the main source of income for higher education in Ontario.

Administrative Functions

The University function under the bicameral system of a board of Governors and a senate as per the provision made under the University of Waterloo Act 1972. The Board of Governors is accountable for university income, affairs, and properties. The University of Waterloo act has a provision of 36 members, and the members should have Canadian citizenship. The board comprised five ex-officio members, including

  • The Mayors of Kitchener and Waterloo
  • The University's President,
  • The Chancellor.

The remaining 32 members of the board are either appointed or elected by several members of the University Community, including the student body, faculty, and Alumni. The Senate is involved in the formation of the educational policies of the University and gives recommendations to the board of governors regarding the management of the Institution. 24 Ex officio positions of the Senate include:

  • The University's President
  • Chancellor
  • The Vice- President
  • The Senior Dean of each faculty
  • The President of the Undergraduate, graduate, and Faculty associated colleges

The Board of Governors appoints the President, plays the role of the University's CEO (Chief Executive Officer), and, with Senate clearance, commands the affairs of the University and acts on behalf of the board concerning the operational control and management of the Institution. He is a member of the board and chair of the Senate. The President also assumes the position of the vice-chancellor and conducts all the duties of the vice-chancellor in his absence or at the time of vacancy. The Chancellor has a tenure of three terms and is elected by the member of the Senate, and the tenure of the Chancellor could be renewed. The primary functions of the Chancellor are to preside over the convocation functions and give candidates for honorary degrees to the Senate. Vivek Goel was declared President of the University on 17 November 2020. His term started on 1 July 2021.

The Campus of the University of Waterloo

The campus of the University of Waterloo is spread over a huge area (1000 acres), and it is situated in the city of Ontario. Wilfrid Laurier University, Laurel Creek Conservation Area, and Waterloo parks surround the campus. The campus is easily accessible via Public Transport.

  • University consists of the following:
  • Health Sciences Campus and School of Pharmacy
  • School of Architecture
  • Stratford School of Interaction Design and Business
  • A major portion of the teaching facilities is pointed on the ring road, which is situated in the Southern area. Most of the campus is new, but there are buildings of the 19th century that emits nostalgic vibes.
  • Campus meal consists of vegan, vegetarian, halal, and kosher.
  • Scores of music venues, restaurants, and café areas are available 20-minute distance from the University
  • The presence of 100 volunteer service and 200+ social, charitable, academic, cultural, political, and sports clubs exhibits that University is a site of growth and learning.
  • The institute also houses "Mike & Ophelia Lazaridis Quantum- Nano Centre", the largest centre for Quantum research, and "Earth Sciences Museum".

Admission Process

The Application process for the University of Waterloo can be explained in easy steps:

  • Create an OUAC Account online
  • Choose a program of your choice (for every program after three choices, an additional fee of 36 USD is levied)
  • Submit the final application and pay the application charge of 117 USD and, in the case of a Foreign student fee of 8 USD.
  • Remain updated with emails regarding application status.

Basic requirements for Undergraduate Admission

  • XII Grade: Minimum final grade of 85%
  • Minimum 90% in the two major subjects in the Undergraduate program
  • English Proficiency requirements:
  • IELTS: 6.5
  • TOEFL Ibt: 90
  • PTE:63
  • Duolingo: 120
