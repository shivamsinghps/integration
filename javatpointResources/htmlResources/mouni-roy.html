Mouni Roy

Mouni Roy is a graceful, gorgeous, and talented Indian actress. Along with the actress, she is a Kathak dancer, singer, and model also. She primarily works in Hindi films and television series. The nickname of Mouni Roy is Mannya and Mon. Mouni Roy is 165 centimeters tall, and her weight is 50 kilograms. Her eye color is black, and her hair color is also black. She was born on 28 Sept 1985. Her present age is 36 years. Cooch Behar is her birthplace. The Zodiac sign of Mouni Roy is Libra. She completed her schooling in Kendriya Vidyalaya in Bahurhat, and she finished her higher study in Miranda House. Hobbies of Mouni Roy are reading, dancing, traveling, painting, and sketching. Mouni Roy has a net worth of 70 crores.

About Mouni Roy family

Anil Roy is her father's name, and he was the superintendent. Her mother's name is Mukti Roy, and she is a teacher. She has a brother, and Mukhar Roy is her brother's name. On 27 Jan 2022, she was married to Suraj Nambiar. They got married at the Hilton Goa resort, and they married in a traditional South Indian manner.

Mouni Roy's television shows

She started her career as an actor in 2006.

1. Kyunki Saas Bhi Kabhi Bahu Thi:

It was her first T.V. show. She performed the role of Krishna Tulsi in this show from 2006 to 2008. Krishna Tulsi is Tulsi's adopted daughter. Where Tulsi's role was performed by Smriti Irani, she was also Eklavya's wife along with Tusli's daughter in this show.

2. Kaho Na Yaar Hai:

In this reality show, Mouni Roy participated as a contestant in episode number 5.

3. Kasturi:

She performed the supporting role in this serial. Her role name is Shivani Sabbarwal, and Shivani Sabbarwal is the sister of Robbie Sabbarwal. Karan Patel performed the role of Robbie Sabbarwal. In 2008 she worked in this serial.

4. Zara Nachke Dikha:

It was a dance reality show. In this show, Mouni Roy participated as a contestant.

5. Pati Patni Aur Who:

It was a reality show. In this show, Mouni Roy participated as a contestant with Gaurav Chopra.

6. Do Saheliyaan:

It was a daily soap. In this show, Mouni Roy performed the role of Roop. Roop was the Maithili's aunt. The role of Maithili was performed by the Sulagna Panigrahi. Her role was a supporting role in this serial.

7. Ssshhhh...Koi Hai:

It was a horror serial. In this show, Mouni Roy performed the role of Koena. She performed the role opposite Naman Shaw. Naman Shaw performed the role of Ajinkya in this show. She was the lead actress in this show.

8. Devon Ke Dev...Mahadev:

This show was her life-changing show, and she became more famous by working in this serial. She was the lead actress in this serial. She performed the role of Sati in this serial. Her co-star was Mohit Raina in this serial, and he performed the role of Shiva. In 2012 she quit the show and again returned in 2014 as Sati.

9. Junoon - Aisi Nafrat Toh Kaisa Ishq:

It was a daily soap. After quitting the Devon Ke Dev...Mahadev show she started working in this show. In this serial, she played the lead role. She performed the role of Meera in this serial. Her co-star was Aditya Redij in this serial, and he performed the role of Prithvi/ Ishwar Pandey.

10. Jhalak Dikhhla Jaa:

It was a dance reality show. In 2014 Mouni Roy participated in this show as a contestant with Punit Pathak. She was the 4th runner-up in this show.

11. Naagin 1:

It was a daily soap. She performed the role of Shivanya in this serial. Her co-star was Arjun Bijlani in this serial, and he performed the role of Sangram Singh/ Ritik/Yuvraj Singh. She performed the role of Shivanya in this show from 2015 to 2016.

12. Naagin 2:

It was a sequel to Naagin 1 . She performed the role of Shivangi / Shivanya in this serial. Her co-star was Karanvir Bohra in this serial, and he performed the role of Rocky Pratap Singh. She performed the role of Shivangi / Shivanya in this show from 2016 to 2017.

13. Lip Sing Battle:

In 2017 Mouni Roy participated in this show as a contestant. It was an Indian musical reality show.

14. Naagin 3:

In 2018 she performed the cameo role in Naagin 3. She performed the role of Mahanaagrani in season 3 of Naagin.

15. Dance India Dance Li'l Masters:

Mouni Roy started her career as an actress, and later she participated in some reality shows as a contestant, and now she is the judge of this dance reality show. Being a judge in any show is a very proud thing. This shows how much success Mouni has achieved in her career. From the actors to judge is a very difficult journey, and Mouni has done this journey in a very good way.

This is all about Mouni Roy's T.V. career.

Films of Mouni Roy

This is an animated movie. In this movie, Mouni Roy gave voice to the character of Sita.

Awards and nominations

  • Mouni Roy won the I.T.A. Award in 2016 for best actress for her work in the Naagin show.
