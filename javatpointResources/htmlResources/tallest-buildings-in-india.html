Tallest Buildings in India

The metro cities in India are famous for their infrastructure and modern age facilities and continuous development in all aspects of the growth and well-being of the people. There are many tallest buildings in different cities of India. If we talk about Mumbai, it has over 4000 high-rise buildings and comes at 6th position in terms of the highest number of skyscrapers in the world.

The list of tallest buildings in India:

  1. Omkar 1973, Mumbai (450 m)
  2. Three Sixty West Tower, Mumbai (366 m)
  3. Palais Royale, Mumbai (320 m)
  4. The 42, Kolkata (268 m)
  5. Lodha-The Park, Mumbai (268 m)
  6. Imperial Towers, Mumbai (254 m)
  7. Ahuja Towers, Mumbai (248 m)
  8. One Avighna Park, Mumbai (246 m)
  9. Crescent Bay T5, Mumbai (234 m)
  10. Auris Serenity, Mumbai (235 m)
  11. Bayview House of Hiranandani, Chennai (204 m)
  12. Raheja Revanta, Gurugram 196
  13. Supertech Nova East & Nova West, Noida (180 m)
  14. Ireo Victory Valley Tower, Gurugram (178 m)
  15. Paras Quartier, Gurugram (165 m)
  16. M3M Golf Estate, Gurugram (159 m)
  17. Mantri Pinnacle, Bengaluru (153 m)
  18. Lodha Bellezza, Hyderabad (150 m)
  19. Choice Paradise, Kochi (137 m)
  20. Gujarat International Finance Tec (GIFT) One, Ahmedabad (122 m)

1) Omkar 1973, Mumbai

Omkar 1973 is a multi-skyscraper project located in Worli Mumbai. It is 3-tower complex> by Omkar Realtors with a maximum height of 450 meters. At present, it is under construction and is supposed to get completed by 2020. However, out of three towers (A, B & C), towers A & B are complete and has 76 and 75 storeys respectively. This project is offering over 400 sky bungalows with area ranging from 2500 sq. ft. to 18,200 sq. ft.

This project with a height of around 450 meters will have 3 underground floors for parking. It is well connected by major roads such as Rajiv Gandhi Sea Link, Khan Abdul Gaffar Khan Marg, and Eastern Freeway, and Worli Road.

2) Three Sixty West Tower, Mumbai

Three Sixty West Tower is located in Worli, Mumbai. It is supported at ground level by a podium and is a mixed-use structure that comprises two towers (A & B); one will have the Ritz-Carlton Hotel and the other will have luxury residential apartments. The tower B that has over 80 floors stands at a height of 366 meters and is higher than tower A. Its construction started in 2011 by Oberoi Realty.

The towers are angled in a way that each residence can have a sea view. Besides this, it offers a wide range of lifestyle experiences such as zones for fitness, sports, kids? activities and lounging, leisure and relaxation.

3) Palais Royale, Mumbai

It is a skyscraper residential tower with 88 floors located in Lower Parel of Mumbai. It stands at a height of 320 meters and has 56 floors. At present, it has 120 apartments with lots of facilities like a spa, a cinema house, badminton court, football pitch, swimming pools, etc.

The area of the apartment ranges from 8,000 sq. ft. to 14,000 sq. ft. There are a total of 10 elevators in the building that can move with a maximum speed of 7m/sec. Its construction started in the year 2008 and may get completed by May 2020. The companies involved in its construction include Shree Ram Urban Infrastructure, Talati Panthaky Associates and Sterling Consultancy Services Pvt. Ltd.

4) The 42, Kolkata

The 42, Kolkata is a residential skyscraper in Kolkata. Its height is 268 meters that makes it the largest building in the Kolkata. It consists of 61 floors, however, four more floors will be added after a change in the rule related to the construction within a kilometre of the Metro Railway corridor. However, it has 56 flats with a triplex on the top floors.

It is located in Chowringhee road in the central business district of the city between two high-rise buildings called Jeevan Sudha and Tata Centre. The developers involved in its construction are the Mani group, and Alcove Realty & Diamond Group.

5) Lodha-The Park, Mumbai

Lodha-The Park is a skyscraper with beautiful landscapes located on the Pandurang Budhkar Marg in Lower Parel in the city of Mumbai in India. It stands at a height of around 268 meters and consists of 78 floors. It is spread over an area of 717.5 acres out of which .5 acres is used for a park.

Its land was purchased from DLF Limited for around Rs. 2800 crore in 2012. It is inspired by the famous urban parks of the world and is developed by the Lodha Group. It is offering a wide range of facilities related to comfort and security such as 24/7 power backup and water supply, car parking, firefighting systems, theatre, indoor games, banquet hall, library, sun deck, community hall, and more.

6) Imperial Towers, Mumbai

Imperial Towers Mumbai stands at a towering height of 254 metres. It is a twin residential skyscrapers situated in Tardeo, Mumbai. It was built in 2010 and has a podium on the ninth floor provided with green space facility for the residents. It is a 60 storey towers that offer luxury apartments to live and is spread over 4 acres of land.

The Architect Hafeez Contractor has designed this tower. It was constructed on the former slum land in Tardeo, South Mumbai. It is a successful project in terms of redeveloping slum and cotton mill land across the Mumbai and as well as India.

7) Ahuja Towers, Mumbai

Ahuja Towers is located at Prabhadevi in Mumbai. It is a 53 storey high-rise residence that stands at a towering height of 248 meters and offers 4BHK apartments and 6BHK penthouses starting at 17 crores onwards. Ahuja Towers offers a perfect setting to live a comfortable life in the prime location of Worli, Mumbai close to the Chhatrapati Shivaji International Airport.

Some of the major facilities provided by the Ahuja Towers to the residents are club house, children play area, indoor games, intercom, jogging track, swimming pool, gymnasium, car parking, 24/7 security and power back up, etc. It is well connected to South Mumbai and is very close to Siddhi Vinayak Temple.

8) One Avighna Park, Mumbai

One Avighna Park is located in the Lower Parel, Mumbai. It is a twin-tower complex that stands at a height of 246 meters and consists of 60 floors. This project has won seven international awards apart from 32 other national awards. Furthermore, it was pre-certified by IGBC as a platinum-rated green building.

This 61-storey tower offers luxurious residential apartments with world-class design and interiors. It was launched in June 2013. The unique features of this project include a private plunge pool in every apartment, area for sports, grand entrance lobby, and a spectacular view from the flats.

9) Auris Serenity, Mumbai

Auris Serenity is built by the Sheth Creators also known as Seth Group. It is one of the tallest and largest residential skyscrapers in Mumbai, India. This residential complex is spread over 5.73 acres of land and stands at a height of 235 meters. However, it is under construction at present. The possession will be offered in December 2021 as per the builders.

Auris Serenity comprises four towers with 62 floors offering apartments with sea-view including rejuvenating ambience and other comforts of modern times. The top facilities provided by the project include restaurants, high-speed elevators, waiting lounge, swimming pool, steam room, skating rink, squash court.

10) Crescent Bay T5, Mumbai

Crescent Bay is a residential project in Parel, Mumbai. It is developed by L&T Realty in collaboration with Omkar. It is spread over 5.5 acres and is a gated complex of six residential towers with a maximum height of 233 meters. There are around 60 floors in a tower and 1282 apartments in the whole township.

The configuration of the apartments is 2, 3 and 4BHK and the area of the apartment ranges from 63 sq. metre to 250 sq. metre. The project offers all basic and modern facilities for a peaceful, comfortable and modern lifestyle. Some of the major amenities offered by Crescent Bay include swimming pool, play area, indoor games, badminton court, restaurant, party hall, landscaped garden, rain water harvesting, jogging and cycling track, and more.

11) Bayview House of Hiranandani, Chennai

It is a skyscraper residential complex located off Old Mahabalipuram Road, Egattur, Chennai. It is a 40 storeyed residential tower that stands at a height of 204 meters and is believed to be the tallest building of South India. It is a blend of style and comfort. Each apartment has marble or wooden laminated flooring and vitrified tiles in other parts of the apartment. Along with all modern times facilities, a puja room is also provided in the apartments.

From the apartments, you can get the view of backwaters, sea or Bay of Bengal. This residential high-rise complex was launched in April 2012. The amenities provided by the project include high-speed elevators, gymnasium, table tennis, lawn tennis, salon, squash, play area for children, lawn tennis, aerobic room, badminton, and billiards and more.

12) Raheja Revanta, Gurugram

Raheja Revanta is one of the tallest residential towers located in the sec-78 in Gurugram. Its height is 196 meters and is built over 18.39 Acres of land by the famous real estate company Raheja Developers. It has over 700 apartments each of which offers the view of scenic Aravallis ranges from the balconies. It is very close to various renowned buildings such as 5-star hotels, shopping malls, and golf courses and Indira Gandhi International Airport.

The major world-class facilities offered by Raheja Revanta, Gurugram include world-class clubhouse, and fitness centre offering Jacuzzi, Steam Bath, Spa, Sauna, and more. Other services include mini theatre, firefighting system, valet parking, smoke alarms, and more.

13) Supertech Nova East & Nova West, Noida

Supertech Nova East and West are two residential towers that stand adjacent to each other and are located in Sector 94 of Noida. Its height is 180 meters and is built by the famous Supertech group and is spread over 17.5 acres. Both the towers have spectacular features and offer a mesmerizing view of the city and have their own separate entrance lobbies. Each apartment has full-width balconies and full-size terrace at the penthouse levels. Its 2 and 3 BHK flats range from 1320 sq. ft. to 2040 sq. ft. in size.

These towers are designed with the support of London based architects, Benoy. As of now, it is under construction and the possession will start from December 2020. The highlight and amenities provided by these residential towers include a view of the bird sanctuary, high-speed elevators, club house, fitness centre, health club, piped gas, jogging track, badminton court, kids water park, etc., and is close to 8 lane Kalindi Kunj expressway.

14) Ireo Victory Valley Tower, Gurugram

Ireo Victory Valley is skyscraper residential complex located in the Sector 67 of Gurgaon. It is offered by Ireo Developers and consists of 51 floors. The maximum height of this township is 178 meters. It has a total of 30 towers spread over an area of 25 acres and houses around 700 residential apartments. This township is a blend of excellent architectural design and features and facilities to provide a comfortable living.

The township offers 2BHK, 3BHK, 4BHK and 5BHK flats. Some of the top facilities provided by the Ireo Victory Valley Tower include swimming pool, reading lounge, golf course, conference room, bar/chill-out lounge, salon, football ground, banquet hall, gymnasium, and more.

15) Paras Quartier, Gurugram

Paras Quartier is a super luxurious residential complex located on the Gwal Pahari Road in Gurugram. It is spread over 10.5 acres and comprises two high-rise residential towers and a clubhouse. The height of Paras Quartier is around 165 meters and is designed by WOW Architects Warner Wong Design and landscaping is done by Cracknell.

It offers 4 BHK ultra-premium residence provided with separate lift for each apartment. The built-up area of the apartment is 5350 sq. ft. and is provided with world-class interiors and modern times design aesthetics. The price of the apartment starts at 7.8 crores. It also offers a panoramic view of the city and green landscapes to the residents.

The major amenities offered by Paras Quartier include rooftop swimming pool, state of the art clubhouse, community hall, jogging track, badminton court, indoor games, high-speed elevators, aerobics and yoga room, 24 hr security and power backup, and more.

16) M3M Golf Estate, Gurugram

M3M Golf Estate is a residential township that is located on Golf Course Extension Road in Sector 65 Gurgaon. It is spread over 50 acres of land and is designed by Arcop International, Montreal Canada. The towers stand at a height of 159 meters and are supported by the 8.5-meter high podium.

The configurations of the apartment are 2,3, 4 and 5 BHK and the price of the apartment ranges from 5 crores to 10 crores. The apartments are spacious with open balconies offering the view of a 9-hole executive golf course. Premium tiles, imported marble, POP punning with Acrylic emulsion paint have been used inside the apartments.

The features and amenities of this project include roof top jogging track, large and open balconies, world-class club house, multi-purpose hall, drainage and sewage treatment, 24 hr power back up, water supply, security, tennis court, cafeteria, fire safety system, health facilities, and play area for kids, and more.

17) Mantri Pinnacle, Bengaluru

Mantri Pinnacle is a high-rise residential complex located at Banerghatta Road in the Bengaluru city of India. It consists of 46 floors that contain 133 apartments of different types such as 3, 4 and 5 BHK apartments. Each apartment has modern times design and marble flooring and other features to assure comfortable living.

The height of Mantri Pinnacle, Bengaluru is 153 metres. There is a sky lounge on the 41st floor, an observatory on the 42nd floor and the facility of a helipad on the 46th floor.

Some of the major features and amenities provided by the Mantri Pinnacle include gated community, indoor games, landscaped garden, library, club house, cafeteria, tennis court, rain water harvesting, meditation hall, play area, gymnasium, and more.

18) Lodha Bellezza, Hyderabad

Lodha Bellezza is another high-rise residential complex that stands at a height of around 150 metres. It is offered by the famous builder and developer, Lodha Group. It is located at Kukatpally, Hyderabad and offers 3 and 4 BHK sky villas with area ranging from 3447 to 4761 sq. ft. and a beautiful view of the city. It has a total of nine blocks that contain 362 villas. Each villa offers a private pool, home theatre lounge and a private elevator that takes you directly into your apartment.

It offers all architectural features and facilities that help you in living a comfortable modern life. The external amenities offered by this project include swimming pool, tennis court, play area, gymnasium, party hall, 24/7 power backup, library, and more.

19) Choice Paradise, Kochi

Choice Paradise is the tallest residential tower in Kochi as well as in Kerala. It is a 36 storey skyscraper located in Tripunithura. Its height is 137 metres and is spread over 2.5 lakh sq. ft. and has 132 residential apartments. The total cost of this project was around 80 crores. The project was commissioned by the famous south Indian actor Mohanlal in 2012.

According to the MD of the Choice Group, this complex was designed to withstand earthquakes of intensity 7.2 in the Richter scale. The construction of this luxury residential tower was completed in four years.

It offers world-class amities such as a helipad, high-speed elevators, state of the art security system, and various other facilities like party hall, club house, play area, gymnasium, power backup, and more.

20) Gujarat International Finance Tec (GIFT) One, Ahmedabad

GIFT city is located on the banks of the Sabarmati River and is 12 km far from the Ahmedabad International Airport. This project was promoted by the Govt. of Gujarat as a Greenfield project. It comprises offices space, residential apartments, office spaces, schools, hotels, clubs, and more. It is based on the concept of "Walk to Work" City.

The GIFT One is a part of Gujarat International Finance & Tec (GIFT) City. It is a 28-storey world-class commercial tower that is constructed as a business landmark that is offering strategically located office and commercial spaces with world-class facilities. The height of GIFT One tower is 122 metres that makes it the tallest building in Gujarat.
