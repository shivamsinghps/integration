Best Hollywood Movies

Like there are many film industries in India; similarly, there are many film industries in other countries too, but there are hardly any famous film industries like Hollywood in the whole world. Thousands of movies are released yearly in Hollywood, and many people like watching or are fans of Hollywood movies. Some people like to watch Hollywood movies so much that they like to watch any famous or best movies of Hollywood again and again. Hollywood has always been winning hearts with its best movies without disappointing audiences, and because of this, everyone likes to watch Hollywood movies. Today in this article, we will talk about some best Hollywood movies to watch.

1. The Shawshank Redemption

IMDB Rating: 9.3/10

Movie Cast: Tim Robbins, Gil Bellows, James Whitmore, Bob Gunton, William Sadler, Morgan Freeman, Clancy Brown and many more.

Directed By: Frank Darabont

Original Language: English

Release Dates: September 10 (TIFF), September 23(United States), 1994

Country: United States

It is an American drama movie and is regarded as one of the best films ever made. This film, which is based on Stephen King's novella Rita Hayworth and the Shawshank Redemption, was released in theatres in the year 1994. This film follows a guy who is given a life sentence for killing his wife. If you want to know more about this man and what he does after, you must watch this amazing film.

2. 12 Angary Man

IMDB Rating: 9 /10

Movie Cast: E.G. Marshall, Henry Fonda, Lee J. Cobb, Ed Begley, Jack Warden, and many more.

Directed By: Sidney Lumet.

Original Language: English

Release Date: April 10, 1957 (Fox Wilshire Theatre).

Country: United States

Even more than 50 years after it was released in 1957, 12 Angry Men remains one of the greatest films ever made. One of the best courtroom drama movies is this one. The producers of this American courtroom drama movie are Henry Fonda and Reginald Rose. The teleplay by Reginald Rose with the title Twelve Angry Men served as the inspiration for this film, as did the title of the film. This film centres on a youngster who is charged with murdering his father. In order to determine whether the boy was guilty or not of this crime, the court assembled a team of 12 investigators to investigate this case.

3. Titanic

IMDB Rating: 7.9/10

Movie Cast: Leonardo DiCaprio, Jonathan Hyde, Danny Nucci, David Warner, Kate Winslet, Billy Zane, Kathy Bates, Frances Fisher, Bernard Hill, Bill Paxton, and many more.

Directed By: James Cameron

Original Language: English

Release Dates:

  • November 1, 1997 (Tokyo)
  • December 19, 1997 (United States)
  • December 20, 1997 (South Africa)

Country: United States

American romance drama film Titanic was released in 1997. If you enjoy watching movies, you must have seen Titanic at least once, and if you haven't, you probably have heard about it. This film is among the top choices for you if you enjoy watching romantic movies. The main lead actor and actresses in this film are Leonardo DiCaprio and Kate Winslet. The main characters of this film are a guy and a girl who fall in love on a ship. Do not miss this masterpiece if you enjoy romance movies.

4. The Dark Knight

IMDB Rating: 9/10

Movie Cast: Morgan Freeman, Christian Bale, Michael Caine, Heath Ledger, Gary Oldman, Aaron Eckhart, Maggie Gyllenhaal, and many more.

Directed By: Christopher Nolan

IMDB Rating: 9/10

Movie Cast: Morgan Freeman, Christian Bale, Michael Caine, Heath Ledger, Gary Oldman, Aaron Eckhart, Maggie Gyllenhaal, and many more.

Directed By: Christopher Nolan

Original Language: English

Release Dates:

  • July 14, 2008 (New York City)
  • July 18, 2008 (United States)
  • July 25, 2008 (United Kingdom)

Country: The United States and the United Kingdom.

The Dark Knight is a movie you should watch if you enjoy action and suspense. It was released in 2008, and it's a superhero movie. Upon the superhero Batman this film is based. Batman Begins, which came out in 2005, has a sequel called The Dark Knight. Batman tries to put an end to crime in his city in this film, but the Joker, a master criminal, escapes after each crime he commits. If Batman captures the Joker or not must be seen in the film. It would not be incorrect to state that the Joker is superior to Batman in this film, which received excellent accolades for both the hero and the antagonist's acting. In this film, you will witness some fantastic action.

5. Inception

IMDB Rating: 7.6/10

Movie Cast: Tom Hardy, Leonardo DiCaprio, Marion Cotillard, Elliot Page, Ken Watanabe, Joseph Gordon-Levitt, Cillian Murphy, Tom Berenger, Michael Caine, and many more.

Directed By: Christopher Nolan

Original Language: English

Release Dates:

  • July 8, 2010 (Odeon Leicester Square)
  • July 16, 2010 (the United States and the United Kingdom)

Country: The United States and the United Kingdom

Each year, Hollywood produces many films, some of which are ranked among the all-time greatest pictures, including the movie Inception. It is a science fiction action film that was released in the year 2010. In the movie, Leonardo DiCaprio plays the part of a criminal who steals data or information by penetrating his targets' subconsciouses.

6. Avengers: Endgame

IMDB Rating: 8.4/10

Movie Cast: Don Cheadle, Paul Rudd, Brie Larson, Karen Gillan, Danai Gurira, Benedict Wong, Jon Favreau, Bradley Cooper, Robert Downey Jr., Chris Evans, Mark Ruffalo, Chris Hemsworth, Scarlett Johansson, Jeremy Renner, Gwyneth Paltrow, Josh Brolin, and many more.

Directed By: Anthony Russo and Joe Russo

Original Language: English

Release Dates:

  • April 22, 2019 (Los Angeles Convention Centre)
  • April 26, 2019 (United States)

Country: United States

Hollywood has produced many films, but few of them can compare to Avengers: Endgame in terms of quality. Young people enjoyed this film. The Marvel Comics superhero squad known as the Avengers is the inspiration for the 2019 movie Avengers: Endgame. If you enjoy action movies a lot, this is probably the action movie where you'll see the best action. All of the superheroes unite together in this movie to fight Thanos, who wants to wipe out half of all life on Earth. If Superhero can stop Thanos from doing this or not, you'll have to see the movie to find out.

7. The Lord of the Rings

IMDB Rating: 8.8/10, 8.8/10, 9/10

Movie Cast: Andy Serkis, Elijah Wood, Ian McKellen, Liv Tyler, John Rhys-Davies, Christopher Lee, Billy Boyd, Dominic Monaghan, Orlando Bloom, Hugo Weaving, Viggo Mortensen, Sean Astin, Cate Blanchett, Sean Bean, and many more.

Directed By: Peter Jackson

Original Language: English

Release Dates: 2001-2003

Country:

  • New Zealand
  • United States

There have been many series movies produced to date, but The Lord of the Rings is one of the best. Three movies in a series, released in 2001, 2002, and 2003, make up this one. The Fellowship of the Ring is the first instalment in The Lord of the Rings film series, followed by The Two Towers and The Return of the King. There are many movie lovers that enjoy this epic fantasy adventure series. Start watching this movie series right away if you haven't already.

8. Avatar

IMDB Rating: 7.8/10

Movie Cast: Sam Worthington, Michelle Rodriguez, Zoe Saldana, Stephen Lang, Sigourney Weaver, and many more.

Directed By: James Cameron

Original Language: English

Release Dates:

  • December 10, 2009 (London)
  • December 18, 2009 (United States)

Country: United States

In recent years, many movies have been produced and released, but some of them have gained such a following that viewers enjoy re-watching them. One such movie is Avatar. This 2009 epic science fiction picture continues to be popular even today. Despite being released long ago, the film's popularity has not diminished. In this movie, someone is sent on a covert mission to another planet to do a task. What is the task, and will the person succeed in completing it? To understand this, you must watch the film.

This is a must-see film that you will want to see again and over again. This is also a film series, with three films already released. The second film in this series was released in 1974, followed by the third in 1990. These films earned numerous honours, and the acting of the star cast in these films is outstanding.

10. Forrest Gump

IMDB Rating: 8.8 /10

11. The Empire Strikes Back(Star Wars: Episode V)
