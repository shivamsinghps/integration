Java Rain Resorts

Are you searching for the best resort near Bangalore? Then Java Rain Resort is the right choice for you. It is a new luxury vacation destination surrounded by India's original coffee hills. Java Rain Resort is one of the best resorts in Chikmagalur. Located on Murayangiri Hill in the midst of a lively coffee plantation, Java rain offers stunning comfort and spectacular views of the Baba Budan Hills on one side while on the other side there are breath-taking panoramic views of the city of Chikmagalur. The resort has a total of 20 villas. Java rain is covered with fog and lush greenery on all sides, which makes it the best destination to come close to nature and spend your vacation in its arms. The resort is at a distance of about 4 and a half-hour by car from Bangalore and 3 and a half-hour by car from Mangalore. This oasis of natural beauty and elegant luxury makes Java rain one of India's top luxury resorts. That's why it is a perfect weekend getaway for families and nature lovers. Java rain is a symbol of relaxation and rejuvenation with world-class amenities and professional staff which all is focused to give you a memorable experience.

Java Rain is dedicated to providing the love of luxury and leisure and was built up by a couple named Shubha and Shiv. The resort is spread in the area of 100 feet where the couple gives their best to impart high-quality hospitability services.

Location

The resort is located in Girija Estates Survey No.: 618, Mullayanagiri Road (next to NMDC), Chikmagalur 577101, Karnataka, India. The city is about 243.2 km far from Bangalore, the capital of the state.

How to Reach?

  • By Air
    The nearest airport to the city is Mangalore Airport located in Mangalore. It is 113 km away from Chikmagalur. You can get well-connected flights from this airport to all the major cities of the country like Delhi, Mumbai, Kolkata, Chennai, and others. You can hire a taxi from here to reach Java Rain Resort. Other than Mangalore Airport, the airports of Bangalore and Hubli are also located near Chikmagalur.
  • By Rail
    The city does not have a railway station. The nearest railway station to Chikmagalur is located in Kadur which is about 40 km away from the city. There is a number of taxis that run regularly from the Kadur railway station. So, you can take a taxi from here to reach Chikmagalur. Other nearby railway stations to the city are Birur and Terikere.
  • By Road
    Chikmagalur has a well-connected road network with cities like Bangalore, Hubli, Mangalore, Hassan, Kadur, Sringeri, Birur, Koppa, and others. There is proper service of state-run as well as private luxury buses in the city. Two national highways also pass through the city.

Services Offered by Java Rain Resorts

As discussed above, Java Rain Resort is very famous for its top-class services. The services that make it different from other resorts located in Chikmagalur are given below:

1. Villas

There are a variety of villas available, including Single, Twin, and Presidential Suites. Each villa is spread over a total area of 1,250 square feet. These Villas were designed by Cadence architects with input from the directors on a regular basis. The Villas are beautifully designed with a butterfly roof idea, textured walls, and furnished patios and viewing decks so that you can enjoy the panoramic vistas. The paintings and interiors in the Villas were all hand-picked in Indonesia.

Types of Villas Available

  • Kaffa

    Kaffa got its name from a former province in Ethiopia's western region, where coffee originated. Kaffa is suitable for a modest family with children because it has a spacious bedroom with an attached living area and an open shower. The terrace area of the villa has a beautiful view of the estate. But the open shower with a pleasant view of the open sky is the prime factor that makes this single villa most special and unique.
  • Qahva

    The name Qahva is derived from the Arabs' strongest drink. These villas are meant for close family and friends. It is also the best honeymoon villa for those couples who want to be close but also need privacy. There is a private single and double Jacuzzi in each villa.
  • Fika
    Fika, or coffee break in Swedish, is a perfect villa for a large family or a group of 6 to 8 people. The Presidential Suite has three bedrooms: one with a private pool, living space, and bedroom, another with a jacuzzi, and a third with a great view over Chikmagalur town, living area, and bedroom. The Jacuzzi villa stands out because it includes a large living room and dining area. Fika is ideal for a getaway in the middle of a coffee plantation so you can witness the greenery and aroma of coffee here.

2. Shvah Spa

  • Relaxing Massages
    Various relaxing massages offered by the spa are aromatherapy, Swedish, Indian deep tissue, and foot reflexology. The cost of these massages is around Rs. 4,000 for 60 minutes.
  • Teaser Massages
    Various teaser massages available at the spa are Indian head, spritz back, and spritz foot. The charges of these massages are around Rs. 2,000 for 30 minutes.
  • Face Care
    Deep cleansing, nourishing moisturizing, skin balancing, sensitive skin, and Java Rain Signature are various face cares that are offered by the Shvah Spa. The costs of these cares are Rs. 2,000 for 60 minutes except for Java Rain Signature which costs Rs. 2,500 for 90 minutes.
  • Scrubs and Wraps
    Organic bath salt, Marine clay wrap, aloe vera wrap, and honey & flower wrap are various scrubs and wraps that you can take at the spa. The cost of these scrubs and wraps is Rs. 1,500 for 30 minutes.

3. Dining

  • Restaurant
    Mirra is a mystical and sumptuous name derived from a well-known Turkish bitter coffee. It is an all-day restaurant with views of the infinity pool and lush green valleys, as its name suggests. In the evenings, outdoor seating provides the greatest view.
    Here, you can enjoy the delicious bakery and confectionery food items and international cuisine. The local authentic cuisine, brimming with fresh flavors and the commitment to authenticity, is also a specialty of the restaurant.
    Mirra does their best to accommodate the preferences of all the customers. Here, you can also opt for a BBQ for special events and can request group reservations for corporate dinners. The restaurant has a team of in-house chefs who have over 20 years of combined expertise in the hospitality industry, including cruise lines, flight catering, and hotels & resorts.
  • Rooftop Bar
    The name of the rooftop bar of Java Rain Resort is Crimson Peak which is derived from the name of a mountain located in the United States. This meticulously crafted tree bar is made at a height of 50 feet which gives an amazing experience to the customers. The best panoramic view of the Chikmagalur town and the Mullayanagiri range can be seen from this bar. With water pouring down the walls and vast lounging areas, the unusual architecture allows you to spend hours marveling at the beauty while sipping a delicious drink or reading a beautiful novel. At the bar, you can also enjoy handcrafted cocktails and appetizers. Crimson Peak is called the town's sunset point and it gives a 360-degree panorama of the magnificent mountain range. The villas are only a short distance away from here.

4. Activities

There are so many adventurous activities at Java Rain that you can experience. You can enjoy these activities inside the resort or outside the resort amid the gorgeous mountains which are the neighbors of Java Rain.

Some indoor activities are relaxing on the pool, going on a hike, playing a round of golf, archery, and others, or simply wandering through the lushly landscaped grounds. The resort encourages you to let your imagination run wild and unleash your madness.

Various outdoor activities that you can try include the following:

  • Kemmanagundi Trek

    Trekking is an exciting activity for individuals who want to enjoy a long drive. It is at a distance of about 60 kilometers from Chikmagalur and it takes about two hours to get there. Kemmanagundi is the part of Bhadra Wildlife Sanctuary, which is notable for its small waterfalls along the road.
  • Spice and Coffee Plantation

    You can also go for a tour of spice and coffee plantations. As you stroll inside the Baba Budangiri and discover the lush, verdant, and stunningly magnificent coffee-producing landscape of Karnataka's Western Ghats, you will be able to witness the coffee brewing process. The fragrance of spices and coffee can't be compared with any other thing.
  • Cycling

    You can take the adventure of cycling around the property's gorgeous pathways with a professional guide. You can hire the cycles from the resorts for you and your guests. You can choose the activities as per your level of adventure. So, you should follow your heart's desire and hear the call of adventure.

Places to Visit near the Resort

Chikmagalur is not just famous for trekking but there are also several most beautiful and known temples and dargahs. The city is the home of mountains, coffee, and verdant landscapes. Various tourist destinations located in and around the city are given below:

  • Mullayanagiri Peak
    The height of Mullayanagiri Peak is 1,925 m which makes it the highest peak in Karnataka. The peak is located in the Chandra Dhorna Hill Ranges. It is also the highest peak in the Krishna River basin. The peak is well known for its small temples and the photogenic scene of the city and mountains. Mullayanagiri is located at a distance of 28 minutes from the resorts.
  • Baba Budan Giri
    Baba Budan Giri is located at a distance of 58 minutes from the resorts. The place is also known as Dattatray Peeta. Budan Giri mountain range is located in the Western Ghats of India. The place is well known for its shrine to a pilgrimage site for Hindus. This peak is the part of Chandradrona Parvatha Shreni. You can also try trekking from Baba Budan Giri to Mullayanagiri.
  • Hebbe Waterfalls
    The waterfalls are located at a distance of 1 hr 42 min from the resorts and about 10 Km away from the beautiful station Kemmangudi. This waterfall is surrounded by a coffee estate. The waterfalls gush from the height of 551 ft and are divided into two stages named Dobba Hebbe and Chikka Hebbe. You can try trekking and can taste leeches here. Monsoon is the best time to visit the place.
  • Honnammana Halla Waterfalls
    The waterfalls are located at a distance of 24 minutes from the resorts. It is a small waterfall located on the way to Baba Budan Giri from Chikmagalur. This artificial waterfall is covered by concrete structures which make it safe for everyone. Honnammana Halla Waterfalls are also known as Manikyadhara Falls. It is one of the best places to take a rest from driving.
  • Hoysala Temples
    These temples are located in the way of Bangalore to Chikmagalur. Hoysala is a collection of temples that were constructed between the 11th and 13th centuries. It mainly includes the Belur (35km), Halebidu (51km), and Somanathapura. Also, you should not miss visiting Belavadi Temple of this group.
  • Other Places
    Some other beautiful tourist destinations located near the hotel are Mahatma Gandhi Park, Hirekolale Lake, Coffee Museum, Seethalayanagiri Parking Lot, Mutthodi Safari, Jhari Waterfalls, Deviramma Betta, Sitalayyanagari Temple, Galikere, Shri Guru Dattatreya Swamy Dattapita, Shravanabelagola, Sringeri Sharda Peetha, Amrithapura, Hornadu Annapurneshwari Temple, Kundremukh Hills, Bhadra Wildlife Sanctuary, Kadambi Falls, Shanti Falls, Manikyadhara Falls, and others. You can also enjoy some activities to unleash your free spirit and heed the call of adventure by cycling tour with a professional guide, tour of spice and coffee plantation, and trekking at Kemmanagundi.
