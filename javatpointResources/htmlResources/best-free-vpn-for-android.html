Best free VPN for Android

A Virtual Private Network (VPN) is an essential tool for internet users. It diverts your data via a secure tunnel to its server and encrypts it before sending it to the website you are visiting. A VPN changes your actual IP address with a virtual one and lets you access web content anonymously. For example, if you live in the US and accessing the website using the VPN server location of Australia, the website will show that you are accessing the website from Australia. A VPN keeps your data and internet network secure that no one can track your online activity.

However, there is some hidden downside of using a free VPS service that may include data leaks and may sell your data to third parties for more income.

Some of the free VPN we discuss here that claim to protect your data, secure your connection, and strictly keep no-logs activity.

List of some free and best VPN for Android

Hotspot Shield VPN

Hotspot Shield VPN app is available in both free and paid versions that fulfill your online privacy. Its free version offers 500MB of free data per day. Once you install and enable this VPN app on your device, your data and internet connection becomes secure. Hotspot Shield VPN uses military-grade 256-bit encryption and provides a no-logs policy. Its free version contains ads and offers a limited server location to connect. You can upgrade to its premium service to get great performance, including unlimited data and 115+ servers located across 80+ countries. You can check its premium service trial for a 7-day and 45-day money-back guarantee.

Download the Hotspot Shield VPN for Android.

Windscribe

Windscribe is a completely openhanded free VPN that offers 10GB of data per month for those users who share their email addresses with the service provider. If a user doesn't share their email address, they get 2GB of data per month. Users can even get more 5 GB of data per month as a bonus by tweeting the company.

Windscribe VPN uses military-grade encryption to secure your online activity and keep your data private and safe from hacking and location tracking. It includes a built-in ad and malware blocker to avoid disturbance while surfing web content. It also claims for a no-logs policy that means VPN providers don't collect or share user's information when users are connected to the server.

You can manually choose a server to connect from 20 different servers from 10 countries. Upgrading to its premium version offers more features, including more server locations from more countries.

Download the Windscribe VPN for Android.

TunnelBear VPN

TunnelBear is a free VPN app for Android that offers 500MB of data per month. Its user interface is attractive and simple to use. It allows choosing servers from 23 countries globally, including the US, Australia, India, and lots more.

TunnelBear uses a 256-bit encryption technique to secure your internet traffic and your personal data. It also keeps no user logs so that no one can track user's online activity. This app also bypasses block apps and websites.

If you need more than 500MB of data per month, subscribe to its premium version starting from the price of $3.33 per month and get unlimited data and 5 simultaneous connections. However, it doesn't give a money-back guarantee like other paid VPN services.

Download the TunnelBear VPN for Android.

NordVPN

NordVPN provides security to your online activity, protects your privacy, and secures your traffic with few clicks. It allows connecting six devices simultaneously with a single account. Once you connect with this VPN service, no one can see what you are accessing on your browser. App's advanced security feature blocks displaying the suspicious website, malware, and annoying ads on your device.

NordVPN uses 256-bit encryption technology that keeps your connection safe from hackers. It operates 5500+ server locations across 58 countries of the world. It claims a strict no-log policy, which means they can't collect your data and not share it with anyone. You can even upgrade to a premium version with a risk-free 30-day money-back guarantee.

Download the NordVPN for Android.

Kaspersky VPN Secure Connection

Kaspersky Secure Connection VPN is a free VPN app that acts as an alternative to the proxy server. It allows you to enjoy web content privately and securely on your Android device. It provides 200MB of data per day or 300MB for connected Kaspersky account users. The app automatically gets launched when a public Wi-Fi hotspot network is detected. It hides the device's actual IP address with a virtual IP address and protects all your data and internet connection with powerful encryption technology. Kaspersky VPN lets you access geo-restricted TV shows and blocked websites. You can also go for its premium version with a 7-day free trial.

Download the Kaspersky VPN Secure Connection for Android.

Speedify - Fast & Reliable VPN

Speedify's VPN app for Android offers 2GB of free data per month. It allows establishing a connection across more than 1,000 servers in over 50 countries of the world. This VPN uses 128 GCM encryption technologies, which is weaker than 256-bit encryption, but it is still secure throughout the connection time. The app establishes a highly secure connection so that one can see your personal and sensitive information. As the app runs in the background, it gives security to data transmission through your other apps.

Speedify VPN is easy to install; simply download the app, connect, and use. If 2GB of data per month is not sufficient for you, it facilitates paid plans to access unlimited data. Users can upgrade their plan to get unlimited access at $9.99 per month or $71.99 per year.

Download the Speedify - Fast & Reliable VPN from Google Play.

Proton VPN

Proton VPN is one of the best VPN for Android or other supported devices as it offers unlimited free data and bandwidth. It allows you to access web content and unblock websites without disclosing your identity to anyone or a service provider. Proton VPN encrypts your internet connection to protect your privacy. It uses high-level security, including 256-bit encryption technology, DNS leak protection, and an automatic kill switch. It uses Swiss-based protection to protect your data, one of the world's strongest privacy laws.

The free version of Proton VPN grants access to the server of three countries, including Japan, the US, and the Netherlands. Upgrading the app to its premium version allows access to servers in over 54 countries. Users can try its premium version with a 30-day money-back guarantee.

Download the Proton VPN for Android.

Hide.me VPN

Hide.me is an Android VPN app that helps in privacy and security while surfing the web. It provides 10 GB of data access free of cost per month. Free users will enjoy five server locations, including the US (eastern and western coast), Canada, Netherlands, and Singapore. If users require more data and server locations to access, they may go for its paid subscription. Paid subscribers will get more than 50+ server locations. Hide.me also promises that they can't keep user logs, never store user data, and even sell any data to third-parties agencies. The app is easy to set up and start; it protects your online identity and activity. It also facilitates to stream live videos anywhere in the world and download files anonymously.

Download the Hide.me VPN app for Android.

PrivateVPN

PrivateVPN is another Android app that allows accessing any websites anonymously. They claim that their server is located worldwide, and they facilitate uninterrupted connection for your smooth workflow. PrivateVPN can't keep any user logs so that no one can track your location and online activity. The app provides the feature to connect upto six different devices simultaneously, each with a unique IP address. It uses the highest encryption level, 2048-bit encryption with AES-256, to protect from IPv6/DNS leak and fraud attacks. Some of its key features include: protects user location tracking, high-speed servers in more than 60 countries, allows connecting public Wi-Fi hotspot, etc.

Download the PrivateVPN app for Android.

Opera Browser with VPN

Opera browser is a fast and safe web browser for Android devices that comes with a built-in free VPN. Users have to turn on Opera VPN in the settings, and the device's actual IP address gets replaced with a virtual address. It improves user privacy and security. Opera VPN uses three server locations, including Asia, US, and Europe. This application comes with a native ad blocker that avoids annoying ads and its built-in QR & barcode scanner, accessible from search bar that scan and read QR code.

Other features

  • Provides trending viral videos and short clips.
  • Easier payments with an autofill credit card.
  • Manage downloads easily.
  • Offers night mode.

Download Opera browser with VPN for Android.

Surfshark VPN

Surfshark VPN offers a fast and secure connection to access web content. It makes your device's location private and keeps your browsing activity secure and protected. This VPN service has more than 3200 servers across 65+ countries to provide worldwide coverage. Its kill-switch feature automatically disconnects your internet if the VPN service disconnect. This VPN app hides your actual IP address and sets a virtual one. Surfshark VPN uses powerful end-to-end data encryption technology that makes users safe even in a public Wi-Fi hotspot.

Surfshark VPN gives seven days free trial, and when the free trial over, they will charge you if you want to continue the VPN service. Surfshark also gives a money-back guarantee if you are unsatisfied with their paid VPN service during the first 30 days.

Download the Surfshark VPN for Android.

IPVanish VPN

IPVanish VPN app automatically hides your device's IP address and encrypts internet connection to keep you protected from third-parties onlookers and trackers. It has more than 1400 VPN servers across 75 locations in the world. Launch the app and select one of the locations for your new IP address. Once you get connected to the IPVanish VPN server, your online activities get protected, such as location tracking and stealing your information. IPVanish commits that they never keep any logs activity as a record. It also allows accessing popular websites without local restrictions. You can also go for their paid subscription to get more features and 24/7 customer support. IPVanish VPN offers a 30-day money-back guarantee if you are unsatisfied with their paid service.

Download IPVanish VPN for Android.

CyberGhost VPN

CyberGhost VPN hides your device's IP address and provides absolute privacy to enjoy web content. It encrypts internet connection and keeps you safe online. The app keeps you safe even connected with public Wi-Fi hotspot. The VPN routes your traffic through an encrypted VPN tunnel. CyberGhost VPN never records any user's logs so that no one can track the user's activities. It has more than 7000 VPN servers located across 90 countries in the world.

Other features

  • It gives instant protection within a single tap. The app is user-friendly, containing excellent features.
  • Allow accessing web content from across the world tracking free. It has more than 7000 VPN servers located in 90 countries.

Download the CyberGhost VPN for Android.

Avira Phantom VPN

Avira Phantom VPN is a free app that secures your data, web surfing, etc., without displaying your identity. It offers 500MB of data per month for unregistered users, which is quite less than other VPN service providers in this list. It also unblocks the geo-restricted website for TV and video streaming. Avira Phantom VPN uses the military level of encryption technology to secure your data VPN routes your traffic through a secure and private VPN tunnel.

You can connect to 38 VPN server locations in Asia, Europe, America, Russia, North America, Russia, and many other countries. To use this VPN app, launch it, choose a location, and turn on the VPN to anonymously start web surfing without sharing personal data with the service provider.

You can also get the seven-day free trial premium version with unlimited traffic. After the trial period gets finished, use up to 500MB traffic per month as unregistered or register their premium service for unlimited traffic.

Download the Avira Phantom VPN for Android.

Betternet VPN

Betternet VPN Android app hides your IP address with a virtual one, encrypt your internet traffic and turn your public Wi-Fi network connection to private. Its free plan gives 500 MB of data per user. The app allows accessing web content (included restricted) and unblocks websites and apps anonymously on your Android device. The app not stores any user logs and activity for the record.

Betternet VPN hides your IP address, bypasses the internet network, and allows you to access geo-restricted web content. Using this app, a user doesn't need to install any incognito browser; just turn on the VPN, and all your internet traffic gets encrypted. The app uses a 256-bit encryption technique. However, it doesn't include an automatic kill switch feature.

It also offers a premium version with unlimited data. You can try the Betternet VPN premium feature for a 7-day free trial, but there is no money-back guarantee.
