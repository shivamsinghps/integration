Best Honeymoon Places in India

India is a diverse nation, not only in terms of tradition & culture but also in terms of geography. From North to South, the country is filled with beautiful places to explore and visit. Whether you are a newlywed couple or just a pair looking for a breath of fresh air from your daily lives, your needs are fulfilled with these beautiful and memorable places in India, which are highly recommended to visit for your honeymoon. A honeymoon will be a trip that both you and your partner are going to remember for a long time, so it is especially important to pick a good location.

This list is here just to make that task easier with the help of the finest handpicked locations. All these are great places to explore and make some memories for the rest of your life. Each of them has its own unique landscapes, geographical features, and climates. So, pick the one that suits you the most.

1. Ladakh, Jammu & Kashmir

Ladakh is the one and only cold desert in India. Choosing Ladakh means you also get a chance to see Pangong Lake, which is the world's highest saltwater lake. Not only is Ladakh an amazing place for couples, but it also gives you an opportunity to get a little spice of adventure with your partner, which is sure to make up for a delightful memory. You can do many tourist activities here with your partners, like biking, rafting, safari, and many more. And if that's not enough, Ladakh is also home to many beautiful landscapes and offers you its exclusive Ladakhi and Tibetan food.

2. Srinagar, Jammu & Kashmir

Another splendid honeymoon location from J&K. You get to enjoy beautiful gardens and shimmering lakes around you, not to mention the snow-topped hills, which make for a stunning view. Srinagar almost feels like a true paradise. The landscapes are perfect, the air always sets everyone in a good mood, and the weather is perfect. To top of all, there are a bunch of activities you can do during your stay, such as camping, skiing, rafting, trekking, and many more. You cannot forget Srinagar once you have visited it, even if you tried. It is a heavenly experience for the couple.

3. Goa

If you love the sea, Goa is definitely the perfect place for you. It is filled with commercial beaches for you to explore. Goa also brings in its unique experience of scuba diving. The scenery itself in Goa is always shining beautifully with the water around you. Safe to say, it makes up for one of the best places filled with beautiful sunrise and especially sunset spots. The seafood from Goa is nothing to sneeze at either, and it brings a taste to your mouth you might not have had before. It is not just beaches that account for a great experience in Goa, and the place also houses many nightclubs which you can visit with your partner.

4. Andaman & Nicobar Islands

If you have already been to the beaches of Goa, and are willing to try a more refreshing beach experience, then look no further than Andaman & Nicobar Islands. There are about 300 islands that can be explored. You will find yourself staring at the ocean, which seems to be meeting the clouds. And during sunsets, the scene gets even better, it almost feels like heaven, and the air gets as romantic as it can be. Unlike Goa, you also get to experience some adventurous experiences here. You can board a ferry, go swimming, do sea karting, and do other similar water activities, plus an experience of rich vegetation as well.

5. Coorg, Karnataka

Coorg is a little town in Karnataka that has earned the title "Scotland of India". Unlike some other places, where it can be crowded or noisy at times, Coorg serves as a perfect spot for couples with its quiet and soothing aura along with splendid sceneries to capture in your hearts. The smell of its coffee and fresh greenery makes the mood even better. Coorg is well-known for serving as a beautiful place for couples to visit and enjoy their time in a solitary environment, allowing people's feelings to bloom.

6. Nainital, Uttarakhand

Nainital is very well-loved by everyone for being one of the best hill stations for couples to visit. Whether your eyes are looking for the pretties of lakes, ancient temples, or splendid mountains, Nainital has it all. The climate is no less than ideal for making up for a great experience. Nainital Lake, Naini Peak, and Tiffin Top are only a few parts of its package. Being a hill station, it has some of the most jaw-dropping sceneries one could ask for. You can also go boating here, which is an experience like no other in the beautiful landscapes and mountains of Nainital.

7. Shimla, Himachal Pradesh

Shimla attracts new couples and honeymooners like a magnet. The reasons are that not only does it provides a delightful time, but it is also rather affordable for a honeymoon destination compared to some of the other options. Shimla is called "Queen of Hill Stations", and that is for a good reason. The gorgeous sceneries, mountains, and white snow are all unforgettable. Shimla is flooded with tourist spots to explore - The Ridge of Shimla, Green Valley, Summer Hill, Himalayan Bird Park, and Kuthar Fort, just to name a few. Shimla is a perfect hill station that offers both adventurous flavors with trekking and skiing and a good-old romantic destination with its tourist spots, activities, and sceneries.

8. Jaisalmer, Rajasthan

Perfect for those who don't want beaches or hill stations. Jaisalmer is one of the best spots for those not interested in adventures and who prefer dealing with only hearty affairs on their honeymoon. This place is better than any other for letting your feelings blossom. It is also referred to as the "Golden City". It is situated right at the heart of the Thar desert. Jaisalmer is all about beauty, quality time, delicious cuisines, and arts. Not to mention, there are Forts you don't want to miss exploring with your partner. If you go to this Golden City, you are sure to make some of the sweetest and heartwarming memories of your life.

9. Lakshadweep Islands

Time for another beach location. Lakshadweep is not like most other beaches you might've heard of or visited yourself. Lakshadweep Islands serves as a home to more spacious and low-crowded beaches, unlike Goa, where beaches are the 24/7 business. Going here means that you will also be staying at beach cottages instead of regular hotels, which you do not get the chance to do very often. The islands' beaches also give you a different view. The sand around you, the trees, and the vegetation all have a unique coloration to them. The beaches have earned the name "Emerald beaches".

10. Chikmagalur, Karnataka

The moment you witness the spectacular scenery of Chikmagalur and its environment, you will feel like a dream. This is the place where, once you look at the hilltops, it seems as if they are meeting to be one with the clouds in the sky and unforgettable sight to behold. On top of that, you get the green surroundings which only add to the beauty and the aroma of fresh air all around you. Chikmagalur has been a perfect place for newlyweds on a honeymoon as well as married couples on their vacations.

11. Cherrapunji, Meghalaya

You might not know this place as being one for honeymoons. While it indeed is more popular as a tourist and travel spot, it certainly is a memorable and remarkable honeymoon spot as well. It has one of the best exploring environments for couples to have a great time. Greenery, soothing environment, fresh air, open spaces, beautiful waterfall - you get to enjoy all of it in Cherrapunji. It is definitely one of the underrated and hidden gems for honeymoon trips.

12. Udaipur, Rajasthan

Another amazing honeymoon destination is Rajasthan. It lies in the hills, where it gets panoramic scenery like no other. This place is also called the "White City". Especially during the winters, the climate, the surroundings, and the eye candy of this place are much more enjoyable. You can go and do a variety of tourist activities here, just like most other hills and honeymoon locations. Udaipur is one city you need to visit. It is a popular honeymoon spot and a major tourist spot as well, and that is for a reason.

13. Kovalam, Kerela

Kovalam is a town in Kerela which is full of beautiful beaches. Needless to say, the seafood you get to enjoy is just something else. Combine that with the Ayurvedic massages, which this place is known best for. And it is not just any massage; the stress relief, relaxation, and pampering that you receive from any Ayurvedic Centre in Kovalam make for a spectacular vacation. This place also lies close to the Arabian Sea, and it is blessed with the most relaxing and enjoyable climate. There is nothing better than exploring the beach all day and then receiving an ayurvedic massage in Kovalam.

14. Manali, Himachal Pradesh

Manali stands as one of the most visited places in India. It is a mighty fine hill station known for its sceneries with snow-top peaks and the availability of activities to do in leisure. You can also go to explore Hindu Temples, beautiful waterfalls, and monasteries. Rafting in Manali and camping out in the breathtaking beauty of its surroundings during the night along with the sky of stars are two well-known experiences. Since it is a hill station, you also get access to some of the more common activities, such as skiing, trekking, or paragliding.

15. Bandhavgarh, Madhya Pradesh

Bandhavgarh will add a unique experience to your honeymoon. You would still get to enjoy the pretty landscapes and sceneries and do activities that you would be doing at other places, but in addition to that, you also get to explore the National Park full of wild species and nature's beauty like no other place. What makes the experience even better is the resorts, all of which follow nature's theme and relay the sensation of being close to nature while having the comfort of your own home.

16. Jim Corbett National Park, Uttarakhand

If you and your better half love wildlife, there is no question about your honeymoon trip. The Jim Corbett National Park boasts an extremely wide variety of species from both flora and fauna living in it. It will definitely be a site to behold. This place is best suited for couples who like their trips to be more adventurous and thrilling. This place makes sure that your honeymoon is an eventful one with activities like wildlife scouting, jungle safaris or camping.

17. Alleppey, Kerela

Alleppey is known as the 'Venice of the East. It is home to most of Kerela's backwaters. What makes it a great honeymoon destination is the experience it provides. Just imagine sitting in a comfy houseboat with your partner with water on every side during a sunset. That is one of the most delightful and romantic sights for couples. You also get to explore a 17th-century lighthouse here. The houseboats themselves will have all the features you could ask for in a comfortable and good hotel room. You can also go to watch boat racing here which can be a unique experience for many.

18. Gulmarg, Jammu & Kashmir

Gulmarg is not just any hill station you see every day. If you want to enjoy the prime ski resort, look no further than Gulmarg. The place is titled "Meadow of Flowers" and sits at over 2700m of elevation. The view of its valleys and hills is superior to other hill stations that you can visit, especially with the pines enhancing the beauty. It is a given that skiing is one of its top activities, but the ride to the top of Mt Afarwat is no less enjoyable, not to mention that you will also be able to witness the Nanga Parbat on your way.

19. Wayanad, Kerela

The best honeymoon trip you could get from Kerela. The greenery of its lush green vegetation and the peaceful environment just makes it a great place to enjoy yourselves at. The green landscapes are stunning, to say the least, and all your surroundings make you feel soothed. This place is filled with tourist spots and resorts for tourists as well as couples. You also get to explore Asia's second largest earth dam Banasura Sagar Dam here. The geography of the land is perfect for peaceful walks as well as activities such as hiking, trekking, climbing, camping, etc.

20. Kabini, Karnataka

This place is not only a great honeymoon destination but also an abode of rare species which you most likely have not witnessed before. This river also serves as a lush green encampment of nature. The river itself and the Kabini dam are a sight to be held. You can also opt to explore the Nagarhole National Park and do activities like jungle safari, coracle rides, camping, etc. This place is amazing for peaceful and nature-loving couples due to its pretty sceneries and, at the same time, is amazing for pairs that are active due to its adventurous and thrilling opportunities available.
