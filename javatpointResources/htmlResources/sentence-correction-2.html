Sentence Correction

6) Because of his mastery in yoga, his yoga academy have grown in popularity.

  1. were growing
  2. is grow
  3. is growing
  4. has growing

Answer: C

Explanation: Because of his mastery in yoga, his yoga academy is growing in popularity.

7) Young men and women should get habit to waking up early in the morning.

  1. used
  2. trained
  3. prepared
  4. practice

Answer: A

Explanation: Young men and women should get used to waking up early in the morning.

8) The small girl does whatever her mother was done.

  1. has did
  2. do
  3. had done
  4. does

Answer: D

Explanation: The small girl does whatever her mother does.

9) Maria's fans were all shocked at her failure in the Olympics.

  1. shocked by
  2. had shocked all at
  3. were shocked at all
  4. None of the above

Answer: D

Explanation: no correction required

10) The population of China is higher than that of all other country in the world.

  1. higher than all other
  2. higher than all other
  3. greatest than all
  4. higher than that of any other

Answer: D

Explanation: The population of China is higher than that of any other country in the world.
