Best Fiverr Alternatives

What is Fiverr

Fiverr is a web-based platform that offers freelance services. This platform is designed for freelancers to offer digital services to customers all over the world. The jobs offered by this site are related to writing, translation, audio editing, digital marketing, video editing etc.

This site was founded by Micha Kaufman and Shai Wininger and launched in February 2010.

Alternatives of Fiverr

There are some alternatives of Fiverr along with their features are given below:

1. Upwork

Upwork is a web portal that provides freelance jobs to freelancers. This platform offers jobs related to data science, networking, accounting, admin work, translation, and many more.

This platform offers a wide range of services and a long-lasting reputation that makes it a tough competitor of Fiverr. You can send unlimited offers to the customers, and once the relationship with the customer starts, you will be charged 20% of the total amount.

To visit the official website of Upwork, click on the given link: https://www.upwork.com/.

2. Freelancer

Freelancer is a crowdsourcing marketplace web-based platform that allows users to post those jobs and freelancers place bids to complete those jobs. The platform provides freelance jobs in almost all fields such as web designing, web development, content writing, digital marketing, translation, and more. This site charge 10% of the total amount, but this can be reduced by purchasing a monthly subscription plan of $5.

To visit the official website of Freelancer, click on the given link: https://www.freelancer.com/.

3. Guru

Guru is a freelance website that allows the user to find freelancers to complete their work. This site was developed in 1999 in San Francisco. This site provides jobs related to finance, marketing, engineering, administrative, etc. The site allows users to contact potential employers. Every day a large number of jobs related to many fields are posted on this site. This site reduces 5% to 9% of the total amount after completion.

To visit the official website of the Guru, click on the given link: https://www.guru.com/.

4. Servicescape

Servicescape is a freelance website that works with startups and SMBs. This site provides jobs related to translation, graphic, designing, writing, and many more. The Freelancer can interact with the client in many ways, such as by making a phone call, or sending a message, etc.

To visit the official website of Servicescape, click on the given link: https://www.servicescape.com/.

5. PeoplePerHour

PeoplePerHour is a web-based platform that provides jobs to people who are interested in freelancing work. This site helps the freelancers to connect to the clients for freelancing jobs.

To visit the official website of the PeoplePerHour, click on the given link: https://www.peopleperhour.com/.

6. Hubstaff Talent

Hubstaff Talent is a tough competitor for Fiverr. This platform allows the user to do both full-time as well as part-time work. It offers jobs in almost every field. The Freelancer needs to fill in the details, skills, and availability, and you will be notified through email when someone wants their work to be done by you.

To visit the official website of Hubstaff Talent, click on the given link: https://talent.hubstaff.com/.

7. Toptal

Toptal is a web-based platform that is designed for freelancers. The jobs provided by this platform like finance experts, project managers, healthcare, e-commerce, banking, and many more. Some reputed companies hire freelancers from Toptal to get their work done.

To visit the official site of the Toptal, click on the given link: https://www.toptal.com/.

8. CloudPeeps

CloudPeeps is a web-based platform that provides jobs to freelancers in marketing, copywriting, social media, email marketing, etc. This site is very similar to Fiverr. There are thousands of professionals who do a freelance job at this platform.

To visit the official website of the CloudPeeps, click on the given link: https://www.cloudpeeps.com/.

9. Truelancer

Truelancer is a web-based platform that provides work and freelance jobs to freelancers. The jobs offered by this platform are work from home jobs, virtual assistance jobs, marketing trainer jobs, etc. Freelancers can easily earn money by doing these jobs. It can be accessed on the browser, Android, and iPhone devices.

To visit the official website of the Truelancer, click on the given link: https://www.truelancer.com/.

10. Flexjobs

Flexjobs is an alternative to Fiverr that provides jobs to freelancers. The user needs to sign-up first and then create a profile to check job availabilities. This site gives an email alert when any employers post a job.

To visit the official website of Flexjobs, click on the given link: https://www.flexjobs.com/.

11. Zeerk

This platform allows employers to post jobs of several categories. These jobs are related to website reviews, writing, posting articles or blogs, data entry, sales promotion, and many more. This software comes with a transaction and conversation management system that allows the freelancers to give feedback to the freelancers.

To visit the official website of the Zeerk, click on the given link: https://zeerk.com.

12. Codeable.io

Codeable.io is a platform that provides freelance jobs related to WordPress. This platform searches the jobs based on your profile details. Whether the employer wants to fix an error or need a custom plugin developed, this site provides developers who can do all types of work. The site also offers a full refund policy if the employer does not like the work. Employers and freelancers can communicate via email and phone calls.

To visit the official site of Codeable.io, click on the given link: www.codeable.io

13. SEOClerks

SEOClerks is a platform for those who want to expand their search engine traffic and grow their search visibility and website sale. This platform provides a number of jobs related to SEO (Software Engine Optimization). This platform is specially designed for freelancers who are good at SEO and can get jobs related to Link building services, Search engine traffic, SEO, On site-related services, Off-page services, and so on. This will charge a 20% commission fee from every successful project.

To visit the official site of the SEOClerks, click on the given link: https://a.seoclerks.com/linkin/799310.

14. Twine

This platform provides millions of freelance jobs in almost every field. The freelancers need to search for jobs according to their skillset. This platform provides jobs related to graphic designing, copywriting, web development, music, video, animation, etc. The best thing about Twine is that freelancers do not need to pay any fees. This platform charges commission fees from employers.

To visit the official site of the Twine, click on the given link: https://www.twine.fm/signup.

15. Freee

Freee is a new competitor of the fiver, who hires the best professionals from all over the world. The user can get potential jobs and can make more and more money by working as a freelancer. This provides the jobs in almost 85 different skills from various categories at an hourly and fixed rate.

This site offers jobs related to Web Development, Admin Support, Accounting, Translation, Advertising, etc.

Freelancers receive their payments weekly (every Thursday) directly into their bank account, set up on the free.

To visit the official site of the freeup, click on the given link: https://freeup.net/.
