(14 - y) - y = 4

10 = 2y

⇒ y = 5

x - 5 = 4

  2. The larger of two supplementary angles exceeds the smaller by 18 degrees. Find them.

  4. The taxi charges in a city consist of a fixed charge together with the charge for the distance covered. The difference of x and y is given to be 26 and x is 3 times that of y, therefore

x - y = 26

x = 3y

3y - y = 26

2y = 26

⇒y = 13

x = 3 × 13

⇒x = 39

Hence, the smaller number is 13 and the larger number is 39.

2y = 162

x = 81+18

⇒x = 99

Hence, the smaller angle is 81 degrees and the larger angle is 99 degrees.
