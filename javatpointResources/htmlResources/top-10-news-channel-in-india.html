Top 10 News Channel In India

Top 10 news channels in India help us stay ahead and updated with the fast moving world. There are numerous incidents happening each second. With so much happening around it becomes extremely imperative to stay up-to date and abreast with all the information, incidents and world events.

In today's world after print media, new channels are the major means of updating the public and society with the latest and important information. Every home has a TV set, so news channels become the most easy and assured means of providing information to the audience and keeping them update with the current events, world affairs, politics and entertainment news, thereby making the list of top 10 news channels in India a hit for gathering and disseminating the information.

These days viewers spend their crucial time in watching the news on these channels, so that they can stay updated, this has compelled us to make a list of top 10 news channels in India that have upped their game and have also brought forth a lot of programmes, serious and shows to keep the viewers engaged while providing all the information and minute to minute updates through programmes like quick headlines, top 100 thereby providing the viewers with all the world events in time span of 30 minutes.

New Channels these days are trying their best to provide the most quick and updated information, there has also been rise in the talk shows, debates, entertainment news- including Bollywood news etc. All this had gathered the attention of the masses irrespective of their age group and gender.

Everyone today trusts and watches the news channel to stay up to date.

Questionable journalism has also become a part of news channels these days that has helped them to gain trust and value of their viewers.

However, some news channels just hype up the event, and audiences being intelligent understand and quickly recognise the authentic news channels vs the not so genuine news channels.

So as long as the news channel is providing genuine, authentic and true information it remains as one of the top news channels amongst the viewers, this is a crucial attribute of a channel that makes it one of the top 10 news channel in India.

List of top 10 news channels in India

So let us explore some of the top news channels in India.

1. Times Now

Number one on the list of top 10 news channel in India is The Times Group. The group owns and operates The Times Now, an English language news channel. The channel debuted on January 23, 2006, in collaboration with Reuters. It is a paid television service available across India. It was India's leading and most-watched English news channel till 2016. Since 2016, when Arnab Goswami (the previous editor-in-chief) exited the channel, it has consistently ranked the second most viewed English news channel in India, trailing only the Goswami-edited Republic TV, with the provison that the latter has larger terrestrial coverage.

Over the years this channel has enjoyed enormous success due to its hardwork and efforts.

Times Now, like other Times Group channels (Zoom, ET Now, and Movies Now), is broadcast by Media Network and Distribution (India) Ltd (MNDIL), a cumulative effort amongst The Times Group and cable and satellite industry expert Yogesh Radhakrishnan, under label Prime Connect.

However, the channel has many controversies and many law suits against it.

2. ABP Network Pvt Ltd (ABP)

Number two on the list of top 10 news channel in India is ABP Network Pvt Ltd (ABP). It was established in 1998. Its headquarters are located in Noida, India. In India, the ABP Network is incredibly popular. In various languages, the news channel provides news, entertainment, and digital content. ABP Network is one of India's top 10 news channel India.

ABP News is a Hindi news network in India run by the ABP Group. It is a free-to-air television station that debuted in 1998. Prior to getting purchased by ABP Group, it was called as STAR News. ABP Live is an English news platform in India run by ABP Network. This channel is also available to View on YouTube under the title ABP News. This channel has been enjoying success since a long time due to its crisp and authentic news presented by various anchors in the most refined way.

3. India Today

Next on the list of top 10 news channel in India is India Today. This is a 24x7 television channel headquartered in Noida, Uttar Pradesh, that broadcasts news, recent events, and business programmes throughout India. It is an English language channel. TV Today Network Ltd, a subsidiary of Living Media, owns the channel. The station debuted in 2003 as a sister channel to the Hindi media outlet Aaj Tak. This is amongst the four news stations owned by the TV Today Network, along with other channels like Aaj Tak, Tez, and Delhi Aaj Tak(this has been closed in 2020). Alok Verma was hired as Executive Producer to help TV Today Group effectively make its debut into the English news channel market. In September 2015, media viewers reported the station for unethical exploitation of recordings and sexist material.

4. Aajtak

Next on the list of top 10 news channel in India is Aaj Tak. It is a popular Indian channel. It is a renowned Hindi-language news channel operated by TV Today Network, a division of the New Delhi-based Living Media company. It is one of India's earliest Hindi news stations. Aaj Tak is India's most popular Hindi news station, as well as the most followed Indian news channel on YouTube. Aaj Tak first aired on Doordarshan's DD Metro channel in the year 1995. This was later aired as a 10- to 20-minute news programme. This channel started as an individualistic news channel in December 1999, and it eventually became the nation's earliest 24X7 news channel in the Hindi language. Surendra Pratap Singh was one of the presenters at the moment.

Aaj Tak's tagline was "Yeh thi news aaj tak, Intzar kijiye kal tak" which was translated as That's all for today; stay tuned for more tomorrow. Aaj Tak was nation's initial news channel to deploy OB vans.

5. CNN News 18

Number 5 on list of top 10 news channel in India is CNN-News18. It is a Contemporary Indian news television channel located in Noida, Uttar Pradesh, India, initiated by Raghav Bahl. Network 18 and WarnerMedia presently own it. CNN supplies the station with international coverage, whereas Indian Broadcasting Network focuses on Indian and local news. Reliance Industries announced the acquisition of Network18 in May 2014. The transaction was billed as "the biggest-ever deal in the Indian media market." Reliance Industries always had an indirect ownership of the TV18 network due to investments it made in Network18 beginning in January 2012.

CNN International only impacted India's urban elite. To reach the Indian public, Time Warner founded CNN-IBN on December 18, 2005, in collaboration with a Local company, Global Broadcast News (now TV18 Broadcast Limited). The station was entirely owned and operated by TV18 Broadcast Limited, which only utilised the Cable News Network (CNN) popular brand title.

6. India TV

India TV is next on list of top 10 news channel in India. It is an India's popular Hindi news station situated in Noida, Uttar Pradesh. Rajat Sharma and his wife Ritu Dhawan founded the channel on May 20, 2004. The station is a division of Independent News Service, that Sharma and Dhawan co-founded in 1997. They founded Independent News Service (INS), the main business of India TV, in 1997.

In April 2004, he and his wife co-founded India TV from a facility in Film City, Noida. Its Broadcast Center is located in Sector 85, Noida, Uttar Pradesh, India. Rajat Sharma earlier presented Aap Ki Adalat on Zee News and other program that he hosted was Janata Ki Adalat on Star News before founding India TV.

Aap Ki Adalat, India's oldest continuously telecasted television program, it will celebrate its 28th anniversary in 2021.

7. NDTV India

Next on the list of top 10 news channel in India is NDTV India. New Delhi Television Limited is the owner of NDTV India, a Hindi language news channel in India. In June 2016, NDTV planned to start two individual stations in the United Kingdom which were named as NDTV India and NDTV Spice.

Prannoy Roy, is the chairperson of the firm, and Radhika Roy, his spouse and managing director, formulated NDTV.

NDTV created the news and current affairs programme The World This Week for Doordarshan in 1988. The programme was liked, and NDTV established itself as a private news provider and producer. It went on to become the main provider of news content and producer for country's premier 24x7 news channel, Star News. They started two 24-hour news stations, NDTV 24x7 in English and another channel was named as NDTV India in Hindi, on April 15, 2003.

8. BBC World

On number eight in the list of top 10 news channel in India is BBC World News. This is one of nation's top ten news networks. BBC Worldwide India Pvt Ltd has over 81 million people watching globally, making it one of the remarkable English News Channels. Since then, the channel's audience has grown to incorporate individuals from all over the globe. It transmits on radio, television, and online, delivering news and information in a variety of languages, as well as world service English. The corporation provides nationwide television services that include entertainment, news, current events, and the arts.

9. Zee News

Next on the list of top 10 news channel in India is Zee News. It debuted in the year 1999. Its head office is based in Noida, Uttar Pradesh. It is one of India's most popular news stations. It has sister channels such as Zee Business and some other like Zee 24 Taas, 24 Ghanta, Zee Kalinga, and so on. It runs a number of news and local entertainment stations in several Indian states. Essel Group is its parent firm. It is one of India's top ten news channels.

The Essel Group owns Zee News, an Indian Hindi-language news station. It is affiliated with a number of other sister news networks that provide coverage in Hindi, English, but several regional Indian languages. Zee News was established on August 27, 1999, as Zee Sports Ltd. On May 27, 2004, the organization was reincorporated as Zee News Ltd. Zee News Ltd was renamed Zee Media Corporation Limited in 2013. The Zee News channel is the organization's corporate channel.

Subhash Chandra, a Rajya Sabha member supported by the Bharatiya Janata Party, serves as the company's chairman. Sudhir Chaudhary is the editor-in-chief of the most respected and loved new channel, Zee News and the host of the channel's news program.

10. DD News

Next on the list of top 10 news channel in India is DD News. It was established in 2003. The station is available in a variety of languages, namely Hindi, English, Sanskrit, and Urdu. DD National, DD India, DD Bharti, and DD Sports are its sister networks. Arun Jaitley introduced the mobile application for Android users as well as Apple users on May 7, 2015.

This is one of India's top ten news stations. DD News is part of prominent Doordarshan news channel in India. This channel is nation's earliest 25x7 terrestrial TV news program that broadcasts only in Hindi language, and earlier it was in English language. The Prasar Bharati Board of Directors authorised the idea to start a 24x7 news channel in lieu of the defunct DD Metro. DD Metro was transformed into a 24-hour TV news station, DD News, which debuted in 2008.
