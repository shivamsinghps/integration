What is coronavirus?

The word Corona means "Crown." As their structure resembles a Crown under the electron microscopy, they were named as Corona Virus. They have numerous spikes glycoprotein on their envelope, which gives them this crown-like structure. Their genetic material comprises of a Positive-stranded RNA.

Initially thought as a Hoax and a conspiracy by the Chinese authorities and now described as a worldwide pandemic by WHO with no vaccine or any FDA approved Medication. Due to the adverse effect of this Virus, the World Economy is dwindling down, half of the world is complete lockdown, COVID-19 is a virus strain first identified in the meat and seafood market of Wuhan, China in December 2019.

In this world of modern technologies, very little is known about this Virus. What we have known so far is that this Virus is infectious, which spreads by direct or indirect contact from the contagious host, and Its victim growth is in an upward trajectory and no sign of slowing down.

Coronavirus belongs to the family: Coronaviridae.

Subfamily: Orthocoronavirinae.

Order: Nidovirales.

They are classified under four genera of Corona Virus:

  • Alpha coronavirus (alphaCoV).
  • Beta coronavirus (betaCoV).
  • Delta coronavirus (deltaCoV).
  • Gamma coronavirus (gammaCoV).

Genomic characterization has shown that probably bats and rodents are the gene sources of alphaCoVs and betaCoVs.

Avian species seem to represent the gene sources of deltaCoVs and gammaCoVs.

Members of this large family of viruses can cause respiratory, enteric, hepatic, and neurological diseases in different animal species, including camels, cattle, cats, and bats.

In Humans:

1. Common human CoVs:

HCoV-OC43, and HCoV-HKU1 (betaCoVs of the A lineage); HCoV-229E, and HCoV-NL63 (alphaCoVs). They can cause common colds and self-limiting upper respiratory infections in immunocompetent individuals. In immunocompromised subjects and the elderly, lower respiratory tract infections can occur.

2. Other human CoVs:

SARS-CoV, SARS-CoV-2, and MERS-CoV (betaCoVs of the B and C lineage, respectively). These cause epidemics with variable clinical severity featuring respiratory and extra-respiratory manifestations. Concerning SARS-CoV, MERS-CoV, the mortality rates are up to 10% and 35%, respectively. The genome of the new HCoV (Covid-19), isolated from a cluster-patient with atypical pneumonia after visiting Wuhan, had 89% nucleotide identity with bat SARS-like-CoVZXC21 and 82% with that of human SARS-CoV.

For this reason, the new virus was called SARS-CoV-2. Its single-stranded RNA genome contains 29891 nucleotides, encoding for 9990 amino acids. Although its origins are not entirely understood, these genomic analyses suggest that SARS-CoV-2 probably evolved from a strain found in bats. The potential amplifying mammalian host, intermediate between bats and humans, is, however, not known. Since the mutation in the original strain could have directly triggered virulence towards humans, it is not certain that this intermediary exists.

Coronavirus Symptoms:

Corona Virus is primarily spread by respiratory droplet, which means one will get affected if someone is six feet near the contagious body or touching the object which has the Virus on it. It spreads from various sources.

The symptoms of COVID-19 can appear as early as two days or as long as 14 days after exposure to the Virus, and it includes Fever, Cough, Flu-like symptoms, and shortness of breath.

Coronavirus Vaccine/Medication:

There aren't any single FDA approved vaccines and drugs to cure this Virus directly. However, the FDA will allow treatment by Plasma treatment from the survivors on a conditional basis.

Coronavirus Treatment:

The patient is mostly kept under complete isolation inside a particular ward, and they are cured with supportive care, such as rest, Fluid dose, and fever relief to relieve symptoms. For critical cases, treatment mostly includes the ICU Intensive care unit to support the functioning of vital organs such as lungs and heart.

Coronavirus Preventive Measures:

We have been repeatedly told by Authority and the expert concerned to maintain the social distance. The best we can do is to stop Viruses from spreading by avoiding Social gathering, unnecessary foreign traveling, and maintaining distance from the crowd.

Most of the country has gone into complete lockdown to avoid gathering and spreading of this Virus since this Virus can spread in one way or another.

The World health organization gives these of some of the preventive measure and instruction:

  1. Wash your hand for at least 15-20 seconds with soap and water.
  2. If soap and water are not available, use a hand sanitizer with at least 60% alcohol in it.
  3. Avoid touching your eyes, nose, and mouth with unsafe and unwashed hands.
  4. Use masks when you are sick and stay at home and isolated from the rest of the family members.
  5. Cover your cough and sneeze with a tissue and dispose of it properly in the trash.
  6. Clean your house with cleansers phenyl and your surroundings.
  7. Avoid close contacts.
