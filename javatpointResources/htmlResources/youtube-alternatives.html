YouTube Alternatives

YouTube is one of the largest video streaming platforms that allow uses to watch free online videos. YouTube is owned by Google. Currently, more than 1 billions viewer watch YouTube videos every day.

The most popular YouTube Alternatives

  1. Dailymotion
  2. Vimeo
  3. Metacafe
  4. Veoh
  5. The Internet Archive
  6. Facebook Watch
  7. TED
  8. 9GAG TV
  9. Vevo
  10. Internet Archives Video Section
  11. Crackle

1. Dailymotion

Dailymotion is a simple and clean video strimming platform most probably similar to YouTube. It was introduced in March 2005. Its home page includes various trending videos, playlists, categories, and a strong search bar. Its search bar allows us to easily search our desirable videos within seconds.

Advantages of Dailymotion

A list of advantages of Dailymotion is given below -

  • Dailymotion provides better video quality at less price.
  • It is easy-to-use.
  • It provides an excellent way to deliver content.
  • It is a more flexible video strimming platform.
  • Less risk of content lost or block.
  • Visitors do not need to pay money to watch the published content.
  • No need to create an account to watch videos.

Disadvantage of Dailymotion

  • Dailymotion is not an ad-free platform.
  • Its free version does not allow creators to upload more than 4GB of data.
  • The video length is 60 minutes.

Platform

Dailymotion is supported by Web, Andriod, iOS, and Windows devices.

Website:

Click on the given link to learn more about Dailymotion - https://www.dailymotion.com/in

2. Vimeo

Vimeo is one of the best and most popular alternative as well as competitors of YouTube. It allows creators to upload high-quality video content using 4K Ultra HD visuals with HDR. This platform is mainly used by professionals who work in the fields of music, cinematography, photography, etc. Currently, more than 240 million active users present on Vimeo.

Advantages of Vimeo

A list of advantages of Vimeo is given below -

  • Vimeo provides better and high-quality video content.
  • It is an extremely easy-to-use platform.
  • It is an ad-free platform so that you can watch videos without interruption.
  • It provides a neat and good looking interface.
  • It supports 360-degree videos and TV series.

Disadvantages of Vimeo

  • Limited creators.
  • It allows creators to upload 500MB of data weekly.
  • It is not completely free for creators.

Platform:

Vimeo is supported by Web, Android, Windows, Xbox One, Chromecast, Fire TV, and macOS.

Website:

Click on the given link to login with the Vimeo platform - https://vimeo.com/log_in

3. Metacafe

Metacafe is the oldest video strimming platform introduced in 2003. It allows the creator to create short video clips within the range of 90 seconds. Due to the video time limit of 90 seconds, Metacafe is also known as the mini YouTube alternative.

Advantages of Metacafe

A list of advantages of Metacafe is given below -

  • Metacafe is an excellent platform for short video strimming.
  • There is no age restriction to use the Metacafe platform.
  • No need to create an account to watch videos.

Disadvantages of Metacafe

  • Metacafe is not a good platform for creating a video on complex content.
  • Its privacy policies are not good for creators.
  • Videos on Metacafe will not appear after 24 hours.

Platform

It is supported by the Web and Android platforms.

Website:

Click on the given link to learn more about Metacafe - https://www.metacafe.com/

4. Veoh

Veoh is another most popular YouTube alternatives, launched in the year 2010. It provides a vast library of TV shows and films. It provides high-quality content with various videos, including games, education, film, TV shows, and many more. It allows users to connect with other users, join their groups, and chat with each other.

Advantages of Veoh

There are the following advantages of Veoh -

  • Veoh is easy-to-navigate.
  • It allows creators to upload videos of unlimited length.
  • It is easily embedded with any other website.
  • It allows visitors to watch HD video content.
  • To view video content, it is not compulsory to download Veoh Web player.
  • It provides a clean UI with categorized menus.

Disadvantages of Veoh

A list of disadvantages of Veoh is given below -

  • Its libraries are messy and unorganized.
  • Diversity of content is limited.

Platform

It is supported by Web, Android, and Windows platforms.

Website:

Click on the given link to know more about Veoh - http://ll-appserver.veoh.com/

5. DTube

DTube is one of the easy, simple, and interesting YouTube alternatives. It is the strongest live streaming platform owned by Amazon. It is the more secure video sharing platform as compared to YouTube. To prevent all available content from the hackers, DTube uses Blockchain to store available video content.

Advantages of DTube

There are the following advantages of Dtube -

  • Dtube is an ad-free video strimming platform.
  • It is a more secure video sharing platform as compared to other YouTube alternatives.
  • It provides a user-friendly interface.
  • It is a decentralized platform.
  • In DTube, users who only leave comments on the videos will also earn money.

Disadvantages of Dtube

  • Most of the users face problems related to uploading.
  • In DTube, if you upload a video, then it is not possible to delete the video.
  • Need to improve UI.

Platform

The Web and Andriod platforms support it.

Website

Click on the given link to know about Dtube - https://d.tube/

6. Twitch

Twitch is also one of the best open-source live video streaming platforms in recent years. It is mostly used by gamers. It is similar to YouTube. It is a more user-friendly and free video hosting platform for the users. Except for games, it also includes content related to music, arts, and TV series. It is owned by Amazon. More than 611.40M users per month visit the Twitch video sharing platform.

Advantages of Twitch

A list of advantages of the Twitch video sharing platform is given below -

  • Twitch allows players to share their game tips on a social platform and also interacts with other players.
  • It allows gamers to live broadcast their game.
  • It provides various free games, subscriptions, and ad-free streaming.
  • It is a free platform for everyone.
  • All registered users have the capability to stream all content.
  • It allows us to interact with other users.

Disadvantages of Twitch

  • Twitch is only targeted towards games.
  • It is not suitable for non-gaming content.

Platform

Twitch platform is supported by Web, Android, macOS, Windows, and Android.

Website

Click on the given link to know more about the Twitch video sharing platform - https://www.twitch.tv/

7. TED

TED is the best video platform used to holds conferences around the world. It is a simple video sharing platform that allows creators to share video content on many topics, including nature, technology, science, education, music, social change, health, psychology, and many more.

Advantages of TED

A list of advantages of TED is given below -

  • TED includes a vast library of inspiring videos related to technology, science, health, nature, and more.
  • It allows creators to hosts more than 3000 videos.
  • It provides reliable information.
  • TED is completely (100%) free and limitless.
  • It is a suitable platform to learn something new.

Disadvantages of TED

  • The information presented by TED is not very comprehensive.
  • It does not allow users to upload their own educational videos.

Platform

TED is supported by Mac as well as Web.

Website:

Click on the given link to learn more about TED - https://www.ted.com/attend/ted-on-screen/ted-live

  • It is very easy and quick to search videos on 9GAG TV.

Advantages of Vevo

A list of advantages of Vevo is given below -

  • Vevo provides a large number of high-quality music videos.
  • It is a more user-friendly video strimming platform as compared to YouTube.
  • It is more suitable for music enthusiasts.
  • It always provides High definition content.

Disadvantages of Vevo

  • It not available worldwide.

10. Internet Archives Video Section

Internet Archives Video Section is a web-based website. This video strimming platform is used to store various libraries related to books, software, movies, TV shows, as well as old documentaries.

Advantages of Internet Archives Video Section

A list of advantages of the Internet Archives Video Section is given below -

  • It allows visitors to search their desirable videos by year, topic, subject, and language.
  • It allows us to upload video content for free (freely).
  • It has a vast collection of historical content.
  • It offers an easy and straightforward way to download videos.
  • Its User interface is simple.

Disadvantages of Internet Archives Video Section

  • It is not a good platform for creators.

11. Crackle

Crackle is a free, ad-supported video streaming platform founded by Sony Picture Entertainment. It is the best YouTube alternative to watch TV shows and Hollywood movies such as Night of the Living Dead, Animal House, Paranormal, Rudy, and many more.

Advantages of Crackle

A list of advantages of Crackle is given below -

  • Crackle provides free on-demand entertainment videos.
  • We don't need to create an account for watching videos.
  • Crackle registration process is simple, easy, and straightforward.

Disadvantages of Crackle

  • Lots of ads appear while watching the videos.
  • It does not support some sophisticated features like full HD streams as well as offline downloads.
  • A high-speed internet connection is required to watch videos.

Platform

Crackle video streaming platform used on Android, Mac, iOS, as well as Web platforms.

Website

Click on the given link to watch on-demand entertainment videos on Crackle - https://www.crackle.com/out-of-region.html

12. Flickr

Flickr is the most popular platform to upload, organize, share and, host photos as well as videos. It is developed by a small Canadian development team in 2002 and acquired by Yahoo. It allows us to watch and upload videos in less than 90 seconds.

Advantages of Flickr

A list of advantages of Flickr is given below -

  • It allows us to upload High-quality, HD video content.
  • Its free account offers 1 TB memory space and the pro account offers unlimited memory space.
  • It supports any video format.
  • It is the best website for those who take more interest in photography and videography.
  • It is used to download free videos and images.
  • It is very easy to search content on the Flickr

Disadvantages of Flickr

  • Flickr support limited features.
  • It allows creators to upload videos up to 3 minutes only.

Platform

Flickr is supported by Mac, iOS, Android, and Web platforms.

Website

Click on the given link to learn more about Flickr website - https://www.flickr.com/about

13. PeerTube

PeerTube is an open-source, decent, clean, fresh, decentralized, peer-to-peer, and ad-free high-quality video streaming platform. It is introduced by ActivityPub and WebTorrent. It provides a creator-friendly environment. More than 99.56K users monthly visit PeerTube.

Advantages of PeerTube

A list of advantages of PeerTube is given below -

  • It is an ad-free platform, so it doesn't interrupt users while watching online videos.
  • It provides a simple, neat, and ad-free platform.
  • It allows creators to easily publish their content across the world.
  • It is an open-source and decentralized platform for watching and sharing videos.
  • No signups are required to use the PeerTube video streaming platform.
  • It uses peer-to-peer technology.

Disadvantages of PeerTube

There are the following disadvantages of PeerTube -

  • PeerTube is quite difficult to install because it requires a lot of JS packages.
  • It does not support a huge collection of content.
  • Less interactive UI.
  • Video making library is not good as compared to other its competitors.

Platform

PeerTube is more suitable for the web.

Website

Click on the given link to visit and learn more about PeerTube - https://joinpeertube.org/

14. IGTV

IGTV stands for Instagram TV. It is one of the best free platforms to watch vertical and long-form videos on our Android and iOS smartphones. It was released in June 2018. It is a mobile-optimized application that can be used alone or with Instagram.

Advantages of IGTV

A list of advantages of IGTV is given below -

  • IGTV provides an easy-to-navigate interface.
  • It allows us to share videos with our friends.
  • It is used by a large community.
  • Any user can set up their own channel and share video content on it.
  • It offers an attractive and easy-to-navigate UI (User Interface).

Disadvantages of IGTV

  • IGTV does not support horizontal videos.
  • Restricted video length (up to 1 hour).
  • Limited vertical videos are available on IGTV.
  • Video editing with good production value is difficult.

Platform

IGTV is supported by various platforms like Web, Android, and iOS.

Website

Click on the given link to visit and learn more about the IGTV - https://about.instagram.com/blog/announcements/welcome-to-igtv
