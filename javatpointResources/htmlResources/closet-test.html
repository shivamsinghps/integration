Closet Test

In Closet Test verbal ability questions, there will be a paragraph with missing words or empty spaces which are numbered from 1 to 5. You are required to fill this space one by one by choosing the most appropriate word out of the four options given in the question.

Solved "Closet Test" verbal ability questions:

1)

Today, doing business is not easy for businessmen, as they are not used to competition. In the past they were selling whatever .....(1)......produced and at their desired price. But, 2... the competition has increased over the years, customers started to ....3.....and choose. Imports have become.....4....available and that too at cheaper .......5...........

1. Solve as per the direction given above

  1. he
  2. them
  3. it
  4. they

Answer: D

2. Solve as per the direction given above

  1. with
  2. as
  3. when
  4. only

Answer: B

3. Solve as per the direction given above

  1. pick
  2. select
  3. want
  4. take

Answer: A

4. Solve as per the direction given above

  1. costly
  2. not
  3. easily
  4. hardly

Answer: C

5. Solve as per the direction given above

  1. inputs
  2. costs
  3. profits
  4. prices

Answer: D
