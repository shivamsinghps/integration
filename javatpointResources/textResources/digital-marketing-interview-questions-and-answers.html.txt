h3 => [ 7) What do you know about PPC? ]
p => [ PPC stands for Pay Per Click. It is a digital marketing method in which an advertiser has to pay to the publisher for each click on the links of his website. It is also known as Cost Per Click (CPC). ]
p => [ PPC is associated with Google AdWords or Microsoft Bing Ads. ]
h3 => [ 8) How many ways are there to determine the charges for PPC? ]
p => [ There are two ways to determine the charges for PPC: ]
p => [  PPC Bidding:  In this method, an amount is specified by the advertiser to go for a bid. The advertiser specifies a maximum amount which he is ready to pay for each time a user clicks on ad. This method is also called max CPC. This is the most used type. ]
p => [  Flat rate PPC:  As the name specifies, it is a predefined charge for advertising between the advertiser and the publisher. The charges may differ from page to page and content to content. ]

li => [ How competitive your keywords are  ]
