h3 => [ 27) What is trial balance, and what is the process to check the trial balance in Tally ERP 9? ]
p => [ Trial balance is mainly a list of all the general ledger accounts within the ledger of a business. This list consists of the name of each nominal ledger account and the value of that nominal ledger balance. Each nominal ledger account contains either a debit balance or a credit balance. All debit and credit balances are kept and compiled into the debit and credit account column. Every organization prepares a trial balance periodically, usually at the end of every reporting period.  ]
p => [ Trial Balance is a way to verify that the journal entries are entered correctly in the general ledger. In the debit and credit column, all debit balances should be equal to the total of all the credit balances.  ]
